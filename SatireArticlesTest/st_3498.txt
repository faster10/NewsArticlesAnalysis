Here is some background about the issue : In the late 19th century the Ottoman Empire ' s Armenian minority , numbering an estimated 2 million , was encouraged by exiled groups in the United States , Geneva and in the Georgian capital , Tbilisi , to assert its nationalism . Repression by Ottoman irregulars , mainly Kurds , led to the massacre of hundreds of thousands of Armenians in eastern Anatolia in 1894 1896 . Several thousand more were killed in Constantinople in August 1896 after Armenian extremists seized the Ottoman Bank to draw attention to their cause .
