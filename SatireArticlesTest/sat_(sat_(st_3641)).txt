Banks raked in record breaking profits of 120 . 6 billion in 2003 , 15 percent over the previous high profits set in 2002 . Citigroup , the world ' s largest bank , announced 18 billion in profit . Bank of America , the world ' s second largest bank , reported 10 . 8 billion in profit , and the next three largest banks , JP Morgan , Bank One and Wells Fargo , collectively reported 16 . 4 billion in profit . Bank profits now amount to one third of all company profits , according to The Banker magazine , up from less than 5 percent 20 years ago and 25 percent above their average .
