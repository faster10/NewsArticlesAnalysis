The legal battle that lasted for years , was centered on the possession of Ch teau du Plessis Bourr , a Ch teau of the Loire Valley in France , situated in the commune of cuill in the Maine et Loire department . It was built in less than 5 years from 1468 to 1472 , by the Finance Minister Jean Bourr , an advisor of King Louis XI . It was acquired by the Reille Soult de Dalmatie family in 1911 and classified as an historical monument in 1931 .
