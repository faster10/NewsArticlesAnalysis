During this particularly brutal winter , bitterly cold temperatures have not been limited to the Eastern region of the United States . Reports of a record breaking freeze covering over 80 of the Great Lakes ' total surface area have stunned meteorologists and weather fans alike . A " thundersnow " event just one of many extreme weather incidents occurring this year sent The Weather Channel ' s Jim Cantore , " America ' s Meteorolgist , " into a frenzy during a live Winter Storm Neptune report from Plymouth , MA . His excitement was called " Howard Dean esque " by some .
