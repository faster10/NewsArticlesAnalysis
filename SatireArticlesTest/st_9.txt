The 50 year embargo policy against Cuba from the United States has kept the country , only 90 miles off the coast of Florida , one of the poorest in the world . Economist say the embargo has not worked . Stanford economics professor Walter White told The Wall Street Journal , " Any common sense would say the embargo was a failure . All it did was make people suffer while we gained nothing in return . If we are still afraid of Communism , then God help us all .
