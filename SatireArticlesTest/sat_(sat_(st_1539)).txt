The actors ' agents and representatives put a dollar figure on the table , the network counter offered , and when the agents refused the initial counter offer , FX reportedly went straight to " Plan B , " replacing the original cast with a team of backup actors , standard practice in Hollywood as " insurance " in case performers leave a show due to death or injury , or become unavailable due to previous contractual obligations or disputes . To the actors ' credit , the entire cast decided if one person were let go , they would all leave together .
