Man ' s ancestors knew the importance of stocking up on food to survive through the long and harsh winters . This instinct is a genetic trait that remains with us still to this day , with some people experiencing it more keenly than others . The foresight to pack on pounds shows an undeniable edge in the person ' s evolution . Like a bear devouring hundreds of salmon in a creek prior to hibernation , geniuses can often be observed " bulking up " on fast food before hunkering down for a long session perusing their craft .
