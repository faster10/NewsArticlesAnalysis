Nestled among gently rolling hills , Silicon Pines is proud to be the only Assisted Computing Facility in the world to practice " Degenerative Computing in Place . " This is a unique blend of housing and personal computing assistance that allows you to maintain your independence and maximize your quality of technological life , while allowing you to fall further and further behind at your own pace , without any pressure . Regardless of how technologically impaired you are , or how often your computing care needs change , you will always have a home at Silicon Pines .
