" If it rains tomorrow , will you get back together with me ? " , " What if I ' m not good enough ? " , " Jennifer wondered if David was over her or not . " Look at those previous sentences very closely . They have one thing in common : the word " if . " As a man , you may have noticed that your actions have consequences . Indeed , many consequences are conditional upon some antecedent . This is the power of " if . " " If " enables a speaker to specify a condition which , if fulfilled , may lead to some consequence . " If " may also express uncertainty . There are many more uses for " if . " Consult a dictionary for more information .
