BIRMINGHAM , AL Swimming in a large sea of running apps helping to get people active and create runners out of previous non runners is a lone game changing app that is slowly gaining momentum and , in doing so , slowing down everyone else ' s . The app is called 5K to Couch or 5K2C . What is the goal of the app ? To quickly transition you from an active , healthy 5 kilometer ( 5K ) runner to a lazy , apneic couch bum ( 0K ) and help you in the fight for obesity .
