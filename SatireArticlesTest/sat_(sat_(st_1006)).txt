" Karen said she no longer wanted to be a healthcare practitioner , she just wanted to be comfortable , " said palliative care physician Laura Harrison . An expert in burnout , Harrison has helped hundreds of thousands of nurses , nurse practitioners , physician assistants , social workers , therapists , nutritionists , doctors , and others by attending to their pain and symptoms as well as their emotional and spiritual needs . " We had a family meeting . Karen ' s a DNR . We took away her stethoscope and white coat . We inactivated her pager . A chaplain was present . "
