The implementation of the new tax credit originates from the most recent in a string of outside the box executive orders given by president Obama . It comes as no surprise that representatives from the Republican Party have spoken out against the implementation of the corporate tax credit however economists speculate that even the most republican owned corporations will be taking advantage of the new tax credit . Craig Willford , a 20 year political commentator for The San Diego Star wrote " We should all face the facts , as much as the GOP will want to publicly refute this tax credit , the savings will be too large for even the most red of corporations to pass up .
