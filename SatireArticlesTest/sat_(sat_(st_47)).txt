Most of these papers carry orders emerging from German ambassador Hans Freiherr von Wangenheim , and are destined to two Russian agents codenamed " der Bankier " ( the banker ) and " Maulwurf " ( the mole ) , which have been identified respectively as Alexander Parvus and Vladimir Lenin . According to these documents , Parvus would have served as Lenin ' s liaison officer , transmitting him the orders and the material that came from the Germans through Jakob F rstenberg , Lenin ' s most trusted agent who was also known by various aliases such as Hanecki , Kuba , or his Party name , Ganetsky .
