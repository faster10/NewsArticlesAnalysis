Lynn ' s career , full of ups and downs , has in recent years hit a series of high notes . Her popularity was reinvigorated in 2004 with the Jack White produced Van Lear Rose . She charmed audiences at the 2011 Bonaroo Festival in Manchester , Tennessee . The 1980 film Coal Miner ' s Daughter , earned actress Cissy Spacek an Academy Award for her portrayal of Lynn . Politics , it would seem , would be the furthest thing on the 83 year old country singer ' s mind or bucket list .
