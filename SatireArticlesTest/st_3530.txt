" We created reasonable measures of what constitutes a decent overall economic climate , " said Robert Pollin , professor of economics and co director of PERI . " You can ' t make a case that bad working conditions for workers are good for business . The correlation is stronger , that good working conditions make for a better business climate . " The study ranked each state in three categories : Job opportunities , including unemployment and part time work job quality , including average wages , adjusted cost of living , and health and retirement benefits and workplace fairness , including gender equality , minimum wages , collective bargaining rights , and right to work programs , to form a work environment index .
