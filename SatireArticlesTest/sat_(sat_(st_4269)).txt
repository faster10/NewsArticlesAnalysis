Bush also achieved new records in foreign relations as Europeans declare him the most despised U . S . president in history . After gaining the sympathy of the world after the attack of September 11 , 2001 , Bush ' s invasion of Iraq turned world opinion against the U . S . One Islamic educator said , " It took Israel 55 years to create the hatred and enmity with the Arab world . But it took Bush one year . " Now the public in formerly friendly countries such as Morocco and Jordan register confidence in the U . S . in single digits , while support in Saudi Arabia has fallen from 66 percent in 2000 to below 10 percent today .
