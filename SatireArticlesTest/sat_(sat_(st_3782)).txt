Sen Rick Renzi ( R AZ ) Rick Renzi is currently the subject of two Justice Department investigations . First , he is under investigation for his involvement in a federal land swap deal . While officials have not revealed details of the probe , it is widely believed that it surrounds legislation Renzi agreed to introduce in 2005 completing a land swap for a former business partner , James Sandlin . Second , Renzi is being probed for introducing legislation ( which became law ) exempting the Fort Huachuca Army base in Arizona from maintaining water levels in the nearby San Pedro River .
