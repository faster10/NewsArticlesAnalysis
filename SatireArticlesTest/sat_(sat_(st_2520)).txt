The documentary ' s producer called for help using a satellite phone , as the group floated into the " Knik Arm , " a body of water between Palin ' s hometown of Wasilla and Alaska ' s largest , most populated city , Anchorage . The US Coast Guard scrambled two helicopters at approximately 11 : 38 am local time , finding and saving the group about an hour later .
