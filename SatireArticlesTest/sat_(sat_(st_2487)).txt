According to authorities , the new system ' s first offender was Roberto Fett . He was arrested in Roswell on suspicion of bounty hunting without a license and was jailed and later deported . Fett is now facing federal charges for re entering the United States . Nash Olo was also recently apprehended . He is accused of drug smuggling and human trafficking . According to RPD , 20 year old Olo is here illegally and could be deported under the new policy . Officials said he had a history of these crimes as a juvenile , but the current system would not have detected his immigration status because it does not apply to minors .
