Fugitives Richard Matt and David Sweat , who escaped from a New York State prison on June 6th , have left behind what one State Trooper refers to as " a trail of breadcrumbs , " which have led investigators to searching in counties in southwestern New York along the Pennsylvania border . But as details emerge regarding where the escaped convicts have hidden in the past few weeks , one fact becomes clear : the two men have taken to hiding in plain sight , even right under the noses of the news media literally .
