DALLAS , TX As construction continues on the new 300 bed children ' s hospital here , officials today announced some exciting design details to the general public . According to the newly released plans , the cutting edge pediatric medical center will feature a soaring atrium with a four story waterfall , lovable cartoon character exhibits to greet children inside the front doors , a half dozen gourmet restaurants , and adorable animal footprints on the floor that will lead arriving patients and families from the entrance directly to the hospital ' s billing department .
