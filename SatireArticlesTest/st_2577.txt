HOLLYWOOD , CA A remake of the HBO series The Sopranos has begun production , set for a fall 2016 debut . Actor Val Kilmer will produce , direct and star as mob boss Tony Soprano , according to television industry insiders . Kilmer , best known for 1995 ' s Batman Forever and director Oliver Stone ' s 1991 rock biopic The Doors is confident that The Sopranos II , an eight part miniseries , will be appreciated by the millions of fans devoted to the old series , while introducing new fans to the backstory of how Tony Soprano became Tony Soprano .
