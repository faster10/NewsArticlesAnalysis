Pamphlets written in Spanish have turned up in border towns all across the American southwest encouraging illegals to head north to weed friendly states like Colorado and Washington . One such pamphlet ' s cover photo depicts a giant pot leaf over an American flag with the words Cultivar y Vender Mota , which translates to Grow and Sell Marijuana . The pamphlet gives all the information needed in order to claim everything from Social Security and EBT cards , Earned Income Tax Credits , welfare , driver ' s licenses , and now marijuana seller permits .
