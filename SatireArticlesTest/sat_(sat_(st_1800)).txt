Technology , having enhanced society with much good , has exponentially increased the allure of the jamband stage show . Kesey ' s ink blot projections may have delighted the acid test hippies in the 1960 ' s , but compared to the stunning jamband shows of today with their banks of spinning multicolored lights , fog machines , screens , and exploding pyrotechnics , they seem quite quaint and harmless . All one has to do for proof is book The Phish at the Winterland Arena and see how fast it sells out .
