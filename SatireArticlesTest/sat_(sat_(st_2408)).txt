The Ronald Reagan Memorial Presidential Library , which acts as a repository for important papers related to the Reagan presidency , contains among it ' s campaign records for that year several references to walk in volunteers " J . B . Duggar " and " M . Duggar " , who were , according to the documents , on vacation with their little children , and just showed up to volunteer . But it seems the family posed some logistical problems for the campaign staffers , due primarily to the presence of so many young children .
