News of the Clinton pardon comes in the wake of seven federal lawsuits filed against the former Secretary of State for activities surrounding her private email accounts , which prosecutors allege Clinton improperly used to run a " criminal enterprise " by engaging personal email servers in order to barter access to influential Clinton Foundation potential donors . Highly confidential and potentially damaging national security information is said to have been put at risk , suppressed , or deleted . Legal and ethical protocol violations also have been alleged against Clinton , the presumptive 2016 Democratic nominee for president .
