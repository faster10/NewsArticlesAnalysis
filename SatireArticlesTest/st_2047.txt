Dogg will be making his TV debut as Doc Baker , the tough and wizened caretaker of the Walnut Grove citizenry . Also noteworthy is Justin Beiber in the role of Nellie Ingalls . " The Beeb " was a long shot to land the part , but hard work and determination won out as he impressed the casting director and staff with his portrayal of the nasty Nellie Oleson . Other notables include Miley Cyrus in the challenging role of Laura , ex porn star Ginger Lynn Allen as Caroline , and Helena Bohnam Carter as Mrs Oleson , the stuck up proprietor of the Walnut Grove Mercantile Exchange .
