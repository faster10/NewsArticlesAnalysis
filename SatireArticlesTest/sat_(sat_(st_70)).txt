" We have found hundreds of religious and household items , like statuettes , vases , dishes and tools " states Semir Osmanagi . " Some bowls still contained traces of a mixture of bananas and beans , a dish greatly resembling the porridge like " kiembo " , which still constitutes the main dish of the Ugweno people . They seemed to have been more advanced in terms of technology than their descendants , since we found many clay representations of bullock carts as well as a few iron tools and spear heads . "
