The film , set in current day Iraq , follows Dick and George from humble beginnings as buck privates as they soar up the ranks to positions of power . They two meet many familiar faces such as Sadam Hussein and even his two boys , who they wipe out in a hilarious scene featuring a box of missing explosives and a vial of nerve gas . The action is absolutely non stop as the movie races towards a rip roaring reprise of the most famous movie ending in history .
