While the words " under God " were only added to the Pledge by Congress in 1954 , God has been the title patron of the United States since its founding in 1776 , and the God brand adorns everything from U . S . currency to the phrase " So help me God " used to swear in the President . The three judge panel who voted to sever that 226 year relationship has come under heavy fire , but many have defended the decision , saying it is wrong to force religion on anyone .
