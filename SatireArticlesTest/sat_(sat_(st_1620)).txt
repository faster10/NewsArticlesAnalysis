It has been an active week for the former Bruce Jenner . On July 15 in Los Angeles , Caitlyn Jenner stood before the country ' s biggest sports stars and accepted the Arthur Ashe Courage Award , challenging the audience to accept transgender people for who the are , and asking them to focus their concerns on transgender youth , affected by higher rates of bullying , assault , homelessness , depression and suicide . The Arthur Ashe Courage Award honors sports figures whose courage and honesty " resonates beyond sports . "
