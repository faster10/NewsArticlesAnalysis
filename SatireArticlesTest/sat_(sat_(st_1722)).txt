With seemingly nowhere to go , Stapp ended up at the gates of The White House , where he screamed vulgarities at uniformed Secret Service agents for about six minutes before they managed to get him to leave . But Stapp returned later that evening , at approximately 11 pm , and after successfully jumping the White House fence , proceeded to curl up with a gray blanket under a bush on the White House lawn , where he slept until about 5 am , when he woke up and jumped the fence again to leave the area .
