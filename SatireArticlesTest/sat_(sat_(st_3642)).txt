Oil profits soared as oil prices doubled over the past three years . Oil reached a record 78 a barrel , gas prices broke one record after another , and , in January 2006 , Exxon Mobil announced record profits for 2005 of 36 billion on 371 billion in sales , a record revenue for any American company ever . Profits rose 40 percent over previous record profits set in 2004 , and this October , Exxon announced 10 . 49 billion in profit , the second largest quarterly profit in their history . Chevron also announced 5 billion in profit in October , their highest quarterly profit ever , overshadowing the former high set during the previous quarter .
