After years of antagonistic partisan debate following tragic school shootings such as the one at Sandy Hook Elementary School in Connecticut in 2012 , NRA President James W . Porter decided it was necessary to find a way to ease its tensions with gun control advocates . In an official statement regarding the NRA re branding , Porter wrote that " the American conversation about gun ownership has stalled due to heightened emotions on both sides of the discussion about guns ' rightful place in our society . I commit myself today to changing that dialogue . " His solution is to encourage discussion through the shared joy of entertainment .
