Bruce Jenner ' s transition generally has been met with positive reaction from both corners of the political spectrum . " President Obama said , " It takes courage to share your story " , although Fox Business Channel reporter Dagen McDowell repeatedly referred to the former Olympian derisively with male pronouns , and Neil Cavuto likened the news to the fall of civilization : " Rome , final days , " he said , before asking , " what is going on ?
