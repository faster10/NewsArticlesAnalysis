At the Alpine Hills Music Center near East Troy , WI , a riot broke out during the second set of The Phish ' s concert . Phans ripped up sod and hurled it through the crowd along with pool cue sized glow rods . Over 150 were injured with concussions , some severely enough to require emergency medical attention . A simultaneous " We Hate Jesus " drum circle and fireworks riot erupted outside the venue which was finally quelled by national guard troops ordered into emergency duty by Gov . Scott Walker .
