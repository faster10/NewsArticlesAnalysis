According to the deal , Bachmann herself will star in at least half of the programs , and will make occasional guest appearances in all of them . At launch , the network will feature Bachmann hosting a talk show , a " variety show , " and a program where Bachmann interviews Americans on the street to ask them about domestic and foreign affairs , similar to a segment on the Palin Channel that recently sparked controversy , only extended for a full half hour . She ' ll also host a fashion show and even a weekly cooking show , with celebrity chefs partnering up with her to teach Bachmann how to make a variety of dishes . Paula Deen is already signed up for the premier episode , and will make six appearances in the show ' s first season .
