When purchasing tickets online passengers will be asked to enter both their itinerary and their height and weight . If their dimensions exceed what is considered to be reasonably healthy , a 5 fee will be added for every ten pounds above the standards set by the AMA . Since online fraud is a very real possibility , check in stewards will have the right to insist the passenger stand on the luggage scale before receiving their ticket . Passengers who don ' t match the provided information will be fined double . Passengers who become pregnant between the time of purchase and the actual flight should notify the airline in advance .
