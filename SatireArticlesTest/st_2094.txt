When asked why George R . R . Martin is considering such a unique maneuver , one of his publicists said that the author is " sick unto death of all the whining " . His followers , at times , have become almost violent in their demands for more tales from the Seven Kingdoms . It is hoped by Martin ' s team that this tactic will at least soothe the most aggressive of the bunch rather like tossing one red , juicy steak to a massive pack of hungry lions .
