The tributes to late pop singer Prince have been many in the days following his untimely passing at the age of 57 . In Minnesota , the Interstate 35 West bridge was lit purple in Prince ' s honor . Similar tributes happened in New Orleans , San Francisco , and New York . The death of Prince has affected millions of Americans , and the first family is not immune to the sadness of a life cut short too soon . President Obama issued a statement shortly after Prince ' s death was confirmed proclaiming his and the First Lady ' s condolences for Prince ' s family , friends , and fans . However , as time passed , many in the White House said that Obama ' s mood took an " even more somber turn " .
