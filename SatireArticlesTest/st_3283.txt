A scimitar is a sword characterized by its curved , single edged blade . Most scimitars are about 76 92 cm in length and weigh less than three pounds . 1 Their light weight and curved design are a deadly combination on horseback . In the Ottoman Empire , scimitars were generally forged of Valyrian steel , but nowadays most are made of metal .
