Not much is known about the North Korean Sports Hall of Fame as , with most things in North Korea , information is hard to come by . The Hall is assumed by many to only include one other inductee up to now Kim Jong Il , the late former leader of the nation . Kim Jong Il ' s sporting feats , as reported to the North Korean citizens , were legendary . As Kim Jong Il was only reported to be a world class athlete at golf , bowling , soccer and basketball it is also assumed that Pete Rose will be the first baseball player to be inducted into the nation ' s Hall of Fame .
