After Mr Pettinger had been apprehended and taken to the county prison , he called Everett M . Clark , pastor of Opp ' s Holy Tabernacle of the Flaming Tongues . When Rev . Clark arrived at the prison , Mr Pettinger said he was ready to accept Jesus into his heart . He asked Rev . Clark to pray with him , and the two men knelt in the cell . No sooner had Mr Pettinger finished his prayer than Rev . Clark heard a voice saying , " Tell him I just said no . "
