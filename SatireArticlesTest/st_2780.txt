FAIRFAX , VA At a press conference early Friday morning , National Rifle Association Executive Vice President Wayne LaPierre broke down into uncontrollable sobbing when he started talking to reporters about " all the guns that won ' t get sold after people fail Obama ' s background checks . " Dabbing away tears with gun purchase receipts , LaPierre further bemoaned President Barack Obama ' s recently announced gun control executive orders as being " just one step shy of putting law abiding gun owners in the ovens , burning us , eating our ashes , and then pooping them back into our mothers ' mouths , literally . "
