The book attempts to paint the picture that in her view , minorities are " of a lesser species of human , " and that whites are , quote , " the one true and glorious master race the culmination of everything God has intended for mankind . " In her draft , she cites and editorializes scientific studies , criminality figures , and numerous other statistics , all of which , in her opinion , points to whites being " the stronger , smarter , and generally more advanced race of humankind .
