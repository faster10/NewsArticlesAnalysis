In Williamsburg , N . Y . , an enclave of hipster culture , N gre Chic caught on almost overnight . Tobias Djuna Holmstrom , a co owner of The Bonny Iver clothing store , says sales of slave clothing , chains and whips have far outpaced everything else . " Last week , nobody had ever heard of N gre Chic , which was a major selling point , " Holmstrom says . " Now , everyone is dressing la N gre . It ' s a great way to assert your individuality in a society where everyone looks the same . "
