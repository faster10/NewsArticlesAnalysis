Client confidentiality prevents Ford Center employees from divulging patient information , but a resident of the Center apparently contacted an outside family member and broke the news of Biden ' s arrival . According to the client ' s relative , the Vice President and his wife entered the facility and spoke with a few residents to offer encouragement and to provide information about the Affordable Care Act . The visit lasted for about an hour . When contacted , a spokesperson for the Betty Ford Center said that the family member " was playing a prank " and no such visit had officially taken place .
