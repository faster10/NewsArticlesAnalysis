Martin " Muddy Sanchez , father of a Wisconsin 8th grader , is suing the Brown County school district for a sharting incident that left his daughter humiliated and possibly scarred for life . Mr Sanchez claims that shortly after his daughter finished her lunch , she started feeling ill and before she could even make it to the bathroom , had accidentally soiled her pants . When other students realized what had happened they began to relentlessly tease and bully her . One student named in the lawsuit even recorded the whole embarrassing ordeal on his Smartphone and shared the footage on social media . Muddy says his daughter was left traumatized from the experience and has yet to return to school .
