President Obama set the tone early in his presidency when he and the First Lady set off in Air Force One for a date night to New York City , taking in dinner and a Broadway play . Round trip fuel cost alone for the date exceeded 300k , not including costs for security by an already stretched thin Secret Service . Weekend getaways to the President ' s hometown of Chicago became the norm , including many dinner dates to Table Fifty Two , co owned by Oprah Winfrey ' s former personal chef .
