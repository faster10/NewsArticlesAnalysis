Effective immediately , all Americans ages 18 and older will be expected to register to vote no later than October 1st , 2016 , and must vote on November 8th , 2016 . Those who fail to vote will be penalized with a fine of up to 200 , with repeat offenses increasing that fine by 50 , up to a maximum cap of 500 per federal election . The rules and fees do not apply to local or state elections , nor are they applicable to party primaries or caucuses .
