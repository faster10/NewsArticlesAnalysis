At the turn of the 19th century when Utah was denied statehood because of Federal Law proclaiming polygamy illegal , the Quorum announced that it would no longer sanction the practice , though marriages of the spirit were still allowed , and riding the wave of the Civil Rights Movement in the 1960 ' s , the Quorum proclaimed that Black people do , in fact , have souls , but shouldn ' t be allowed to hold any significant roles within the church . That revelation was amended in the early 1980 ' s , just in time for the founding of a new church in the predominantly black Buenos Aires .
