Though small and large corporations alike are undoubtedly grateful for a large tax break in a struggling economy , the employees of these companies are very concerned about what the new tax break will mean for them . The seemingly obvious downside of this tax credit is that it persuades corporations to hire several new employees without creating new jobs . Expert economists have said the tax credit essentially encourages corporations to terminate tenured employees in order to hire employees far less qualified and in many instances not qualified at all . Gary Rungberg a successful columnist for the Texas based republican publication The Lone Starlight Journal wrote , " Since I can remember , American citizens have complained about illegal immigrants taking their jobs , with the implementation of this new tax credit , it ' s a very real possibility that illegal immigrants will literally take our jobs . "
