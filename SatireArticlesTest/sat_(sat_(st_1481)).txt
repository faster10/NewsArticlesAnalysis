A search request for " reincarnation in YouTube generates thousands of videos about children who somehow seem to be connected to a life long gone . While the natural reaction is to dismiss such talk as made up or fake , Professor Tucker says these " coincidences are , indeed , sometimes only ideas suggested by the child ' s relatives . There are significant cases , however , where private information ( that could only be known by someone intimately familiar with the deceased ) has been revealed and confirmed by the living relatives of the departed .
