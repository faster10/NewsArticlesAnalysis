NRA deputy spokesperson Kyle Lannister explained that the idea for producing " Annie Get Your Gun came from Porter who , last October , took a week long cruise with a friend , and both had greatly enjoyed the nightly musical entertainment . A fellow cruise member had gotten into an argument about the NRA with Porter earlier on the third day of the cruise , but after that evening ' s show , each man found his demeanor transformed by the singing and dancing . The two apologized for their earlier argumentative tone , and Porter realized that , indeed , " music has charms to soothe the savage breast . " A musical theatre lover since his teens , Porter quickly developed this radical idea of producing a show , and the more he thought about it , the more passionate he grew . Back on shore , that passion inspired his NRA team to try something new and entirely unexpected of them .
