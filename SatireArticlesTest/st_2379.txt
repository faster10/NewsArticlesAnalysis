WASHINGTON , D . C . Sources close to the White House are confirming that things " got a little awkward " between Pope Francis and President Barack Obama earlier this week . Apparently , the impetus for the brief bit of tension between Obama and the Pope was over whether or not the White House would validate the Pope ' s parking . His High Holiness had apparently parked his Pope Mobile in a garage a block and a half away from the White House and walked , and was hoping that Obama or his staff could stamp his parking ticket , so he didn ' t have to pay the full day rate . That ' s when things took a minor turn for the slightly less than mundane .
