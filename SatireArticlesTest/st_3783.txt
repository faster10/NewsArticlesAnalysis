Sen . Bob Menendez ( D N . J . ) Bob Menendez is under federal investigation by the U . S . attorney ' s office in Newark , N . J . Specifically , investigators are examining whether Menendez had an improper financial relationship with the North Hudson County Action Corp . , a non profit organization in Union City , N . J . Menendez leased a house to the group from 1994 2003 . During this period , in which he was a member of the House Menendez received over 300 , 000 from the organization in rent payments .
