James Tobin , President George W . Bush ' s New England Campaign Chairman was indicted on four counts of phone harassment conspiracy and aiding and abetting charges . Mr Tobin , who resigned from Mr Bush ' s staff was quoted as saying as he pulled out the sword from his chest that he had fallen on , " I have great respect for the justice system , especially now that the Republicans have won all three Branches of Government , and plan to fight back to clear my name . " ( Mr Tobin was convicted of these charges in December 2005 , leaving his name somewhat less than cleared . )
