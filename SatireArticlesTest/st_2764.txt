Most people know actor Vin Diesel for his role as Dominic Toretto in the " Fast and the Furious " film series . However , there is a darker side to Diesel that only his most diehard fans are aware of . Diesel , 47 , has been an avid gamer for over 20 years , ' Dungeons and Dragons ' being one of his favorites . While this may seem like a strange , albeit benign , hobby for one of Hollywood ' s premiere action stars several members of the underground role playing game community have begun to speak out on issues they have had with Diesel for some time .
