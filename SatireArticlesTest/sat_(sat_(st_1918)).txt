Two , separate , unnamed sources each reported that at a private retreat with major Democratic donors this weekend , Howard Dean spoke about Hillary Clinton ' s plan to ask Republican Jeb Bush to be her running mate for the 2016 Presidential election pending Bush not receiving the Republican nomination . Speaking with the guarantee of anonymity , each of these two sources ( one a fellow speaker , the other a donor ) told of Dean ' s twenty minute dissection of how Hillary ' s team planned to eliminate any potential Democratic rival before he or she can declare their candidacy as well as how she will capture centrist voters by adding Bush to the ticket .
