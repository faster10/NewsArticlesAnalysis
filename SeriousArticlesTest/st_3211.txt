FAILURE OR NOT , H BOMB CLAIM TROUBLES EXPERTS , via our colleague Darius Dixon : Nuclear weapons experts found several large holes Wednesday in North Korea s claim that it has joined the ranks of hydrogen bomb wielding nations , but some called its boasts alarming nonetheless . North Korea announced Wednesday that it had tested a miniaturized hydrogen bomb , calling it a perfect success . Even if that ' s not true , a failed H bomb test can still teach plenty of lessons to a country trying to develop one , said James Acton , who co directs the nuclear policy program at the Carnegie Endowment for International Peace .
