The Giants have struggled all season to find a semblance of a pass rush without Pierre Paul , who led the team in sacks in 2014 with 12 . 5 and has 42 sacks in his five year career . NFL free agent rules allow for only two medical visits to a team , and this is the last one allowed for JPP and the Giants . The two sides have until Nov . 17 to come to a contract agreement , or else Pierre Paul must sit out the entire season .
