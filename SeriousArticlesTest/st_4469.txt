For the second year in a row , One Direction was named artist of the year at the American Music Awards on Sunday night . One Direction also won best pop rock duo or group for the second year running . Taylor Swift , 2013 artist of the year , won honors for best pop rock album ( 1989 ) and song of the year ( " Blank Space " ) , and as favorite adult contemporary artist of 2015 . Other winners include Sam Hunt ( new artist of the year ) , Ed Sheeran ( pop rock male artist ) , Ariana Grande ( pop rock female artist ) , Luke Bryan ( male country artist ) , and Carrie Underwood ( female country artist ) . Celine Dion sang an Edith Piaf song to honor the victims of the Paris attack , while Jennifer Lopez opened and Justin Bieber closed the show with medleys .
