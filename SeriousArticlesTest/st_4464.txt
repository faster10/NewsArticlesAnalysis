While Putin described the main enemy as international terrorism , he uttered Islamic State not once , nor any of the group s other names . A casual listener could have been excused for concluding that the real enemy is Turkey . Putin only made a passing reference to the Russian passenger plane that blew up over Egypt in October , killing all 224 people on board in an attack for which Islamic State claimed responsibility . He also made no mention of an Islamic State video that purportedly shows the beheading of a Russian spy .
