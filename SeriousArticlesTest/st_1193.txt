Highly placed sources told ET that among the documents that will be put out for public display starting January 23 ( also to be put on website ) , the birth anniversary of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose , are reports by Soviet news agency TASS and clippings of news reports from Germany , the United States and Nepal , among others , which claim that the freedom fighter was sighted at certain locations across countries in the 1950s . Images of a person with strong resemblance to Netaji seen at prime minister Jawaharlal Nehru ' s funeral , snapped through video footage shot by the Bombay Films Division , will also be on display at the National Archives , sources confirmed .
