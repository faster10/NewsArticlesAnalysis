Until this recent test , there had been signs that North Korea was opening up , albeit cautiously . There has been a new inter Korean dialogue and family reunions were recently allowed . The Chinese government had recently reached out to North Korea s leadership after a long cooling off period , following the execution of their main interlocutor , Kim s uncle Jang Song thaek . Even the Japanese had some new initiatives in mind to work with North Korea . All of that will now be placed on indefinite hold .
