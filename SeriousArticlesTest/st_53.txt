Former Browns quarterback Brian Hoyer leads the Texans into the playoffs after a saga in which Ryan Mallett , T . J . Yates and Brandon Weeden all saw action because of either the coach s decisions or injuries . Hoyer returned from his second concussion this season in time to lead the Texans to a Week 17 win that clinched their first playoff berth in three years . Despite his injuries , Hoyer set career highs in touchdowns ( 19 ) , completion percentage ( 60 . 7 ) and passer rating ( 91 . 4 ) .
