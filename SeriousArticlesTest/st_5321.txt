Brock Osweiler got the Broncos to the playoffs . But it was Manning who got them to the best record in the AFC with the comeback in the regular season s last game . And it was Manning who got them to the New England Patriots and Tom Brady with perhaps the Broncos quarterback s final chance at a Super Bowl . He did it , not as he has so many times in the past , with long gorgeous spirals into the hands of his receivers but with one of his most rickety games ? an imperfect grind with wobbling throws , dropped passes and a painful plod across the crusty sod .
