Boxing legend Muhammad Ali has criticized Trump ' s proposal to ban Muslims from entering America . Without naming Trump , Ali said that Muslims " have to stand up to those who use Islam to advance their own personal agenda " . The three time world heavyweight champion , 73 , is a cultural icon and one of the world ' s most famous Muslims . Ali ' s statement was directed at " presidential candidates proposing to ban Muslim immigration to the United States " . " They have alienated many from learning about Islam , " he said : BBC
