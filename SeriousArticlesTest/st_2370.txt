Paul Rothemund ' s famous DNA smileys that adorned the cover of Nature magazine in 2006 . Rothemund ' s work led to a boom in DNA nanotechnology . Dr Rothemund used these smileys to show a simpler way to rearrange DNA . He showed that long , single strands of DNA can be folded back and forth to form a basic scaffold . The basic structure is then supplemented by around 200 shorter strands , providing a shape that can bear a complex pattern , such as this smiley face
