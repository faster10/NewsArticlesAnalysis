Finally , of course , he believed with complete determination that democracy is a battle of ideas and non violent dissent must always be given expression and space , however subversive it may seem on the surface . When TV anchors dubbed him a pro Pakistani separatist after Masraat Alam was released , he did not react at all . In fact , he met them later in Mumbai and other cities with the affection reserved for old friends . Forgiveness and an unwillingness to be petty were part of his DNA .
