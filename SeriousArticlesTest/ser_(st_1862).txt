Jameis Winston might not have played his best game of the season , but he might have put together his most impressive drive . With the Bucs trailing 19 16 in the fourth quarter , Winston directed a 12 play , 80 yard drive that led to the game winning touchdown . the most impressive play of the drive came on a third and 19 where Winston looked left for dead after about a nine yard gain . However , Winston somehow spun out of the Falcons ' defense to gain a total of 20 yards on the play .
