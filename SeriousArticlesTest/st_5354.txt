The killing of 129 persons in Paris terror attacks linked to the Islamic State ahead of the Summit has put pressure on global leaders to enhance cooperation in their fight against terrorism . Russian President Vladimir Putin said , " We have all seen the horror that took place recently in Paris and we sympathise with the affected people . " Russia is " always in favour of joining efforts to deal effectively with the terrorist threat , " he said . " Of course it is necessary to act in strict accordance with the United Nations . "
