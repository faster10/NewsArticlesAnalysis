As it stands , the headline consumer price index in the euro zone hovers around zero , and even core inflation remains below one per cent too far for comfort from the ECB ' s target of around two per cent . While a new round of weakness in global commodity prices earlier this year contributed to these figures , it does not explain the weakness in longer term inflation expectations , which have improved little since March , when the ECB started its massive Euro 60 billion ( 66 . 3 billion ) per month bond buying program .
