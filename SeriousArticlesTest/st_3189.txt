Overseas , there are a lot of hopes riding on the European Central Bank ' s ( ECB ) scheduled meeting on Thursday , December 3 , and a lot of fears centred on the US Federal Reserve ' s meeting on December 16 . Pertinent data about US employment in November are due this week and the jobs data are likely to shape the Fed ' s decision to hike rates , or hold . Some form of easing is expected from the ECB . The euro has tumbled against the US dollar , as global currency markets discount this likely policy divergence .
