Facebook owned Oculus started accepting pre orders for its virtual reality headsets on Wednesday , setting a price of 599 . The headsets , which are called the Oculus Rift , are scheduled to be shipped starting on March 28 . The price includes a headset , sensor , Oculus Remote , cables , Xbox One Controller and the games " EVE : Valkyrie , " and " Lucky ' s Tale , " according to Oculus ' website . A bundle that includes an Oculus ready PC and a headset will cost 1499 with pre orders for that package starting in February .
