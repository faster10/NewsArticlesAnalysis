" Burma ' s leaders must come to realize after five decades of sustained internal conflict , economic mismanagement , and international pariah status that Burma needs a better way forward , a way that does not rely on suppression but rather strives to create a truly democratic and prosperous future for the Burmese people , " Clinton said . The United States continues to insist on the " immediate and unconditional release of all political prisoners , " including Suu Kyi , and will maintain its sanctions against leading members of the regime , she said .
