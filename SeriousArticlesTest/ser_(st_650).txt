OIL : An early rise in the price of oil faded . Benchmark U . S . crude slipped 35 cents to close at 37 . 16 a barrel in New York . Brent crude , the international standard , fell 15 cents to close at 40 . 11 a barrel in London . In other trading , wholesale gasoline rose 2 . 8 cents to close at 1 . 232 a gallon , heating oil fell two cents to 1 . 239 a gallon and natural gas fell 0 . 8 cent to 2 . 062 per 1 , 000 cubic feet .
