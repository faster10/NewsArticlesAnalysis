A pair of women suicide bombers killed four people and left a trail of injury in north Cameroon Thursday , the second such attacks this week in a region targeted by Nigeria ' s Boko Haram Islamists . Only two attacks in the town of Kerawa were successful and three others were foiled , a member of a local vigilante group said . " There were new suicide attacks this morning in Kerawa , " said a regional security source contacted by AFP . " Four civilians were killed . Many others were hurt . "
