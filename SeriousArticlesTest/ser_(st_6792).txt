The eight leading Republican presidential candidates faced off in a Milwaukee debate that aired on Fox Business Network . Frontrunner Donald Trump kicked off the debate by decrying American wages as " too high , " while Marco Rubio lamented that America needs " more welders and less philosophers . " Surging candidate Ben Carson , responding to a question about the controversy surrounding whether he was actually offered a scholarship to West Point , suggested the media is biased against him , and argued that it was Hillary Clinton who needs more scrutiny from the press .
