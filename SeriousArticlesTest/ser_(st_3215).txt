A HASC aide tells Morning D that placing this as the first hearing of the year is intended to make a statement about the priority that Thornberry places on his acquisition reform efforts . He s concerned about the lack of agility in the current Pentagon buying system and wants to tackle that problem . If it takes years just to identify requirements and get budget authority ? not to mention testing and fielding a new system ? how are we ever going to keep up with technology and the changing threat profile ? the aide said .
