COACHING CAROUSEL : As expected , there has been plenty of activity since the end of the regular season . The Philadelphia Eagles didn t even wait that long , firing head coach Chip Kelly last week . The Cleveland Browns and San Francisco 49ers made their moves Sunday night , firing Mike Pettine and Jim Tomsula . The Dolphins named Holliston native Chris Grier their new GM . Miami is said to be interested in interviewing Patriots coordinators Josh McDaniels and Matt Patricia . Tom Coughlin resigned as coach of the New York Giants , prompting Belichick to pay tribute to the man who beat him twice in the Super Bowl . Tampa Bay s Lovie Smith was shown the door after just two seasons .
