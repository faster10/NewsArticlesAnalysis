Even Roy ' s grandmother Sarvamangala Dasi was an ardent devotee of Netaji , and used to hear her broadcast speech covertly . " My father used to tell me that the moment the Japan Radio used to broadcast Netaji ' s Speech , each and every member of our household would close all the doors and windows of the house , and glue their ears to the radio set to hear their beloved leader , " Roy said , and added that Netaji used to begin his speech with a typical salutation " Aamee Subhash Bolchi ( This is Subhas speaking ) .
