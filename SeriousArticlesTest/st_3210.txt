The decrepit aura of Vigoda and Soo , with monumental bags under their eyes , affectionately reflected the rotting refuse heap of New York City in the 70s and 80s , and their prototypical turns as ancient detectives exhausted by a trip to the lavatory set the stage for Jerry Orbach s later much acclaimed performance as the sometimes fatigued Lennie Briscoe in TV s Law and Order . His father was a tailor . The youngster stayed in shape by excelling in handball , then got his start on TV on an episode of Suspense in 1949 . He also had a role on the spooky daytime serial Dark Shadows .
