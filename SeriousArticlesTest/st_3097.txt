Last year , no driver at Homestead was accused of going over the line of the " driver code , " a vague and self determining policy of what is considered acceptable for the final laps while going for the championship . For all the talk about how teammates can help one another , the only thing that people wondered about a year ago was whether Gordon really needed to take four tires on a late pitstop and whether he did that to help the Hendrick alliance car of Harvick .
