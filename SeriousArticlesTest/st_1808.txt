Even if Manning retires , Brady is showing no signs of aging and claimed back in October that he wants to play 10 more years . And after a few years or retooling , Roethlisberger s Steelers ? if they can stay healthy ? look to be right back as one of the top teams in the conference and will be an AFC favorite next season along with the Patriots and Broncos . Brady , Manning and Roethlisberger have owned the AFC for 13 years , more than three times the length of the average NFL career and a period over which the Cleveland Browns have started 21 different quarterbacks , yet the future still remains pretty bleak for the rest of the conference .
