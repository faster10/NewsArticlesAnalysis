For the vast majority of Japanese voters , support for the LDP stemmed from the prospect of economic revival , and they were prepared to swallow parts of Abe ' s security agenda if that was part of the package . Unfortunately for Abe , the economy has slowed in recent months with Japan now technically in recession after two consecutive quarters of negative growth . Companies are doing well , but wages remain stagnant , and with inflation picking up an integral part of Abenomics is getting consumers to spend before prices rise pay packets are shrinking .
