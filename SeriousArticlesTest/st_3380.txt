Once a major gateway to information , communities and entertainment on the Internet , people have in recent years ditched Yahoo in favor of Google , Facebook Inc . and other companies at the center of people ' s digital lives . Since her hiring in 2012 , Mayer has made scant headway in efforts to restore growth at the Web pioneer . Now , she ' s mired in a complex project to decouple Yahoo ' s main business from its 25 billion stake in Alibaba Group Holding Ltd . Activist investors dissatisfied with progress have all but threatened a proxy war .
