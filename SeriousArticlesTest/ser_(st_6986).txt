Our colleagues have been shot dead by LEAs in Karachi and we would not let their blood go waste and will bar government from privatizing PIA at any cost , said Zaigham Sajjad Kiyani , the General Secretary Air League of Employees Union ( CBA ) . He said that he along with his colleague faced atrocities in Musharraf regime while supporting Prime Minister Mian Nawaz Sharif when he ( PM ) was not allowed to touch land of Islamabad and sent back to Jeddah by the military dictator . Now Mian Nawaz Sharif has become our enemy and is trying to snatch loaf of bread from the mouths of our families by privatising PIA , he said .
