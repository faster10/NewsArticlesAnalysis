It will go down as the deadliest mass shooting since the massacre at Sandy Hook Elementary School in Newtown , Connecticut claimed the lives of 20 children and 6 adults in 2012 . That is , until another one takes its place . It could take days or weeks , perhaps months or years , but it will happen . After all , there have been more than 1 , 000 mass shootings since Sandy Hook . At least 1 , 312 people have been killed and another 3 , 764 have been wounded . In 2015 alone , there have been more mass shootings in the U . S . than there are days in the year .
