Carson Palmer kept his faint MVP hopes alive by throwing for 356 yards and two touchdowns . The biggest question for in this game for Arizona was whether or not David Johnson and Kerwynn Williams could fill in for the injured Chris Johnson . The answer to that question is yes . The duo totaled 158 yards and a touchdown in the win . Overall , the Cardinals ' 524 total yards was the most that Arizona has put up in a four quarter game since 1963 , when the team was still in St .
