Gordon headlines a field of 43 starters Sunday in the Ford EcoBoost 400 ( 3 p . m . ET , NBC ) at Homestead Miami Speedway , the last Sprint Cup race of his glorious 23 year career . Past Chase formats haven ' t treated him kindly , and this final season has featured just one of his 93 career victories .
