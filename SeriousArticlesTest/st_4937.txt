Some sex workers are also injection drug users , and sharing needles during injection drug use accounts for 7 14 of new HIV infections in the United States each year . Syringe exchange programs ( or programs that provide clean syringes , STI testing , and non judgmental advice and referrals ) are highly effective in reducing HIV transmissions , yet a ban exists on federal funding for syringe exchange , limiting the reach of this valuable intervention . Additionally , many states still have laws criminalizing syringe possession , leading drug users to refrain from carrying excess syringes and forcing drug users to share needles .
