Hockeyfights . com has fight data as far back as the 2000 01 season , and a quick look at that information shows fighting is on pace to hit its lowest numbers . Through the All Star break there have only been 347 fights in the league this year , an average of just 0 . 28 per game ( down from the 0 . 34 a year ago ) , while only 178 of the league ' s 740 games this season have had at least one fight in it .
