For Democrats , the top issues influencing their vote are income inequality ( 16 . 8 percent ) , the economy ( 15 . 7 percent ) , health care ( 13 . 7 percent ) and the environment ( 12 . 4 percent ) . Meanwhile , national defense ( 16 . 6 percent ) and terrorism ( 14 percent ) only show up as important issues among Republican voters , which suggests that if Clinton were to win the Democratic nomination , her national security experience will help her against a Republican candidate .
