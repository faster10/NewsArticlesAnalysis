Attacking the Obama administration , Rubio called former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton a " liar , " and accused her of " lying to our faces " about the cause of the Benghazi attacks . Rubio promised , " If I am our nominee , voters will be reminded of it time and time again . " Turning his talk to his GOP nomination rivals , Rubio said , " We have isolationist candidates who are apparently more passionate about weakening our military and intelligence capabilities than about destroying our enemies . They talk tough , and yet they would strip us of the ability to keep our people safe . "
