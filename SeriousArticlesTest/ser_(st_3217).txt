WAR DEBATE ? RYAN TESTING THE WATERS ON AUMF : House Speaker Paul Ryan is kicking the tires to determine whether the long stalled war authorization can gain enough support to pass in the House , reports our colleagues Jake Sherman and John Bresnahan : Speaker Paul Ryan is urging top Republican committee chairmen to explore whether there s support in the House to formally authorize a military campaign against the Islamic State in Syria and Iraq , the early stages of what appears to be Capitol Hill s most serious effort to define the effort against the terrorist group .
