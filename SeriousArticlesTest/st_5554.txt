Originally from Drancy , Aminour was one of the suicide bombers at the Bataclan . He was know to French security services , and was indicted October 19 , 2012 , for being part of a criminal association with links to terrorism . He along with others had been trying to leave for Yemen . He was not jailed , but placed under judicial supervision . In the fall of 2013 , he violated his judicial review , and an international warrant for his arrest had been issued . Three people from his family circle are in custody as of Monday morning .
