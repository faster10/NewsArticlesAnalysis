When I was kid , the Tooth Fairy , if the wench remembered to come all , would leave a quarter . Maybe 50 cents if she was having a good year . A 2012 survey reported that American parents give their children 3 per tooth , so even considering 20 years of inflation , my fairy was obviously from the wrong side of the tracks . When I was a child , my mom was studying to become a dentist . Maybe to her , the idea of celebrating calcified structures separating from mouth tissue seemed like overkill .
