Andy Murray ended Britain s 79 year wait to reclaim the Davis Cup here today in the way that only he can . David Goffin had played one of the matches of his life in a heroic attempt to keep Belgium s hopes of winning the final alive , but on Murray s second match point the home team s No 1 could only watch in despair and wonderment as the Scot ended the contest with a superb backhand lob . It landed just inside the baseline to secure his own 6 3 , 7 5 , 6 3 victory and his team s 3 1 triumph .
