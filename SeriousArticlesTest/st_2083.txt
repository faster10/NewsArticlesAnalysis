James Ward played in that match , so while praising the contribution of the Murray household it is worth noting that , without his point scored against the USA in this year s first round , this triumphant campaign would probably never have got going . The country s one truly outstanding player needed convincing that the Davis Cup was not some futile exercise in faux patriotism . The effort of players like Ward and brother Jamie , plus the professionalism of Leon Smith and other backroom staff , successfully made that case .
