A little over two weeks after terror attacks by the Islamic State ( IS ) rocked the city , the government has imposed a ban on public demonstrations citing security concerns . However , on Sunday , activists and residents assembled at the Place de la R publique , defying the ban , walking together and shouting slogans . Around 4 , 500 linked hands in a peaceful protest near the site of the deadliest of the attacks . The colourful human chain passed near the Bataclan concert hall where 90 people were killed in the suicide bombings and shootings .
