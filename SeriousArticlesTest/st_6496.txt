The stories on AreYouDense . org of average risk women , who never missed a mammography appointment resulting in advanced disease and , for some , death , might be looked upon and dismissed as anecdotal evidence . However , more than two decades of science refute that claim . The main goal of mammographic screening is to reduce advanced disease , as stated in the ACS guidelines . Giving women at average risk with dense breast tissue access to screening beyond the mammogram is essential to further that goal .
