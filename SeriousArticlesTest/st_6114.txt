With the presidential primaries in full swing , Trump maintains his sizable lead over the rest of the Republican field . Among Republican or Republican leaning registered voters , Trump has 35 percent support compared to 18 percent for Ted Cruz and 13 percent for Marco Rubio , according to results from the latest NBC News SurveyMonkey Weekly Election Tracking Poll for the week of Dec . 28 , 2015 through Jan . 3 , 2016 . The poll was conducted online among a national sample of 3 , 700 adults aged 18 and over . Trump leads among men with 39 percent of support , compared to 21 percent of men who support Cruz and 13 percent of men who support Rubio . Trump also leads among women with 29 percent support , while Cruz and Rubio have about equal support from women ? 13 percent and 14 percent , respectively : NBC
