A little used enforcer with the Arizona Coyotes at the start of the season , Scott was included in a mid January trade to the Montreal Canadiens , who immediately sent him to their AHL team . His lack of playing time this season ( which is even lower than it ' s been throughout his career ) and uncertain future as an NHL player comes down to two things . He is simply not as talented as the majority of the players in the NHL , and he plays a role ( the heavyweight enforcer ) that simply no longer exists because of the huge decline in fighting in recent years .
