Unconfirmed reports surfaced that Mansur had succumbed to his injuries . Sultan Faizy , spokesperson for First Vice President Abdur Rashid Dostum issued a statement about the clash and injuries to Mansur . The office of Dr Abdullah Abdullah , CEO , endorsed this a day later . The Pakistani Urdu channel 24 even mentioned the name of Maulvi Haibatullah Akhund , chosen as interim leader of the Afghan Taliban after the incapacitation of Mansur . Subsequently however , the Xinhua ( on 6 December ) came out with an audio message from Mullah Mansur , claiming to be safe and sound .
