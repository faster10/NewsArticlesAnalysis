After three plus quarters of sloppy , undisciplined football at MetLife Stadium , the Jets scored 13 unanswered points for a stunning , 23 20 overtime win their biggest December win since 2011 . Fitzpatrick was the catalyst , passing for 390 yards and two touchdowns , converting on a key fourth down scramble and committing no turnovers despite getting knocked down nine times . His bloated passing total was the most by a Jets quarterback since Vinny Testaverde ' s 481 yard losing effort against the celebrated Baltimore Ravens in the 2000 season finale .
