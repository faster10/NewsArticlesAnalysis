The standings are so tight in the NHL right now that every point in the standings matters , putting a much bigger emphasis on maximizing the production teams get out of every single roster spot . There is just no room for the enforcer in today ' s game , which is why players like Scott , George Parros , Paul Bisonnette , Colton Orr , Cam Janssen , and Frazer McLaren have been gradually phased out of the league in recent years . The ones that have been able to hang on in the league have nobody to fight and are healthy scratches more often than not .
