The online hysteria over this show is just another instance of our modern obsession with true crime stories and miscarriages of justice . The matter at the heart of the Serial podcast , HBO s The Jinx , and Making a Murderer is the innate fallibility of the American criminal justice system . That lack of faith in the law is the kind of thing that makes the modern conservative assume we re only a few years away from adding Karl Marx to Mount Rushmore and that the Statue of Liberty is going to pick up and walk off like in Ghostbusters II .
