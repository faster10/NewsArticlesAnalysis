The bright , blinking yellow light of caution , however , is that North Korea is not at all interested in denuclearization a process whereby Pyongyang admits IAEA inspectors into the country , agrees to forgo any further nuclear weapons tests , and gradually dismantles its nuclear infrastructure in much the same manner as the Iranians . In addition to remarks from Kim himself that North Korea would never willingly disarm itself , it would be the point of insanity from the North ' s point of view to voluntarily give away the one weapon that guarantees that Washington or South Korea would refrain from pursue aggressive regime change . If the United States continues to resist dropping its demand of full and complete denuclearization on the Korean Peninsula and North Korea refuses to even broach the subject of ceasing its nuclear work , diplomats running the Six Party Talks wouldn ' t have much to talk about .
