Finally , he set up a fourth set with a mighty serve . A few sploshes of rain briefly stalled his momentum , as the roof across the arena was closed . But his surge of inspiration withstood the delay ? never more tellingly illustrated than by a glorious backhand winner up the line as he toiled to stay on serve . His fist pump for the spectators suggested that it was a turning point , but Djokovic would have none of it . Two rapier groundstrokes left Federer floundering and sealed the decisive break . Serving out the match to love , he turned to coach Boris Becker and raised his arms in salute .
