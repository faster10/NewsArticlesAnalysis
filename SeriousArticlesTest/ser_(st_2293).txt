The famed Wallenda circus troupe perform at the Michigan State Fairgrounds in Detroit , January 30 , 1962 . Two of the group were killed and a third injured when this pyramid formation collapsed and the performers fell to the ground . Left to right ( bottom row ) : Dieter Schepp , 23 Mario Wallenda , 21 Richard Faughnan , 29 Gunther Wallenda , 42 . Second row , left to right : Karl Wallenda , 57 Herman Wallenda , 60 . Sitting on chair atop pyramid is Jana Schepp , 17 . Dieter Schepp and Faughnan died in the fall . Mario Wallenda and Miss Schepp were injured
