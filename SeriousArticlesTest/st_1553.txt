But it ' s not only Democrats who have done that . Step forward , Richard Nixon . In 1968 , the presidential election looked close . With the Johnson administration edging toward a peace deal in Vietnam , Nixon ' s team rolled the dice . According to an account in Politico , Anna Chennault , a Republican activist , was given a message to pass onto the South Vietnamese government : If they undermined the peace talks by being stubborn , the Democrats would lose the election and Nixon as the next president would offer them better terms .
