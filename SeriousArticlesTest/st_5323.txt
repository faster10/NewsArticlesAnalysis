The one big drive careened down the field for almost seven minutes , finally ending when CJ Anderson plunged into the end zone . Then , rather than get seven , Denver went for eight and Manning threw one last pass on Sunday : a toss to Demaryius Thomas that put the Steelers a full touchdown behind . Soon after it was over , the hardest playoff win in the toughest of seasons . Manning trudged off the field with a small smile and a weariness that said at 39 years old with an aching foot and a rebuilt neck there can t be too many more of these .
