All sports are starkly stratified , of course . Take athletics : on British Airways Beijing London flight back from last summer s world championships , Usain Bolt luxuriated in first class , while senior members of the British team sat with their knees around their chins in cattle . But no sport deals in disparity in quite such a cavalier fashion as tennis , where Federer is so feted that Manhattan s Carlyle Hotel has named a suite after him , while the waifs and strays in US Open qualifying make do with couch surfing .
