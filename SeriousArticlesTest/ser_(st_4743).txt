Heavily armed special forces take combat position outside a house being searched in the Rue Delaunoy in Brussels on November 16 , 2015 . During the weekend searches were carried out and multiple people were arrested in relation to Friday ' s terrorist attacks in Paris . Several terrorist attacks in Paris , France , have left at least 129 dead and 350 injured . Most people were killed during a concert in venue Bataclan , the other targets were a restaurant and a soccer game . The attacks have been claimed by Islamic State .
