Sindh Health Minister Jam Mahtab Dahar said that political and scientific commitment is a key to combat deadly disease of HIV AIDS , which has assumed critical dimension over the period of time stalking larger segment of the population . While pursuing wide ranging activities to combat lethal HIV disease , we must not overlook some of the vital aspects aimed at overcoming the issue of HIV AIDS which includes political commitment , multi spectral support , public health surveillance and monitoring , evaluation , prevention , care , support and treatment facilities besides health delivery systems he said .
