This streaming video service offers a wide array of Indian movie and television content . It has three subscription offerings Free , Premium ( Rs 300 month ) and Special ( Rs 60 movie ) . These packages determine the catalog of content a user has access to . All of these enable view on Mobile , Tablet , PC , Mac , and Smart TV , including the ability to download videos to a mobile or tablet . While the free version supports ads , which can be removed by opting for any of their paid services .
