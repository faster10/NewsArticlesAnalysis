An estimated 20 to 40 of women at high risk of HIV infection in the United States reported having sex in exchange for money or drugs within the past year . Sex workers engaged in injection drug use face additional risks due to needle sharing arising from barriers to accessing clean syringes and intersecting stigma . Sex workers are also especially affected in the south , where HIV transmission rates are among the highest in the country .
