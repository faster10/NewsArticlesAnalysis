With Europe confronting a heightened threat , the measures announced Friday underscored a trade off between security and the open borders the European Union once held dear . Moving to shut down a network of homegrown jihadists who are slipping between the continent and the battlefields of the Middle East , officials agreed to come up with a proposal before the end of the year to enhance abilities to track airline passengers . Salah Abdeslam , 26 , a French national wanted in connection with the attacks , is believed to have been stopped in Greece in August .
