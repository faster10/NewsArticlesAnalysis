GABBY GIFFORDS BACKS CLINTON AMID GUN DEBATE : ? As the top two Democratic candidates feud over their position on guns , Gabby Giffords and her husband Mark Kelly announced Sunday that they are endorsing Clinton for president , ABC s LIZ KREUTZ notes . Only one candidate for president has the determination and toughness to stand up to the corporate gun lobby ? and the record to prove it . That candidate is Hillary Clinton , Giffords and Kelly said in a statement . In response , Clinton said : As an admirer of their courage in the face of incredible odds , their service to the country that they love , and their commitment to ending the epidemic of gun violence , I am humbled to receive the endorsement of these American heroes . ?
