The Modi government has promised to reduce its emissions intensity by 33 to 35 by 2030 from the 2005 levels . But emissions would continue to grow under the plan , as its economy grows . Modi has stressed that climate change is a global problem " not of our making " , and called for restoring " a balance between economy and ecology " . His government has outlined measures such as a plan to add 175 GW of renewable energy by 2022 as well as development strategy that will enable India to transition to a more sustainable energy mix .
