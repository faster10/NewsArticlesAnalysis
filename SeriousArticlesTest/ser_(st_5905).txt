In April , we went on a trip to the Gal pagos with 100 ocean scientists , activists and artists , filmmakers and musicians , to talk about what ' s going on in the ocean and what we can do to help . On the Mission Blue Voyage , we heard things and saw things that shocked us like Brian Skerry ' s picture of shrimp bycatch , the 8 pounds of dead sea creatures that are thrown away as garbage in order to catch 10 ounces of shrimp .
