As Lloyd recalled : I told Dan , Just listen to me here , look in my eyes , do not hit one more ball to your opponent s backhand . Not one more . The instructions were retained for about two points before an overwhelmed Evans reverted to type and went down in four sets . Great Britain had slipped into a relegation play off to avoid the humiliation of Europe Africa Group III , alongside the likes of Andorra , San Marino and Moldova . Lloyd was sacked a couple of days later , and the reign of Leon Smith was soon under way .
