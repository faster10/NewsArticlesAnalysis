A profile in Melbourne s The Age in 2002 had it about right , acknowledging that Hewitt could be a raging , foul mouthed punk . In retirement , however , the slate has been wiped clean . On Thursday at Rod Laver Arena , he was introduced as the brash teenager who became a statesman . The last straw was when he turned up to his final press conference flanked by his three children . A salute to the ultimate dad ? Perhaps . Until you remembered that Hewitt , upon the birth of his first daughter in 2010 , made his own fans subscribe to a premium text message service to find out the baby s name .
