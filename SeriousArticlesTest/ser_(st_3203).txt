New England has been ravaged by injury this season and they still continue to rank among the NFL s best teams . The reason why is because the Patriots are the best coached team in football , as Belichick is a master at both personnel and game planning . Osweiler s lack of experience could actually be an advantage in that respect , but the Broncos are going to have to play mistake free football if they want to knock off the Pats and establish themselves as perhaps the team to beat in the AFC .
