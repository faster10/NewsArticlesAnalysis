While Trump s campaign spent 6 . 3 million on ads in January , according to The Tracking Firm , his spending up to that point was enigmatic . Of the 7 million his campaign spent in the final quarter of last year , 940 , 000 , went toward campaign paraphernalia , including yard signs , bumper stickers , buttons , t shirts and , of course , hats . In fact , about 450 , 000 or nearly 7 percent of all Trump s fourth quarter spending went towards hats , presumably including the now iconic hats bearing Trump s campaign slogan , Make America Great Again .
