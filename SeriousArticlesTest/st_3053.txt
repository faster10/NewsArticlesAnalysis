Recalling the moment , when the police from Hazratganj Kotwali came to their shop and asked them to remove the portrait from the window , 59 year old RK Roy ( which he heard as anecdotes from his family members ) , said , " Even at that time , police used to dread Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose and his followers , as compared to Mahatma Gandhi . So , there was no problem , if we had put up a photo of Mahatma Gandhi instead of Subhas Chandra Bose . "
