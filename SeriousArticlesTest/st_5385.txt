Officers from the elite RAID anti terrorism unit many of whom had been in action during the attacks on Friday moved into the apartment block on Rue du Corbillon , hoping to blast their way into the suspects ' third floor flat and capture them before they could react . But their way was barred by a reinforced door which held out against the explosive charges placed against it . What had been planned as a lightning raid was about to turn into one of the fiercest gun battles on French soil in decades .
