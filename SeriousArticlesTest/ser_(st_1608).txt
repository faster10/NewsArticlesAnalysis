So what did it all prove ? Aside from the fun aspect , and the cleverness of Boyd s use of and allusions to so many aspects of 20th century art , it showed , as his imaginative and brilliantly executed project probably intended , that the art world , perhaps the whole cultural world , is scared ever to admit to a lack of knowledge , scared ever to use the words : I ve never heard of him . How quickly the great and the good of that world convinced themselves of Nat Tate s existence . That s something we could still all do well to ruminate on .
