So , although Zika appears to be a health crisis , it s more a crisis about extreme inequality and underfunded public services . The precious Rio de Janeiro Olympic Games this year aren t likely to be threatened by the virus : Brazil has a history of protecting its rich and forgetting its poor . Even the illegal abortions being performed on women diagnosed with the virus show an economic marker : the procedure can cost from R 5 , 000 to R 15 , 000 , a sum most low income Brazilian women can only dream of .
