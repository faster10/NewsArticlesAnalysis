Missouri ( 5 ) . Indications are that athletic director Mack Rhoades is progressing into the latter stages of his search , but who will be introduced as the new coach of the Tigers ? Rhoades has been in Denver this week conducting interviews for the school s vacant football coaching job , sources told Yahoo Sports . Among the candidates in the region Rhoades is believed to have interviewed : Air Force coach Troy Calhoun Utah State coach Matt Wells and Colorado State coach Mike Bobo . But a fourth candidate from outside the Rocky Mountain area is meeting with Rhoades in Denver on Tuesday , according to sources : California coach Sonny Dykes .
