Right now , it s just tough to imagine another matchup of future Hall of Famers like this because nobody has assembled a similar resume yet . The New England Patriots Brady and the Denver Broncos Manning have played against each other 16 times leading up to Sunday s AFC Championship game . That s an entire NFL season of head to head matchups dating back to 2001 , when Manning was not only playing for the Indianapolis Colts , but was doing so in the since demolished RCA Dome ( which fell in 2008 , but is still being paid off through 2021 ) .
