The year Kaepernick led the 49ers to the Super Bowl , he ran 25 times for 264 yards in three postseason games . You watched it and thought this was the guy who would be the prototype for 21st century quarterbacks . The feel good story about the tattooed second round pick out of Nevada continued in 2013 with Kaepernick leading the 49ers back to the NFC championship game with his legs as much as his arm before suffering a close loss to the eventual Super Bowl champion Seattle Seahawks .
