In 1995 , the combative if squeaky voiced figure of David Lloyd was appointed for what was ? on the face of things ? a highly successful run . Having already shocked Britain s sluggish tennis ecology with the creation of 18 superclubs , Lloyd was now trying to boost the Davis Cup team out of their slumber in Euro Africa Zone Group Two . The upside was that the opponents were weak , and Greg Rusedski had just converted from Canadian nationality in a similar manoeuvre to the one Slovenian born Aljaz Bedene is now trying to perform .
