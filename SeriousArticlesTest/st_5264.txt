Bowman converged to tackle Jermaine Kearse , who had caught a pass over the middle . Bowman , instead of diving for the tackle , planted his feet and remained standing to strip the ball from Kearse . And while he was prying the ball from Kearse ' s hands , giving the 49ers the turnover they desperately needed , 49ers safety Eric Reid ran from the end zone to finish off the tackle . Reid ' s hit thrust Kearse into Bowman ' s planted left knee , and the knee folded inward . Gruesome .
