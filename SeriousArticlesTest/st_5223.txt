The Chiefs and the Texans get the postseason started on Saturday at 4 : 35 p . m . This is actually a rematch from Week 1 , when Kansas City jumped out to a big lead and won , 27 20 . Alex Smith threw for 243 yards and three touchdowns , while tight end Travis Kelce had six receptions for 106 yards and two touchdowns . Brian Hoyer struggled for Houston and was benched in favor of Ryan Mallett . There will be no Mallett riding to the rescue this time . The last time Chiefs won a playoff game on the road was in Houston , but that was 22 years ago . The Kansas City quarterback was Joe Montana , and the Oilers were the opponent . Jenny Vrentas of Sports Illustrated looks at how the Chiefs turned things around .
