Chinese Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Ming told reporters China was not taking sides . " Regarding some of the region ' s problems , China has always taken a balanced and just position , " Zhang said , when asked about tensions between Riyadh and Tehran . " If the Middle East is not stable , I ' m afraid the world can ' t be very peaceful . If a country or a region is not stable , it cannot realise development , " he added . " China firmly supports regional countries individually exploring a development path that suits their national conditions . "
