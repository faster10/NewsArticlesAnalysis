Big picture : After being doubted by some and questioned by many this season , the Carolina Panthers are advancing to their second Super Bowl in franchise history . This is a team with few glaring weaknesses and an MVP caliber quarterback who played like it Sunday . Following a regular season in which they navigated a modest schedule , winning all but one game , the Panthers have authored back to back impressive playoff efforts . They knocked out the two time defending NFC champs , the Seattle Seahawks , and on Sunday thoroughly beat the Arizona Cardinals , who had looked like a Super Bowl worthy team through most of the season .
