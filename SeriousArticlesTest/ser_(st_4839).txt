Having swept to Oscar victory with the faux one shot gimmickry of Birdman , director Alejandro Gonz lez I ? rritu once again hitches his wagon to the technical brilliance of cinematographer Emmanuel Lubezki , working wonders with his digital Arri Alexa cameras . Via Lubezki s sweeping widescreen lenses we find ourselves viscerally dragged through the wilderness , violent ambushes and life threatening confrontations caught in superbly orchestrated lengthy takes , the camera following on foot , on horseback , through woods and plains , air and water , often without apparent edits . This is muscular film making , and much has been made of the punishing physicality of the living hell shoot in Canada and Argentina , with a digital grizzly bear one of the few obvious concessions to artificiality .
