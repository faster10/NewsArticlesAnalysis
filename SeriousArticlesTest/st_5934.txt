Mad Max : Fury Road isn t going anywhere . Many people were pleasantly surprised to see Mad Max : Fury Road selected as the National Board of Review s best film of 2015 . Though the prevailing sentiment was something along the lines of , That s nice , but it won t be around come Oscar time , George Miller s feminist action flick isn t exactly receding into the distance , either . With Golden Globe nominations for Best Picture ( Drama ) and Best Director , the movie s awards season presence is a reminder that action films can survive the long summer and live to see the glory winter brings .
