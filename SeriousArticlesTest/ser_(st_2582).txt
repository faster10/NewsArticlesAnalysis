Since early October , dozens of private class action lawsuits have targeted DFS operators . Two lawsuits one in New York and one in Florida also named individual company executives , successful fantasy players , investor media companies , payment processors and certain equity holding sports leagues , such as the NBA and MLB , as additional defendants . All the lawsuits are at very preliminary stages and have yet to result in any meaningful decisions by either a judge or jury . While attention grabbing , many of the cases are duplicative and have yet to be served upon individual defendants .
