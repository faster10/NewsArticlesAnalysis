SHOULD RAMP UP PACIFIC MISSILE DEFENSE : Two Republican leaders of the House Armed Services Committee Wednesday urged the Obama administration to press South Korea to allow deployment of the THAAD missile defense system in the wake of claims by North Korea that it s tested a hydrogen bomb . While the commander of U . S . forces in South Korea , Gen . Curtis Scaparrotti , has pushed for the deployment of the missile defense system to the Korean Peninsula , South Korea so far balked over opposition from China and concerns that hosting missile interceptors could destabilize relations with North Korea .
