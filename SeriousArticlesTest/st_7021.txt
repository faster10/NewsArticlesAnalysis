Rampling , who began her career as a model in the swinging sixties before being discovered and cast in the 1966 drama Georgy Girl starring Lynn Redgrave , is known for her leading turns in Woody Allen s Stardust Memories , Sidney Lumet s The Verdict and Liliana Cavana s The Night Porter . She has been nominated for France s highest film awards , the Cesars , four times , twice for director Fran ? ois Ozon , in 2000 bereavement drama Under the Sand and 2003 erotic thriller The Swimming Pool but has never before reached the final stage of the Oscars race .
