While Netaji ' s daughter Anita Bose Pfaff has called for an end to speculation on Netaji ' s death , saying he was killed in the Taiwan air crash , the latest round of declassified files to be released by the National Archives of India could continue to fuel theories surrounding his mysterious disappearance . The BJP and more recently Mamata Banerjee , have raised questions about claims of Netaji having died in an air crash off Taipei in 1945 . On the other hand , a UK website titled Bose Files has been put out documents indicating that Bose was likely to have died in the air crash .
