The couple met at the University of California when Ron starred on the football team and Stephanie played basketball before a knee injury as a freshman ended her career . She graduated with a psychology degree and a thirst for coaching , a passion she pursued as her husband established himself on the Bears defense . During more than a decade in Chicago , Stephanie coached the Chicago Twisters of the now defunct semipro Women ' s Basketball Association to a championship and spent time as an assistant for the Chicago Condors in the former ABL and with Trinity University in Deerfield .
