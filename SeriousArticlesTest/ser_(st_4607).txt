Eye test : RB Derrick Henry is one of the best players in the country and has overshadowed quarterback Jake Coker , but the defense is the difference for Alabama . The Tide rank No . 1 in ESPN ' s defensive efficiency and that has been the team ' s identity this season . Since its Sept . 19 loss to Ole Miss , Alabama has ranked No . 1 in the FBS in yards allowed per game ( 244 . 3 ) , Total QBR allowed ( 8 . 1 ) , and percent of drives it has allowed that have ended in a touchdown ( 8 . 1 ) . The front seven is arguably the best in the country .
