Trump delivered a raucous speech for thousands Monday in Lowell that was interrupted several times by protesters , who were later escorted from the building . This was Trump ' s fourth major political event in Massachusetts this year . The Bay State holds its presidential primary on March 1 , three weeks after the New Hampshire primary . After Trump announced his visit to the Paul E . Tsongas Center , groups quickly began to organize protests of the event . Trump was interrupted during his speech at least seven times . Many times the groups of protesters , spread throughout the audience , found their chants inaudible and shouted down by his supporters calling out , " Trump , Trump , Trump .
