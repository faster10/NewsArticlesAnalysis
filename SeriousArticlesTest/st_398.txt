As our focus remains on countering the last attack , we are likely to find that even a marginal quantum of innovation would throw responding agencies into utter disarray . As far as the possibility and potential of chemical , biological , radiological and nuclear terrorism ( CBRN ) is concerned , we have our heads buried deep into the sand ? and rightly so , since we have not the faintest idea of what we are to do about these potential threats . This is the case along all parameters of prevention , response and retaliation .
