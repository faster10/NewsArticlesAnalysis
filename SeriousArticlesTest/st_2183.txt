Prosecutors on Friday said they had confirmed that another of the suicide bombers who died in last week ' s attack on the Stade de France had traveled through Greece , adding more evidence of how militants have been taking advantage of the same routes used by migrants to flee into Europe from the war torn Middle East . The man , officials said , apparently entered Greece on the same date and location Oct . 3 at Leros island as an attacker who arrived with a fake Syrian passport under the name Ahmad Almohammad .
