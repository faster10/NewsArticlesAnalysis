The Mate 8 was actually announced a few months ago . But it ' s only now that Huawei has started pushing it out in markets . The phone packs in the high end HiSilicon Kirin 950 processor , which is made by Huawei . It has two versions one that has 32GB internal storage and 3GB RAM and other , a more pricier option , with 64GB storage and 4GB RAM . It has a 16 megapixel rear camera . The phone uses Android Marshmallow customised with Huawei ' s own user interface called Emotion UI .
