Once the outrage subsides a bit , the expert community and the media , collectively known as the chattering class , will resume a familiar discussion about whether China can be persuaded to intervene and solve the North Korea problem . All eyes will be on China to see whether this nuclear test near the Chinese border will finally compel a change in Beijing s support of the regime , Victor Cha , senior fellow at the Center for Strategic and International Studies , wrote last Wednesday . While it might lead to some short term titration of assistance , it is unlikely to cause China to abandon the North , he said .
