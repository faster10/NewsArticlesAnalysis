GOP frontrunner Donald Trump may say a lot inaccurate things on the campaign trail , but calling North Korean dictator Kim Jong un a " maniac " is not one of them . This was confirmed yet again late on Tuesday night , January 6 , when North Korea claims to have conducted its fourth nuclear test in nine years . The underground explosion was registered as a magnitude 5 . 1 seismic event by the U . S . Geological Survey in an area roughly near the site of previous tests that were carried out over the years .
