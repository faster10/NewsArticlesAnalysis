The North Korean problem won t go away on its own , despite magical thinking to the contrary . Both South Korean Park Geun hye s recent emphasis on unification and perhaps Obama s strategic patience seem to be waiting for North Korea to collapse . But there is no sign that North Korea is facing an imminent domestic crisis its economy is doing reasonably well by the country s own low standards , and the scheduling of a Korean Workers Party Congress for May 2016 ? the first Party Congress in 36 years ? indicates a good deal of confidence on the part of the Kim leadership .
