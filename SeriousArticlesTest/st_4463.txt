The widows of the two Russian servicemen killed during the shoot down incident were present in the audience , and Putin acknowledged their sacrifice with a moment of silence . In contrast , the Russian soldiers killed in the Kremlin s covert war in eastern Ukraine never received any official recognition . From Putin s point of view , the dismemberment of Ukraine is no longer a priority . Riven by infighting and increasingly unpopular , the pro Western government in Kiev may self destruct all on its own . An eastern sliver of the country is firmly under the control of Russian backed separatists . And if the Kremlin can appear to be an invaluable partner in fighting Islamic State , European countries may find it more difficult to renew sanctions imposed because Russia s intervention in Ukraine .
