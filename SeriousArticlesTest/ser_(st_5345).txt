U . S . Sen . Ted Cruz speaks in Des Moines , Iowa , after winning the state ' s Republican caucuses on Monday , February 1 . With about 99 of precincts reporting , Cruz had 28 of the vote compared with 24 for Donald Trump and 23 for U . S . Sen . Marco Rubio . " Iowa has sent notice that the Republican nominee and the next President of the United States will not be chosen by the media , will not be chosen by the Washington establishment , " Cruz said .
