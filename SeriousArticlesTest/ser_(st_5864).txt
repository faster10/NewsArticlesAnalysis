The diplomatic damage caused by the incident , which follows the visit , last month , to the U . S . of President Xi Jinping already appears extensive . A commentary by the Xinhua news agency pointed out that the intrusion of the warship breached the understanding between President Xi and his U . S . counterpart of building a new model of major country relationship featuring no confrontation , no conflict , and mutual respect and win win cooperation . Analysts say that the U . S . action has triggered speculation inside China that Washington has decided to bury the formulation proposed by President Xi that China and the U . S . should establish a peaceful and collaborative major country relationship .
