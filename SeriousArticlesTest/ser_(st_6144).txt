4 . Amartya Sen on Subhash Chandra Bose : We are in a situation , where Netaji ' s vision is very badly needed in every sphere of life . Right now in the country , there is a sense of communalism being created so much so that secularism is being considered a bad word . Netaji ' s vision would not countenance sowing of discontent between social groups .
