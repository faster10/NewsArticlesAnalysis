Ogle is perhaps the only streaming video service in the country that offers international movie and TV content along with Indian content . Its pricing plans span Rs 49 day , Rs 249 month and Rs 2 , 400 year , although the subscriptions aren t permanently open potential users need to check the site and sign up as and when subscriptions become available . A unique feature they talk about is the ability to stream on lower bandwidths ( from 512Kbps , ) with their purportedly patent pending video compression algorithms . This service is also available across devices ( TV , laptop , phone , tablet ) with the ability to handoff across devices .
