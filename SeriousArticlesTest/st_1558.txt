Authorities are continuing to search for three inmates who escaped from a Southern California maximum security jail by cutting through half inch steel bars and rappelling from the roof by a makeshift rope . " It was very well thought out and planned , " Orange County sheriff ' s Lt . Jeff Hallock said Saturday at a news conference . The inmates ? one of whom is an alleged killer ? were last seen at 5 a . m . Friday at the Orange County Central Men ' s Jail in Santa Ana , about 40 miles southeast of Los Angeles . They could have escaped any time between then and late Friday night .
