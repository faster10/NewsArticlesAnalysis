This is the world that tennis chiefs forget about when they bristle at the timing of the fixing claims , as if they were simply an unfortunate change in the weather . They neglect to note that it is the lopsidedness of their rewards system , where the rich grow exponentially richer and the poor are utterly disenfranchised , that emboldens the fixers to strike and weakens certain players into succumbing . They forget that at the lowest rung of the professional ladder , resources are so denuded that competitors are not even guaranteed a fair and safe court to play on . At an event sanctioned by the ITF last year in Sousse , Tunisia , players had to hit over a net shredded in several places .
