TRUMP S PRIVATE PLANE MAKES EMERGENCY LANDING IN NASHVILLE . Donald Trump ' s private jet made an emergency landing in Nashville Wednesday afternoon after the pilot reported engine problems , officials said . The Boeing 757 landed in Nashville at 4 : 40 p . m . , the Federal Aviation Administration said . Trump eventually made it to the event he was attending in Little Rock , Arkansas , but the event started late at 7 : 45 p . m . , more than an hour and a half off schedule .
