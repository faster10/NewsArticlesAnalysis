The Honor 5X , which may sell in India for a price of around Rs 15 , 000 , is a phone that may find a lot of takers in India . It is powered by Qualcomm ' s Snapdragon 615 processor and has a 5 . 5 inch screen with FullHD resolution . Just like the Mate 8 , the Honor 5X too is likely to be available in two versions . While both versions have 16GB internal storage , one would have 2GB RAM and other will come with 3GB .
