Britain traditionally votes on a Thursday , but there are reasons to rule out the four other possible dates for a referendum in June , assuming Cameron can seal a deal on the terms of the United Kingdom ' s EU membership with fellow leaders next month . June 16 , for example , sees the England soccer team playing Wales in the afternoon a match that will mean millions of potential voters are glued to the television with Northern Ireland taking to the pitch that evening . There are no such sporting distractions a week later .
