Easy street : Washington . Huskies fans should be excited about the trajectory of their program entering 2016 the return of quarterback Jake Browning , running back Myles Gaskin , a blossoming offensive line and the league ' s best defense sets the table nicely for 2016 . But Washington ' s nonconference slate inspires only yawns : vs . Rutgers ( 4 8 ) , vs . Idaho ( 4 8 ) , vs . Portland State ( 9 3 ) . The only winning team in that trio hails from the FCS ( though , to be fair , the Vikings did win at Washington State last year ) , and there ' s not even a road game here to spice things up for the Huskies .
