Despite Indian viewers being price conscious , Netflix ' s billing plans in the country are on par with the US . While the first month is free , viewers can choose from three monthly options henceforth : the Basic at Rs 500 ( 7 . 50 ) , Standard at Rs 650 ( 9 . 70 ) and Premium at Rs 800 ( 12 ) . The Basic plan allows for streaming in standard definition resolution only on one device at a time . The Standard option has high definition content on two screens , while the Premium one has ultra high definition content that can be viewed on four screens .
