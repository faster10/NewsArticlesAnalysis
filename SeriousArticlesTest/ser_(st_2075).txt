This year , New Hampshire voters are showing off their independence by giving big early leads to two unconventional candidates : Republican Donald Trump and Democrat Bernie Sanders . The brash billionaire and the democratic socialist may seem like the logical heirs to John McCain , the maverick Arizona senator who upset establishment favorite George W . Bush in the 2000 primary and won the contest again in 2008 on his way to the nomination . They do like to claim the mantle of " telling it like it is . " ? But in important ways , Trump and Sanders are far from the McCains of their respective parties .
