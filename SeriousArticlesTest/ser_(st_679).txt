It seems inevitable Syria s future will be mapped out by others ? and desirable , to a point , for every day there are reminders of what happens when a civil war is allowed to fester , its consequences spilling across an entire region , contaminating other continents . But still the great powers would do well to show a degree of humility and restraint , for as negotiations take shape the priority must be to put Syrians at the forefront . The prize might be a solution that will last .
