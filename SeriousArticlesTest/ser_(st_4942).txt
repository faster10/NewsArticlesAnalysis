On December 12th , Desiree Alliance and Best Practices Policy Project will be releasing a joint report : " Nothing About Us , Without Us : HIV AIDS related Community and Policy Organizing for U . S . Sex Workers " at the U . S . Human Rights Network Conference in Austin , TX . The report highlights the needs and experiences of transgender persons who are also sex workers ( or who are profiled as sex workers ) , and ways the U . S . government can improve the HIV response to this community .
