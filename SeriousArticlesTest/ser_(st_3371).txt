Just in time for the Democratic debate on Sunday , Democratic candidate Bernie Sanders released more details of his Medicare for All health care plan . Sanders asserts that it will cut health care costs while providing universal health care . The cut in health care costs that Sanders claims he can wring out of the system is getting savaged even by even leftish pundits as hopelessly unrealistic . Hillary Clinton tried to paint it as a risky trade of Barack Obama s hard won health care victory for a leap into the unknown . And right from birth it s already mired in the rhetorical morass of whether it includes a middle class tax increase .
