Many Kremlin watchers say a deal would depend unilaterally on the Russian President Vladimir Putin , who sees oil as only a small part of the puzzle which also includes dialogue with the West and Saudi Arabia on the war in Syria as well as sanctions on Russia imposed by the West over its actions in Ukraine .
