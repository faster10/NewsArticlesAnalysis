The Obama administration has proposed a largely political , containment based solution that is fraught with risks , including coordinating with the Russians and Iranians to win back Islamic State controlled territory in Syria and Iraq . The problem with this strategy is that neither of these nations is a trustworthy ally legitimizing them would lead only to further destabilization . Empowering Russia would only embolden Syrian dictator Bashar Assad . Doing business with the Iranians encourages their ambitions to control the territory from Iran ' s eastern border to the Mediterranean , threatening our allies in Israel and Saudi Arabia .
