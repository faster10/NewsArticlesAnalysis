Security forces personnel arrive during ongoing operations in the Molenbeek district of Brussels on November 16 , 2015 . Belgian police launched a major new operation in the Brussels district of Molenbeek , where several suspects in the Paris attacks had previously lived , AFP journalists said . Armed police stood in front of a police van blocking a street in the run down area of the capital while Belgian media said officers had surrounded a house . Belgian prosecutors had no immediate comment . AFP PHOTO BELGA DIRK WAEM ( Photo credit should read DIRK WAEM AFP Getty Images )
