Actor Charlie Sheen s announcement that he is living with HIV officially added him to the estimated more than 1 . 2 million Americans who have the virus that causes AIDS . Almost one in eight of those with HIV are unaware they have the virus , according to the Atlanta based federal Centers for Disease Control and Prevention . New infections in the U . S . have remained at about 50 , 000 per year for the past decade , compared with 130 , 000 at the height of the 1980s epidemic . Gay , bisexual and other men who have sex with men account for 63 of all new U . S . HIV cases .
