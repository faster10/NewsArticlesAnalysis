Statutes criminalizing engaging in sex work while living with HIV , are often broader than general statutes that criminalize engaging in sex while HIV positive . No intent or transmission is required . No activity likely to transmit HIV is even required , and people can be charged for oral sex , hand jobs , and sex with condoms . Since many of these charges occur following sting operations , no actual sex act must take place .
