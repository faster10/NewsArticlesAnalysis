Bush and his allied super PAC raised a whopping 150 million last year , and entered the caucuses with nearly 48 million left to spend . But 90 percent of that resides with the super PAC , Right to Rise USA , and while it can continue its furious ad blitz , it can t pay Bush s rent or staff , and both his campaign and the super PAC saw their fundraising dwindle in the final months of 2015 . In fact , there s a bit of a parlor game in GOP finance circles predicting how Right to Rise will spend all its cash if Bush doesn t pull off a miraculous turnaround .
