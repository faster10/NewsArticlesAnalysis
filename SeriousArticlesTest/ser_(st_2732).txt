4 . Kobe ' s retiring , the Lakers are a mess , it ' s a glorious circus : It is somehow fitting that the most divisive player of the last 25 years is going out in a blaze of conflicting narratives . On the one hand , Kobe Bryant is being lauded for his career accomplishments , which is very much deserved . Standing ovations every night , gifts , video montages , the works . The Atlanta zoo has even named a snake after him . He ' s handled it all with humility and grace , which is great to see . He ' s managing to appreciate the accolades while also acting embarrassed by them . It ' s a fine line to walk and he ' s handling it with great class .
