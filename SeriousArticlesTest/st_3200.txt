The Patriots are a solid team on both sides of the ball , but their offense is the team s real strength . The Patriots entered Monday night s game first in total offense and their four time Super Bowl winning quarterback is a big reason why . Despite a group of no name receivers , Brady leads the NFL in passing yards and has thrown 25 touchdowns against just four interceptions . Along with Carolina QB Cam Newton , Brady is considered one of the favorites to win the MVP award .
