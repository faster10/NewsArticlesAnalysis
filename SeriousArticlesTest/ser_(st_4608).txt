Eye test : Clemson has arguably looked like the most complete team in the country , ranking at or near the top 10 in both offensive and defensive efficiency all season . It has a Heisman hopeful quarterback in Deshaun Watson , who has showed his ability to take over a game in performances against South Carolina , NC State and Syracuse . Clemson seemed to reach its peak in the Florida State game , and the defense has given up too many big plays since then especially against the struggling Gamecocks but overall has continued to find a way to win , even when one phase of the game struggled .
