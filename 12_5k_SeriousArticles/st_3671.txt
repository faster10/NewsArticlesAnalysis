The University of North Dakota announced that the nickname Fighting Hawks had been chosen by students , faculty , staff and others for the school s sports teams . The teams had previously been known as the Fighting Sioux , but the name was retired in 2012 the NCAA had banned the use of Native American related mascots in its postseason tournaments beginning in 2006 , but years of legal battles had delayed UND s change . A new logo and other visuals are expected to debut by the fall of 2016 .
