Since then , science has only proven the need to act more urgently and more decisively . The planet ' s atmosphere can take only a certain amount of carbon dioxide before the global average temperatures reach a point that forces nature into irreversible changes . The tipping point , these 196 countries have collectively decided , is a two degree Celsius temperature rise above pre industrial era . Some contend it should be 1 . 5 degree Celsius . The average temperatures have risen by one degree Celsius already . With developed countries unable to reduce their emissions substantially and emissions from emerging economies quite naturally rising , the space for greenhouse gas emissions in the atmosphere has only shrunk .
