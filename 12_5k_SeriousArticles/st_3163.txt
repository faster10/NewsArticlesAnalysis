At the time of writing , the French authorities have not released the names or the backgrounds of the murderers . A senior police figure disclosed that they spoke with perfect French accents , indicating that even if they were working for Islamic State they were also most probably homegrown . In the past few weeks , the French have been re examining the riots of 2005 and asking what , if anything , has changed since ? for this reason the identity of the killers is a most dangerous fact . As Paris awoke on Saturday morning , the politicians declared that France was now at war . The problem is that nobody knows with whom .
