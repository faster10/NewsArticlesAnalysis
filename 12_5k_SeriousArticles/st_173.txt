Dole in Bush camp : Former Sen . Bob Dole endorsed Bush on Wednesday . MSNBC has a piece on why the formulaic Dole endorsement ( Dole said the former Florida governor is the most qualified candidate ) was remarkable . For a generation , there was no more bitter , colorful and consequential rivalry within the Republican Party than between Dole and Jeb Bush s father , former President George H . W . Bush . Dole s endorsement of a Bush once would have been unimaginable , MSNBC says .
