The provincial legislators also joined the protesting newsmen and condemned the law enforcers for an indiscriminate use of force against the media personnel and PIA workers in the vicinity of Jinnah International Airport . The brazen use of force left two PIA employees dead and several cameramen and photographers injured . " We condemn use of force against journalists and PIA employees , " Sindh Senior Education Minister , Nisar Khuhro said and assured the protesting newsmen that the government would take action if police were found involved in use of forces , killing PIA employees and injuring media men .
