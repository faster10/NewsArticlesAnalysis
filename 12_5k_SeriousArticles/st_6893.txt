AT T : WE VE SHELVED IDEAS THANKS TO NET NEUTRALITY RULES ? The FCC s new net neutrality rules have created enough legal uncertainty that AT T has paused plans to offer some new services , AT T Senior Vice President Bob Quinn said Tuesday . He didn t provide any details . There s no way AT T would have been able to first offer something like T Mobile s Binge On video streaming plan , since the company couldn t predict how the FCC would respond , Quinn said at the Phoenix Center s annual symposium .
