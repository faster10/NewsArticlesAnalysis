The Trilateral Dialogue , which is the first such think tanks initiative among China , Afghanistan and Pakistan , is serving as a recognised primary platform in the three countries . Two rounds of the international conference were held previously , in Beijing in 2013 and Islamabad in 2014 , which were attended by government , diplomats , academicians , scholars and the civil society . Besides the three countries , the third round of the dialogue will also have perspectives from prominent scholars of the United States and Europe .
