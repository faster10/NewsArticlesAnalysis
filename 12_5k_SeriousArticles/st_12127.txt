Iran announced a major boost of 500 , 000 barrels per day in oil production on Monday a move Tehran had long planned for once its nuclear deal with world powers took effect . The next budget starting in March is based on a projected oil price of 40 per barrel price and exports of 2 . 25 million barrels per day . Iran , a member of the Organisation of Petroleum Exporting Countries ( Opec ) , now produces 2 . 8 million barrels of oil per day and exports just over one million barrels .
