WHAT S AHEAD : The stage is set for yet another postseason series clinching victory in Kauffman Stadium . It would be a remarkable fifth over the past two seasons if the Royals are able to close out the Blue Jays either Friday night or Saturday in a potential Game 7 . Last season , the Royals won the AL wild card game at home before finishing off the ALDS and ALCS in their park . Last week , they finished off the ALDS at home with a victory over the Houston Astros .
