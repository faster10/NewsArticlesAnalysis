Several jewellers and consumers that TOI spoke to were divided in their views on whether the new annual interest rate of 2 . 25 2 . 5 being offered to consumers for gold deposits is big enough a bait to lure unused gold out of Indian closets . " The interest rate is quite low . We have to wait for consumers ' reactions . But , the government has taken into consideration the huge volume of unused gold , " said Chirag Verma , director , Lalsons Jewellers , a New Delhi based jeweller .
