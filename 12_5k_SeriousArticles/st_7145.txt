Media attention since North Korea ' s nuclear test yesterday has been focused on the veracity of its claims to have exploded a thermonuclear device or " hydrogen bomb . " This is understandable given that a thermonuclear weapon has a destructive power many orders of magnitude greater than a purely fission based device . However , even if Pyongyang ' s claims prove to be unfounded , its fourth nuclear test ( the first in nearly three years ) will still yield valuable data , moving it closer to a nuclear tipped missile capability .
