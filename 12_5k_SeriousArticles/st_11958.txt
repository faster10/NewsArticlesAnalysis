The position of Syria as a primary Soviet partner in the region in the first half of the 1970s had a background of strong cooperation in the 1950s and an expansion in the following decade . From 1967 new links would be established at the level of political parties between the Baath party and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union . While the coup d ' tat in Damascus that brought Hafez Assad to power was initially perceived with preoccupation and caution by the Soviets , these worries would quickly dissipate .
