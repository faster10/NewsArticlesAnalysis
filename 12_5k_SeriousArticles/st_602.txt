In that sense , this year ' s race is looking more like 2012 than 2008 . In 2012 , Iowa evangelicals ' support was relatively divided . Entrance polls showed Santorum with 32 percent of evangelicals ' support , compared with Ron Paul ' s 18 percent ( remarkably close to the Cruz Trump split that this latest poll shows ) . Three other candidates each took 14 percent . Santorum went on to win the caucuses by just 34 votes over Mitt Romney . Paul finished a strong third .
