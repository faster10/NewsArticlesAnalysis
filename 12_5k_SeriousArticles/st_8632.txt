As the Patriots were beginning their playoff game against the Chiefs on Saturday , the Titans announced that they were removing the interim tag on Mike Mularkey and making him the permanent head coach . The significance for the Patriots is that it takes a potentially intriguing option off the table for offensive coordinator Josh McDaniels , which all but ensures his return to the Patriots in 2016 . I m not sure McDaniels would have pursued the job anyway , as he seems extremely happy in his current post , but in a league where there is considerable coaching change every offseason , the Patriots are once again positioned for stability . As for Saturday , McDaniels called one of the best games of his career Bill Belichick called him magnificent .
