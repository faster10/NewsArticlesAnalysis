While the team awaits the OK to move from testing on mice to larger animals , Almaz n Anaya has more time to dedicate to " Centro de Atenci n al Talento " ( the Talent Attention Center ) , a group that pushes for the early identification and support of gifted children , where he serves as head of psychological research . He hopes his internationally recognized work will spread awareness about gifted kids , and equip them to make the most of their potential , as he has .
