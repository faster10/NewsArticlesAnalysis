The 49ers have the fifth pick in the 2016 NFL Draft , showing that the team is going to soon have another elite college football player on the roster . That will be a good asset for either Chip Kelly or Mike Shanahan to work with , so the task for Jed York is to figure out which coach would be best to rebuild the franchise . The team has struggled since getting eliminated in the 2013 NFL Playoffs , and has a lot of money still owed to quarterback Colin Kaepernick . The new coach will have to decide whether Kaepernick is the answer at quarterback , or of the franchise needs to look in the 2016 NFL Draft for a new one .
