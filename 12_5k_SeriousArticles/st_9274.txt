On some levels , " Sylvia " is a show about a guy going through a midlife crisis who takes up with a dog and comes to see that walks in the park and loving another creature bring him more happiness than another stressful day in the office . That part still lands . But Sylvia the dog is also a metaphor for a mistress . Greg ' s wife , Kate ( Julie White ) , is no fan of the pretty canine nor its influence on her husband .
