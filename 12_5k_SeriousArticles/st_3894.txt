The internment ( not a " sequester " ) was not of Japanese " foreign nationals , " but of Japanese Americans , two thirds of whom were U . S . citizens . I was one of them , and my family and I spent 4 years in prison camps because we happened to look like the people who bombed Pearl Harbor . . . . We were judged based on who we looked like , and that is about as un American as it gets . . . . Mayor Bowers , one of the reasons I am telling our story on Broadway eight times a week in Allegiance is because of people like you . You who hold a position of authority and power , but you demonstrably have failed to learn the most basic of American civics or history lessons . George Takei
