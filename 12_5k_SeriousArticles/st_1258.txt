Rickman debuted his acting chops for a whole new generation in 2001 when he brought to life J . K . Rowling s formidable Professor Severus Snape in the beloved fantasy franchise . The actor played the sarcastic , snakelike potions master to a tee over eight films as he antagonized hero Harry Potter and toed the line between the light and dark sides , even killing off a major character . In his increasingly nuanced portrayal of Snape over 10 years , he reveals himself to be redeemably loyal .
