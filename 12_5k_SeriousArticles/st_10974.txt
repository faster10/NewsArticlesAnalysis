Mufti Mohammed Sayeed , chief minister of Jammu and Kashmir , passed away on Thursday morning at the AIIMS hospital in New Delhi . He became the chief minister of Jammu and Kashmir last year in March . He was also India ' s first Muslim Home Minister . In all probability , according to various sources , Sayeed ' s daughter and PDP president Mehbooba Mufti will take over the reins of the state .
