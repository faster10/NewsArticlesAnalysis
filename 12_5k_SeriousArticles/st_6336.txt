Political pundits , of whom I know a few , are pleased to at last see something in the 2016 Republican nomination contest that they recognize . As our John Sides has explained , the pattern of an upstart candidate is usually a period of " discovery " filled with positive press as a jump in the polls followed by " scrutiny , " in which the media looks more closely at a candidate , often to his or her detriment .
