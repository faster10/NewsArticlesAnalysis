What s next : The two best teams in the NFC all season were the Panthers and Arizona Cardinals . They were a cut above everyone else . And now they will meet Sunday at Bank of America Stadium for the right to go to the Super Bowl . The Cardinals were the trendy pick at the close of the regular season to win the NFC . But they have looked vulnerable in their last two games , including Saturday s overtime victory over Green Bay . This should be a highly competitive matchup .
