China s crude oil imports for the first 11 months of the year rose 8 . 7 per cent to 6 . 61 million barrels per day while its November crude imports grew 7 . 6 per cent from the same month a year ago . China s November new vehicle sales also jumped 17 . 6 per cent over the same period . With crude prices near record lows , China is seen as likely to double its strategic crude oil purchases in 2016 , adding some 70 90 million barrels to its strategic petroleum reserves ( SPR ) .
