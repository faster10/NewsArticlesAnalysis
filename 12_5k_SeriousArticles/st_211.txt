RUBIO GETS GREENISH TINGE : GOP presidential candidate Sen . Marco Rubio told a New Hampshire crowd yesterday that he wants to see the U . S . lead on renewable power , according to the Washington Examiner : " I want us to lead the world in everything , " he said . " Let ' s be number one in wind , let ' s be number one in solar , let ' s be number one in biofuel , number one in renewables , number one in energy efficiency . Let ' s lead in all of these things . " Rubio added that he ' d like to see an " even playing field " for all fuel types , including oil , gas , and renewables .
