Finnmark , Norway Millions of people know Clement Clarke Moore ' s poem " The Night Before Christmas , " written in New York in 1822 and believed to describe Santa ' s mode of transportation , a reindeer driven sleigh , for the first time . But Santa ' s reindeer have a story and a history all of their own , one tied to the oldest indigenous culture in Northern Europe and accelerated by an American entrepreneur whose principal intention was not delighting children around the world , but creating an appetite for what he hoped would become a mealtime staple as ubiquitous as beef .
