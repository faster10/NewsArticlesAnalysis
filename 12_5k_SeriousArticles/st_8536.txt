For the year and a half he was behind bars , Guzm n lived in Cell 20 , the last of ten cells at the end of a dingy hallway in the wing for the country ' s most dangerous criminals . There were surveillance cameras that could see everything , except the small portion shielded by a waist high privacy wall in his shower stall , the exact spot he escaped . That hole descended 30 feet until it reached a mile long tunnel that ended at a cinder block house in the cornfields south of the prison .
