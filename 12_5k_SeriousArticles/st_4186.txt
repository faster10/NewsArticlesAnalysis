Lava flows from the Mount Etna volcano on the southern Italian island of Sicily , near Catania . The volcano on the Mediterranean island threw up an ash column some seven kilometres ( 4 . 3 miles ) high with lava flowing from the Voragine crater for the first time in two years , the National Institute of Geophysics and Vulcanology ( INGV ) said .
