This term has not been without its slip ups . There was a thumping Super Cup defeat to Athletic Club and the embarrassing 4 1 loss away to Celta Vigo . But ever since , it ' s been another purple patch . Ten games played , nine victories , 32 goals scored . 14 of those have come in their last three games . One of those games was an El Clasico massacre ( a 4 0 win in Madrid ) and another a Champions League assignment against the second best team in Italy ( a 6 1 humiliation of Roma ) .
