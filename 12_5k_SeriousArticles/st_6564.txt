The 1970s the decade that saw him dominate the British music scene and conquer the United States brought forward a string of successful albums . " It ' s like two testaments " , he said . He was a convincing alien visitor in " The Man Who Fell to Earth " , a vampire lover in " The Hunger " , a ramrod P . O . W . in " Merry Christmas , Mr Lawrence " , an oddly sympathetic Pontius Pilate in " The Last Temptation of Christ " , and the only Goblin King who could hold his own with a roomful of Muppets in " Labyrinth " .
