The oil rich African countries are now a high priority foreign policy area . Given that the continent is expected to contribute approximately 25 per cent of the growth in the world ' s petroleum and LNG supplies in the coming years , there is enormous potential for India to increase its volume of crude oil imports from African countries . The continent holds 10 per cent of the world ' s oil reserves , with five countries dominating its upstream oil production . Together , they account for 85 per cent of the continent ' s oil production .
