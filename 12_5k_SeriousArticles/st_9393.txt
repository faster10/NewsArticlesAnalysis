Entering Saturday ' s game , Hoyer had 19 touchdowns and seven interceptions . He had passer ratings better than 100 in five of the 11 games in which he played . Hoyer regained his starting job in Week 6 , but missed three games after that due to two separate concussions which he suffered in the span of less than one month . In his absence , the Texans went 3 0 , with T . J . Yates and Weeden playing quarterback against the Jets , Titans and Colts .
