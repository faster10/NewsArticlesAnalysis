It s easy to forget , but so many coaches failed before Saban arrived from the Miami Dolphins in 2007 , with the previous decade featuring five coaches and four losing seasons . It s easy to forget the bridge from Paul Bear Bryant to Saban was littered with so many forgotten seasons , that the fallen power only again became an annual front runner after Saban broke a 17 year title drought in 2009 . Prior to that , the school had won just one championship in the previous 29 years .
