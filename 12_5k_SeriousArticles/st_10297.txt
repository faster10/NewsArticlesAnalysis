The University of Chicago said last month that an analysis by its civil rights and police accountability clinic found of 56 , 000 complaints against Chicago police but only a fraction led to disciplinary action . Among the most notorious cases , dozens of men , mostly African American , said they were subjected to torture from a Chicago police squad headed by former commander Jon Burge during the 1970s , 80s and early 90s . Burge was convicted of lying about the torture and served 4 1 2 years in prison .
