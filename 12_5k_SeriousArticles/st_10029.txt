OBAMA PUTS SOUTH AFRICA IN TIME OUT : It was a busy day for the president , releasing the text and notifying Congress that he wants to sign the deal , but he also found time to suspend benefits for South Africa under the African Growth and Opportunity Act . The action , effective 60 days from now , relates to a protracted dispute with the African country over its barriers to U . S . meat exports , particularly South African anti dumping duties on U . S . poultry .
