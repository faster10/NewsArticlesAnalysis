San Diego Chargers : San Diego ' s iron man Philip Rivers made his first appearance on the team ' s injury report this year Wednesday because of a foot issue . Rivers appeared to suffer the injury at the end of the second quarter when Jaguars defensive end Chris Smith hit him on a pass rush . Rivers limped through the second half of last season with back and rib injuries but did not miss a game . He has been healthy this season , and is currently riding a franchise record 154 straight starts . Rivers ' foot injury is worth monitoring this week . Eric D . Williams
