Two gunmen armed with Kalashnikov assault rifles and explosives stormed the hotel before 7 a . m . on Nov . 20 , striking just as security guards were about to change shifts . After shooting four of the five guards , killing one , they fired wildly in the lobby and breakfast dining area before heading to the hotel s upper floors . Malian troops , backed by French and American special forces , swarmed in to retake the building and free terrified guests and hotel staff during a siege that lasted more than seven hours .
