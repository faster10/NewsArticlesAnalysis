ASU guard Tra Holder , a member of the Pac 12 all freshman team last season , has emerged as the Sun Devils ' primary scorer . The ball dominant guard emerged as one of the better offensive guards in the conference early in league play but is 12 for 43 during ASU ' s three game skid . Junior Obinna Oleka , a transfer from State College of Florida , has been in the starting lineup for the Sun Devils ' last six games and provided inside scoring presence .
