A Christmas decoration representing a shooting star is pictured under the Dole summit in Cheserex , Switzerland . The installation , 160m wide and 75m high with a linear length of about 800m , is attached every summer to the rocks under the radar station on top of the Dole summit ( 1677m 5500 ft ) by its instigator Stephane Richard and a team of 20 volunteers . This year marks the 20th anniversary of the installation that can be seen most clearly near Nyon between Lausanne and Geneva , it will be lit until 6 January 2016
