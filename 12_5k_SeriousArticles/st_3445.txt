In Davenport , in the eastern part of the state on the border with Illinois , Trump touted his savvy when it comes to protecting the country . He reminded his audience that over a year before the 9 11 attacks , he expressed his apprehension about the possibility of a major terrorist attack and singled out Osama Bin Laden by name in his 2000 book , The America We Deserve . He also touted the wisdom of his call for Western airstrikes against ISIL controlled oil fields , which have since come to pass .
