But even a promise of equal time will be incredibly difficult to honor . In the much maligned CNBC debate , Carly Fiorina got the most time ( 10 minutes , 13 seconds ) while Rand Paul got the least ( 6 minutes , 22 seconds ) . But a large reason why Carly topped that list was because toward the end of the debate she began jumping in even when she wasn ' t called on . The rest of the numbers were also skewed because candidates named by a rival were allowed time to respond . Given the campaigns will likely want that practice continued , it would be hard if not impossible for the moderators to guarantee equal time .
