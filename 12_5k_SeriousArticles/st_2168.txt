Senior ministers of Modi s Cabinet are displaying utter contempt at the growing protests of a cross section of enlightened Indian minds . The protests against this growing intolerance , which began with eminent Sahitya Akademi awardees returning their awards , have now snowballed to include litt rateurs , poets , cinema personalities , historians , scientists and others . PM Bhargava , founder chief of the Centre for Cellular and Molecular Biology , has announced the return of the Padma Bhushan , India s third highest civilian award , as a mark of protest .
