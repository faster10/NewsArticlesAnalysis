It has generally improved during strong economic times , according to previous Pew research , like in the 1990s . Public trust in government fell from 77 in 1964 to 26 by 1980 , recovered to 46 by 1991 and fell to 19 by 1994 , a year when President Bill Clinton s health care reform plan failed and the Republicans gained control of Congress for the first time in four decades . After a recent high of 54 in 2011 , it has averaged at or below 25 since 2007 .
