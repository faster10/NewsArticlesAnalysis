The 20 point triple double might have undersold Green s performance , as he played expert defense on Knicks rookie sensation Kristaps Porzingis . From the depths of Green s smothering , the Latvian big man occasionally beamed out flashes of brilliance . If Green s versatility is the vanguard of the NBA , Porzingis might offer the next step .
