With four meetings in Dubai , Federer won their first contest in the 2007 quarter finals , and Djokovic won the next clash in the 2011 final . They met again in the 2014 semi finals , with Federer securing victory , and last year the final saw Federer win 6 3 7 5 . As both players push each other to the limit , there is no doubt that the skill and talent of one has led the other to raise the level of their training and game even further .
