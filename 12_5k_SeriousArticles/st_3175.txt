In his meeting , South African President Jacob Zuma strongly called for reform of global governance system and said the UN structure which was set up after the second world war cannot remain relevant in 21st century . ? He said it was important that all like minded countries come together to ensure reform of the UN , particularly the UN Security Council , observing that many conflicts in the world including the Syrian crisis could not be resolved because of veto powers of the UNSC member countries , sources in the Ministry of External Affairs said .
