Dinwiddie update : Spencer Dinwiddie remains with the Grand Rapids Drive of the NBA Development League , and Van Gundy reaffirmed plans Friday to keep him there for the rest of the season barring injury on the main roster . Dinwiddie is averaging 18 . 2 points and 6 . 3 assists per game since being assigned to the Drive full time at the beginning of the month . In Grand Rapids ' 90 87 overtime win over the Iowa Energy on Thursday , Dinwiddie scored 24 points on 7 of 14 shooting and hit six 3 point shots . " They ' ve been really happy with him , with his work ethic , " Van Gundy said . " He ' s shown leadership around his teammates , helping them out a little bit . He ' s played and acted very maturely , so they ' ve been happy . "
