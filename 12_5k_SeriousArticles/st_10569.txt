Harrison Barnes canned two 3 pointers in the final 1 : 15 to give the Warriors another exhausting but also exhilarating victory , 106 94 over the Chicago Bulls on Friday night at Oracle Arena . It was Golden State ' s 14th win without a loss , one short of the best season start first established by the Washington Capitols of 1948 49 and equaled by the Houston Rockets in the 1993 94 . The Warriors tied the record for best start by a defending champion , set by the 1957 58 Boston Celtics .
