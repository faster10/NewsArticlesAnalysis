Apple ( AAPL ) today said it sold a record 74 . 8 million units of its flagship iPhone during the last three quarters of 2015 . Sounds impressive . But that ' s below the 76 . 54 million unit estimate from analysts polled by Fortune magazine and marks the slowest pace of growth since the device was introduced in 2007 . To top it off , Apple said revenue in the current quarter will come in lighter than had been expected , marking the first year over year quarterly sales decline since 2003 .
