Brussels , with a metropolitan area population of approximately 1 . 8 million people , has been shut down under a threat level of four the highest possible since Saturday . On Monday , schools , banks and malls were closed , and the metro was shut down . Some shops in the suburbs were open , and buses were running normally , Belgian Interior Minister Jan Jambon told RTL radio . But the city ' s center was quiet with a heavy presence of armed police , locals reported .
