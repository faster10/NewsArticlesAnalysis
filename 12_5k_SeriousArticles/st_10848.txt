A police project in Cologne called NAFRI has been exploring the workings of possible groups of North African criminals since Jan . 2013 . Three analysts have analyzed data on more than 21 , 000 criminal offences and 17 , 000 people of northern African origin . But NRW state Interior Minister Ralf Jaeger said the suspects from New Year s Eve were not part of the Antanzen scene . In a report dated Jan . 19 , the NRW interior ministry said of the 29 New Year s Eve suspects just one had turned up as part of the NAFRI project .
