In September , Chinese President Xi Jinping pledged to match US President Barack Obama ' s promised 3 billion in aid to the UN ' s Green Climate Fund . Britain is diverting 8 . 9 billion from its overseas aid budget to climate related aid over the next five years , and France is promising 5 . 6 billion annually by 2020 , up from 3 . 4 billion today . For many delegates here , the goal is for the amount spent internationally on climate aid to add up to an astonishing 100 billion a year .
