At a joint news conference with Mubarak in April 2004 at Bush ' s ranch in Crawford , Texas , he offered support for his Egyptian counterpart . " Egypt is a strategic partner of the United States , and we value President Mubarak ' s years of effort on behalf of the peace and stability of the Middle East , " Bush said . " President Mubarak can be confident in my friendship and America ' s partnership as he moves forward to realize the hopes of his people . "
