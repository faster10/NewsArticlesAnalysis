Days after Obama announced that Bergdahl had been freed , the Taliban released a propaganda clip on social media showing Bergdahl for the first time since his captivity . A visibly gaunt Bergdahl , dressed in all white with his head shaven , emerged blinking at the sun from the back of a pick up truck . He was handed to US special forces in southeastern Afghanistan . He then recovered at a military hospital in Landstuhl , Germany , before being taken to another military hospital in San Antonio in June .
