Not only may Zika have become more dangerous ( it has also been recently linked to a post viral illness which causes temporary paralysis ) , there are signs it has become more efficient in infecting humans it has rapidly raced through the Americas . Alternatively , this speedy expansion may be due to it having access to new populations with no immunity , living in dense , impoverished urban environments that provide ideal breeding conditions for the mosquito that carries it . The warmer , rainier summers associated with El Nino in the region could also be encouraging its transmission .
