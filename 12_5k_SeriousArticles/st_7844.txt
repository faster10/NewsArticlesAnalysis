Mayer s big turnaround plan at Yahoo has focused on rebuilding new businesses apart from its core search and display ad business , a group of businesses she dubbed MAVENS , for mobile , video , native and social . During her prepared remarks Tuesday , Mayer pointed out how Yahoo had developed this new business group that did not exist before her tenure , but analysts pointed out during the company s call that the business group is heading for a sharply slower growth rate , a statement that was not refuted by Mayer .
