Journalists in Turkey are split over the fate of a media company many ridicule for its abrupt switch on the government . Calling Koza Ipek media independent is nonsense , and opposition is a recently earned title born of a grudge . Nevertheless , what happened yesterday was unacceptable . The timing of the raid , the involvement of AKP players and shaky evidence of financial crimes , coupled with similar attacks on opposition papers in recent weeks , all point to a concerted effort of repression by the government ahead of elections that many fear will not be conducted fairly . What was extraordinary about yesterday s raid was the anger it provoked in elements of Turkish society that were previously silent .
