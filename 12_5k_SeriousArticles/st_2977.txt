In their first one on one debate and final face off before the New Hampshire primaries , Hillary Clinton and Sen . Bernie Sanders argued over the definition of " progressive , " the plausibility of Sanders ' campaign promises , capital punishment , and Clinton ' s 2002 vote in favor of authorizing the Iraq War . The two candidates said they agreed about many major policy issues , but Clinton said she disagrees with Sanders ' plan to make public college free and fight for single payer health care , and considers his focus on Wall Street too narrow . Sanders touted his large small dollar donor base and criticized Clinton ' s Wall Street donations . Sanders is favored to win New Hampshire .
