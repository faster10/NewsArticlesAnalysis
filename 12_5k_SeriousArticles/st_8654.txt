In interviews before Sunday s election , Suu Kyi said if her party won , she would run the government and be above the president ? a statement meant to encourage voters but which raised eyebrows among analysts who wondered what she meant . Suu Kyi has not explained or said whom she would nominate as president . If her National League for Democracy does win enough seats to form a government , there is also the challenge of how to lead it . Nobody in her party has any experience running a government .
