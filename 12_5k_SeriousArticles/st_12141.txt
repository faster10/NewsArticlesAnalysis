RAND PAUL GOOFS ON FED VOTE ? POLITICO s Zachary Warmbrodt from POLITICO s Wrongometer : Rand Paul said his Senate colleague , Ted Cruz , was the only Republican to skip a vote on an Audit the Fed bill earlier this month . Actually , Cruz wasn ' t the only GOP lawmaker who failed to participate in the Jan . 12 vote on the measure that Paul wrote . Sen . Dan Coats ( R Ind . ) didn ' t cast a vote on the bill , either . Democrats blocked the bill in a 53 44 vote . The bill needed 60 votes to advance . Cruz responded to Paul ' s attack by saying the bill didn ' t have the votes to pass anyway and that he had commitments in New Hampshire . I look forward to signing that bill as president , Cruz said .
