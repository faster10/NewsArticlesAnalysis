The PMO also has three files about the personal life of Netaji Bose , which are not being made public . These are about Netaji ' s wife , his daughter and his alleged mistress . Prime Minister Modi has promised Netaji ' s family members that ALL the files in the possession of his office will be made public . However , no deadline has been fixed . Even though all the Netaji files are not being declassified , a very important chapter in modern Indian history is about to be re written .
