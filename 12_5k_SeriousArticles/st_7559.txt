Emerging from the fog of sleep , Gregoire Badufle , 42 , and his wife Gwenaelle , 35 , at first wondered whether the gunfire 120 metres from their flat in the Rue de la R publique was the start of another terrorist attack . They were far from the only ones to entertain such fears . But then reason kicked in . If you want to kill people at that time of day , said Mr Badufle , you don t go to the centre of Saint Denis . There s no one about .
