Kyle Banks , ? Andr Verdun Jones and Khary Septh are Brooklyn based artists who came together to create The Tenth , a groundbreaking magazine that explores the experiences of being black and gay . The biannual publication , which is filled with glamorous images , amazing art and powerful written pieces , shatters stereotypes around black gay youth and brings dimension to the experiences and battles they face . " A huge issue for us is the black church and the hateful abomination doctrine being spewed from pulpits all across this country . We stand as a line of defense for so many LGBT youth that lack the proper defense against such rhetoric , " Banks told HuffPost . " As we continue to build our platform , this is just one of the issues we intend to tackle head on . "
