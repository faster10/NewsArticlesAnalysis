At around 0250 GMT , US benchmark West Texas Intermediate ( WTI ) for delivery in February was down 30 cents , or 1 . 02 , at 29 . 12 a barrel ( nearly Rs 1 , 970 . 26 ) . Brent crude for March was trading 24 cents , or 0 . 88 , higher at 28 . 79 ( nearly Rs 1 , 947 . 93 ) . Brent plumbed below 28 ( nearly Rs 1 , 894 . 48 ) for the first time since November 2003 on Monday .
