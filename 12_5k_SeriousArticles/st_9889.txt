Nearly all recent acts of foreign inspired terrorism on US soil have been motivated by an ideology that is widely believed to be endorsed by Saudi Arabia , and almost all the perpetrators of these crimes have had some connection to Saudi Arabia . Yet , the U . S . has opted to take military action against Afghanistan and Iraq in response . Even recent discriminatory American visa regulations exclude a second look at those that have traveled to Saudi Arabia , while scrutinizing ethnic Iraqis , Iranians , and Syrians .
