Saying Cutler , 32 , has arrived now that he ' s working with Gase is a challenging case to make because of the small sample size . The offense in 2013 performed at a high level from the start under Marc Trestman . Statistically , he was better through five starts with Mike Martz in 2010 and Ron Turner in 2009 . Cutler threw an end zone interception at Detroit in the most recent loss , lost a fumble for a touchdown at Kansas City and threw a pick six against the Cardinals in Week 2 . He also led comeback victories over the Raiders and Chiefs , which rank 32nd and 23rd , respectively , in pass defense .
