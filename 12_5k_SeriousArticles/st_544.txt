When we read that headline and saw the powerful side by side photos ? one of Steven Avery the day he was released , smiling , surrounded by family , and the other , flanked by deputies and wearing black and white stripes ? we recognized Steven as offering a unique window into the criminal justice system , Demos tells The Post . He was a man who was failed by the system in the mid 80s and was now thrown back into it . We were there to ask the question : Has the system improved ? If so , how ? If not , what can we do to improve it ?
