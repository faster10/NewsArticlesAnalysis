Under different circumstances a year ago , Chicago easily could have eclipsed Ferguson , Mo . , New York City or Baltimore as the epicenter of the Black Lives Matter movement . The delayed release of the McDonald video spared Chicago only from the immediate protests and outrage that engulfed those cities . But the damage is now being squarely inflicted on Chicago . If Chicago is ever to secure its rightful place among world class cities , it must overcome the violent image conjured by its endonym , Chiraq , and rid itself of the yoke of decades of questionable police conduct .
