In Russia , Putin described the incident as a " stab in the back by the accomplices of terrorists , " adding that it would have serious consequences . He insisted that the Russian jet was operating in Syrian airspace , not Turkey ' s , and thus was not a threat to Turkish national security . Putin went on to accuse Turkey of buying oil from the Islamic State , implying that they were supporting terrorists . He also criticized Turkey for not trying to contact Russia in the wake of the incident and rushed to call a NATO meeting .
