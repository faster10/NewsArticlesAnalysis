Seeing the first set of images from the typhoon zone in the Philippines is like experiencing a dreadful sense of d j vu : Flooded roads , fallen huts , small buildings with the rooftops ripped off , and dead animals littering the streets . If this devastation appears eerily familiar , it is because we also saw the same horrific spectacle last year when Typhoon Haiyan struck . Sadly , both the Philippines and the rest of the world are proving slow in learning the most important lessons of these disasters .
