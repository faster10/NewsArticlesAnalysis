MSCI s broadest index of Asia Pacific shares outside Japan slipped 0 . 7 per cent . China s main indexes slid more than 2 per cent at one point , pressured by concerns about weak bank profits and growing bad loans , but pared losses by early afternoon . Japan s Nikkei eased 0 . 8 per cent , though it remained near a two month high . Even as investors moved to the sidelines after a rally that has seen regional stocks rise more than 12 per cent in a month , market strategists are becoming more optimistic over the outlook for equity markets in the near term .
