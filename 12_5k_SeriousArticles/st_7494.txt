As its name suggests , the Nationalist Movement ( MHP ) , is a nationalist party . In the June 2015 general elections , the party polled 16 . 3 and won 80 seats , becoming the joint third largest political party . The MHP used to be described as a neo fascist party linked to extremist and violent militias . Since the 1990s it has , under the leadership of Devlet Bah ? eli , gradually tried to foster a more moderate image , turning from ethnic to cultural nationalism and conservatism and stressing the unitary nature of the Turkish state . Since 2007 , the MHP has been continuously represented in the National Assembly with a voter share of well above the 10 threshold . It is strongly against the peace process with Kurds , and believes that Turkey s problem is PKK terrorist activity and not Kurdish rights .
