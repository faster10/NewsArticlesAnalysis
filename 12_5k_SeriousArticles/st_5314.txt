During his talks with Pakistani leadership , the sources said the visiting Saudi Foreign Minister will also share details of the 34 national Islamic military coalition against terrorism which the kingdom announced on December 14 , 2015 . Pakistan welcomed the formation of the coalition but had sought further details to determine the envisaged extent of its participation in different activities of the coalition . " We have already stated that we support the coalition . . . there would be consultations bilaterally and collectively , which would be followed by the decision regarding the level of participation by any country , " Prime Minister ' s Adviser on Foreign Affairs Sartaj Aziz told reporters on December 23 .
