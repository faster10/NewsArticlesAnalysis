The water resource sites are places in Flint where residents have been able to pick up bottled water and filters . Fallout from the Flint water crisis is mostly responsible . Then , in January 2015 , Flint violated the U . S . Safe Drinking Water Act because its water contained high levels of total trihalomethanes or THM , byproducts of the water treatment process that , over time , can increase the risk of cancer and cause health problems of the liver , kidneys and central nervous system .
