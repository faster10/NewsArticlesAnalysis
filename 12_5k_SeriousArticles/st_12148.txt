THE BIG IDEA : WHY LOWER OIL PRICES ARE BAD ? Bloomberg s Asjylyn Loder and Matthew Philips : The meme that cheap oil acts like a tax cut goes back decades . Among the reasons the analogy didn t work : The drop in revenue for domestic energy companies can trigger economic contraction not seen with a tax cut . And , surprisingly , the total economic benefit actually shrinks the further prices fall . Increased U . S . energy independence has made the math even trickier . With domestic output near a 43 year high and fuel imports down to 24 percent of consumption , a glut of crude isn t a problem just for OPEC and Texas anymore .
