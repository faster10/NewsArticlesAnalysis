The main security intelligence agency is the General Directorate for Internal Security ? founded in 2008 and tasked with counter espionage , counter terrorism and the surveillance of potential threats on French territory . It has an estimated 3500 employees . This compares to ASIO ' s 1829 employees last financial year , for an Australian population of 24 million , and a Muslim population of about 500 , 000 . In other words France has about twice the security service for a notional target population 12 times larger than Australia ' s .
