Stop The War provided Jeremy Corbyn with his intellectual and political safe space during his years in the wilderness . They were the cocoon that shielded him from the harsh realities of the world as it really was , not how he longed for it to be . When ? after all the marching and all the protesting and all the campaigning ? Tony Blair had the temerity to win the 2005 general election , it was Jeremy s friends in Stop The War who told him not to worry , it was all a bad dream . When David Cameron had the equal temerity to win the 2015 general it was his friends in Stop The War who reassured him this was further proof of how the country was secretly yearning for a genuine , radical , Left wing alternative .
