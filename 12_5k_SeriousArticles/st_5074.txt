Stella Gall , as she is now , recalls the band being driven to the photo shoot in a van by Mr Hadfield . Her mother would often sit in the passenger seat as chaperone . The girls dresses and the boys jackets were specially made although Bowie protested at their uniformity . He would later paint black stripes on the green jacket when modifying it for his next band . The jacket was subsequently bought by Bromley Museum for ? 400 at a Christie s auction of pop star memorabilia in 2006 .
