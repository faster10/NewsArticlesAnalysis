Hollande has announced a raft of new security measures in response to last Friday ' s attacks in the center of Paris , which killed 129 people . That has wide support a poll in Wednesday ' s edition of the French daily Le Figaro found that 84 percent of respondents nationwide were willing to give up liberties in exchange for security . Saint Denis is no different : It has France ' s highest violent crime rate , at 28 . 1 per 1 , 000 people a year , and is begging for more security .
