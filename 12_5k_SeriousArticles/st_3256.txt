On paper , Rubio is the nominee apparent . He is young , good looking , articulate , ruthlessly ambitious , Hispanic and inexperienced enough to pass as a coveted outsider . While he has shown a willingness to change his views when the wind shifts ( immigration , for instance ) he has up to now not exhibited a pitiless meanness so essential to a winning candidate . Last night , he showed that neither truth nor nuance nor fairness will stop him . He could go all the way .
