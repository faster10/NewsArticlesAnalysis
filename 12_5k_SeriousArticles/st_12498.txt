Commodities guru Jim Rogers also feels that 2016 will be a bad year for stock markets . However , Rogers is long China and short American junk bonds . Asked about what convinced him to be long on China , Rogers told ET Now , " Nothing convinced me . China is overdue for a serious correction in the markets . China did not have a recession for many years and that is very unusual . I am not convinced that China has scary problems . America , as it grew to become a great country , had many problems . We had 15 depressions . China is overdue . "
