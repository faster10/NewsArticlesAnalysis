According to AJC Angelil was Celine Dion manager for decades , and the couple were constantly seen together . Angelil was a huge part of molding Dion and a huge part of her international success . Angelil s final wishes were to have his funeral at the same church where he wed Dion . The funeral for Angelil is scheduled for Friday at Montreal s Notre Dame Basilica , the same church the two were married in 1994 . There will be a wake held on Thursday allowing the public to pay their respects .
