The conciliatory approach of the Prime Minister in inviting the leaders of the Congress party for discussion has once again brought the spotlight on the GST Bill . Hopefully , the move will clear the way for the Constitutional Amendment . It is fervently hoped that the government s new engagement with the Opposition will help to build consensus on many important economic policy reforms , including the implementation of GST . The Opposition parties , on their part , have an important role in ensuring smooth passage of important Bills , while at the same time raising legitimate concerns to ensure that architecture , engineering and management aspects of reforms are appropriate .
