As seen in the Montgomery bus boycott , Bull Connor ' s Birmingham and the Selma beatings , local authorities tried to stop political organizing through police and vigilante violence , mass jailings and brutality behind bars . Ferguson protesters , who faced down tanks , tear gas and assorted forms of military grade hardware , tasted what many activists suffered throughout the 1950s and 1960s . As these examples show , the fight to end the criminalization of black protest , and of black people more broadly , lies at the heart of African American freedom struggles then and now .
