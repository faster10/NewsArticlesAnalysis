While the Prime Minister ' s demands for EU reform won ' t significantly change the bloc or the UK ' s relationship to it , they might give him enough ammunition to make the case to the British electorate for the country to remain wedded to the continent . That matters to all Europe for reasons of trade and economics , but also because Britain has been a flag bearer for the free market and EU enlargement towards the East , even as it refused to go along with more integrationist policies like the single currency and the removal of border checks .
