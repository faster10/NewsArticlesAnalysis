Ramadi , located about 130 kilometres west of Baghdad , and nearby Fallujah , which lies halfway on the road to Baghdad and remains under IS control , saw some of the heaviest fighting of the eight year United States intervention in Iraq . Gen . Ismail al Mahlawi , head of the Anbar military operations , told the AP that the advance was hampered by suicide bombers , snipers and booby traps . Gen . Ahmed al Belawi , told The Associated Press that the prime minister kicked off his tour by meeting with security and provincial officials . A USA military spokesperson said that the success today was a proud moment for all Iraqi forces . The troops only entered the government complex , he said .
