As we noted , the Surface Pro 4 has a 12 . 3 inch screen . And it is a glorious screen . With a resolution of 2736 x 1824 pixels , it is a sharp screen . It is also vibrant with excellent viewing angles . If you compare the Surface Pro 4 screen to the displays in other high end tablets like the iPad Pro , you will find a lot of similarities . But compared to the displays in most laptops with similar price , the Surface 4 has a sharper and more vibrant screen . The only exception for this are the MacBook Pros , which too have similar high resolution screens .
