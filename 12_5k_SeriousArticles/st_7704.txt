The district attorneys alluded to general elements for the general public to also keep an eye out for with the hopes of detecting human trafficking , which flourishes both in the sex trade and in the form of illegal or unpaid labor . Such telltale signs include people who avoid eye contact , appear malnourished , show signs of physical abuse , have few personal possessions , are not allowed to speak for themselves , children who take on adult tasks like paying a bill , and an inability to identify where they are or what city they are in .
