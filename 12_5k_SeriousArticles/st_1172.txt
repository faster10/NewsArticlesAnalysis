Once a source of pride for Pakistan , flights of the loss making carrier are now frequently cancelled and many of its aircraft have been cannibalised to keep others flying . The government expressed " sadness and sympathy " for those killed or injured on Tuesday , but also warned against further protests . " Creating problems for the public in the name of protest , creating blockages in the coming and going of passengers and destroying public property is not lawful , " an interior ministry spokesperson said in a statement . Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif on Monday enacted legislation that effectively restricts the airline ' s employees from striking for six months , the government said in a statement . Those convicted of infringements face prison terms of up to a year , and an unspecified fine .
