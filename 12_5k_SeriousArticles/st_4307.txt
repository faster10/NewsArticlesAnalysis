What about increased competition ? If the margins are so good , you d expect smaller housebuilders would want to get in on the act . That hasn t happened . Bank lending is still tight . And smaller players struggle to deal with planning and building regulations . This has , in turn , taken the tension out of land prices , dampening the interest of speculators . A recent report from Savills showed that land prices in some parts of the country are actually falling even as house prices increase .
