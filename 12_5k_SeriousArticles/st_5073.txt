At the time of the photograph , Mr Hadfield was dating Stella Patton , then a 19 year old who appears in the photograph kneeling on the front of the stage . Mr Hadfield recruited Stella and her sister Christine , then 17 , as singers in The Konrads . Stella and Mr Hadfield were married for three years . She subsequently remarried and had three children with her present husband . Her sister Christine Gerety , who also had three children , sadly died of cancer in 2007 at the age of 61 .
