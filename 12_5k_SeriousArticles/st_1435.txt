It was the game of the year , with stakes about as big as you can have in the month of December in the NBA . The Golden State Warriors met the Boston Celtics on Friday with their winning streak on the line . Golden State would have to find a way to keep its winning ways without two starters , as Harrison Barnes and Klay Thompson sat out with ankle injuries . Boston took it right to the Warriors from the start , playing tough , physical defense , and matching them in the transition game .
