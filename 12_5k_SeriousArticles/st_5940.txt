Super Bowl 50 also marks the launch of a comprehensive redesign of CBS Sports ' iconic logo . The broadcast will implement a new on air graphics look for CBS Sports and CBS Sports Network that will debut as part of the Network ' s Super Bowl Week programming and its exclusive broadcast of Super Bowl 50 . Following the Super Bowl broadcast , the new look will be implemented across all CBS Sports and CBS Sports Network ' s telecasts . The previous CBS Sports logo made its debut in 1981 .
