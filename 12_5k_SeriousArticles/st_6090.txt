Groups like ISIS are posting gruesome video of decapitated heads as trophies of battlefield victories , or images of victims from their own side , captioned with vows to avenge them . Links to grainy phone camera footage abound , documenting everything from group executions to a video appeal summoning Muslim women to come to Syria to find a husband among the Islamist rebels . The content is disseminated swiftly around the world on the Internet through a diverse network of jihadists and their supporters , journalists , analysts and onlookers .
