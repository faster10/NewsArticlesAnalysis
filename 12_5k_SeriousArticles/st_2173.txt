5 . In 1962 , Mufti Mohammad Sayeed was elected to the state assembly from Bijbehara , the seat which he retained five years later . He was appointed a Deputy Minister by Sadiq , who by then had become Chief Minister . However , he fell out with the party a few years later and joined the Indian National Congress , a courageous but risky decision at that time given the unstinted support of most Kashmiris to Sheikh Mohammad Abdullah , who was in jail .
