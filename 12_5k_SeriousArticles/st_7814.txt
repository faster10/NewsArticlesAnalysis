Europe also has to deal more comprehensively with the fact that hundreds of its citizens are fighting in the IS ranks . France tops the list , but the presence of Dutch , Swedish and Norwegians fighters in IS boggles the mind . Netherlands has taken a tough stand on jihad after two of its citizens , Sultan Berzel and Omar Yilmaz , were revealed to be prominent members of IS . The Dutch have developed what they call an " Integrated Approach to Jihadism " intended to protect Netherlands and prevent radicalization . As the French are learning , this is easier said than done .
