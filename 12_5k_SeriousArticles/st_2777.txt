At the start of the crackdown on cartels , Mexico requested and received support from the United States in the form of the M rida Initiative . Agreed on in March 2007 , U . S . funding to Mexico increased from 40 million per year in the mid 2000s to 1 . 5 billion in stage one of the initiative . The focus of M rida was to provide Mexican security forces with equipment and training to counter drug trafficking . In response to the criticism that stage one had not addressed capacity building and institutional reform , " Beyond M rida , " or stage two , included resources to improve the rule of law and strengthen communities .
