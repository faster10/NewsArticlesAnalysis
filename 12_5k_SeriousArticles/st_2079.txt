UPDATE : Los Angeles ' acquisition of Chapman is reportedly being held up due to an allegation of domestic violence against the Reds ' closer . According to Yahoo ! Sports ' Jeff Passan and Tim Brown , Chapman was investigated by police in Davie , Fla . , for an October incident in which he allegedly attacked his girlfriend at his home and fired several gunshots into his garage . Though no arrests were made , Chapman could be punished by Major League Baseball under its new domestic violence policy , through which commissioner Rob Manfred has the power to suspend an accused or convicted player for an indefinite period of time .
