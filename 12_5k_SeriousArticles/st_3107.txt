When Mustafa Kemal Atat rk founded the Republic of Turkey in 1923 , after years of war , he was embraced by a population eager to return to the days of the great Caliphs . But Atat rk chose , instead , to modernize , Westernize , and secularize the country . He disbanded the Caliphate , secularized the education system , outlawed Sufi Islam , enforced gender equality , Westernized the Turkish alphabet , and famously banned the fez . But these radical and sometimes ruthless steps , especially those that ran counter to perceived Islamic mores , engendered deep resentment and resistance .
