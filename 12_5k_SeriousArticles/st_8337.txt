Having spent most of his career in Cincinnati , Palmer is used to playing in the elements . But a few of his teammates are not , such as center Lyle Sendlein , who grew up in Scottsdale , Arizona , played at Texas and has only played for Arizona or cornerback Patrick Peterson , who grew up in South Florida , went to LSU and was drafted by Arizona in 2011 . There are about seven players who grew up and went to college in the South and have played in Arizona their entire careers .
