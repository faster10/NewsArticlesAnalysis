Recent statistics have revealed that five per cent of male students have worked in the sex industry in some capacity ( compared to 3 . 5 of female students ) . This film looks at those who have ventured into the online trade and features a number of contributors , including a gay man who believes appearing on webcams saved him from drugs and others who are mere exhibitionists . The film asks what it takes to go online , and looks at their motives as well as how it impacts on their lives .
