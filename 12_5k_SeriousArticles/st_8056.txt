The Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development ( OECD ) has analysed 70 of total global development aid and found that about one in four of those dollars now goes to climate related aid . On Tuesday , Australian Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull pledged to divert almost 1 billion of Australian development aid to climate aid . In October , World Bank president Jim Yong Kim pledged a one third increase in the bank ' s direct climate related financing , bringing the bank ' s annual total to an estimated 29 billion by 2020 .
