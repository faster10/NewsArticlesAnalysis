As d tente took hold and the U . S . emerged from under the cloud of Watergate , the affable Roger Moore was a welcome change of pace from the hard charging Connery . His arched eyebrow and ease with one liners personified the rise of light , audience pleasing entertainment that began in the late 70s and reached its peak in the 1980s . The often overlooked Timothy Dalton rounded out that decade , with a more chaste take on Bond that overlapped with the AIDS crisis in America .
