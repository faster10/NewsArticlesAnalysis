Abdelhamid Abaaoud , a 27 year old Belgian man , was believed to be the ringleader of the attacks on Nov . 13 that left 130 dead and hundreds more wounded . He was one of three people killed during a police raid in the Saint Denis neighborhood of Paris on Nov . 18 . His female cousin , Hasna Ait Boulahcen , also died in the raid , while an unidentified third person is believed to have detonated a suicide vest , which led to part of the apartment collapsing .
