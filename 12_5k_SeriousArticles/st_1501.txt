Before arriving in Casablanca , Rick fought on the Loyalist side in the Spanish Civil War and ran guns for Ethiopia in its fight against Mussolini . At his caf he has made a point of hiring European refugees . He has a Dutch pastry chef , a German refugee headwaiter , and a bartender from the Soviet Union . When a young Bulgarian woman asks for his help in getting her and her husband to America , Rick comes to their rescue by allowing them to win the money they need for papers at his gambling table .
