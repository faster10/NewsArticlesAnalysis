With Germany struggling to handle the inflow and its European neighbors expressing reluctance to help , a political brawl broke out between the three governing parties over how to reduce the influx while integrating those who ' ve fled war and oppression . Looming over Merkel ' s open door stance on refugees was a threat by Seehofer ' s CSU to challenge her policy in Germany ' s highest court . Merkel ' s Christian Democrats and the smaller CSU form a single parliamentary caucus in Berlin , governing in a coalition with the junior Social Democrats .
