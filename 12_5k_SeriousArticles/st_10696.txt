If you believe in liberty , Paris is your hometown , said Bono early on , while during City of Blinding Lights , the names of the victims were projected on the huge walkway running through the center of the venue to create a visual of the tri color French flag . But U2 also made a point of empathizing with the other side of the equation . Images of war torn Syria and displaced refugees were played on the same screen during the howling , anguished Bullet the Blue Sky . Bono even specifically called for the audience to think of the families of the Paris attackers , and consider the pain of losing loved ones to what he termed a perversion of Islamic ideology .
