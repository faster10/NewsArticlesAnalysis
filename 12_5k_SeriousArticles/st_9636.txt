Serious matters were at the heart of the new spectacle . How were so many pleas for greater security lost in the State Department bureaucracy from a part of a world that was , literally , on fire at the moment ? Why was Ambassador Chris Stevens in Benghazi , when international aid organizations and other governments saw it as a place too dangerous to remain ? Why was the line of communications between Clinton and Stevens virtually non existent , given Libya ' s importance in the fight against terrorism and despotism ? The day produced no clear answers on any of it .
