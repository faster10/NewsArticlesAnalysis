South Africa ' s trade benefits under AGOA were subject to a special review following complaints from U . S . agricultural producers . The U . S . and South Africa announced a deal in June that would allow a 65 , 000 metric ton quota for imports of frozen bone in U . S . poultry that would be exempt from the duties . South Africa has yet to put the quota in place , and U . S . producers were concerned the country would use the avian flu outbreak to avoid doing it .
