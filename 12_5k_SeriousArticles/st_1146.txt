Former senator and actor Fred Thompson died on Sunday after a recurrence of lymphoma . He was 73 . In Thompson ' s wide and varied career , he was a Watergate lawyer , a longtime star of the television crime drama Law and Order , a U . S . senator from Tennessee , and a candidate for the Republican presidential nomination . Thompson " was the same man on the floor of the Senate , the movie studio , or the town square of Lawrenceburg , his home , " his family said in a statement .
