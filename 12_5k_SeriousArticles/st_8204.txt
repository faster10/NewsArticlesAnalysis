ELSEWHERE ON THE CAMPAIGN TRAIL BUSH CALLS FOR CABINET LEVEL DRUG CZAR ? GOP presidential candidate Jeb Bush today will tout his plan to elevate the director of the Office of the National Drug Control Policy to a Cabinet level post to coordinate efforts to battle drug abuse . The " drug czar " was a Cabinet level position in Bush ' s brother ' s administration , but not during the Obama presidency . The Bush campaign released a plan Monday evening ahead of candidate s appearance today at the New Hampshire Forum on Addiction and the Heroin Epidemic ? a timely political topic in a state that has been hard hit by overdose and abuse . New Hampshire Sen . Jeanne Shaheen is slated to speak at the forum as well .
