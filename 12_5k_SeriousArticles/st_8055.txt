One of the things we ' re hearing more and more about here in Paris is so called ' climate aid ' . There has been a huge push from climate NGOs to convince rich countries to spend a fortune to help poor countries adjust to global warming . This term is a catch all for money being given from rich countries to poorer countries for global warming education , solar panels , adaptation , or anything you can imagine that can be linked to global warming . This push has already had an effect .
