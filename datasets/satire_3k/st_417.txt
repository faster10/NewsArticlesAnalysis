" When you take into account everything HD 904790 b has going for it , it makes you feel embarrassed to be associated with our dumb world , " said astronomer Gary Lopes , pointing to the planet ' s dozen colorful , reflective moons that make Earth ' s sole natural satellite look like the total joke that it is . " I used to think that the Himalayas were impressive , but not after we received data showing that this new planet has thousands of towering volcanic peaks that blow Mount Everest right out of the water . It ' s flat out humiliating to have to compare our planet against that . "
