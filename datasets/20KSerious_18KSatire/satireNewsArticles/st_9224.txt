Leonard , who joyfully married the pretty and petite Beverly Cline on a lovely day in 1947 , is survived by five children , 12 grandchildren , and countless fans who seek to emulate his perfectly worded and concise , or perhaps the word is curt , or rather laconic , fiction , which was never better than in those narrative situations where tension is at a fever pitch , one character boldly utters something provocative to another , and all hell breaks loose .
