State game officials confirmed that for the next three weeks it will be open season on the fallen leaves of silver maples , cottonwoods , all oak varieties , flowering dogwoods , shagbark hickories , sycamores over 10 feet , larches , and hackberries . As usual , New Hampshire ' s state tree will be fair game for one weekend only , during the annual Great White Birch Hunt , in which each participant is limited to one leaf per 20 permit .
