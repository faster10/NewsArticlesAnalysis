Kamma h na , a radical Mah y na Buddhism movement , was founded in Nepal in the late 1970s with the explicit mission to spread a fundamentalist interpretation of the Four Noble Truths to all nonbelievers . According to experts , the multinational organization operates somewhere in a remote region of the Himalayas , though the current whereabouts of Rinpoche and Kamma h na ' s other high ranking members are reportedly unknown .
