DAYTONA BEACH , FL Daytona 500 organizers and NASCAR executives announced Tuesday that they would honor Dale Earnhardt on the fifth anniversary of his fatal Daytona crash with a new memorial " Wall of Fame , " a six foot tall , 40 yard wide monument to the seven time champion that will be built across Turn 4 of the track and unveiled during the first lap of Sunday ' s season opening Nextel Cup race .
