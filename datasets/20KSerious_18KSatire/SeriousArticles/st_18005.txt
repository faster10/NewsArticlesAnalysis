The defending NBA champs have the reigning MVP Stephen Curry and the Larry O Brien Trophy in tow yet still manage to have a chip on their shoulder , disrespected by those who view their title as a fluke . Their defense will bring more challenges , but they appear equipped , featuring the same sharpshooting ( Curry and Klay Thompson ) and a bit more size ( adding power forward Jason Thompson ) . Missing , at least initially , will be Coach Steve Kerr as he recovers from back surgery , was Golden State seeks to replicate another hot start .
