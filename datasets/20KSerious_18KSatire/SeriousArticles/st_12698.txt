No sooner did the Indian PM Narendra Modi break the news about his short visit to Lahore to meet PM Nawaz Sharif through a twitter message last week than Pakistan s keen eyed electronic media started viewing the instant melting of ice vis vis the deep frozen Pak India relations . An army of political analysts , commentators and TV anchors instantly landed on various TV channel to extensively discuss and analyze this historic event . Equating it with so called cricket diplomacy , a new diplomatic strategy , the birthday diplomacy , was introduced . The body language of both world leaders during the meeting was also tried to be read . At the same time , the nitty gritty of the reception menu was another favourite topic for the media discussion .
