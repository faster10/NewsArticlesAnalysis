The three ruling parties Merkel ' s Christian Democrats , their Bavarian allies , and the SPD are eager to show voters that the government is in control of the refugee crisis before three state votes in March and a general election next year . A dispute over tighter immigration rules has nonetheless been straining the ruling coalition . Merkel has also faced criticism from other European Union countries for her stance on migration , including from Italian Prime Minister Matteo Renzi who she meets later on Friday .
