Merkel , like many mainstream conservatives , has battled against the anti immigrant and anti euro parties that have proliferated across Europe . Yet her refugee policy has , at least in the short term , led to a surge in support for the far Right Alternative f r Deutschland . Her failure to consult her own party , the public and other EU members when making the decision to welcome the refugees was dangerous . Nonetheless , she has on balance been good for Europe . The euro crisis is already a fading memory for the rest of the world . Merkel has done her maths and knows Germany can afford the refugees . In time , public resentment against her should recede . She can be expected to continue to rule both Germany and , unofficially , Europe for some more time to come .
