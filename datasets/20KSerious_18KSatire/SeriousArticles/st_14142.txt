All of which brings us back to Kroenke . This is why Dallas Cowboys owner Jerry Jones loves him . Only the Seattle Seahawks ' Paul Allen outpaces him in net worth , but Kroenke just pulled a move that has to be the envy of every NFL owner not named Spanos or Davis . From the start of this , he sent out all the signals that the league wanted to hear when it came to a Los Angeles prospect . First , he was going to throw the full heft of his significant bank account into a stadium . Second , he was going to be an aggressive and competitive owner in a market that needs a front running team . That ' s an impressive double threat that the league has sought for the last 21 years as it has tried and failed to get another Los Angeles birth past the embryonic stage .
