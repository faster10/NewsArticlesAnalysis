Part of the problem with the Labour leadership s reluctance to rise to the occasion is that no one else is doing so either . The business led campaign has been both lacklustre and lacking vision . The Liberal Democrats , after generations of service to the pro European cause , are in total eclipse . The Scottish National party is unequivocally pro European but is always keener on constructing a grievance against Westminster than in speaking for a Britain in which it does not believe . This week s SNP led dispute about the risk of muddling the referendum with the devolved elections in May fell squarely into the essentially tactical category .
