However , given that the films had to get past the censors , there was less emphasis on sexual sadism than in the books and rather more on blemishes . These were simply another way of performing evil . Dr No ' s steel hands Scaramanga ' s tell tale third nipple in The Man with the Golden Gun ( 1974 ) and the scar that sliced down Donald Pleasance ' s face in his defining performance as Ernst Stavros Blofeld gave material expression to wickedness . It ' s a ploy that , even in recent years , has continued to work a treat . Mads Mikkelsen ' s Le Chiffre in Casino Royale , the first of the Daniel Craig films , has a constantly bleeding eye .
