In his 1am press conference , Raonic safely dispatched after more than four hours of jousting , Murray looked exhausted . Slumped in his chair and hands dug into his pockets , he barely seemed able to hold a coherent train of thought . This has been an acutely stressful interlude for Murray , more so than any of his Wimbledon campaigns . Not that he has acknowledged it publicly , but the situation involving Kim s father Nigel Sears , taken to hospital after collapsing at Melbourne Park last Saturday , had been very precarious . But for the swift intervention of three doctors inside the stadium , the outcome could have been far more grave .
