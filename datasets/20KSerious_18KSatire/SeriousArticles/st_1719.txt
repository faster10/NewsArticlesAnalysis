Boko Haram stormed homes in northeastern Nigeria on Saturday night , killing dozens of people that included sleeping children , the latest in a recent spate of horrific attacks by the Islamic extremist group . Boko Haram fighters opened fire on residents , set fire to houses and targeted a crowd with suicide bombers , as seen on pictures that show the result of the attack with burned out buildings and charred livestock . The assailants also tried to strike a nearby camp for people displaced by Boko Haram but were repelled by troops , he added .
