25 bodies found after landslide in Chinese village : The death toll from a landslide that buried homes in eastern China has risen to 25 , with 12 people missing , a local government official said . A torrent of mud and rocks unleashed by heavy rains buried nearly 30 homes in a village in Zhejiang province late last week . Four people were confirmed dead soon after the landslide . The Lishui city government , which administers Lidong village where the landslide occurred , said one survivorwas in stable condition .
