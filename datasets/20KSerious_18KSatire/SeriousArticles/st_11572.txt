One other QB taken No . 1 overall has made it to the Super Bowl ? Drew Bledsoe ( 1993 ) . Fourteen other No . 1 overall QBs have not made it to the Super Bowl but that number will drop by one come next Sunday between Newton and Palmer . The other 12 : Jameis Winston ( 2015 ) , Andrew Luck ( 2012 ) , Sam Bradford ( 2010 ) , Matthew Stafford ( 2009 ) , JaMarcus Russell ( 2007 ) , Smith ( 2005 ) , David Carr ( 2002 ) , Michael Vick ( 2001 ) , Tim Couch ( 1999 ) , Jeff George ( 1990 ) , Vinny Testaverde ( 1987 ) and Steve Bartkowski ( 1975 ) .
