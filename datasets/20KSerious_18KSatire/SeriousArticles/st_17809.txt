If the results of this year s Hall of Fame balloting are any indication , Bonds and Clemens might want to start working on their Cooperstown speeches now instead of putting it off until the last minute . While neither of them will be joining 2016 inductees Ken Griffey Jr . and Mike Piazza for this summer s enshrinement ceremony , they received enough support from voting members of the Baseball Writers Association of America to suggest that prosperity ? theirs ? could be just around the corner .
