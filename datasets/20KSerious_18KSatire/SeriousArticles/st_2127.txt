Gore Vidal was a man of immense literary talent , some of which he used well , some of which he wasted . His essays are widely thought to be his best work . Two of his historical novels , " Burr " and " Julian " are gems . The more widely read and prize winning " Lincoln " ruffled Lincoln scholars and many Americans , who objected to Vidal ' s depiction of our 16th president as a ruthless proto fascist intent on crushing the South and transforming the small government American republic into a version of Bismark ' s Germany .
