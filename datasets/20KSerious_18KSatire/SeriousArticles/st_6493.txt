Netflix has launched in 130 more countries , CEO Reed Hastings announced at CES in Las Vegas , hailing the moment as the " birth of a global TV network " . Netflix is now available in 21 different languages . It looked like the Los Gatos , California , company had plenty of work ahead it because it ended December in 60 countries . Filling that hole will likely require potentially hard negotiations with a government that prevents its population from seeing material it considers objectionable or incendiary .
