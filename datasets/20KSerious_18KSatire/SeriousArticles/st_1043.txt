Tom Hardy in " The Revenant " : The talented actor had one electrifying year , from playing the killer Kray twins ( a two pack of performances that should have landed him a best actor nod ) in the misguided " Legend , " to perfectly filling Mel Gibson ' s shoes as Mad Max . While his co star Leonardo DiCaprio was garnering most of the glorified attention for " The Revenant , " Hardy , nearly unrecognizable in his villainous turn as a greedy and insane frontiersman , demanded Oscar love . And he got it . Richly deserved .
