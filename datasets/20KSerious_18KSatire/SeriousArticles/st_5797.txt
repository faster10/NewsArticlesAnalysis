Paul would exclude these refugees : Kentucky Sen . Rand Paul pledged Monday to introduce legislation that would bar the State Department from allowing any refugee fleeing a country with a jihadist movement into the U . S . The Fiscal Times writes Paul , who is also seeking the GOP presidential nomination , told reporters the Paris attacks should wake us up to the fact that we can t just let anyone come to this country . At least one of the Paris attackers was carrying a Syrian passport , which French officials said was a fake .
