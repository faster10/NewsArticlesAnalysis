MAINE GOV PROMISES TO VETO MEDICAID EXPANSION ? Gov . Paul LePage is once again vowing to veto any Medicaid expansion bill the legislature approves . His comments come after two moderate Republican senators announced that they would push the issue again this year ? citing the difficulties the uninsured face in accessing substance abuse treatment programs . The Portland Press Herald reports that the number of people seeking treatment for opioid abuse increased from 1 , 115 in 2010 to 3 , 463 in 2014 .
