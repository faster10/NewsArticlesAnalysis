" The one thing I can tell you is that what Mr Trump ' s saying about how to handle this war is empowering the enemy , " said the South Carolina senator , referring to Trump ' s recent assertion that the U . S . should " go after " the families of terrorists . " ISIL loves Donald Trump because he is giving them an opportunity to bring people their way , " Graham said during a gathering with reporters following his formal speech to the Republican Jewish Coalition .
