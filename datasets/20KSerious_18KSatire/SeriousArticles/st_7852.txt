On the sticky financial issue which is seen by the rich world as something which may even be looked into outside the agreement , Mathur clearly said , " We look forward to an agreement that enables financial support from the countries that have developed on the back of cheap energy to those which have to meet their energy demand by more expensive but low carbon zero carbon energy .
