Contested convention ? Republican officials are preparing for the possibility of a brokered convention as Trump continues to dominate polls in the GOP presidential race , the Washington Post reports . The Post writes about a dinner in Washington where more than 20 officials and other figures gathered to discuss the possibility . People familiar with the meeting told the Post that several longtime GOP power brokers argued that if Trump storms through the primaries , the party s establishment must lay the groundwork for a convention floor fight in which the GOP s mainstream wing could coalesce around an alternative .
