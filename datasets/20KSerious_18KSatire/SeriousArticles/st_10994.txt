Less than 24 hours after I left the Ritz Carlton , the Airbus carrying Russian tourists from the Red Sea resort of Sharm el Sheikh to St . Petersburg crashed in the Sinai desert . While investigators pick over the wreckage and examine the black box data , the media has run with the possibility that the plane was brought down by a missile or by a bomb on board . Egyptian and Russian investigators will take their time to reach conclusions the consequences could be disastrous for either country if terrorism or negligence are shown to be the cause . Even without the announcement , the British government has stopped flights from the UK to Sharm while security arrangements at the Egyptian airport are assessed .
