WRDA work begins again : The ink is barely dry on 2014 s Water Resources Reform and Development Act , but work has already started on the next bill to authorize new lock , dam , ports and ecosystem projects . Senate Environment Public Works Chairman Jim Inhofe and Ranking Member Barbara Boxer last month put a call out for locals priorities with an eye toward a February hearing . So far , nearly two dozen new projects have received sign off from the top general at the Army Corps of Engineers , making them ripe for authorization . Lawmakers are likely to try to keep the bill simple . WRDA 16 should be much smaller than WRRDA 14 , being that WRDA 14 was the major , major policy reform measure , said Tracy Zea with the Waterways Council , Inc .
