It s way too early to predict whether Zuckerberg and Chan will in fact ever be good at philanthropy or whether their style and strategies will spread to the sector at large . And there are plenty of additional dangers that lurk in the announcement ? in the letter , for instance , the couple commit themselves to advocacy towards policy change at a moment when the power of wealth over the polity is a particular sensitive topic . But what is clear is how complex , consequential and difficult to characterize the new generation of large scale philanthropy has become . The Smithsonian s curators have their work cut out for them .
