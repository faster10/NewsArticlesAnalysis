CAUCUS APPEAL : Los Angeles Times looks at MARTIN O MALLEY s newest attack line ? highlighting HILLARY CLINTON s distaste for caucuses . O Malley has seized upon a private comment Clinton made in 2012 about political caucuses , referring to the precinct level gathering of campaign activists and partisan faithful as creatures of the parties extremes . . . . O Malley immediately declared his umbrage on behalf of the Hawkeye State and its civic minded masses . The people of Iowa who go to their caucuses are people that care very passionately about our country , O Malley said . O Malley also noted that Clinton has a long strained relationship with Iowa .
