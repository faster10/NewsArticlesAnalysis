Scorsese ' s ambitious film links the handmade , hand painted , and hand cranked movies the magician Georges Meli s made between 1896 and 1913 to the digital wizardry of films made today , 100 years later , especially to the Harry Potter films . " Hugo , " a beautiful and overwhelming phantasmagoria , chooses 3 D and color over " The Artist " ' s flat screen black and white . Those choices inject the film with a layer of whimsical irony : Today ' s technology is contrasted with yesterday ' s . Scorsese uses the most sophisticated digital technology available to reconnect us to a cinema that was optical and mechanical glass and gears hardware , not software .
