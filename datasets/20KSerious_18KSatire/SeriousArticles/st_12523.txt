German Foreign Minister Frank Walter Steinmeier said world diplomats who gathered in Vienna had agreed on a fixed calendar for Syria that would see a transition government in six months and elections in 18 months . We have agreed that this transition process must be over in 18 months , and will include the creation of a Syrian transition government in six months , Steinmeier . The American and Russian diplomatic teams were among the larger group of participants in the Vienna talks among the backers of rival parties in Syria .
