As a consequence , Iran has supported violent extremist groups , including Hezbollah in Lebanon , the Houthis in Yemen and sectarian militias in Iraq . Iran or its proxies have been blamed for terrorist attacks around the world , including the bombings of the United States Marine barracks in Beirut in 1983 and the Khobar Towers in Saudi Arabia in 1996 , and the assassinations in the Mykonos restaurant in Berlin in 1992 . And by some estimates , Iranian backed forces have killed over 1 , 100 American troops in Iraq since 2003 .
