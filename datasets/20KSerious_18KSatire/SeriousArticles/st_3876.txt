Saudi Foreign Minister Adil bin Ahmed Al Jubeir is arriving in Islamabad Thursday ( today ) to hold talks with civil and military leadership on issues of mutual interest , including the current escalation in rhetoric between Iran and Saudi Arabia . Jubeir , in his first visit to the country , would meet Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif , President Mamnoon Hussain , Chief of Army Staff General Raheel Sharif and also hold bilateral talks with Adviser to the Prime Minister on Foreign Affairs Sartaj Aziz .
