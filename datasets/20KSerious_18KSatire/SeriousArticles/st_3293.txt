Serena , the world number one , had won all six previous Melbourne Park finals she had played and had also triumphed in her last eight Grand Slam deciders . But Kerber , inspired by a message from Graf , made clear before the match that all the pressure was on her opponent and she mercilessly exploited her weaknesses in a thriller at Rod Laver Arena . " My whole life I was working really hard and now I ' m here and I can say I ' m a Grand Slam champion , so it sounds really crazy , " she said , choking back tears . " The best two weeks of my life and career . I had goose bumps here on the centre court when I was playing . "
