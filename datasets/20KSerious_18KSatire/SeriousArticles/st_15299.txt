Sanders and Clinton have both recently acknowledged the importance of South Carolina ? the fourth voting state ? to their bids . For Clinton , it could serve as her late February firewall should she struggle in the three states that precede it , thanks to her broad lead in the polls here and her long standing connections to the local African American population . For Sanders , it presents an opportunity to prove he can appeal to black voters and a chance to build on any momentum he might have after Iowa , New Hampshire and Nevada vote .
