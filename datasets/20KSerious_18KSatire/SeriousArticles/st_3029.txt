Clinton and her team say they always anticipated the race would tighten , yet they were not prepared for Sanders to become so popular with young people and independents , especially women , whom Clinton views as a key part of her base . Given her many advantages , like rich donors and widespread support from Democratic Party elites , she is also surprised Sanders fund raising has rivaled hers and that her experience ? along with her potential to make history as the first woman president ? has not galvanized more voters .
