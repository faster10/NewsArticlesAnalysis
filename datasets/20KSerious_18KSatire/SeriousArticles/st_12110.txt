Mexico extradites 2 drug suspects to U . S . : Mexico said it has extradited two drug trafficking suspects to the United States , one of whom is thought to be a major cocaine distributor for the Sinaloa cartel . They were identified as C sar Gastelum Serrano and Pedro Alejandro Rubio P rez . Gastelum Serrano was arrested in April in the resort of Cancun . The U . S . Treasury Department placed him on its drug kingpin list in December . Rubio P rez was arrested in 2013 on drug and organized crime charges .
