Yet , Washington s neutrality and its inclination to accept any agreement that falls in line with its policies to establish a balance of power between the two camps in Syria may end up serving the interests of Russia and Iran . The latter two have been trying to achieve some military successes on the ground to use on the Vienna table of negotiations . To prevent negotiations from becoming a political cover for the continuation of Russian and Iranian military assaults in Syria , parties to the conflict must at least agree to spare civilians from bombardment while talks are underway .
