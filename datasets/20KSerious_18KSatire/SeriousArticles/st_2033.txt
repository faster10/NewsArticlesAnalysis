To those who know Kovaleski well , the impression was unmistakable . As Trump spoke at the rally and spoke of Kovaleski , he gyrated his arms wildly , imitating the unusual angle at which Kovaleski s hand sometimes rests . Kovaleski suffers from Arthrogryposis , a congenital condition that limits the movement of the joints and weakens the muscles around them . Trump is familiar with Kovaleski and has seen him many times , since Kovaleski covered and met Trump as a reporter at The New York Daily News .
