Are these the whitest Oscar nominations ever ? Following announcements that only 20 of 2015 ' s Black List scripts are written by women , only four women were nominated across both writing categories . In a promotional Oscar video , Rock pours white talcum powder over his hands and claps them . In acting categories , omissions included Idris Elba in " Beasts of No Nation " and Will Smith in " Concussion " . But one would have hoped that given the nominations a film like Straight Outta Compton has received from other guilds , it would have received a best picture nomination .
