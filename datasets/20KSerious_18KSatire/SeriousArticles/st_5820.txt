This figure came out of the ill fated Copenhagen climate summit six years ago , when developed nations made a rash promise to spend 100 billion a year on ' climate finance ' for the world ' s poor by 2020 . Rachel Kyte , World Bank vice president and special envoy for climate change , recently stated that the 100 billion figure " was picked out of the air at Copenhagen " to rescue a lastminute deal . Yet , in the way that these things often go , achieving that arbitrary goal has become fundamental to the success of the Paris summit .
