The attention given to Thursday ' s announcement underscores how much the Halftime Show has transformed since the first Super Bowl was held in 1967 in Los Angeles . For the event ' s first decade , marching bands from such universities as Grambling State and Florida A M ruled , with an occasional name celebrity like Carol Channing or Ella Fitzgerald joining in . In the late ' 70s and ' 80s , the focus turned to colorful themed productions , saluting Motown , Mardi Gras , Hollywood and other iconic cultural treasures .
