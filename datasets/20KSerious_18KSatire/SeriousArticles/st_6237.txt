No president , regardless of popularity , can go it alone . Important unfinished business awaits bold leadership from Congress , as the president s speech underscored . Despite an improved job market , too many Americans have seen their economic fortunes dwindle as wages fail to keep pace with the cost of living , the president said . He challenged Congress , regardless of who takes over the White House next year , to devise measures aimed at giving everyone a fair shot at opportunity and security in this new economy .
