In any event , barring another committee mood swing , Ole Miss will go to the Sugar Bowl for sure if Florida loses to Alabama . The Gators may not even stay in the top 25 with a loss . If Florida does pull off the upset , Alabama will almost certainly remain as the highest rated SEC team , but the Gators would go to the Sugar Bowl as SEC champs . It is difficult to imagine that the committee could have such a complete change of heart regarding Florida and consider it a playoff contender with a win .
