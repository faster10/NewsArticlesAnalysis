A moderation in world ' s second largest world economy China means that there will more ( downward ) readjustment to the global growth . The World Bank has already lowered its forecast for 2016 growth to 2 . 9 from a 3 . 3 projection in June as slowdown in China prolongs a commodity slump . What aggravates China ' s problems is the increasing leveraging of China ' s economy . The total China ' s debt is about 28 trillion ? roughly half of the world ' s entire debt . China ' s high debt levels are the nation ' s main short term risk , the World Bank said , noting that debt to output ratios are larger than in most developing countries .
