Obama s eighth speech at the yearly breakfast also capped a trio of addresses over the past week that focused on themes of religious tolerance . Last week , he appeared at an Israeli embassy event honoring gentiles who saved Jews from the Holocaust , and on Wednesday , he made his first appearance at an American mosque . At each , he has called for unity , and while Wednesday s mosque speech was intended to reassure a marginalized community , Obama used the prayer breakfast to challenge his fellow Christians and , implicitly , his Republican political opponents .
