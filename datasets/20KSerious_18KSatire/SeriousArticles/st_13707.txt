More and more , we put ourselves in the strange position of using technology to get away from technology . When we travel , smartphones connect us with places to find quiet and solitude , sometimes from within quiet cars on trains when we shop , we browse computer screens for hand sewn fashions and crafts . When we watch TV , high def screens show us the natural world in never seen before detail , taking us to places we will never visit , including , recently , thermal volcanoes on the bottom of the ocean , where undreamed of species exist , looking uncannily like the papier m ch creatures in Meli s ' " A Trip to the Moon . " The world is in our face , but untouchable .
