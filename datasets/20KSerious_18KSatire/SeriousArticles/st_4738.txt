Litvinenko ' s murder is one of the most publicly investigated in history , so much of what ' s in the report isn ' t new . But to have a recently retired British High Court judge say he is " sure " ( for which read " beyond a reasonable doubt " ) about the guilt of Andrei Lugovoi and Dmitry Kovtun is a sobering moment for Britain and should be just as serious for Russia . Since the murder , Putin has personally honored Lugovoi , who has been turned into a national hero and senior member of the Russian parliament .
