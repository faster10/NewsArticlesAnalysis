Subway style Taking the subway in Paris can be a drag . Striking drivers and filthy platforms can make the Metro a misery . But the light at the end of this often dark and dingy tunnel is the Palais Royal Mus e du Louvre stop , or namely the Palais Royal entrance . Like a lot of things in Paris , it ' s an artwork in its own right , by French artist Jean Michel Othoniel . His design of brightly colored class beads is a delight , and nearby Place Colette is a great spot for people watching : pull up a pew and enjoy .
