Fifteen men have held the mantle of world No1 since Hewitt s Australian Open debut , from Thomas Muster and Pete Sampras to Marcelo Rios and Juan Carlos Ferrero . Hewitt himself pinched it off Gustavo Kuerten and eventually handed it over to Andre Agassi . That sounds like a game of pass the parcel at a tennis retirement home . Yet Hewitt s endured , linking the eras of Sampras and Agassi to that of Federer , Nadal and Djokovic . Until this week he s also been keeping players almost half his age honest .
