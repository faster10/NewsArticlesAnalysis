Riyadh reacted after protesters burned Saudi diplomatic missions in Iran after the kingdom on January 2 executed Shia cleric Nimr al Nimr . He was among 47 people put to death in a single day for " terrorism " . Most of those executed were Sunnis . According to the Saudi Press Agency , Salman " welcomed the prime minister of Pakistan and his delegation " to his palace . The report gave no details of their discussions , which SPA earlier said were to touch on regional as well as bilateral issues .
