It was the first time Alphabet reported results since Google announced a change to its business structure last August . The company broke out separate results for Google , which includes its search , ads , YouTube , Android products , and other traditional Internet based businesses , and for what is now called Other Bets , which include Access Google Fiber , Nest , its life sciences business Verily , GV , formerly called Google Ventures , Google Capital , its biotech arm called Calico , and X , its R D arm that handles projects such as driverless cars and Google Glass .
