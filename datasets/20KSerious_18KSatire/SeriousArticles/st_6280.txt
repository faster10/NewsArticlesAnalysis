Unless the dollar strengthens considerably , stocks are poised for a rebound , said analysts . " Market should rally whatever may be the Bihar election results outcome as Indian markets are oversold without any valid reasons . Indian markets have lost 4 in the last one month as against a 7 rally in the US markets , " said Raamdeo Agrawal , joint managing director , Motilal Oswal Financial Services . " Many informed investors have started taking positions as they feel there is limited downside from the current levels . " All sectors ended weak on Thursday . Among the top losers , BSE ' s Realty index fell 2 . 6 and BSE ' s Healthcare index fell 2 . 07 .
