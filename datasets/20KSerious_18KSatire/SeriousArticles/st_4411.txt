BJP President Amit Shah , who returned to the Capital after personally handling a hard fought campaign that became unprecedentedly fierce , refused to answer questions at an event at party headquarters . Shah said he will address the media on Sunday after the results are announced . Party workers , however , were confident about victory and said that young voters had backed the NDA in large numbers . RJD chief Lalu Prasad countered by saying that the grand alliance would sweep the Bihar polls by winning 190 out of 243 seats .
