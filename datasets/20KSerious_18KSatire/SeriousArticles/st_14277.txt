Construction payrolls increased 46 , 000 last month , the largest gain since January 2014 . Retail jobs rose 30 , 700 and transportation and warehousing employment rebounded after two straight months of declines . Professional services added 27 , 000 jobs and government payrolls increased 14 , 000 last month . " It ' s hard not to like today ' s reading on the labour market . We can anticipate further improvement . . . next year , " said Scott Anderson , chief economist at Bank of the West in San Francisco .
