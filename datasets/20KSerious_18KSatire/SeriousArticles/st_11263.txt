The President of France and the Prime Minister of India agreed to intensify cooperation between the Indian and French security forces in the fields of homeland security , cyber security , Special Forces and intelligence sharing to fight against criminal networks and tackle the common threat of terrorism . They looked forward to early conclusion of an Agreement on Prevention of Consumption of Illicit Drugs Psychotropic Substances between the two countries and expressed hope that cooperation in this field will also lead to the disruption of terrorist financing structures .
