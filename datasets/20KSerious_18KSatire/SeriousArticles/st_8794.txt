Although the rebuttal was initially well received by several Republican presidential candidates , Haley s response speech far from a guaranteed stepping stone to political stardom . Louisiana governor Bobby Jindal s maladroit response to Obama s first State of the Union address in 2009 was roundly mocked , and his national profile never recovered . Senator Marco Rubio , the only current Republican presidential candidate to have seen the president s final address in person , is still haunted by internet gifs of his clutching for a bottle of water .
