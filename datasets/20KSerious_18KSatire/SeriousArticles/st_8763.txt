MQM ' s lawmaker , Muhammad Hussain Khan condemned the torture by law enforcers against journalists and PIA workers . " The protesters were using their Constitutional rights and similarly the journalists were performing their duties , " he said , asking the government to take notice of the violence . PML F ' s Imtiaz Shaikh felt sorry for the torture against journalists and PIA employees . " Protests and struggle were the democratic rights of all , " he said , adding that his party would highlight the issue in the provincial legislature in the next sitting . PML F ' s Nusrat Sehar Abbasi demanded for an action against the responsible personnel .
