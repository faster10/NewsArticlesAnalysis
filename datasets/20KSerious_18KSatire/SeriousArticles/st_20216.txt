The battle for the top spot between Trump and Cruz is one between precision and performance art . Cruz , whose campaign is relying on a sophisticated data operation and massive army of volunteers on the ground to turn out the specific voters it needs to win , is taking the more traditional approach to Iowa s caucuses . The Texas senator , with just hours to spare , on Monday completed his " full Grassley " of visiting all 99 of Iowa ' s counties ahead of the caucuses . Cruz said he did it to " show the respect I think anyone who wants to compete in this state owes to the men and women of Iowa , to look you in the eyes , answer your hard questions . "
