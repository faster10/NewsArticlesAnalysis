Last year , Daniele Bracciali of Italy was banned for one year and fined 22 , 500 . The Italian Tennis Federation had previously suspended Braccali and another Italian , Potito Starace , for life , in connection with a criminal match fixing probe , but the players appealed the ban and Starace had his overturned completely . Also last year , Australian police charged 26 year old Nick Lindahl with corrupt betting offenses , accusing him of intentionally losing a 2013 match on which his friends had placed bets . Lindahl , whose career topped out with a world ranking of 187 , promptly retired .
