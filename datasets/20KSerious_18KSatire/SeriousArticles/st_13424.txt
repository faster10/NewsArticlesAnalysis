Editor ' s note : For 85 years , Yankee Stadium has hosted some of the greatest moments in sports . On Sunday , the Yankees will play their last game before the stadium is torn down . Former Yankee pitcher Jim Bouton played on the 1963 American League All Star team and in two World Series . He wrote the classic baseball book , " Ball Four , " named as one of the " Books of the Century " by the New York Public Library , and has been a sportscaster and actor .
