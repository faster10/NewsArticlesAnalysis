For example , during an interview with Moyers in 2004 , Warren , then a Harvard Law professor , talked about a meeting she had in the late 1990s with Clinton , who was then first lady . Clinton called Warren in to talk about a piece of bankruptcy legislation that Warren opposed . Research done by Warren showed it would negatively affect women and children . After the two women met , the Clinton administration reversed its position , Warren said , and President Clinton ultimately vetoed the measure .
