The Fox Business debate has shrunk the size of the main debate stage , from the 11 candidate high at the CNN hosted GOP debate in September , down to eight candidates for next week ' s debate . Gov . Chris Christie and former Gov . Mike Huckabee will now appear for the first time this cycle on the undercard debate stage , alongside former Sen . Rick Santorum and Gov . Bobby Jindal . Donald Trump , Ben Carson , Carly Fiorina , Sen . Marco Rubio , Sen . Ted Cruz , former Gov . Jeb Bush , Gov . John Kasich and Sen . Rand Paul will appear on the prime time debate stage .
