Last May , as the Iranian nuclear deal was being finalized , Mahan Air was able to purchase nine used Airbus commercial airliners , taking advantage of a relaxation of sanctions that came with an interim agreement Iran struck with Western powers . Experts say that the new lifting of the Interpol red notices ? essentially arrest warrants ? on Arabnejad and Mahmoudi further reduces pressure on Mahan Air and , by extension , on the Assad regime and Hezbollah , even though their Treasury Department sanctions remain in place .
