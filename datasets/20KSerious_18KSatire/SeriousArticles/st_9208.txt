So far , Russian statesmanship has gotten the upper hand as all pre conditions have been scrapped off the negotiating table ? including any mention of President Bashar Al Assad . All talk of him having to step down has been removed from the terms of reference of what is popularly being called Geneva III . According to US Secretary of State John Kerry , this is up to the Syrian people to decide in any upcoming election . If the Saudi backed opposition continues to boycott the process , Geneva III will kick off without them .
