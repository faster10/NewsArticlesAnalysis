Yemen s warring sides agree to talks , U . N . envoy says : Yemen s warring parties have agreed to hold peace talks in Switzerland next week , U . N . special envoy Ismail Ould Cheikh Ahmed said in Geneva . He said the talks between Yemen s government and Shiite Houthi rebels would begin Dec . 15 in a venue to be determined . He said only Yemenis would take part . The United Nations estimates that at least 5 , 700 people have died since the conflict escalated in March and a Saudi led air campaign in support of the government began .
