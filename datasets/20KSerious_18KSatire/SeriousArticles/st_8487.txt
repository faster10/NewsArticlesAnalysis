THE BOTTOM LINE : While never graphic in an R rated sense , there are highly intense scenes : Maud on hunger strike in jail , being force fed with a tube up her nostril marching women beaten bloody by police suffragettes throwing rocks through shop windows , blowing up mailboxes and bombing a government minister s empty house a re enacted 1913 incident , when suffragette Emily Wilding Davison ran onto a racetrack at an event attended by King George V and was trampled to death , clutching a Votes for Women banner . A bullying supervisor at the laundry sexually paws and harasses a young woman and Maud .
