In 2010 , however , Mostefai came on the radar screen of French intelligence because of his association with radical Islamists at a mosque in Luc , near Chartres , a city southwest of Paris . The mosque is a modest , wooden shuttered building surrounded by wrought iron railings . Its leaders were bewildered to find themselves in the limelight Sunday . They called a news conference to deny any connection to extremism . The current administration , they said , took over in 2013 after Mostefai had left the area and they said no one recalled ever seeing him there .
