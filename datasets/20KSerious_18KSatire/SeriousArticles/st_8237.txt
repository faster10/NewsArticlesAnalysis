Despite having trailed in the polls for months before Election Day , Bevin managed to eke out a victory in a state that has long trended conservative . Barack Obama lost the state by a margin of 60 to 37 in 2012 and the last Democratic presidential candidate to carry the state was Bill Clinton . However , Democrats had maintained a foothold in state level politics . The state has only elected one Republican governor in the past 40 years and the state house of representatives is the only state legislative chamber still held by Democrats in the entire south .
