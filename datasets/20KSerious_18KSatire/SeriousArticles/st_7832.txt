Ingo Westen , vice president of the German Moroccan society based in Dortmund an hour s drive from Cologne , said young North African men arrive with high hopes for lives in paradise but quickly become disillusioned by life with just a bed and a small stipend . That easily results in people who are not particularly strong getting corrupted by ringleaders who say : let s rob the department store over there or steal a mobile phone or clothes , and then we ll have a little bit of money when we sell them . His organization worries that longer settled migrants who have long been integrated into German society are increasingly falling under blanket suspicion too . It called for the German government to declare North African countries like Morocco to be safe and rigorously deport people who commit crimes .
