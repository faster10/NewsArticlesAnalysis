Along with his father , Mohammad was placed under the microscope , as everyone attempted to assess his capabilities , with scores deriding his limited skills . Gratuitous rhetoric reached new heights after the execution of a convicted Saudi national , Shiite Shaikh Nimr Al Nimr , with Bruce Reidel , a former CIA analyst , adding insult to injury when he wrote that the move signalled royal worries . It took the enviable skills of Adel Al Jubeir , the affable Saudi Foreign Minister , to silence such nonsense when he challenged western leaders to respect the Kingdom s legal system and , equally important , to stand by it against extremist forces that threatened all GCC states .
