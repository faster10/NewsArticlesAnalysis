JonMichael , whose parents say he ' s normally shy and quiet , stood up at a school board meeting for Comal Independent School District in New Braunfels and delivered an impassioned speech about why every school in the district should be closed on Martin Luther King Day so that students and teachers can honor the civil rights pioneer . Then over the proceeding year , he kept the pressure on , speaking out on radio stations , writing letters and gathering a diverse group of friends together to join his cause .
