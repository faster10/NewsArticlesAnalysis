At times like this we tend to wonder aloud where a player like Hewitt sits in the nation s cultural millieu and contextualise him in our lives all those summer nights , every single fist pump , the echoed cry of C mon ! Most likely we ll remember him at his early 2000s peak , when he competed for our sports watching attention with cricket hero Shane Warne . As sporting figures they shared some similarities . Both courted controversy with their controversial and unrepentant behaviour . Both had their private lives splashed across every magazine , newspaper and television station in the country on a regular basis .
