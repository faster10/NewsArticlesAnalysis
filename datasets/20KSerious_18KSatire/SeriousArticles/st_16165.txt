Vnukovo airport employees unload luggage from Emergency ministry airplane IL 76 at Vnukovo airport outside Moscow , Russia , Sunday , Nov . 8 , 2015 . Emergency ministry airplane IL 76 delivered luggage of tourists from Egyptian Sharm El Sheikh . Around 3 tons of luggage of tourists who departed with 5 flights before were reloaded by emergency ministry staff . Luggage of tourists from other regions of Russia will be delivered by local delivery companies . Russians flying out of Egypt are allowed to take only cabin baggage . The Ministry of Defence said Sunday that it has sent two Il 76 cargo planes to Egypt to bring back larger luggage the Russians had to leave behind . ( AP Photo Denis Tyrin )
