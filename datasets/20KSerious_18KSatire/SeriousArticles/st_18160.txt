Recent polls show 43 percent of Brits in favor of leaving the EU , 36 percent wanting to stay , and 21 percent undecided . And the number of pro Brexit voters has been increasing . An exit could have serious consequences for Britain as well as the rest of the EU , ranging from financial markets to scientific research . And with serious challenges including terrorism and migration in need of unified solutions , it would deliver a brutal blow to the union s political credibility at a time when it doesn t have a lot to spare . ?
