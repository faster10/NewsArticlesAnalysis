Veerayatan has a charitable school at Lachuar that imparts education to 2 , 000 children for poor families . The organisation also runs a charitable school and a BEd college at Pawapuri , the place where Lord Mahavir attained ' nirvana ' . It also runs an eye hospital at Rajgir . Pradeep Jain , general secretary of Patna based Jain Sangh , said the idol of Lord Mahavir made from black stone was installed at Chatriakund , Lachuar , some 2 , 600 years ago . He said it is a very precious and spiritual asset for Jains living across the world .
