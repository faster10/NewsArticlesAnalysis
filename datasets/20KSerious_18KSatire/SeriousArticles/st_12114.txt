Mosque in Canada is set on fire : Police say a mosque in Peterborough , Ontario , was deliberately set ablaze , and they are looking into the motive and searching for suspects . Police said Sunday it was unclear whether the fire was connected to the attacks in Paris . The mosque was set ablaze at 11 p . m . Saturday , about a half hour after members of Kawartha Muslim Religious Association were in the building celebrating the birth of a baby , said Kenzu Abdella , the association s president . No one was injured .
