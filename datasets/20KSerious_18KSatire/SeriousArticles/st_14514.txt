While the focus at Le Bourget has been on climate change , there is little getting away from the November 13 terror attack in Paris . India ' s engagement with Pakistan even in this limited manner at a time when the both climate change and terrorism are top global challenges is worthy of note . The question is whether this brief encounter in a northeastern suburb of Paris will lead to external affairs minister Sushma Swaraj travelling to Islamabad for the regional conference on Afghanistan on December 7 and 8 . Neither side divulged the content of the conversation . Though at odds with each other , there has been some cooperative action by the two countries in the area of climate change .
