2 . Break up the biggest banks . If elected , Sanders said he would direct the Treasury Department to compile a list of the institutions whose failure would pose a catastrophic risk to the United States economy without a taxpayer bailout . Using the power of executive authority , he would break up these institutions . Within one year , my administration will break these institutions up so that they no longer pose a grave threat to the economy as authorized under Section 121 of the Dodd Frank Act .
