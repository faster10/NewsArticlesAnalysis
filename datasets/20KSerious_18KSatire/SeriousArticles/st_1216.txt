Salah Abdeslam , 26 , French , born in Brussels , Sept . 15 , 1989 . Source : notice published by French police police sources . Suspected of having rented black VW Polo car used in attacks in Paris . Lawyer Xavier Carette told Belgian broadcaster RTBF that Abdeslam returned from Paris to Brussels on Saturday morning after being stopped by French police three times along the way . His brother , Brahim , was killed in the attack ( below ) . Both Salah and Brahim were known to Belgian authorities for having radicalized and were questioned earlier this year , Belgian prosecutors said . It was not clear whether Salah had traveled to Syria .
