The Saudi move comes as oil prices trade at their lowest in 12 years , flirting with the 30 a barrel level as OPEC floods the market and Chinese demand slows down . On Friday , Brent crude , the global oil benchmark , traded at 34 . 50 a barrel . For investors , a smaller chunk of Aramco could be easier to value and to swallow . And the kingdom has already some experience . In January 2008 , it listed a venture called Rabigh Refining and Petrochemical Co . , with Aramco retaining a 37 . 5 percent stake , Sumitomo Chemical Co . Ltd . another 37 . 5 percent and the rest sold into the local market . Rabigh , which processes about 400 , 000 barrels a day , is today worth about 2 . 4 billion .
