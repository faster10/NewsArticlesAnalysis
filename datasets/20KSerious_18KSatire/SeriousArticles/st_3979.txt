Seven foreign ministers are participants , including all the four neighbouring countries of Pakistan . Visiting Islamabad for the second time this year was Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi , Afghan Foreign Minister Salahuddin Rabbani and , unusually upbeat on arrival , India s Minister for External Affairs Ms Sushma Swaraj with Irani Foreign Minister Jawad Zarif making his third visit in about four months . High ranking delegations from 14 participating countries , 17 supporting countries and 12 international and regional organisations included the former US ambassador to Pakistan , Richard Olson ( now US representative to Pakistan and Afghanistan ) , with senior representatives coming from Russia , Saudi Arabia , Turkmenistan , Azerbaijan , Turkey , Kazakhstan , Kyrgyzstan and the UAE .
