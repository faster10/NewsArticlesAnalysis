Google DeepMind employs more than 200 AI researchers and engineers . Over the 18 months or so it s spent on AlphaGo , the team ballooned from two or three people to 15 , Mr Hassabis said . Go is a pretty sizable project for us , he said . DeepMind recently hired Mr Matthew Lai , a London researcher who developed a system capable of playing chess at the grandmaster level . His software was able to reason in a way similar to how humans do , a more efficient method than IBM s attempt to crunch every possible outcome before making a move in the 1990s .
