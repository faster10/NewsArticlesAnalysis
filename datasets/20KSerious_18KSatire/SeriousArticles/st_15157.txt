It shouldn t be surprising that North Koreans are realists . For decades , Americans thought Mao Zedong was an irrational , unstable dictator . But when the chips were down , he also turned out to be a serious realist by opening better relations with President Richard M . Nixon and Henry Kissinger after his relationship with Moscow deteriorated . In fact , I have been present several times when North Korean officials have met privately with Kissinger . They seemed to view those sessions as unprecedented opportunities to learn at the feet of a US statesman whom they deeply respect and admire .
