At times here , the feeling bordered on outright resentment . Jolly good show ! one wag cried , in mockery of Konta s polished British elocution . After all , how could any self respecting Aussie girl dare forsake the glorious surf of Sydney s northern beaches for a life in Eastbourne ? Konta , to her credit , has handled all the quizzical attitudes to her allegiance with patience and grace . Be grateful for what you have , she told Australians , reaffirming that Britain was where her heart lay . And it is not as if the locals have gone unrewarded in the passport lottery at this tournament , having already claimed Dasha Gavrilova ? the effervescent 21 year old who was competing for Russia only 18 months before reaching the fourth round here ? as one of their own .
