In fact , Saban takes as much pride and satisfaction in the preparation as the result . Just listen to him talk : with Saban , the theme is always putting yourself in the position to be successful . Prior to the national championship game against Texas , the contrast between Saban and then Longhorns coach Mack Brown could not have been more pronounced . Each coach gave a lengthy press conference two days before the game , and Brown was an avuncular , humorous presence . Saban , on the other hand , sounded as much like some new age counselor as a football coach .
