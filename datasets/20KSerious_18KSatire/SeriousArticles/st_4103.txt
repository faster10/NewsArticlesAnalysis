The EC on Wednesday allowed more time to Amit Shah and Rahul Gandhi to reply to the show cause notices served on them for statements that prima facie violate the model code of conduct . The two leaders may now file their responses by November 6 . The EC notices issued on Sunday had asked Shah and Rahul to explain the controversial statements before 3 pm on November 4 . Shah got the notice for saying " crackers would be burst in Pakistan if the BJP is defeated ( in Bihar ) " . Rahul is in the line of fire for accusing BJP of " pitting Hindus against Muslims " in Benipatti , Madhubani .
