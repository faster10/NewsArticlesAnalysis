Mr Strobe Talbott , the president of the Brookings Institution and a former deputy secretary of state , said any real alliance would require a seismic change in the Russians approach towards Syria , where they say they are trying to fight terrorism but appear more bent on preserving Mr Assad . Maybe it s getting through to them , said Mr Talbott . They keep talking about being part of a solution . But they talk the talk of being part of the solution and they walk the walk of being part of the problem .
