As we grew up we dug deeper , backwards into Bowie s back catalogue . But the key connection with suburban girls and boys from outsider backgrounds was that determination to escape , as I found when I made my Radio 4 documentary I Dressed Ziggy Stardust . Dana Gillespie , who went out with the pre Bowie David Jones , recalled the shock of going into his house : David looked like a stranger in his own home . There was a sadness about the whole thing . It was him wanting to get away from his roots that drove him . He had that extra killer instinct which you need if you want to step up and out of where you ve been .
