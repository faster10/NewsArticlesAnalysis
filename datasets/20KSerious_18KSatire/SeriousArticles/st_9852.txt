While his soon to be estranged bandmates stuck to the script vis a vis red carpet attire for men , Harry Styles sauntered into the show in a suit seemingly made from a pair of his grandma s Laura Ashley curtains . With Liam , Niall and Louis dressed for a day at the bank , Harry came as Laurence Llewelyn Bowen and basically owned the evening . Aware that the suit was perhaps a bit much for the stage , he later switched to a pair of black flares , a yellow rose bowtie and some silver sparkly platform boots . This is what being a pop star is about : making the ridiculous look vaguely aspirational .
