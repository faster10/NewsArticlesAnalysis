In the immediate aftermath , national pundits heaped praise on the way Clemson challenged Alabama every step of the way . While it makes ACC fans gnash their teeth to hear this , the reality is Clemson s run was validated because it stood toe to toe against the big , bad Tide and the big , bad SEC . Yes , the Tigers beat Florida State and Notre Dame and North Carolina ? three teams that finished ranked in the top 15 . But for those watching nationally , who have ascribed the SEC as the only entity all should be measured against , a close loss equaled much more respect .
