" It ' s Kind of a Funny Story " Craig Gilner ( Keir Gilchrist ) is a stressed out 16 year old who bikes to a mental health clinic one morning and checks himself in , hoping to stop the noise in his head . The youth ward is closed for the day , so he ' s placed in the adult ward and an unhinged patient named Bobby ( Zach Galifianakis ) takes him under his wing . Craig falls for another displaced teen , Noelle ( Emma Roberts ) . Craig is stuck in the ward for the minimum five days stay but accidentally finds what he needs . Cute . Out October 8 .
