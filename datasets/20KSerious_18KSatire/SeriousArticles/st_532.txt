The outbreak of another all engulfing conflict in Europe is an eventuality many of us would rather not contemplate but it is a possibility that senior military and intelligence chiefs regularly play out in the form of deadly serious war games . Such events are conducted in secret behind closed doors , but here the BBC recreates one in which a committee of formerly senior figures explore a scenario that has haunted strategists ever since Russia got involved recently in Ukraine . What would happen if Putin decided to target other states with large Russian communities , such as Lithuania , Estonia and Latvia ? all of which are members of Nato ?
