Newton was a five star recruit , ranked 28th in the nation overall and second at the dual threat quarterback position in his class . He ranked one spot below Tyrod Taylor in the Rivals 100 ( Jimmy Clausen was first that year , and Ryan Mallett was the other quarterback ahead of Newton , at fourth overall ) . We all know Newton ' s story after that , going to Florida , he left after some off field issues , led Blinn College to a junior college national title , then led Auburn to a national title , and now has the Panthers in the Super Bowl .
