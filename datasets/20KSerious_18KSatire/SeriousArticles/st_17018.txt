Given the current uncertainty about the potential development of the Syrian conflict , Russia called for the resumption of political talks . Moscow was and remains concerned about being drawn into a war of attrition with no clear ending . At the same time , Russian President Vladimir Putin seems also to have become absolutely content by the amount of influence he has so far obtained in Syria , following his decision to intervene military at the end of September . Al Assad s visit to Moscow just a few days before the quartet meeting in Vienna in October 23 , indicated the peak of Russia s influence in Syria . With the military avenue already explored and the imminent danger of escalation for all sides , it seems an apt moment to return to the political track .
