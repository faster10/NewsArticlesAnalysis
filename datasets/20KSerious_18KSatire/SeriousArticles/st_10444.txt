Data from the non Latino sub sample in the American National Election Studies in 2012 showed that when about 4 , 900 eligible voters were asked whether Latinos face discrimination only 8 . 75 said that Latinos do face a great deal of discrimination . Conversely , when asked about gays and lesbians 20 . 2 claimed that gays and lesbians face a great deal of discrimination . In fact , only 67 stated that Latinos face discrimination at all whereas 90 stated that gays and lesbians face some type of discrimination .
