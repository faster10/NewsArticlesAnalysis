Hadiya Pendleton , 15 , was killed by gunfire in North Kenwood in a park on Jan . 29 . When she and a group of students from King College Prep sought shelter from a rainstorm under a canopy at the park , a gunman ran toward them and opened fire . She was not the target of the shooting , according to police . As outrage over Pendleton ' s slaying spread from City Hall to the White House , Pendleton became a symbol of escalating violence in Chicago while fueling the national debate over guns and crime .
