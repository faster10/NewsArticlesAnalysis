Embrace the underdog role : Clemson fans are upset the No . 1 team in the land is a touchdown underdog , but coach Dabo Sweeney is likely to use it as motivation . It should work . Clemson has shown up on the big stage recently , beating Ohio State and Oklahoma ( twice ) . The Sooners are clearly outclassed by Clemson now , and the Tigers have a chance to complete a trifecta knockout of college football royalty by taking down favored Alabama . The opinion of bookies ought to inject emotion into the team , not deflate it .
