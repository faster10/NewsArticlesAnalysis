Answering a question on Pathankot terror strike and that most of the terror attacks in India emanate from Pakistan , Hollande said , " France strongly condemned the attack on Pathankot . India is fully justified to ask for justice against perpetrators . " " India and France are confronted with similar threats : we are attacked by murderers who pretend to act on religious basis . Their real objective is widespread hate . They want to undermine our democratic values and our way of life . India and France are united in their determination to act together against terrorism " , the French President said in a written interview .
