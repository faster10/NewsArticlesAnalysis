What does all of this mean for America s future policy toward North Korea ? Immediate , strong responses to provocations are fine . So are public statements of indignation bigger and better sanctions more pressure on the North s Chinese allies to support these measures military steps to show the North Koreans and our allies that we are resolute . These are all warranted . But the North Koreans are in this for the long haul . They feel that the survival of their country and its government is at stake . Unless Americans take them seriously and formulate a long term strategy for stopping this threat , rather than adopting ad hoc tactical responses , when North Korea conducts its fifth nuclear test a few years from now , the United States will find itself , like Captain Renault in Casablanca , rounding up the usual suspects .
