The exact sequence of the attack in the Baghdad al Jadida area of the Iraqi capital was not immediately clear but security officials and an AFP reporter described scenes of chaos . The attack , which IS claimed in a statement posted online , was a departure from the jihadist organisation ' s usual modus operandi of suicide car bombings . According to an official from the interior ministry , gunmen blew up at least one car bomb before spraying gunfire in the street and storming a mall called Zahrat Baghdad .
