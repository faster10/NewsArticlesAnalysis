She also put a lot of effort into the show s opening seven minutes , crooning her way through a stripped back version of Waiting for Tonight before realising that no one wants to hear her sing ( Tonight is all about the music , she said ) and discarding the mic for a relentless dance assault on the year s best songs ( and Nicki Minaj s Anaconda ) .
