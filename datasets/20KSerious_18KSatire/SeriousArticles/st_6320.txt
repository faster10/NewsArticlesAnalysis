Even though the Clinton team has sought to convey that it has built a national operation , the campaign has invested much of its resources in the Feb . 1 caucuses in Iowa , hoping that a victory there could marginalize Sanders and set Clinton on the path to the nomination . As much as 90 percent of the campaign s resources are now split between Iowa and the Brooklyn headquarters , according to an estimate provided by a person with direct knowledge of the spending .
