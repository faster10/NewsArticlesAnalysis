Gleaner ' s Community Food Bank has played a vital role in fighting hunger in Metro Detroit for over 35 years . Since 1977 , the institution has supplied about 45 million pounds of food to over 600 partnering organizations in Wayne , Oakland , Macomb , Livingston and Monroe counties . The food bank serves as a clearinghouse to gather , store and distribute surplus food to soup kitchens , shelters and pantries . Those outlets then share it with those who need it most in their communities . Gene Gonya , Gleaner ' s founder , also helped establish to a national network of food banks called Feeding America ( formerly Second Harvest ) and the Food Bank Council of Michigan .
