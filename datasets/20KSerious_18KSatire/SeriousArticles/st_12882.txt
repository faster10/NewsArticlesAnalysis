In this August 2012 photo provided by Marta Mirazon Lahr , researcher Frances Rivera , right , Michael Emsugut , left , and Tot Ekulukum excavate a human skeleton at the site of Nataruk , West Turkana , Kenya . This skeleton was that of a woman , found lying on her back , with lesions on her neck vertebrae consistent with a projectile wound . She also had multiple fractures on one of her hands . Writing in a paper released Wednesday , Jan . 20 , 2016 , by the journal Nature , scientists said it s one of the clearest cases of violence between groups among prehistoric hunter gatherers .
