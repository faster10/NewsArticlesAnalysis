Even by Andy Murray s standards of theatre , this Melbourne fortnight has triggered an emotional maelstrom like no other . His heightened state of agitation throughout a churning semi final victory over Milos Raonic , muttering and spluttering at his players box between almost every point , could easily be excused by the tumult of this trip Down Under . The imminent arrival of his first child back home , coupled with the distress caused by his father in law s collapse on Rod Laver Arena last weekend , has brought psychological extremes to which even Novak Djokovic would struggle to take him .
