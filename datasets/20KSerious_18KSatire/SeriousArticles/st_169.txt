FACT CHECK : U . S . IS BIGGEST CARBON CUTTER : In his speech , President Obama said the U . S . " cut carbon pollution more than any other country on Earth . " We rate this as true ! The White House used a tool created by the World Resources Institute which combines multiple databases to show carbon dioxide emissions for every country up until 2012 , the most recent data available . In Obama ' s first term , the U . S . cut 58 . 7 million metric tons of carbon dioxide , the most of any nation in absolute terms , though that represented only a 0 . 9 percent reduction . In percentage terms , North Korea came up first with a 25 . 7 percent reduction , but ME is deeply , deeply skeptical of data reported out of that country . Second was Syria , which cut its emissions by 20 . 4 percent , which will just have to do .
