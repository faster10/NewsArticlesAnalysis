With just three months remaining before the first votes are cast , two candidates who have spent little time here , Donald Trump and Ben Carson , are leading in most national and New Hampshire Republican polls . And Sen . Marco Rubio of Florida is gaining , in large part because of his debate performance last month . Bush , Christie and Kasich , who are languishing in the same surveys , are trying to climb their way back by winning over voters in small towns , schools and senior centers in New Hampshire , a state each of them has cast as crucial to his candidacy .
