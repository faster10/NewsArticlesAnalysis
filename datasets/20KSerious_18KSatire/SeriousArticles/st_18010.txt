On paper , the possibilities are limitless . Reigning rookie of the year Andrew Wiggins and super versatile Karl Anthony Towns , the past two No . 1 overall draft picks , teaming with a presumably healthy Ricky Rubio and Kevin Garnett , who is back for one last hurrah . There s also a blend of youthful exuberance such as point guard Tyus Jones and Zach LaVine and veteran experience in Tayshaun Prince and Andre Miller . But what would make for a competitive fantasy team might in reality be a handful of misplaced , non conforming parts that will extend a rebuilding project that has been going on for a decade .
