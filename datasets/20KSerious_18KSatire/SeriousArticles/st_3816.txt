Mike Evans vs . Saints : If Jameis Winston is the start of the week at quarterback , that obviously means at least one of his weapons should do heavy damage . The Saints cornerbacks , Delvin Breaux and Brandon Browner , play sides and don t shadow opposing receivers , so Evans will run routes against both of them . According to Pro Football Focus charts , Evans runs 43 percent of his routes at right corners ( Browner ) and 40 percent at left corners ( Breaux ) . Breaux is the tougher draw , but he s allowed a league high eight touchdown passes in his coverage . Browner has given up four and is Pro Football Focus 116th ranked cover corner out of 116 qualifiers . That s dead last . Both Evans and Vincent Jackson are strong plays , but Evans has the mammoth upside .
