Evangelical leader and Liberty University President Jerry Falwell Jr . announced his endorsement of Donald Trump on Tuesday . Falwell has long been a supporter of the real estate mogul , giving a gushing introduction when Trump spoke at Liberty University earlier this month . Falwell said in his statement that he considers Trump to be " a successful executive and entrepreneur , a wonderful father , and a man who I believe can lead our country to greatness again . " The support comes at a crucial time for Trump , who is locked in a head to head battle with Ted Cruz in Iowa less than a week before the caucuses .
