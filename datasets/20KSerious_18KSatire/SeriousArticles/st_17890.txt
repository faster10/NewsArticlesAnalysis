The poll and others like it have found support among Americans for policies to crack down on greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide , and Monday s findings show that that support extends even to the worldwide policy realm . Publics around the world back their governments signing an accord limiting greenhouse gas emissions . Sixty eight percent of Democrats said global warming is causing a serious impact now , and just 29 percent of Republicans said so . Just last month , during a trip to Asia , Obama criticized Republicans for opposing the admission of refugees from Syria into the United States .
