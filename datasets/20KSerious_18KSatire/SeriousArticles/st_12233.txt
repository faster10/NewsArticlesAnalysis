Andrew Milgram , Managing Partner of Marblegate Asset Management : We were very concerned to learn that Senators working on the federal transportation bill have added a completely unrelated financial amendment that would retroactively overturn 76 years of legal precedent established under the Trust Indenture Act ( TIA ) , directly impact several high profile court cases pending adjudication and remove vital protections for minority investors for which the law was created . This amendment directly undermines legal due process and Constitutional procedure .
