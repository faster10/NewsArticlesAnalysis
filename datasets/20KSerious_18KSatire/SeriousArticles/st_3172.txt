In a time when almost everyone shares almost everything , the question of boundaries between a doctor and patient is thornier than ever . Some doctors have firm rules against socializing with patients or revealing personal details about their own lives , while others say a closer relationship can build trust and make it more likely patients will follow medical advice . The growth of social media complicates things , too . With this in mind , professional medical organizations are issuing new and updated policies to help doctors navigate these tricky questions .
