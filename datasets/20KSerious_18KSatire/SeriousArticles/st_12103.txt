Last but definitely not the least is a familiar face , the Suzuki Swift in its Suzuki Swift RS incarnation . The car flaunts a red color scheme , a new grille , new headlamp cluster with blacked out casting , projector headlamps , DRLs around the foglamp cluster , all around side skirtings , rear spoiler , a new tail lamp cluster setup and different set of alloy wheels . Under the hood , the car features the same 1 . 2 litre petrol engine with a higher compression ratio , which helps the car produce 91 PS 6000rpm and 118Nm 4400rpm of torque .
