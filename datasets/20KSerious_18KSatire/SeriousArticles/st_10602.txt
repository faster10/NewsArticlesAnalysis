In October , toxic levels of lead were found in the water at three area schools , Michigan Live reported . Flint switched back to Lake Huron as a water source later that month , but the damage had already been done : Water sourced from the Flint River had such high levels of lead that it drew toxic metal out of the city ' s water pipes . In December , Mayor Karen Weaver said in a statement that the lead poisoning " will result in learning disabilities and the need for special education and mental health services and an increase in the juvenile justice system . "
