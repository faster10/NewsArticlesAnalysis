We work with a number of very fresh ingredients in order to serve our customers the highest quality , best tasting food we can , co chief executive Steve Ells said in the statement . Our deepest sympathies go out to those who have been affected by this situation and it is our greatest priority to ensure the safety of all of the food we serve and maintain our customers confidence in eating at Chipotle .
