Most Britons do not want further integration into a unitary European state , which is the present direction of travel . They want greater power and choice over the decisions that affect them , improved protections for countries outside of the eurozone and guarantees that our economy will not be crippled with regulation . They are also concerned about the scale and pace of immigration to Britain . Immigration is the most salient issue ? it goes to the heart of the economic and security fears associated with EU membership . Only comprehensive review of the terms of Britain s membership will likely satisfy swing voters .
