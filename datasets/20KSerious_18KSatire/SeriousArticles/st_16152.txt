Female fans such as the future journalist Shyama Perera were a regular presence on his doorstep at Haddon Hall and at the stage door . She recalls her outraged mother calling her a streetwalker for her devotion to going out at night to spot him about town . She remembers how Angie Bowie wrote beautiful letters to thank them for their drawings and gifts and how his PR woman , Cherry Vanilla , helped them all get into concerts . So while every establishment figure from the prime minister to the archbishop of Canterbury is declaring their love today , I will be remembering David Bowie as the south London boy who encouraged us all to love the alien within .
