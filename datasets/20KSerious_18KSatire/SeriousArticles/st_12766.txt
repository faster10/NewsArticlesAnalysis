A Marylebone Cricket Club ( MCC ) side , captained by India ' s ' God of Cricket ' Sachin Tendulkar , beat a Rest of the World XI led by Australian Shane Warne by seven wickets in a 50 over one day game in front of a packed crowd . Six months after retiring from the sport , Tendulkar is playing alongside his long time teammate Rahul Dravid , West Indies Brian Lara and Australian pace bowler Brett Lee while Warne ' s Rest of the World team includes the likes of Adam Gilchrist , Kevin Pietersen and Shahid Afridi .
