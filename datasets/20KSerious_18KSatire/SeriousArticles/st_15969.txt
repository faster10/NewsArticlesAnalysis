Seattle quarterback Russell Wilson threw a last second touchdown pass to wide receiver Golden Tate that was confusingly ruled a touchdown by replacement officials . Tate who was blanketed by three Green Bay defenders , shoved one defender , which should have drawn an offensive pass interference penalty . Tate and Packers safety M . D . Jennings both caught the ball in the air , but Jennings appeared to have possession for the interception . Officials and the replay booth ruled touchdown for a 14 12 Seattle win . Days later , the NFL settled and brought the normal referees back .
