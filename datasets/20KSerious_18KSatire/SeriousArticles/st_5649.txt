Opinion against the refugees will build up mainly in western and northern Europe . Already , riots have broken out in many parts of Sweden ? on November 12 , the Scandinavian country imposed border controls ? among immigrants , refugees and Swedes . There have been attacks on immigrant schools , while refugees have burnt cars and agitated for better employment prospects . Poland and Switzerland have both imposed their own version of controls . The big losers from the events in Paris are the Syrian refugees , who will possibly find it increasingly difficult to enter Europe .
