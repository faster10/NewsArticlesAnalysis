Often described as Turkey s answer to Greece s Syriza and Spain s Podemos , this democratic socialist party stood for the first time as a party in June with promises to drive forward the Kurdish peace process and end ethnic , religious and gender discrimination . With a programme focused on minority rights , the rights of women and LGBTs , it was founded in 2012 and led by chairman Selahattin Demirta ? and chairwoman Figen Y ksekdag . Before winning 80 seats in June , the HDP s MPs stood as independent candidates since Turkey s election threshold of 10 only applies to political parties . It is the only political party in Turkey that has a 50 women quota . The HDP describes itself as an environmentalist and anti capitalist party that opposes nuclear power and vows to improve workers rights .
