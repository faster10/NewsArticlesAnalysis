The year of 2016 marks the 60th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Arab states . Sixty years have passed , and the two sides are embracing a new round of high speed development nowadays . China and Egypt will hold the grand ceremony of the 2016 China Egypt culture year at Luxor in Egypt , which will become a feast of dialogue between two civilizations and play a significant role in enhancing exchanges between different civilizations and promoting the development of cultural pluralism .
