The timeline said the two Navy Riverine Command Boats deviated from their planned course on their way to the refueling . It doesn t say specifically what caused the boats to stray into Iranian waters , but states , at some point one RCB had indications of a mechanical issue in a diesel engine . Iranian boats then approached the RCBs in Iranian waters , and armed military personnel boarded the Navy boats . At gunpoint , the RCBs were escorted to a small port facility on Farsi Island where the U . S . Sailors disembarked and were detained for approximately 15 hours , the timeline says .
