Living green As part of its post industrial transformation , Malmo has become a leader in environmental design . The city ' s green achievements are reflected by the Bo01 housing development , a waterfront regeneration project with a focus on environmentally friendly living . For the latest in green design , visit the Western Harbor and Augustenborg neighborhoods . For a more informed look , contact the local Environmental Department , which occasionally offers free guided tours of the city ' s ecologically sustainable areas .
