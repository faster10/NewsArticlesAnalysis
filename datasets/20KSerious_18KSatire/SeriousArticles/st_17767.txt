" We ' ve trialed this technology in the Cayman Islands , in Brazil and in Panama through four different trials , and we have shown up to 99 percent control of the mosquito population , " said Derric Nimmo , Product Development Manager at Oxitec , in a company video . In April , Oxitec started releasing its so called " self limiting " mosquitoes in Piracicaba , a city in the Campinas region of Sao Paulo state , and reported an 82 percent reduction in wild larvae by the end of the calendar year .
