Irvin was 30 when he joined the Giants in 1949 , two years after Jackie Robinson broke the color barrier . Irvin spent seven of his eight big league seasons with the Giants and one year ( 1956 ) with the Chicago Cubs . A native of Haleburg , Ala . , Irvin played in the Negro , Mexican and Puerto Rican leagues during his 20s . He batted . 300 or more three times with a high of . 329 in 1953 . He finished with a career average of . 293 with 99 homers and 443 RBIs , numbers that would have surely been far higher if not for the game s racial segregation .
