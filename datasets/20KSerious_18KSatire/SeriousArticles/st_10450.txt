David Bowie , is one of the most influential musicians of the modern era . His passing also ended the music era that made Bowie a legend . He became famous with his innovative songs and by delving into the music genre of art rock , experimental , electronic , glam rock and art pop . He remained a pioneer in these music genres , and he also developed the international music industry by adding a new dimension to it . He performed by adapting varying personas , wearing costumes and adding life to his personalities by giving the audience and listeners what they had never seen or heard before . His dyed hair and exuberant personality added charisma to his songs and made him a music sensation .
