Another transfer window is about to come and go without Arsenal deciding to spend serious money . And while that may be a major disappointment to a large percentage of the club ' s fanbase not to mention media types like us Arsene Wenger appears utterly serene at he prospect of fighting on three fronts with the men who have ( by and large ) served him well this season . The first test of whether that was the right policy comes against Southampton , with Arsenal well aware that they cannot afford to drop more home points .
