The current threats to our security both at home and abroad emanate from groups of people who have torn up the long established rule book of armed conflict and are using extreme and shocking violence , broadcast worldwide , to achieve their objectives through fear and intimidation . To defeat groups such as Isil , al Shabaab , Boko Haram and al Qaeda while remaining within an accepted moral envelope ourselves , we have to be inventive , determined and highly focused . Drone attacks are an important part of this new way of warfare .
