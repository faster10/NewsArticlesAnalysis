How about weapons ? Europe s illegal small arms trade draws on weapons that weren t registered when tougher regulations were imposed in countries such as France . It also involves arms trafficked from the ex Yugoslav countries ( where Bosnians alone are estimated to hold 750 , 000 private guns left over from the war ) , and weapons that were neutralised for sale as souvenirs or props and are subsequently reactivated . Restoring Europe s internal borders might help a little with one part of this trade ? imports from the western Balkans ? but not by much , according to Nicholas Marsh , a firearms researcher at the Peace Research Institute Oslo .
