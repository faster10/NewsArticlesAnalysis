It was a tribute to Edmund s quality that the match finished with a sense of disappointment from the British fans . Had they been told before the start that he would go to a fifth set against Goffin , a man ranked 84 places above him , most would probably have been delighted . But their expectations changed during the first 70 minutes , because Edmund was virtually making scorch marks in the red clay . Brad Gilbert , the highly rated analyst and coach , wrote on Twitter that he was reminded of another redhead with a giant forehand : former French Open champion Jim Courier .
