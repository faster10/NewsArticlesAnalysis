Daesh has divided its territory into a series of governorates , many run by former Iraqi generals who report to the leader , Abu Bakr Al Baghdadi . They have ensured public services are delivered , and in areas such as education and justice they have imposed their own philosophies with great speed . A fascinating new book by Sami Moubayed called Under the Black Flag gives a detailed account of how Al Baghdadi set up his surprisingly well ordered administration , even if it follows dangerously extreme ideas .
