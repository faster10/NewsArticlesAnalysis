While al Qaida and its leadership have been significantly degraded since 2001 by US attrition , Isis is led by its very own Osama bin Laden figure , the self styled caliph , Abu Bakr al Baghdadi . Known as the invisible sheikh , Baghdadi , unlike Bin Laden , has apparently survived several attempts to kill him . And while there have been numerous al Qaida attempts to blow up civilian aircraft in midair , including a 2006 plot targeting aircraft flying from Britain to the US , none , apart from the 9 11 hijackings , has succeeded .
