The 38 year old Elway , who is now Manning s boss and mentor in Denver , enjoyed the rare triumphant finale . After finishing the 1998 regular season with career bests in quarterback rating and yards per attempt , he led the Broncos to a second straight NFL title in Super Bowl XXXIII , against the Atlanta Falcons . Elway completed 18 of 29 passes for 336 yards , including an 80 yard touchdown heave to Rod Smith , and added a four yard touchdown run . He officially retired the following spring .
