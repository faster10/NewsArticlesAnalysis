The moment the new net migration figures were published , the sounds of glee emanating from Ukip and the various Leave campaigns were audible . Immigration is a concern for many people in this country but Leave campaigners are taking people for a ride by saying there is a cost free solution in leaving the EU . Quite apart from the fact that most immigrants to this country come from outside Europe , their claims are misleading and simplistic . They ignore the security built into our current system , and they refuse to be clear on what the real alternative to EU membership is .
