Rubio , meanwhile , is by far the most conservative of the party approved foursome , making him the one best suited to survive the establishment stigma and still appeal to more conservative voters . He also has the best favorability ratings of the bunch , which ? along with his strong debate performances ? allows him to make the best general election pitch of anyone in the GOP field .
