The suspected mastermind behind Paris s devastating terror attacks is dead . That was the word on Thursday from French police , when they announced they had positively identified the body of Belgian Abdelhamid Abaaoud , 27 , and a key figure in the Islamic State of Iraq and Greater Syria ( ISIS ) , which claimed responsibility for the attacks that killed 129 people in Paris on Nov . 13 . Abaaoud had been the subject of a massive manhunt in both Belgium and France , for having allegedly coordinated the six synchronized attacks on the French capital .
