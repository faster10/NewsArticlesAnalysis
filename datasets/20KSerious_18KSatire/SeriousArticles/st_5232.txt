Even though divisions over what to do with Al Assad in a political transition are still a major hurdle in diplomacy to end the fighting , the Vienna meeting was a success in terms of bringing together the many countries involved in the multifaceted conflict . They included the United States and Saudi Arabia , as well as Al Assad s foremost backers , Iran and Russia . The diplomatic push has gained urgency because of the rising power in Syria of extremist groups such as Daesh and the Al Qaida affiliated Jabhat Al Nusra .
