Since Guardiola ' s departure , Barca have found the transition difficult to handle , mainly due to a collection of off field issues ? the majority their fault , others not so much . There was Tito Vilanova ' s initial cancer diagnosis and subsequent death at the age of just 45 ? a harrowing and traumatic experience for everyone closely aligned with the club . Tata Martino arrived for a season but as much as it was Atletico Madrid who walked away with the league title in 2014 , Barcelona were forced to watch Real claim ' La Decima ' ? their unprecedented and desperately longed for 10th European Cup success , and a triumph iconic goalkeeper Iker Casillas said was more important than the World Cup .
