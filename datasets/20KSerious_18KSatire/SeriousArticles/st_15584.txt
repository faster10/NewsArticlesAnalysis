Teresa Barger : On the equity markets , what happens in the China A share market should really not impact the world . It is really a matter of sentiment . On the currency front , most experts believe that the Chinese yuan is overvalued by somewhere between 11 per cent and 15 per cent . We did an analysis of 20 analysts in December and they thought that we were going to see 5 6 per cent yuan devaluation . Now , we have gotten there . So everybody is busy revising their forecasts . It could easily be a devaluation of 8 10 per cent it can even hit its fair value , which according to some experts is 15 per cent . That would affect particularly Korea , Singapore and Taiwan in the sense that that will be seen as a competitive devaluation .
