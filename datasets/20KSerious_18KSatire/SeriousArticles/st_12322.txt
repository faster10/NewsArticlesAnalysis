Paul said he had heard about " many tragic cases " of children who got vaccines and ended up with " profound mental disorders . " That assertion has no basis in medical research . Paul at first blamed the uproar on " inaccuracies " in the media . He later said he believes vaccines are safe and that his own children are immunized . That came after Paul suggested that the national Centers for Disease Control and Prevention made the transmission of Ebola sound similar to that of AIDS . Ebola , he said , is easier to contract . Health authorities worldwide have said that Ebola is only transmitted through direct contact with bodily fluids .
