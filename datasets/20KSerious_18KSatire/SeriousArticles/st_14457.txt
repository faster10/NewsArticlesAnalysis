The superintendent puts himself front and center during the uproar surrounding the May 2012 NATO summit . During violent clashes with protesters he was seen directing officers from the field , visible at the front lines in a white shirt and blue cap . Though known for being previously unpopular with many in the department , he earned high praise . Shortly before the NATO summit , McCarthy doubled the number of police officers assigned to bicycle patrol , and those bikes played a key role in keeping protests in check during the summit . The bikes also made appearances during Laquan McDonald protests .
