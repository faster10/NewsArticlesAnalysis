The latest Des Moines Register poll , which has one of the best forecasting records in Iowa , shows Trump leading Cruz , the firebrand Texas senator , by 28 per cent to 23 per cent , while Marco Rubio , the Cuban American senator from Florida , remains in third place with 15 per cent . Sanders , who trailed Clinton by several dozen points just a few months ago , has closed the gap with Clinton , who now holds a narrow 45 per cent to 43 per cent advantage . Sanders campaign said it had raised more than 20 million ( Dh73 . 44 million ) in January , mostly from small donations .
