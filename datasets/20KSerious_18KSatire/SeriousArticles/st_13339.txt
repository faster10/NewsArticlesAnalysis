The summit in Toluca may be the last hope we have of saving the monarch migration . President Pe a Nieto himself has been committed to the conservation of the monarch sanctuaries of Estado de Mexico since he served as governor there from 2005 to 2011 . He knows firsthand the significant efforts and sacrifices of Mexico ' s local indigenous communities , authorities and civil society organizations to protect the sanctuaries . He also knows the important contribution the butterflies bring to local social and economic well being .
