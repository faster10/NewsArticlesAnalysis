Sheriff Joe Arpaio s investigation of Obama s birth certificate was , according to the Arizona Democratic Party , nothing more than a distraction from the failures of his own agency . The problems included hundreds of sex crimes that the sheriff did not address within a three year period . Those within Arpaio s office claim that the suspicion of Obama s birth certificate was warranted , with the chief investigator Mike Zullo claiming that the birth certificate that was released by the White House in 2011 was completely fake .
