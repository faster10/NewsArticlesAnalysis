ESPN . com ' s Ian O ' Connor writes that even in beating his former team Thursday night at MetLife Stadium , Buffalo Bills coach Rex Ryan was still the biggest loser . Writes O ' Connor following the New York Jets ' loss : " Ryan was some victorious sight on the sideline , wildly swinging his fists , screaming profanities and hugging players to slap an exclamation point on his conquest of the franchise that canned him after four playoff victories in two years and then a whole lot of buttfumbling and bumbling after that . "
