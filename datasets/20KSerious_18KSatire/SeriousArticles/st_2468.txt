Hagel also expressed anxiety about the current state of the Republican party , which has been rattled by the rise of Trump ahead of establishment candidates in the polls . I m not sure what the Republican party is today . It s not the party that I started out in . I think it is an amalgamation of tribes . I think the Republican party is tribal today . It reminds me , in the area I grew up in Nebraska , of the Sioux nation . The Sioux nation was made up of many different tribes and that s where the Republican party is .
