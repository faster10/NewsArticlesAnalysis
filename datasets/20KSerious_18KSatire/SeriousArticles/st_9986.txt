Berlin U . S . President John F . Kennedy ' s " Ich bin ein Berliner " speech delivered 50 years ago was well received by more than 100 , 000 Germans hopeful for America ' s steadfast support as Berlin came to symbolize one of the Cold War ' s main battlegrounds . As presidential candidate in the summer of 2008 , Barack Obama had nearly twice that number of optimistic onlookers at Berlin ' s Victory Column who eagerly anticipated a new American outlook on questions ranging from climate change to nuclear disarmament .
