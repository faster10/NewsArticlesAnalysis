Meanwhile , Chief of Army Staff ( CoAS ) General Raheel Sharif on Tuesday in Tehran met Defence Minister of Iran Hossein Dehghan , a press release of Inter Services Public Relations ( ISPR ) here said . In the meeting with Iranian Defence Minister , matters of regional security came under discussion . CoAS reiterated that Pakistan takes Iran as very important neighbouring Muslim country and people of Pakistan have a great affinity with the Iranian brothers . He said that terrorism is a global threat which has the potential of destabilising the region which needs a well co ordinated response . Iranian defence minister thanked General Raheel Sharif and people of Pakistan for their efforts towards stabilising regional security .
