Ranieri , however , has never got carried away at any stage and he is clearly not going to drop his guard now . After playing a straight bat to a series of questions about Leicester s title prospects , the 64 year old was asked whether it was becoming more difficult to play down their chances of winning the league as the weeks go by and they continue to set the pace . I d like to say : Yes we can ! But I am not Obama , Ranieri said , smiling . Then it is difficult for me to say this . If I maintain this suspense it is much better for you .
