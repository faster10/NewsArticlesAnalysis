DARK MONEY IN FLORIDA SOLAR FIGHT : The ballot petition drive for a utility backed solar initiative received 340 , 000 in ' dark money ' from a 501 ( c ) ( 4 ) group called Let ' s Preserve the American Dream , chaired by Ryan D . Tyson . Tyson is vice president of political operations at Associated Industries of Florida . The group has now given 840 , 000 to the effort . As POLITICO Florida ' s Bruce Ritchie reports , the extra cash comes in as funding dried up for a competing environmentalist backed ballot initiative .
