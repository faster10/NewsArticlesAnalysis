The Shanghai Composite Index closed 2 . 3 percent higher as financial companies rallied on prospects for monetary stimulus . Speculation has intensified that the nation s central bank will add to the six interest rate cuts since November last year amid a recent raft of indicators signaling a deepening economic slowdown , including falling exports , declining producer prices and slowing industrial output . China Life Insurance Co . , China Vanke Co . and China Citic Bank Corp . all surged by the 10 percent daily limit . The ChiNext index of technology shares lost 1 . 6 percent .
