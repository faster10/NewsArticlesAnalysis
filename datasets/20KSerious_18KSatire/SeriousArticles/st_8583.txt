Trump ' s production was equal parts campaign rally , telethon and televised revue . Relishing the attention , he led an event billed as a fundraiser for military veterans more like a variety show host than a politician . He name checked his wife and daughter , who stood to take bows . He introduced Republican presidential rivals Mike Huckabee and Rick Santorum , who served as reluctant sidekicks . Instead of recapping his poll rankings , Trump touted six and seven ? figure donations from wealthy friends who he said helped raise more than 6 million before he left the stage . Trump said he " had a kick " debating . But he added that the Fox News Channel , long beloved by conservatives , was disrespectful for releasing a statement that mocked him .
