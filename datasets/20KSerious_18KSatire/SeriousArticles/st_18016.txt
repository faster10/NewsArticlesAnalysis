While many in Britain celebrated the 2013 vote as an example of British strength and independence ? no longer being America s poodle , they said ? rightly or wrongly , that was not how it was viewed elsewhere . In Washington , where successive American presidents from Ronald Reagan to George W Bush had always been able to rely on Britain when it came to support in military matters , the Syria vote was a direct hit to that previously ironclad assumption . As a result , Britain s failure to join the US in what at the time appeared to be imminent punitive strikes on the Assad regime caused significant bruising to the US UK relationship that continues to this day .
