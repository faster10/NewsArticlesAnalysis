FWD . US RAMPS UP 2016 CAMPAIGN PLANS ? The Mark Zuckerberg funded immigration lobbying group Fwd . us has been pretty quiet since its pricy efforts to convince Congress to pass reform legislation failed , but that s about to change , reports POLITICO s Anna Palmer . The group is launching a multi pronged campaign that could spend as much as 10 million over the next year on digital and TV ads , research and polling , which would include an expansion of its ground operation into 12 states , focusing on presidential battlegrounds and targeted House seats held by Republicans .
