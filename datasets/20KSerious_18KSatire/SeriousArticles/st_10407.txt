Christmas for NBA fans is the day Golden State comes to town , especially in Eastern Conference markets , where it s one and done . The game between the Warriors and Boston Celtics on Friday night was the toughest regular season ticket in Boston since 2008 , the night the team hung its first championship banner in 22 years . Fans among the raucous Milwaukee crowd wore prophetic 24 1 T shirts and celebrated the victory like it was Game 7 of the Finals . Management was just as giddy , releasing confetti from the rafters .
