The dancing , dabbing signal caller was as flamboyant as ever on Sunday as the NFC champions took a cross country flight for Super Bowl 50 , exhibiting the type of eccentric fashion he s become known for . He boarded the team s plane wearing Versace zebra print pants with gold on the sides and waist , along with black and gold shoes and a leather jacket . The Panthers landed in San Jose shortly after 7 p . m . Eastern time . The Charlotte Observer reported the team entered the San Jose Marriott through an underground entrance to avoid the crowd in front of the building .
