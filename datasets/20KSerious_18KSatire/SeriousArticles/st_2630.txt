Kate Winslet , who picked up a Golden Globe earlier this week , makes the shortlist for best supporting actress , for her role in Steve Jobs , the biopic of the Apple founder . Three out of the five best supporting actor nominees are British , with Christian Bale featuring for his role in The Big Short , and Tom Hardy appearing for his part in The Revenant . Mark Rylance , who plays a KGB agent in Bridge of Spies , also makes the list , which is completed by the American actors Mark Ruffalo and Sylvester Stallone .
