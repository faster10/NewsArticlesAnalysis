12 Taliban fighters reported dead in drone strike : A drone strike in a volatile southern province of Afghanistan bordering Pakistan killed 12 Taliban fighters , an official said . Three Afghans and nine foreigners died , said Ahmad Zai Abdulzai , a spokesman for the governor of Nangahar province . He declined to elaborate , but when Afghan officials refer to foreigners , they usually mean Pakistanis . The strike occurred in Khogyani district , where insurgents have been active for months . Abdulzai said the Taliban s shadow governor for the district was among the dead .
