Obama can get engaged in the race later this year , of course . But the second difference is not in his control . Reagan was seen as a success by the general public , not just by people who shared his ideology . Obama isn ' t seen that way . Much of Obama ' s State of the Union address went against the grain of public opinion . According to the speech , Obamacare is working well , the economy is booming even if it is also generating some anxiety , we are taking excellent care of our veterans , America is more respected than ever , and the administration has a strategy that is reducing the threat of terrorism . According to the public , none of this is true .
