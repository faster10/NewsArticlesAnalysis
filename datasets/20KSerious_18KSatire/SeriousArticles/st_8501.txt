In this Nov . 4 , 2014 photo , Anthony Martin and Xzavion Steadman check each others ties before visiting with their daughters in the receiving and discharging room at the Federal Detention Center Miami . The federal prison system hosted the first Daddy Daughter dance , a social for inmates and their daughters . Prison officials support the dance as a way to help inmates engage with their families and to prepare the inmates for the eventual release from prison . ( AP Photo The Miami Herald , Al Diaz )
