" Due to the government shutdown , I regret to report that the William H . Gross Stamp Gallery will remain closed until further notice , " said National Postal Museum director Allen Kane , relaying the information that the earth ' s richest , most powerful nation , and the envy of every other sovereign nation on the planet , cannot support a series of rooms displaying stamps . " Without the necessary personnel , we sadly will be forced to keep our exhibits shuttered indefinitely . "
