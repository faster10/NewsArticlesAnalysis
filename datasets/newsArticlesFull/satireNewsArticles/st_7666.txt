Many of the financial professionals who have supplicated themselves before the sculpture told reporters they believe its weeping foretells an era of doom in which tougher bank regulations will require institutions to maintain higher capital reserve ratios . Others , however , claimed the bull ' s tears were a portent of prosperity and good fortune , heralding a protracted period of high returns , ample arbitrage opportunities , or even the imminent overturning of the Dodd Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 .
