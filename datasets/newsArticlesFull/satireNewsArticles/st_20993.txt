Finland ' s Piet Kvistik , a domestique with the Cr dit Mondial team , was this year ' s highest finishing non doping rider ( 142nd overall ) . Kvistik claimed the maillot propre , the blue jersey worn by the highest placed " clean " rider , on the ninth stage of the race when the six riders who had previously worn it tested positive for EPO , elevated levels of testosterone , and blood packing .
