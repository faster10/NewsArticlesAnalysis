As their subspecies does every year , the Girls Gone Wild , roaming in packs , flocked to bars and clubs during the spring break migratory season . Lured by drink specials , promotional merchandise , and the chance to " go wild , " they were discovered at Se or Chug Chug ' s , a nightspot where the girls gathered to perform a mating ritual in which brief nudity is exchanged for Jell O shots and Smirnoff Ice trucker hats .
