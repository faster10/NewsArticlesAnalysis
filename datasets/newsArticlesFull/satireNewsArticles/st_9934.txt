Though there has been some argument over whether a modern airliner should be equipped with a fuse at all , Pe a is hesitant to legislate such radical change . " When you look at a modern jumbo jet that has been serviced , fueled and readied for takeoff , it weighs three quarters of a million pounds . Roughly two thirds of that weight is highly explosive aviation fuel , making the aircraft basically an enormous flying bomb . To build a bomb that large without a fuse would be bad design . "
