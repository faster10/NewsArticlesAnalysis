As the assemblage feasted on the luxurious entr e , all were transported with bliss by the lengthy discourse regarding the Hatcher family ' s vegetable garden . In a particularly spellbinding monologue , the evening ' s hostess wowed her audience with an anecdote of good fortune turned sour , detailing how her overly prolific tomato plants bore luscious fruit , but in such devastating quantities that she and her husband soon grew tired of tomatoes and tomato based dishes .
