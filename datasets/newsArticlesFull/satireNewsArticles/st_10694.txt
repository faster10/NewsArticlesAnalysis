As punishment for his untoward attitude , M was forcibly removed to the village station house , where he was thrashed severely about the buttocks with a willow switch . In addition , it was required of him to send handwritten notices of apology to all offended constabulary through the registered daily mail , and to ruminate in verse on what he had done to merit such a severe and uncompromising reprimand from the forces of law by writing , " I do not wish to go to gaol " one hundred times neatly .
