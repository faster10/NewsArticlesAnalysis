Leaving behind the woes of their bustling cities , small mining towns , large auto manufacturing towns , suburbs of auto manufacturing towns , and the economically stagnant state of Nebraska , the pioneers will set out across the vast Western expanse with nothing but the promise of opportunity and a few old resum s . Although it remains unclear what they might find in that savage territory , some sources indicate the move may bring a significant boost in self employment , prevent thousands of bankruptcies , and allow settlers an opportunity to learn agricultural techniques from the Mexican folk who sometimes wander over the border .
