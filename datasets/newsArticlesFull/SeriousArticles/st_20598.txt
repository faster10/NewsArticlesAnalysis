It ' s tough to say the NBA has gone away from promoting the big man a position in NBA folklore that has almost always been the most important part of roster building but they certainly don ' t show signs of keeping the NBA a big man ' s sport . The rule changes in the mid aughts coupled with removing the center position from All Star ballots have shown a lack of emphasis on big men and centers , paving the way for a perimeter oriented game that de emphasizes post play and makes the 3 pointer an obvious secret weapon .
