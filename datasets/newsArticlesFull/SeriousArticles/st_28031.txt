Though the alert level was raised on Saturday ? 21 people have so been arrested in Brussels in connection to the investigations of the Paris attack ? it was the start of the working week on Monday when the reality of life under lock down hit home . A text message from my seven month old son s nursery informed us that the government ordered them to close . Schools and universities also shut their doors , so even parents who could get to work without taking the Metro ? which has been closed since Saturday ? had to find someone to look after their children .
