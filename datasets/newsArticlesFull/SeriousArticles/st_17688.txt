" On the evening of January 17 , Netaji arrived at Dhanbad and took shelter with his relative in Jharia . Although , the main purpose of his arrival at Dhanbad is still a mystery among us , but as per our discussion with one of his close aides , RK Sharma , who died almost 20 year sago , Netaji had convened an important meeting with some renowned personalities in an undisclosed area before going for his historic escape on January 18 , " said 60 year old social activist , Manoj Prasad Chowdhury .
