These arguments cannot be waved away . So any move towards a rapprochement with Russia has to be gradual and conditional . The European Union has correctly decided not to ease sanctions on Russia , for now . But if the ceasefire in Ukraine is restored and holds , and Russia is constructive on Syria , then some alleviation of sanctions becomes much more likely in the first half of 2016 . The Russians will , in any case , know that sanctions can be reimposed and intensified if circumstances change .
