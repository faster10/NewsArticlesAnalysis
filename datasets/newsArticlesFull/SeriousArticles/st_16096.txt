1 . Conservatives won big . Matt Bevin lost badly to Mitch McConnell in the 2014 Senate Primary . Earlier this year , he beat the establishment machine when he won the nomination . Tuesday night , Mr Bevin became the second Republican elected governor of Kentucky in the last four decades . A businessman , Mr Bevin ran as a conservative Christian and a Tea Party Republican . According to the conventional wisdom , that should have been the kiss of death . Instead , he crushed his liberal opponent .
