Last month , WHO reported delivering more than 100 tonnes of medicines and supplies for 1 . 2 million people in Taiz governorate , where over 3 million people , almost 400 , 000 of them internally displaced , are in dire need of aid But it said distribution of an additional 22 tones of medical aid to five health facilities in Taiz City was on hold due to access issues . UN Special Envoy for Yemen Ismail Ould Cheikh Ahmed has been trying to broker peace talks between the various factions to end the fighting that has torn the country apart over the past year , but he adjourned them last month until mid January to allow for bi lateral in country and regional consultations to secure full adherence to a ceasefire .
