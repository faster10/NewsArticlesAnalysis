Fourth , there has been a demand by some to stop migration from war torn areas , particularly if it is true that one of the Paris attackers came to France posing as a refugee from Syria . The process of accepting refugees working with security officials on the best way to do this must continue . We cannot lose sight of the fact that most come in search of a better life and to escape the horrors of war . Alienating large groups of refugees , giving them the choice of hopelessness or the lure of ISIS and radicalization creates its own set of problems . It is also useful to point out that some of the Paris attackers were French nationals . The threat from ISIS and other terrorist organizations is global and exists within our own borders . It cannot be pinned on a particular group of people .
