Peterson had a tough game . The NFL s rushing leader with 1 , 485 yards this season , he ran for just 45 yards on 23 carries . In the second half , he had 14 yards on nine carries . But it was what happened after one of his two catches that hurt the Vikings the most : Kam Chancellor ripped out the ball , Seattle s Ahtyba Rubin recovered , and the Seahawks had the ball in Vikings territory just two plays after scoring their first points of the game . Steven Hauschka hit a 46 yard field goal to take the lead .
