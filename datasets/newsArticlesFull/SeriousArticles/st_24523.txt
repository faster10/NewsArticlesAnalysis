A mere coincidence , of course , but it s instructive to recall that Mr Dylan and Ian Fleming s screen idol are , fame wise , around the same age . The singer songwriter s debut album appeared in 1962 , as did the first outing of the 007 franchise , Dr No . But here the similarity ends . For whereas Dylan has chosen to confound his audience with career moves that might spell disaster for many of his peers , Bond s trajectory has been to meddle less , maintain the suggestion that the Cold War anti hero is still a largely useful force against mounting and morphing international evil , whilst keeping the franchise alive with the latest technology and tailoring .
