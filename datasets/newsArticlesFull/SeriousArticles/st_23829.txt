Suddenly , it seems , Hewitt is 34 . He won the first of his two majors against Pete Sampras in New York City , two days before September 11 , 2001 . And for us of his era , Hewitt has always been there in the world of tennis , skipping the baselines red faced and furious . During the late ' 90s , when we began to take ourselves seriously as sportsmen , Hewitt was already doing things that were beyond what we could imagine for ourselves . He was beating Andre Agassi , playing grand slams and , at 20 , taking the coveted mantle as the number one ranked player in the world . He was certainly an inspiration , which is sometimes forgotten after all this time .
