Senate Republicans urged to keep distance from Trump : The Senate Republican campaign arm earlier this fall warned senators up for re election next year to keep their distance from Trump , the New York Times reports . Ward Baker , the executive director of the National Republican Senatorial Committee , called Trump a misguided missile who could undermine GOP campaigns . Let s face facts . Trump says what s on his mind and that s a problem , Baker wrote . Our candidates will have to spend full time defending him or condemning him if that continues .
