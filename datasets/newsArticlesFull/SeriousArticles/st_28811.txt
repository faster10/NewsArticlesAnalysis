German businesses are confident about the outlook for Europe ' s biggest economy , despite China ' s slowdown and the Volkswagen scandal , a top survey showed Monday , as the Bundesbank also insisted economic growth was still on track . The leading economic think tank Ifo said in its closely watched business confidence survey that concerns about slowing growth in China , the Volkswagen pollution cheating scam and the massive influx of mostly Syrian refugees in Europe had dented optimism only slightly this month .
