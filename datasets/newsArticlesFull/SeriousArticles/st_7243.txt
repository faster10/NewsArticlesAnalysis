SPS CHAPTER ON LIST OF WHAT AG LIKES ABOUT TEXT : Agriculture groups are still poring over the more than 1 , 000 pages of TPP text released Thursday , but so far most see the agreement as a giant goody bag , including everything from new tools to tackle disputes over animal and plant product safety to clearer biotechnology policies and better market access for beef and pork , the POLITICO Pro Agriculture team reports . In particular , Western Growers President Tom Nassif mentions the sanitary and phytosanitary chapter as a positive development for the produce industry .
