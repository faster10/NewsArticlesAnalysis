Tunisia has been held up as a model for democratic progress since that uprising , with free elections and a modern constitution . The country managed largely to avoid the violence that marred the political upheaval in other countries . But for many Tunisians , the revolution has not delivered on its economic promises , with the young in particular complaining about a lack of jobs and high living costs . Three major Islamist militant attacks in Tunisia last year have also hit the economy hard , especially the tourism industry which is a key source of revenue and employment .
