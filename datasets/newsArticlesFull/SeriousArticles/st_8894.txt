The doubters were out again this week , questioning whether the Patriots could overcome all of their injuries and recover from a bad finish to the regular season when they lost four of their last six games . Those doubts looked foolish again , as they usually do when people question if the Patriots dominance is about to end . This is New England s 10th trip to the AFC title game in 15 years of Belichick Brady . The Patriots joined the 1973 77 Raiders as the only teams to make five consecutive trips to a championship game .
