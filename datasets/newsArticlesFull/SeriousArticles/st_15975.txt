The NSA and the Mumbai police have a history . In 2005 , the Mumbai police landed in Delhi and intercepted a car to apprehend gangster Vicky Malhotra , Rajan ' s right hand man . In the car along with Malhotra was Doval , at that time recently retired as DIB ( Director , Intelligence Bureau ) . Intelligence folklore has it that Doval was planning something big , but the Mumbai police spoiled it . Former home secretary RK Singh later told a TV channel that Doval had been planning a hit on Dawood Ibrahim along with Rajan ' s sharpshooters .
