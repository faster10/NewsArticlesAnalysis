Prof OSRU Bhanu Kumar , head of the department of meteorology and oceanography in Andhra University , Visakhapatnam , blames the unprecedented rainfall in at least four of the seven meteorological sub divisions in south India on the El Nino effect . " Global warming and climate change have begun to show their impact on rainfall and drought pattern down the Vindhyas , " Prof Bhanu Kumar said . The climate change is responsible for the oft repeat of the El Nino effect , which causes drought during the southwest monsoon and triggers rains during northeast monsoon .
