Political strategy : Here , many Republicans in Washington fear that Cruz s ruthlessness makes him a likely candidate for a so called ? October surprise . They worry that nominee Cruz would burn so many bridges , his campaign would fall to ashes amid a firestorm of his own making . In contrast , Trump has turned the media firestorm into an ally . To be sure , GOP establishment leaders would worry every day with Trump as the nominee . Yet they also believe Trump would energize the electorate , inspire the otherwise apathetic as well as the always angry , and moderate his policies to win independents . Here , Trump s chameleon like political positions encourage establishment confidence . Republican leaders are also increasingly confident that Trump could manipulate Hillary Clinton s seeming lethargy and humorlessness . They are especially excited at the prospect of excited Trump vs . uncomfortable Clinton presidential debates .
