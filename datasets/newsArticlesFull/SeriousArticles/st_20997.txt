Water drawn from the Flint River leached lead from old plumbing for months . Former DEQ Director Dan Wyant resigned last month . According to the World Health Organization , " Young children are particularly vulnerable to the toxic effects of lead and can suffer profound and permanent adverse health effects , particularly affecting the development of the brain and nervous system " . Snyder pledged Monday that officials would make contact with every household in Flint to check whether residents have bottled water and a filter and want to be tested for lead exposure while his embattled administration works on a long term solution to the city ' s water crisis . But many residents remain unsatisfied .
