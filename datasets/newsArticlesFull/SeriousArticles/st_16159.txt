People take part in a memorial religious service for plane crash victims at the St . Isaac s Cathedral in St . Petersburg , Russia , Sunday , Nov . 8 , 2015 . Mourners have packed into the landmark St . Isaac s Cathedral in St . Petersburg for a memorial service for victims of the Russian plane crash , and as a choir sang , the bell of the world s fourth largest cathedral was tolling once for each of the 224 victims . Most of the victims were from St . Petersburg or other areas of northwest Russia . ( AP Photo Elena Ignatyeva )
