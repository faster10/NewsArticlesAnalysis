In the open era , no tennis player , male or female , has featured in more finals of a grand slam tournament without winning than Murray has managed in Australia . Think Andy Roddick was luckless at Wimbledon ? Or that Roger Federer s sequence of defeats at Roland Garros by Rafael Nadal was a mite unfortunate ? Such frustrations are eclipsed by the Scot s four part catalogue of pain at the Rod Laver Arena , where he folded emotionally in the face of Federer s genius ? I can t play like Roger , but I can cry like him , he lamented that night in 2010 ? before a hat trick of losses to the unconquerable Novak Djokovic .
