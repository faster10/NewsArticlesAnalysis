China s CSI 300 Index plunged 7 . 2 percent on Thursday , triggering an automatic shutdown within 30 minutes of the open , as declines in the yuan rattled investor confidence in the world s second largest economy . The nation s market circuit breakers , which halt exchanges for 15 minutes after a 5 percent drop in the CSI 300 and for the rest of the day after a 7 percent retreat , have been criticized by analysts for exacerbating losses as investors scramble to exit positions before getting locked in .
