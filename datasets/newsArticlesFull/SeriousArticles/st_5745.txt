Certainly Miles , 62 , is hardly on hard times . Miles is rich and famous , his children are in college or approaching college , and he has enjoyed decades within the game s top rungs , from a player at Michigan to coaching there and then being head coach at Oklahoma State and LSU . Nevertheless , for a coach who had a 13 win , a 12 win and three 11 win seasons during his 11 years in Baton Rouge , it seemed an inglorious finish . Former UCLA coach Rick Neuheisel said this week he found it despicable that LSU has allowed such uncertainty to hover over Miles .
