When ' The Hurt Locker ' won an Oscar for Best Picture in 2009 , its revenue was only Rs 2 crore in India , while ' Avatar ' broke all records the same year , earning Rs 110 crore . ' Avatar ' did win three Oscars , but those were for visual effects and cinematography , precisely the reason why the film worked here . In 2008 again , ' Slumdog Millionaire ' had Box Office takings of Rs 20 crore , while worldwide , it earned 377 million . In 2007 , the Oscar for Best Picture went to ' No Country For Old Men . ' It earned Rs 28 crore in India , while the highest Hollywood grosser that year in India was ' Spider Man 3 , ' at Rs 68 crore .
