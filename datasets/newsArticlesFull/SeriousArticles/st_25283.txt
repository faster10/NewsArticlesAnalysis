What would Einar Wegener s life have been like if he hadn t unleashed the woman inside him ? The hypothetical question hovers tantalizingly over director Tom Hopper s narrative as we see Einar and his effervescent wife in a happy marriage . They have sunshine , joy , sex and a faithful pet dog running through the spacious rooms of their comfortable home . ( A word about the production designer here : Copenhagen and Paris in the 1920s seem so vibrant and alive , you wish you lived through the breezy era ) .
