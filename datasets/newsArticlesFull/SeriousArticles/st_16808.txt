The significance of Gr ning ' s trial can be measured in one final way . Many have observed that it coincides with the 70th anniversary of the end of World War II . But it also coincides with an anniversary of a different sort . April 24 marks the day that millions mourn the killing of hundreds of thousands of Armenians by the Ottomans during World War I , an event many have described as genocide . And yet the Turkish nation itself remains in denial of the mass killings that unfolded a hundred years ago .
