On immigration , Trump has exposed another gap between the party establishment and voters . While Republican politicians including arch conservative presidential candidate Ted Cruz want to increase legal immigration , 67 percent of Republicans want to decrease immigration flows and 63 percent view immigrants as a burden , according to surveys by the Pew Research Institute . Trump caught fire over the summer with incendiary rhetoric about illegal immigration from Mexico and by being the first to call for deporting an estimated 11 million people now living in the United States illegally .
