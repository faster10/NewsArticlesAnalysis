There has been an outpouring of tributes from fans since Bowie ' s death on Sunday which came as a shock to many as the singer had just released his music album ' Blackstar ' on his birthday , just two days before his death . However , it seems unlikely that there would be a funeral as his body was quietly cremated shortly after he died . The newspaper said that Bowie may have had a direct cremation ' which is " the disposition of human remains by cremation without a formal viewing , visitation , or ceremony . "
