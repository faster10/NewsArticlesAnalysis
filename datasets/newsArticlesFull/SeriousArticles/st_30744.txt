Bicycle trip organizers will drive you up and drop you off on an asphalt road at about 14 , 000 feet above sea level , and you ride or really glide down on bicycles . Make sure your body has acclimated to the altitude of Ollantaytambo , which is about 9 , 186 feet , before you embark on this tour . The ride offers great views of the snow capped Mount Veronica , and you ' ll see livestock grazing and patches of land being tended by farmers . The entire trip takes from three to four hours and costs about 40 per person .
