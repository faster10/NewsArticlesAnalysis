Though there are dueling interpretations of Trump s threat to launch a third party bid , there appears to be little effort to game out what to do if he follows through . A Republican strategist with a rival campaign suggested that some states could invoke sore loser laws that prevent failed primary candidates from appearing on the ballot as a third party bid , but there are questions about whether it s feasible to restrict presidential candidates that way . And the strategist also said campaigns are not interested in that hypothetical right now .
