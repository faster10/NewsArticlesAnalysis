Some Congress leaders said on the issue of framing strategy on the GST bill , " there has been complete convergence " of views of the top leaders , including the Gandhis , Manmohan Singh and P Chidambaram . The party had set up an internal committee to frame the party ' s stand on the bill . Along with the party ' s three MPs in the select committee , it consisted of senior party functionaries and floor leaders who were constantly in touch with Singh and Chidambaram , a top Congress functionary said .
