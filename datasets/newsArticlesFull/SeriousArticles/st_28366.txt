Marketers blame the inherent nature of local consumers for the low response to air purifiers , which remove contaminants from the air in a room . Indians look for instant gratification and visible result while purchasing any goods , especially health oriented or which improve life , they say . Air purifier sales are growing in India , especially after the recent WHO warning . But it is on a small base and the buyers are mostly expats and institutions , such as embassies , offices , government departments and luxury hotels .
