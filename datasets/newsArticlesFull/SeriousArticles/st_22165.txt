The post Snowden relationship between Silicon Valley and Washington is vastly different from the relationship that emerged after 9 11 . Snowden disclosed that the NSA was skimming content from popular messaging applications like Yahoo and Skype , using obscure legal authorities . After Snowden ' s leaks , many tech companies began to better protect their customers from the prying eyes of the U . S . government . On Friday both Apple and Google have said they will make encryption standard on their smartphones . As Google CEO Eric Schmidt told Bloomberg five months after Snowden ' s leaks in November 2013 : " The solution to government surveillance is to encrypt everything . "
