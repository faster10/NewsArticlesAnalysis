" Chi Raq " opens with a shooting at a club . A charismatic rapper and gang leader , also named " Chi Raq " ( Nick Cannon ) , escapes injury this time and is getting busy with his girlfriend Lysistrata ( Teyonah Parris ) when a retaliatory attack sends them into the street and catches a child in the crossfire . Lysistrata sees the grieving mother ( Jennifer Hudson ) the next morning and is moved to action . She gets some guidance from a community leader ( Angela Bassett ) and enlists support from the women of a rival gang led by Cyclops ( Wesley Snipes ) . The women throw down their demand : No sex until you stop shooting each other . ( They deliver it much more colorfully . )
