Disney said the seventh film in the Star Wars saga had grossed 758 . 2 million through Tuesday , when it took in 8 million , and surpassed on Wednesday the 760 . 5 million earned by James Cameron s Oscar winning sci fi epic Avatar . This afternoon , with early box office results in , Star Wars : The Force Awakens became the highest grossing film of all time in the domestic market , Disney said in a statement , without providing figures . The humbled and grateful company thanked its fans for helping it reach the milestone , saying that without them there is no Star Wars .
