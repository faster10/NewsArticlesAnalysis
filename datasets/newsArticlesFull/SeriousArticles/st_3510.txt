Joaquin Guzman , the world ' s most wanted drug trafficker , center , is escorted by Mexican security forces at a Navy hangar in Mexico City , Mexico , on Friday , Jan . 8 , 2016 . Guzman , known as El Chapo , was recaptured by Mexican authorities on Friday , six months after he escaped from a maximum security prison , slipping into an elaborate tunnel under his shower and humiliating the government that had promised tougher public order .
