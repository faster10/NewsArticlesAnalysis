Financially , the international streaming business is a loss maker , but global growth is crucial for Netflix s business , which is already reaching saturation in the United States . According to Netflix financials in 2015 , US subscriber numbers slowed considerably , although the total number ticked upwards , and it faces stiff competition at home not just from streaming services by Sony and HBO , but also from the likes of Amazon Studios , whose original series Transparent won five Emmy Awards last year . The e retailer already has 12 series , two of which have been nominated for the 2016 Golden Globes , and is slated to release more than a dozen others this year .
