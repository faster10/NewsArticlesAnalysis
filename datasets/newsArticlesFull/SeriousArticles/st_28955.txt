About the Vikings : Coach Mike Zimmer has done a terrific job in his second season . Zimmer was a longtime defensive assistant for the Cowboys and part of the staff that helped win the 1995 Super Bowl . He hasn ' t been back since . . . Adrian Peterson led the league with 1 , 485 yards . The previous two times he won the rushing title ( 2008 , 2012 ) , the Vikings were eliminated in the wild card round . The loss in 2008 was to the Eagles . . . Sharrif Floyd ( George Washington High ) had a sack and tied a personal season high with seven tackles in last week ' s upset win at Green Bay which gave the Vikings the NFC North title . . . Linval Joseph , one of the top nose tackles in football , is expected back after missing four of the last five games with a foot injury .
