ZUCKERBERG NOT WORRIED ABOUT AI ? FT s Richard Waters , Hannah Kuchler and Murad Ahmed : Technology leaders such as Elon Musk of Tesla Motors and venture capitalist Peter Thiel have warned that rogue AI , or artificial intelligence , could one day threaten the human race . But Mark Zuckerberg has set himself the challenge this year of building his own personal AI assistant , rejecting fears of asuper intelligence running amok as far fetched . In a post on his company s website late on Sunday , the Facebook founder said he had decided to build a smart machine to control functions in his house and help him work , akin to a disembodied butler .
