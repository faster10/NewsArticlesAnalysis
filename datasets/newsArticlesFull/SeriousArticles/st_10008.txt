According to CBS News , Sheriff Joe Arpaio was in Iowa to attend a Trump rally in Marshalltown , although whether or not this visit was specifically tied to the endorsement is unknown . In December , 2015 , in Mesa , Arizona , Arpaio was with Donald Trump as he prepared to give a speech to thousands of his supporters . Pablo Manriquez , hispanic media director at the Democratic National Committee , says that Trump is simply creating more tension and division among Americans by accepting Arpaio s endorsement , specifically in a city with one of the highest Latino populations in Iowa .
