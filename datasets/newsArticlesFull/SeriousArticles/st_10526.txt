In 1968 , Britain ' s Queen Elizabeth II visited Brazil where she watched a match at the Maracan stadium and met Pel . Then British Ambassador John Russell noted its importance as " football is the soul of Brazil . " But is it ? Perhaps , but that soul shares football with other passions , including desire for more democracy and less corruption . It could be said that protests have added to pressure on FIFA leading to the current questioning of the Qatari bid for the 2022 World Cup , and investigation .
