HHS TO TACKLE RISING DRUG PRICES AT NOVEMBER FORUM ? An invite only meeting at HHS headquarters Nov . 20th will discuss ways to address rising spending on drugs . Also on the agenda : value based and outcomes based purchasing strategies for drugs , as well as the current and future drug classes that contribute to rising health care costs . Pharmaceutical industry representatives , patient advocates , health care providers , employers and payers are listed as invited groups , but HHS has not yet divulged specific names .
