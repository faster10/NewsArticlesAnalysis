Despite his views on immigration , Trump often claims he is adored by Hispanic voters , but a new survey of eligible voters in that coveted demographic strongly challenges that narrative . Eighty percent of Hispanics in the Public Religion Research Institute ' s 2015 American Values Survey have an unfavorable view of the Republican presidential candidate , including 59 percent who view Trump " very unfavorably .
