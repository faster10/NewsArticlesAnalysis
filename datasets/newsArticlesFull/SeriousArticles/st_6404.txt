The chief minister said that PIA was a national institution and added that their sit in was disturbing the smooth flow of traffic and causing hardships to the travellers and passengers to reach Jinnah Terminal . This is not good for our country , its reputation and its economy , he said . The chief minister said that he was shocked over the firing incident that claimed two precious lives and injured some of their workers . I am awarding Rs 2 million compensation for each dead and Rs 200 , 000 for each injured , he announced . The committee thanked the chief minister s personal support and said that the two injured were in critical condition and were taken to a local hospital , upon which the chief minister ordered to shift them to Aga Khan University Hospital for better treatment and said that the expenses of their treatment would be borne by the Sindh government .
