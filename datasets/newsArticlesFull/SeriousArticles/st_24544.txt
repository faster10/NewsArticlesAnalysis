The foreign ministers of major regional players like Iran , Saudi Arabia , and Turkey will be there , along with those of the UAE , Lebanon , Egypt , Iraq , Germany , France , and the UK , Russia and the United States . Although neither members of the Syrian Government or Syrian Opposition have been invited to Vienna , the League of Arab States is expected to attend the Saturday talks and so will the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation ( OIC ) ? two institutions where the Syrian Republic remains an official member .
