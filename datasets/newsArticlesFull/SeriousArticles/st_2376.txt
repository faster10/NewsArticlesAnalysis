Terry Collins took the blame for sending out Matt Harvey for the ninth inning , although the Dark Knight acknowledged heavy lobbying swayed his manager , who preferred to go to Jeurys Familia to begin the frame . In the end , Harvey and Familia combined to surrender two runs in the ninth and the Mets went on to lose to the Royals , 7 2 in 12 innings , in the elimination game . The tying run scored in the ninth when Lucas Duda threw wide to the plate with Eric Hosmer seemingly dead to rights as the third out . It took some guts to do what he did , and I didn ' t make the throw , Duda said . So , no excuses . Familia suffered a World Series record three blown saves .
