This time around , the lead negotiator for India would be joint secretary Ravi Shankar Prasad from the environment ministry who has by now led the talks for the country for successive years . He was one of the first one to reach Paris to take part in the meetings with other countries and partners before the formal negotiations begin . The ministry s secretary Ashok Lavasa and additional secretary Susheel Kumar would also be in Paris and so would be another joint secretary , Sanjiv Kumar who would be incharge of the events beyond the negotiations that India is organising at the venue in Paris .
