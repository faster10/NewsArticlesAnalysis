After the Jets forced overtime and kicked a field goal to take a three point lead , Beckham tried to keep the Giants in the game . He caught a nine yard toss to open the Giants overtime drive . Two plays later , Manning lofted a rainbow pass but Beckham could not haul in an over the shoulder catch . In frustration , Beckham kicked the football back toward the line of scrimmage and officials flagged the receiver for a delay of game , which turned what would have been a fourth and 1 at the Jets 49 into fourth and 6 at the Giants 46 .
