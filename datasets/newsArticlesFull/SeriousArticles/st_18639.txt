Turkish paramilitary police officers stand near a dead body of a migrant on the beach near the Aegean town of Ayvacik , Canakkale , Turkey , Saturday , Jan . 30 , 2016 . A boat smuggling migrants to Greece slammed into rocks off the Turkish coast on Saturday and capsized , killing at least 33 people , including five children , officials and news reports said . Some 75 other migrants were rescued . A Turkish government official said he expects the death toll from the incident to rise as rescue workers try to reach other migrants believed trapped inside the wreckage of the boat which sank shortly after departing from the Aegean resort of Ayvacik .
