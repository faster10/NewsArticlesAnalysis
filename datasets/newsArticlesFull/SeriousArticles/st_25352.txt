KOLKATA : Family members of Subhas Chandra Bose and Netaji activists are divided on the expectations from the classified files that Prime Minister Narendra Modi will release on Saturday . While the Bose family believes the files will conclusively prove the air crash theory was concocted , there are doubts if they will contain anything that throws new light on the mystery surrounding his disappearance seven decades ago . But there is near unanimity that it expose the Congress party ' s repeated attempts to suppress facts .
