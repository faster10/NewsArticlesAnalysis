When things like this happen here it ' s so easy for the mind to unravel but Spieth kept his composure . At the par five second that peerless putting stroke rapped home another birdie putt and one more at the sixth . At the treacherous 9th he seemed to have left himself a difficult second shot , from a downhill lie to an elevated green . Spieth knocked his approach to 6ft and holed that one as well to turn in 35 shots . In its own way , this was the equal of his spectacular bouts of scoring in the first two rounds .
