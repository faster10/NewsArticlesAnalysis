The creation of jobs is clearly an urgent priority for the EU and therefore a commitment to reduce the costs that legislation imposes on business seems at first sight laudable . However , a closer look at what this would actually mean in practice raises a plethora of real concerns . A single clear commitment to slash regulation across the EU would fatally undermine and unravel the safety net protecting the health and environment of all European citizens . Furthermore , a narrow focus on the regulatory costs to business fails to take into consideration the benefits to society as a whole deriving from regulation , which often greatly outweigh the costs even in purely monetary terms .
