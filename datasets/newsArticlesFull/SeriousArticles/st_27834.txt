The RBI will start issuing sovereign gold bonds on November 26 , with a tenure of eight years and an interest rate of 2 . 75 per cent . The bonds will be open for public subscription from November 5 to 20 . The tenure of the bond will be for a period of eight years with exit option from the fifth year . The bonds will be restricted for sale to resident Indian entities only including individuals , trusts , universities , and charitable institutions . The interest earned on gold bonds would be taxable , and capital gains tax shall be levied as in case of physical gold .
