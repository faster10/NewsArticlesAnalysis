Stapleton has been a respected songwriter in Nashville for about a decade , and he s helped pen beloved songs like Luke Bryan s Drink a Beer and Josh Turner s Your Man . Music Row insiders have known his name , but few outside the industry had Stapleton on their radar . However , in a single evening , Stapleton went from relative obscurity to the single buzziest artist in America , and he s become the latest act to disprove Gary Overton s statement , If you re not on country radio , you don t exist .
