The turning point came last year , after what initially looked like a blow : the Lawn Tennis Association s decision to dramatically reduce her central funding . Konta moved her training base to Gij n in the Asturias , where her Spanish coach Esteban Carril agreed to take a pay cut in turn . Still , despite all this austerity , she gradually found her love of tennis returning . Her results picked up from the start of the Eastbourne tournament last year , where she beat Makarova for the first time . Since then , she has won 31 of the last 39 matches ? the sort of form that could take her close to the top 10 if she continues at the same pace .
