According to the Daily Mail , Sean Penn actually met with El Chapo back in October of last year while he was on the run in Mexico . For those who missed it , El Chapo escaped from Federal Social Readaptation Center No . 1 , a maximum security prison , on July 11 , 2015 , and had been on the run until his recent capture on January 8 , 2016 . But during that time , Mexican investigators claim Sean Penn met with him . It has been reported that the meeting was orchestrated by Sean Penn , Mexican actress Kate del Castillo , and a fixer representing the Sinaloa cartel .
