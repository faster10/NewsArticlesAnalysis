The national best seller The China Study rocked the nutrition world in 2004 , when its author , biochemist T . Colin Campbell , Ph . D . , declared , The closer we get to a plant based diet , the healthier we are going to be . Now , WHO is piling on with its blanket condemnation of processed meats and heavy suspicion about red meat . However , other research shows that this fearmongering is unfounded . Eating unprocessed meats as part of a well balanced diet actually has many lasting health benefits .
