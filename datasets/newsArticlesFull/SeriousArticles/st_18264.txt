Spotlight , the award winning drama about the Boston Globe s investigation into the Catholic Church s pedophile priest scandal , received six nominations , including best picture , director and original screenplay . Ridley Scott ( Blade Runner , Alien ) was considered a frontrunner for best director ( The Martian ) but was snubbed yet again . Also snubbed were Steven Spielberg and Tom Hanks , although Bridge of Spies was nominated for best picture , supporting actor ( Mark Rylance ) and original screenplay .
