Save your complaints about the overcrowded slate of country music awards shows each year . We all know there are too many of them . Fortunately for us , the Country Music Association awards are consistently the most prestigious and most well produced affair of them all , and last night s telecast of the 49th annual ceremony was no exception . The CMA awards were filled with great music , a few big laughs , divorce intrigue , shock wins , a surprise album drop and a jaw dropping performance that people will be talking about for years to come . Let s jump right in .
