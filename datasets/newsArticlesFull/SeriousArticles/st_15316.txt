In the past week , Clinton has shifted course in apparent response to Sanders ' strong poll results . Most troubling to Clinton supporters : it has disappeared more quickly than the lead she held over Barack Obama when the two of them squared off in 2008 . Her campaign has attempted to make the vote a major issue in the race , along with Sanders ' proposal for a single payer health care system , which Clinton has attacked as unrealistic . Most of Sanders ' campaign funds have come from small donations .
