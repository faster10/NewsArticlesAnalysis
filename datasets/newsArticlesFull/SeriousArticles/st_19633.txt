Fifth , the crisis in Syria is not all about ISIS . It is also a fight against Assad . He has been brutal with attacks against his own people , including using chemical weapons and the bombing of innocents . Accepting the status quo Assad stays indefinitely without a plan to leave does not work . It will further alienate Sunnis in the region and elsewhere who are fighting ISIS and Assad . There must be a plan for removing him from power . In addition , while there needs to be a more intense focus on ISIS , the battle against al Qaeda and its nodes , including al Nusra , has to be stepped up .
