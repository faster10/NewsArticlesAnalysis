The two leaders boarded the Metro from the Race Course station at 3 : 16 p . m . and got off at Arjangarh . Indian officials and the French delegation , including French Foreign Minister Laurent Fabius , accompanied the two leaders . In fact , going beyond mere symbolism , the leaders again opted for the Metro on their way back from Gurgaon . A special train was arranged by the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation ( DMRC ) for the high profile ride which was point to point ? it did not stop at any other station .
