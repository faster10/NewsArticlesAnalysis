Red meat is far from the enemy , and it is not something we need to avoid , says NYC based nutritionist and registered dietitian Tanya Zuckerbrot , author of The F Factor Diet . In fact , it is a nutritional powerhouse loaded with protein , iron , vitamins A , B12 , D and C , and creatine ? an important amino acid . It also has essential minerals like zinc and phosphorus . Adding this powerful superfood into your diet will amp up your energy levels , strengthen your immune system , speed up your metabolism , keep your skin radiant , increase your brain health and stabilize your mood .
