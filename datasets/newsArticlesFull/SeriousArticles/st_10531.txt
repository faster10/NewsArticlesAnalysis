However , the possibility is always there that faced with present Israeli intransigence , combined with the old frustrations of failed negotiations , lack of international pressure and an armed struggle by some that has achieved nothing , Abbas will dismantle the Palestinian Authority , Palestine s government . This would effectively put the responsibility for the governance of the West Bank ( which is where most of Palestine exists , ever smaller , as Israeli settlements intrude ) on Israel s shoulders . Last year , Abbas said that Palestinians should give up the notion of a two state solution and demand instead civil rights from Israel , as Nelson Mandela did in South Africa .
