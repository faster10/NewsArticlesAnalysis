NOTED : CHRISTIE ANNOUNCES CLOSING NEW JERSEY PRISON TO BECOME DRUG TREATMENT FACILITY . It may not have been a campaign speech , but the overtures of Chris Christie s 6th State of the State address Tuesday were easily transferable to the New Jersey governor s presidential bid . The biggest announcement of Christie s address came on the same issue that has been instrumental in reviving his presidential campaign , especially in New Hampshire : the drug epidemic .
