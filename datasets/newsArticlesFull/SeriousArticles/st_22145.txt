TV shows Mr Robot and Mozart In The Jungle won two Globes each Best TV Series in the separate categories of Drama and Comedy Musical . Mr Robot ' s Christian Slater won Best Supporting TV Actor while Mozart star Gael Garcia Bernal won Best TV Actor ( Comedy Musical ) . Jon Hamm won his last award as Mad Men ' s Don Draper , scoring Best TV Actor ( Drama ) . Taraji P Henson handed out cookies to everyone as she walked up to collect her Best TV Actress ( Drama ) Globe for her work as Cookie Lyon in Empire .
