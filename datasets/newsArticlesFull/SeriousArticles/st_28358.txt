GOOGLE TOUTS NEW COUNTER ISIS AD EFFORT ? The search giant recently launched a pilot program that aims to serve up alternative content when people search for violent , extremist material , the company acknowledged Tuesday . New details of the program , which appears to be limited to the U . K . , surfaced yesterday during a hearing before Parliament , where a top Google executive had been testifying . An initial report from The Telegraph made it sound like Google actually planned to swap search results for groups like ISIS with counter extremist messaging , but a spokeswoman clarified later that the effort focuses on advertising . " We offer Google AdWords Grants to NGOs so that meaningful counter speech ads can be surfaced in response to search queries like ' join ISIS , the spokeswoman said in a statement .
