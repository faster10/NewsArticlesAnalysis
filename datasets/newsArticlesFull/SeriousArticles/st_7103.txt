Some of the most loyal Democrats , who only yesterday saw blue skies and happy times ahead , now see clouds on the horizon a lot bigger than Donald Trump s hand . The president of the Service Employees International Union says her blue collar union will be going into hyperdrive to save the world , or at least her corner of it , from the Donald . She concedes that 64 percent of her members identify as conservatives and are much more interested in the Republican debate than the Democratic debate .
