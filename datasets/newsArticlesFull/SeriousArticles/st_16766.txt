Inasmuch as both Rooney Mara and Alicia Vikander arguably had lead roles in Carol and The Danish Girl respectively , their inclusion in the supporting actress category ? along with Jennifer Jason Leigh ( The Hateful Eight ) , Rachel McAdams ( Spotlight ) and Kate Winslet ( Steve Jobs ) ? might be considered Oscar politics in action . But 2015 was a comeback year for Leigh , who also voiced a character in the stop motion animated film Anomalisa . And it s the first Oscar nomination for McAdams , who was at her best in the Spotlight ensemble .
