However , a section of officers is refusing to make any comments on Bihar poll results even though closely monitoring the poll related developments on TV . " But I am worried how CM Nitish Kumar will handle the family of RJD chief Lalu Prasad to maintain his image of ' sushasan ' babu . It would not be easy for Nitish babu to run the government freely as he had done for nearly a decade , " said a senior IAS officer on the condition of anonymity .
