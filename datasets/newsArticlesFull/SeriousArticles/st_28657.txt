And when he was ordered the next day to spend the foreseeable future focusing exclusively on his recovery from injuries to his shoulder , ribs and foot , fourth year quarterback Brock Osweiler became the Broncos No . 1 quarterback . How long Osweiler will stay in that role remains to be seen . Broncos coach Gary Kubiak won t commit to a plan beyond this week . But at the very least , folks in Denver can get a glimpse of what Osweiler has to offer . The 6 foot 8 inch quarterback was the Broncos second round pick in 2012 , scooped up by general manager John Elway eight weeks after the team won the Manning lottery .
