India ' s escalating energy requirement mirrors the rise of its economy and is expected to double over the next 25 years . Among New Delhi ' s initiatives to promote oil diplomacy in Africa is the Techno Economic Approach for Africa India Movement ( Team 9 ) , initiated in 2004 . India pledged 500 million in the form of concessional credit facilities to eight energy and resource rich West African countries to help private Indian firms carry out development projects . Nearly 280 million worth of projects have already been approved against concessional lines of credit .
