That s the puzzler , of course , that has plagued both cautiously optimistic and deeply skeptical Peanuts followers alike ? from the hopeful , Charlie Brown like fans to the vocally rejection ready Lucy Van Pelts of the world . Even creator Charles M . Schulz s widow , Jean , told me when the film was announced that she was wary of the risk . The family rightly remains so highly protective of Sparky Schulz s creation , which , 15 years after his death , continues to stand as arguably the greatest American comic strip ever .
