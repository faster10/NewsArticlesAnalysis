MINORITY RETORT : Quartz reports HILLARY CLINTON s campaign is counting on strong minority support to buoy its efforts if it falls short in Iowa and or New Hampshire . HILLARY CLINTON s campaign has a fall back argument : If she fares poorly in Iowa , where she maintains a small lead in public opinion polls , and New Hampshire , where Senator BERNIE SANDERS leads , it s because those states aren t very diverse .
