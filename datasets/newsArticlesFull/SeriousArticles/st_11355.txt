In his Sermon on the Mount , Jesus teaches , " Do not resist an evildoer . But if anyone strikes you on the right cheek , turn the other also . " ( Matt 5 : 39 ) Jesus ' teaching seems overly na ? ve to most of us . Yet Jesus knew more about the nature of evil than most . Jesus knew that while evil may seek to destroy us , evil ' s most effective strategy the one few of us anticipate is to tempt us to unwittingly destroy ourselves . We construct the guillotine , we place our heads in it , and we pull the rope .
