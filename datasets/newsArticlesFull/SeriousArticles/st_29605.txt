Dubai , United Arab Emirates During London Mayor Boris Johnson ' s recent visit to Dubai on an investment promotion trip , he jokingly declared that he is " mayor of the eighth emirate . " Though uttered in typical self deprecating jest , the mayor of the world ' s greatest city proclaiming that London is a mere province of the United Arab Emirates is revealing about how Dubai ' s fortunes have revived since the punishing real estate crash and debt restructuring following the financial crisis .
