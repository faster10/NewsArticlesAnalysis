Republicans divided on Obamacare : The Hill writes Senate Republicans are divided over how far to go with an Obamacare repeal bill they plan to send to the president s desk by the end of the year . Senate GOP leaders have told their members they will repeal as much of the health care law as possible . But some Republicans are balking at a proposal to repeal the expansion of Medicaid . As the Hill writes , repealing the Medicaid expansion is a dicey proposition for endangered Senate incumbents in Illinois , Ohio , New Hampshire and Pennsylvania . All those states broadened the program .
