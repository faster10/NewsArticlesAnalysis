Valentin Ribet , 26 , a lawyer with the Paris office of the international law firm Hogan Lovells . He was among scores of people killed at a rock show in the Bataclan concert hall . Ribet received a master of laws degree from the London School of Economics in 2014 , and earlier did postgraduate work at the Sorbonne university in Paris . His law firm said he worked on the litigation team , specialising in white collar crime . He was a talented lawyer , extremely well liked , and a wonderful personality in the office , the firm said .
