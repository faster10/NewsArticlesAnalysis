1 . Rex Ryan throws his headset off in celebration : Ryan tried to keep the spotlight off himself in the days leading up to his return to MetLife Stadium to play the New York Jets on Nov . 12 . The former Jets coach deflected questions about his time in New York at his news conferences leading up to the game , and he took the unusual step of remaining in the locker room during pregame warm ups in an attempt to not attract attention . Whether or not that worked , Ryan came out on top : His Bills defeated the Jets 22 17 and Ryan threw his headset off in celebration when safety Bacarri Rambo snagged the game winning interception .
