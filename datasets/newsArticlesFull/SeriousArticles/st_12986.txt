Languishing in the polls , Bush has tried to cast himself as a reassuring figure at a moment of high anxiety . On Saturday , the pro Bush super PAC Right to Rise debuted an ad featuring images from Paris and San Bernardino , California . It is time for a tested and proven leader who won t try to contain ISIS , the ad declares . Bush s campaign has started airing an ad in which four Medal of Honor recipients give him their endorsement . Jeb Bush is ready to take the role of commander in chief , one of them declares . The ad also makes clear who is allegedly not ready . This is not a reality show , it declares ? an obvious reference to Trump , star of the longtime NBC reality hit The Apprentice .
