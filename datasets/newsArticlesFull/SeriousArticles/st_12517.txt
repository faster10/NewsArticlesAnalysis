These groups activities correspond to growing local anti Muslim sentiment , over and above the backlash created by 9 11 . In my book I use big data analysis to track the number of news stories about mosque controversies in local newspapers , Bail tells me . He found that the combination of violent attacks on mosques and political attempts to stop their construction increased almost 800 percent between 2005 and 2012 . With the surging power of this anti Muslim narrative in the media , combined with everything ISIS says and does , it s entirely understandable how there can be a sea change in American opinion towards Muslims , he says .
