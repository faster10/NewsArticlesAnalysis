The India Today team of Group Visual Director Rohit Chawla and Senior Editor Gayatri Jayaraman spent 48 hours with Ai Weiwei , who is creating a memorial in Lesbos for the refugees in order to highlight their plight and spread the message of peace , brotherhood and trust . Ai Weiwei believes that artists don ' t have to be more political , they just have to be more human . It ' s a line of thinking that India Today , and the annual India Today Art Awards , subscribes to . We believe that art is not just for art ' s sake , but for culture ' s sake , for society ' s sake , and for politics ' sake .
