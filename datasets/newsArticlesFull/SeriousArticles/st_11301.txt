Adam McKay is known for his comedies . Anchorman , Talladega Nights , The Other Guys , and many more . The Big Short , starring Christian Bale , Steve Carell , Ryan Gosling and Brad Pitt , is McKay ' s foray into drama as a writer and director . One has to go for drama if they are to explain Wall Street jargon like ' Synthetic Collateralised Debt Obligations ' . The Big Short has been nominated for its screenplay and direction at many award shows including the Oscars . So far , McKay has lost to Aaron Sorkin at the Golden Globes . The BAFTA and Oscars are still up for grabs .
