McIlroy might be the bookies ' favourite for good reason but his Augusta record offers up a litany of damning statistics he must rectify before he can be considered for a green jacket . Can you believe that as good a driver as the Northern Irishman is , he has hit the par five 13th fairway just four times in 22 attempts in competition ? Or that he has had no birdies in 44 tries on the holes that start the front and back nines . This week we will truly get the chance to gauge his development as a golfer .
