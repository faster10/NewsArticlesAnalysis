Manning got to his third Super Bowl in his second year with the Broncos under Fox . The one time Giants defensive coordinator missed the last two months of the regular season after undergoing an aortic valve replacement but returned for the playoffs . Fox joined Dan Reeves as the only head coaches to win both an AFC and NFC title . Super Bowl XLVII at the Meadowlands might as well have ended on the first play as center Manny Ramirez snapped the ball while Manning was shifting . The ball was recovered by the Broncos in the end zone for a safety and they never recovered .
