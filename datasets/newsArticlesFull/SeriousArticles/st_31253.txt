We are monitoring the ongoing tension between two rival Middle East powers . And we are reminded of how difficult it is to predict anything in this region these days . Things have been very tense between Saudi Arabia and Iran after the Saudi government executed a Shiite cleric . Both of these nations are big oil producers . And in the past , a simmering conflict like this would have led to a spike in oil prices . Instead , though , oil prices have fallen in recent days , actually sling to an 11 year low .
