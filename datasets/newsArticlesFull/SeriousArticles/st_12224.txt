The China ' s central bank has devalued Yuan for the eighth day in a row . This has sent message to investors that China ' s government is using continuous devaluation as a last resort to boost flagging economy as it has run out of avenues to arrest its slowing growth . The steep fall in the Chinese market is reflection of the economy . Typically , it is a common perception that stock market is reflection of the health of an economy . But over the past one and half years China ' s equity markets seem to be totally de linked from the underlying economic trends such corporate earnings ' and GDP growth . Leveraged investors realised that efforts from the Chinese governments have not paid off and there has been no visible improvement in earnings ' and GDP growth . Due to this , they began selling .
