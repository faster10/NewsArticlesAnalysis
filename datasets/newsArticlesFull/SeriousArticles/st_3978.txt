The fifth Heart of Asia Istanbul Process Summit jointly inaugurated by Prime Minister ( PM ) Nawaz Sharif and Afghan President Ashraf Ghani on December 9 , 2015 in Islamabad counts as a major foreign policy success . Given the present situation in the Middle East and the connected problems in adjoining regions this timely initiative to bring focus firmly on Afghanistan was badly needed . Efforts to revive the stalled Afghan peace talks between the Afghan government and Taliban group must be encouraged .
