THE WEEK THAT WAS : It was arguably one of the most eventful and consequential weeks of the year for the defense world , which started with a surprise budget deal that will provide the Pentagon with two years of stable budgeting ? not to mention a higher topline that is likely to prevent any major cutbacks for this fiscal year . The election of a new speaker could be another sign of more stability on Capitol Hill , a request made repeatedly by Pentagon leaders . And the award of the Long Range Strike Bomber contract was one of the biggest in years ? with an upset win of Northrop Grumman over defense giants Boeing and Lockheed Martin .
