It is indeed welcome that Pakistan ' s civilian government , its army and the ISI should in one voice be condemning the Pathankot terror attack and promising to wipe out the menace of terrorism from the region . Welcome as it is , though , it will be meaningful only if it is backed up by actual action not just against terror groups acting against the Pakistani state but those focusing their attentions on India . If that does happen , it could signal a genuinely new phase in Indo Pak relations . If not , Friday ' s official announcement by the Pakistani government will amount to nothing more than an empty gesture .
