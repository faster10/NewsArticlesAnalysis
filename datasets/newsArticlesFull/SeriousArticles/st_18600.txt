Oil prices seek new bottom Brent and US crude futures fell to their lowest since 2003 in Asian trade on Monday as an announcement by Iran to increase its crude exports by 500 , 000 barrels a day put fresh pressure on the commodity . Western powers on Sunday lifted international sanctions on Tehran , removing an obstacle to exports . London Brent crude for March delivery was down 1 . 14 at 27 . 80 a barrel , after touching an intraday low of 27 . 70 earlier , the lowest since November 25 , 2003 .
