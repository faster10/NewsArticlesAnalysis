FIRST LOOK : AMALGAMATED TO LAUNCH ENVIRO DIVISION ? Per release going out today : Amalgamated Bank announced that starting today Ivan Frishberg will be joining their Washington , D . C . office as First Vice President , Sustainability Banking . In this newly created role , Mr Frishberg will be responsible for growing and serving the burgeoning organizations and clients that work and serve in the fields of environmental , green banking and sustainability Mr Frishberg comes to the bank with more than 25 years of public interest advocacy , organizing and policy experience and is a founding partner of FSC Partners , a climate consultancy
