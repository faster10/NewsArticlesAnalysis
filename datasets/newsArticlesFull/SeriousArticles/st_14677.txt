Who exactly fills the vacuum when Isis are displaced ? The case is being made that so called moderate groups will fill the vacuum . But as many are observing , their main battle is with Assad , not Isis . And moderate can be misleading : Theo Padnos , an American held hostage by the al Nusra Front , found to his cost that there was a porous relationship between the Free Syrian Army and the jihadis . We need far more detail about who these supposed moderates are , and whether this label is even applicable .
