em ABOUT THE AUTHOR : Nick Gregory has been a social studies and journalism teacher at Fenton High School since 2000 and he has been a National Writing Project Teacher consultant and a junior varsity basketball coach since 2003 . Gregory is a Michigan Education Voice Fellow and he has exhibited photography related to Detroit and social justice causes since 2011 . Gregory , who has a Masters degree in Educational Leadership , believes that building positive relationships helps students find their passion for learning .
