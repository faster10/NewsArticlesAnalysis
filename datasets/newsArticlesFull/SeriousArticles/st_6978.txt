The Force Awakens is , in many ways , a feminist reinterpretation of the original Star Wars movie that wowed audiences nearly 40 years ago . Both are fairy tales laced with complex politics and really cool space battles . The central characters embark on a quest , grappling ? along the way with themes of destiny , redemption , identity and free will . In the original Star Wars , the action and self inquiry were driven mostly by men . In The Force Awakens , it s the women who grow the most . That s a big deal . Here s why .
