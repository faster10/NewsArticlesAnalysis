In a season of lows , the sight and sound of Manchester United fans jeering Louis van Gaal as he trudged towards the tunnel following the 1 0 defeat to Southampton was probably the nadir . United fans are not known for turning on their own , and even in the David Moyes era they remained generally supportive . The FA Cup victory over Derby eased some of the pressure but the relief will only be temporary if Stoke complete the double over Van Gaal ' s side on Tuesday . Given United ' s struggles at home this season including their infamous barren run in first halves few would bet against it .
