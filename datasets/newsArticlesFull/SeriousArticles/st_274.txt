Volquez started Game 1 of the World Series for the Royals , but the audience at home watched him pitch knowing some devastating news about him that he did not . A report that Volquez s father had died in the Dominican Republic circulated online and through social media . The Royals , acting on behalf of Volquez s wife , said he was not aware of the death because she wanted him to be able to focus on the game . He left for the Dominican and the funeral immediately after , promising to return to the team for his next start , in Game 5 . He met the team in New York shortly before Game 4 , and when he took the field Sunday to begin warming up , he said he felt a presence trailing behind him .
