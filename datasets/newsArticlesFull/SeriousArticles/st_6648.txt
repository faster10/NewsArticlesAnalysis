Moral responsibility : The inclination to want retribution or prevent future attacks is natural , and many people , including presidential candidates , said quickly after the attacks on Paris that the United States needed to step up its fight against ISIS in Syria . Even before last week , when the United States was already launching airstrikes , Americans and their leaders were grappling to determine the proper force needed to deter or halt ISIS atrocities and the spread of its caliphate in the Middle East , let alone its threat to the West .
