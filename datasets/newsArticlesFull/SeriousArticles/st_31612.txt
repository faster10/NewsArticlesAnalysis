When Curry was introduced in the starting lineup , he received loud cheers from most of the 20 , 160 people at the Air Canada Centre . It was the third highest attendance for a regular season game in the history of the arena . Drake sat in his courtside seat for the only time this season other than the night named after him , and the Raptors ' global ambassador had the privilege of witnessing Curry be Curry again . The MVP scored 44 points on 14 for 24 shooting , including 9 for 15 from 3 point range , in a 112 109 Warriors win . It was their 21st victory in a row to start the season , a record unmatched in the history of all four major sports .
