Four arrested in connection with Pakistani college attack : At least four people who allegedly helped militants launch an attack on Bacha Khan University in northwestern Pakistan on Wednesday have been arrested , military sources said Saturday . Four militants stormed the school , killing 21 people before they were shot dead by security forces . Four others who acted as facilitators of the terrorists have been arrested , Lt . Gen . Asim Bajwa said in Peshawar , the capital of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province .
