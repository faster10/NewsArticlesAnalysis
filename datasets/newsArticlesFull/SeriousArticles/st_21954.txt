It all starts with a family fight between Avery and his cousin Sandy Morris and snowballs from there to a 32 year jail sentence for sexual assault , attempted murder and false imprisonment . Despite the lack of any physical evidence against Avery and the strong suggestion by members of the police force that District Attorney Denis Vogel and Sheriff Tom Kocourek arrest the real culprit ? Gregory Allen ? Avery is put away in 1985 without the possibility of parole unless he pleads guilty . He refuses .
