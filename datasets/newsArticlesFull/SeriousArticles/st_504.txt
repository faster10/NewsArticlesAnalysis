An explosion rocked Istanbul ' s historic Sultanahmet district Tuesday morning , killing at least 10 people and injuring 15 , the Istanbul governor ' s office said . Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan blamed the attack on a suicide bomber linked to Syria , and said that both Turks and foreigners are among the dead . The Sultanahmet district is popular with tourists , and includes the Blue Mosque and Topkapi Palace . Turkey has been hit with several suicide attacks in recent months that prosecutors blame on the Islamic State , but Istanbul has also faced sporadic attacks from a far left group , linked to the resumption of hostilities between Turkish forces and PKK Kurdish militants .
