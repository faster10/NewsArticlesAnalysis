The BJP advertisement , which appeared in Kishanganj , Saharsa , Purnia , Katihar and Araria districts having sizeable minority population , questions Nitish ' s silence over repeated insult to cow by his alliance partners . The BJP also asked the CM to stop vote bank politics and answer if he agrees with the statements of his allies on beef issue . The advertisement quoted alleged statements of Lalu in which he said ' Hindus too eat beef ' and RJD vice president Raghuvansh Prasad Singh ' s statement that " it is written in the Vedas and the Puranas that even Hindu sages used to eat beef in ancient times " .
