The backdrop was only art of a very fine semifinal . This was end to end , thrilling fare , partly because Pellegrini , so often criticized for his tactical conservatism pulled a major surprise by beginning with a midfield diamond . Yaya Toure operated at its base between Fabian Delph and Fernandinho , with David Silva in the hole behind Sergio Aguero , only to be supported by Raheem Sterling in a less familiar central role . The experiment ended at halftime when Jesus Navas replaced Delph as the shape reverted back to a more familiar 4 2 3 1 .
