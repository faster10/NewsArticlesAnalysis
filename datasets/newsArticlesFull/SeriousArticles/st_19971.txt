" This could offend everyone in the world , " Gervais says . " Or they might like it . But it ' s the not knowing that ' s exciting . I think if you make it fun for yourself , then that filters through , without being ridiculously self indulgent . ? Sometimes when I write a joke , I get an adrenalin rush .
