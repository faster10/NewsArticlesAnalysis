The Spanish approach provided the template for subsequent EU migration policy : A three pronged strategy of criminalising migrants , militarising border controls and externalising controls by paying non EU states , from Libya to Turkey , huge amounts of money to act as Europe s immigration police . None of this has prevented migrants from trying to enter Europe . They have merely been forced to find different , and often more hazardous , routes . This is one of the reasons why so many of them now travel through Greece and the Balkans .
