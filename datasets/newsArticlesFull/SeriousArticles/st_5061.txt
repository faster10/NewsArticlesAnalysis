After four listless outings in Southeastern Conference play , Missouri s offense figured out how to move the ball in the first half . Scoring touchdowns proved to be more difficult . The Tigers piled up 234 yards of offense in the first half but only had two field goals to show for it , taking a 6 3 lead into halftime . Mizzou had only five possessions in the half with two punts and a turnover to go along with Andrew Baggett s pair of field goals , a 23 and 34 yarder . The Tigers 14 first half first downs were more than they had in entire games this season against Florida , Georgia and Vanderbilt .
