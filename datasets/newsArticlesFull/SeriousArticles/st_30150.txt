They have been here before of course . In the semi final in Glasgow the Murrays steeled themselves for four hours to see off Sam Groth and Lleyton Hewitt . That was their real introduction to the fervour of Davis Cup doubles tennis . The brothers were fused by fire . A late Belgian switch to strengthen their doubles pairing was an attempt to snatch a lead heading into Sunday . They failed , and not only because Andy is the world s second best player . The star of the show drew part of his inspiration from helping his brother through a sticky time . This was not business , or patriotism or even just sport . This was family .
