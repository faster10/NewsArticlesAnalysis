The five time NFL MVP was sidelined for six games because of a plantar fascia tear near his left heel and only reclaimed his starting job from Brock Osweiler , an impending unrestricted free agent , after a relief appearance in the regular season finale against the San Diego Chargers . Manning threw more interceptions ( 17 ) than touchdown passes ( nine ) for the first time since his rookie year in 1998 . The 39 year old had a career low 67 . 9 passing rating . Manning ' s 59 . 8 completion percentage was his first under 65 percent since 2001 and the second lowest of his career .
