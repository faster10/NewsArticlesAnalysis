What are Tomlin ' s plans for maximizing the potential of this group ? Tomlin is one of the league s most accomplished coaches . He has never had a losing season and is the proud owner of two Super Bowl trips , including one championship . And the Steelers beat some good teams this year , including Denver , Cincinnati and Arizona . But two losses to a weak Ravens team loom large , especially the Week 16 defeat , which nearly cost Pittsburgh a playoff berth . The Steelers must figure out how to overcome the bad losses . The 2014 Steelers lost to four sub . 500 teams . If they had taken care of business against inferior opponents , the Steelers would have been playing at home in the divisional round and well rested after a bye week .
