Leela HazzahLeela Hazzah has dedicated her life to lion conservation . In 2007 , she started Lion Guardians , a nonprofit that works with African Maasai warriors to protect lions . The group now employs more than 70 Lion Guardians throughout East Africa and has helped the lion population grow . " I know we ' re making a difference , " Hazzah said .
