Polls : Speaking of polls , none of the ones published immediately before the caucuses , including the Des Moines Register Bloomberg Politics Iowa Poll , nailed the final result of the caucuses . This doesn t mean the polls were less accurate . What it does indicate is there was a lot of voter movement and changes after the polling was finished , including more people deciding to participate . The Register poll and others showed the potential for large shares of caucus goers to change their candidate preference and many did .
