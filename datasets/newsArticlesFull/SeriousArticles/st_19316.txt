There have been other memorable moments through the years . The Patriots were playing the Dolphins at the Orange Bowl on Monday Night Football on December 8 , 1980 , when Howard Cosell interrupted the broadcast to tell the world that John Lennon had been shot dead outside his apartment in New York City . A year later , Foxborough fans rioted during a Monday loss to the Cowboys and the town fathers petitioned the NFL to ban night football in Foxborough . The Patriots did not play another home game on Monday Night Football until 1995 , when new owner Bob Kraft petitioned the town to rescind the order .
