Even before the formal announcement of election dates , the daggers were drawn and pitched verbal battles ensued in July . Election was announced in September . Attacking the Mahagathbandhan , the PM even called the alliance ' Mahaswarthbandhan ' and a formation that would bring jungle raj back in Bihar after 10 years of peace . The war of words was not limited to election rallies but also spilled over on the social media and in the last two months , it became a normal phenomenon for people to follow the barbs of leaders on their opponents . Mahagathbandhan leaders often dubbed the PM as ' jumlebaz ' and claimed that BJP was party of people making hollow claims .
