There s been a lot going on with the Islamic State . How do we think about this attack given its other operations ? In the last few weeks ISIS has suffered losses on the ground , losing the important Iraqi city of Sinjar to Kurdish forces . Yet it has also been linked to recent mass casualty suicide attacks in Beirut that killed more than 40 people and probably downed a Russian airliner over Sinai , killing all 224 people aboard . The Lebanon attack fits its traditional model : The attackers struck in Shia populated areas of Beirut , which is home to the headquarters of the Lebanese Hezbollah , ISIS s nemesis . But even so the massive nature of the attack is an escalation .
