Barely 24 hours after squads of terrorists murdered or wounded nearly 500 people in a series of coordinated attacks across Paris , Hillary Clinton , Bernie Sanders and Martin O ' Malley could have requested that the awkwardly timed debate be postponed . Instead , they went ahead with the 9 pm debate , surely hoping to highlight their advantages : Clinton with her foreign policy experience , Sanders with his progressive appeal on domestic finance , O ' Malley with his youthful energy and relatively successful run as governor of Maryland .
