As December arrives , the Chicago Bears seem to be straddling that fence . With a gutty 17 13 win at Lambeau Field on Thanksgiving night , the Bears announced their candidacy for an NFC wild card berth and improved to 5 6 , now just one win behind the two time defending conference champion Seattle Seahawks . At the same time , the Bears also remain in a three way tie for eighth in the NFC , grouped amongst a cast of mediocre wildcard hopefuls that also includes the 5 6 Tampa Bay Buccaneers and New York Giants with the Atlanta Falcons and Seahawks a game ahead .
