Double nominations for actors and actresses : Breakout star Alicia Vikander should be popping the champagne right now . She earned a nod for best actress in a drama for her outstanding performance as a supportive wife in " The Danish Girl , " AND for playing a robot facing an existential crisis of sorts in " Ex Machina . " Ditto for Idris Elba and Mark Rylance . Elba scored a double for best actor in a limited TV series ( " Luther " ) and for supporting actor in a movie ( " Beasts of No Nation " ) . Rylance scored nominations for his elegant spy turn in " Bridge of Spies " and his rich work in BBC TV ' s miniseries " Wolf Hall . "
