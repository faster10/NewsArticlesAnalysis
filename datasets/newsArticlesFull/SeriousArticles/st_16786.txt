How do the Steelers regain their championship touch ? That s a second consecutive season of double digit wins for the Steelers , who added a playoff win in Cincinnati . Normally , that would be encouraging . Not here . The Steelers are all about championships . As Mike Tomlin likes to say , The standard is the standard . The standard does not include staying home in late January . The Steelers have championship pieces on offense , with a potential Hall of Fame trio at quarterback , running back and wide receiver . The offensive line is above average . The defense was uneven a times but made major strides this year . The next step is rediscovering the edge that made Pittsburgh what it was for so many years .
