Finally , the voters who came out in large numbers to cast their ballots this Sunday are seeking political reform as well as economic advancement . They are seeking opportunities to better their lives . At the same time , they want equity in the distribution of wealth , an end to the culture of corruption as well as of privilege cornered by a minuscule segment of society . Equally importantly , the people want an end to the wars that have depleted the resources in their country . And for all of this to materialise , Myanmar will have to nurture its fledgling democracy and safeguard it against any attempt at subverting the process of political reform . ?
