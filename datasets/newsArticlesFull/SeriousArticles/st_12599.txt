The West has caused massive disruption in the region by removing the secularist despots who once held sway , thereby allowing the religious fanatics to fill the vacuum it must not repeat such folly . Mr Cameron conceded the point . Unlike Tony Blair 12 years ago urging the Commons to back a war , there was no sense he was hiding something or putting the best possible gloss on his case . He acknowledged that the invasion of Iraq and its chaotic aftermath had poisoned the well but hoped it would not influence decisions today , though inevitably it does .
