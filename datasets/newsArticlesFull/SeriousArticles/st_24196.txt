That ubiquity is , thus , a double edged sword . After David Bowie s death , every single bar or restaurant I went to the following day played his music on repeat . I couldn t help but be deeply touched . What made that parallel gesture so beautiful is that it was accidental , a way of subtly connecting those of us who grew up idolizing him while paying tribute to the immense body of work he left behind . But at its worst , the outpouring of remembrance can verge on the exploitative when it s part of a growth strategy , like tragedy porn you can t click away from .
