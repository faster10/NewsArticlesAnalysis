When Pope Francis visits Kenya , Uganda , and the Central African Republic from Nov . 25 ? 30 , he will be visiting three countries with some of the highest HIV infection rates in the world , and three countries with large populations of Catholics , for whom condom use is not allowed . He could save lives here by reversing the Catholic Church s rule . Some observers thought that Francis would at least relax the ban on condoms . He has not . The only church approved method of contraception is abstinence .
