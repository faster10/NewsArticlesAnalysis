The timing was important too . Five years before , almost to the day , Barcelona humiliated Real Madrid and their then boss Jose Mourinho . It wasn ' t just the five goals that separated the sides that day . On the field , the players preached the Gospel According to Pep ? the pointed , careful , precise , patient style that revolutionized the way the game was played . It left their great rivals their anti thesis , complete with a glittering and expensively assembled galaxy of imports unable to process much . Afterwards , a broken Mourinho spoke of feeling ' impotent ' while he admitted the result could prove psychologically damaging . He was right , of course . From then on , Mourinho refused to bring a knife to a gun fight . He brought grenades .
