The difficulty is finding them in the original format . An inveterate meddler in his own films , Lucas released a series of special editions of the original trilogy in the late nineties , while refusing to re release the films in their original format . So now , the 1977 classic has incongruous CGI inserts , including the infamous decision to make Han Solo shoot first in his confrontation with the alien bounty hunter Greedo . Your best bet is to find someone who still has copies of the films on VHS , before all the meddling . And a VHS player .
