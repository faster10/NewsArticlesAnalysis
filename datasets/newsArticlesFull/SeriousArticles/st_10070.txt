The chancellor owes much to the extra wiggle room delivered by the Office for Budget Responsibility , which handily raised its forecasts of tax revenue . Having in effect used that money partly to pay for the cut in tax credits and a retreat from expected reductions in spending on police , Mr Osborne went hunting for more . An extra 3 per cent stamp duty for buy to let properties is welcome . The 0 . 5 per cent payroll levy to fund apprenticeships , when set alongside the rise in the living wage , will unsettle business unless it sharply raises the quality of the labour force .
