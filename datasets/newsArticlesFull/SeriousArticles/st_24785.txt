Many other serious concerns must be allayed before parliament , and the country , can be expected to support operations in Syria and here the shadow of Iraq looms large . How long would a military campaign last and what are the criteria for success ? Would expanded operations truly be restricted to the air or would British special forces be asked to join their US counterparts in the hostage rescue and ground raids recently authorised by President Obama ? How resistant , in fact , would any such campaign be to mission creep , especially if bombing does not bring quick results ?
