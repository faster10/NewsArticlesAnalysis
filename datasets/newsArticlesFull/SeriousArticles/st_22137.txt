A message from PhRMA : Have you ever been to the pharmacy and been told they can t give you the medicine you were prescribed ? This can happen for a few reasons . You may not realize health care decisions are being made on your behalf ? by your insurance company . Even though insurers are not involved in your direct care or privy to why one treatment was prescribed over another , many try to limit the use of certain prescriptions . Sometimes , this can mean unnecessary barriers between you and the medicine you need . Learn more about the ABCs of health care coverage and the questions to ask to make sure you get the care you need . Learn more at AccessBetterCoverage . org http : onphr . ma 1kRSeHa .
