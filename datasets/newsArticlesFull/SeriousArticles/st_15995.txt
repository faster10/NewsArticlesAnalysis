Czech government backs tougher gun laws : The government of the Czech Republic has approved a plan to tighten the country s gun laws , a move prompted by a rare mass shooting this year . Under the plan , Prime Minister Bohuslav Sobotka said , the mandatory license for gun holders will be effective for only five years instead of the current 10 and police will get increased powers to seize guns and ammunition . The plan awaits parliamentary approval . On Feb . 24 , a gunman in a small town killed eight people and seriously wounded one before fatally shooting himself .
