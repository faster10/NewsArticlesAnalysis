Even after Friday ' s meeting with the PM and two ministers at 7 Race Course Road , party sources said both Sonia and Manmohan spoke separately to senior party leaders , briefing them about what transpired and the stand conveyed to the government side . The Gandhis , Manmohan , Chidambaram and some senior leaders have together prepared the party ' s stand and they will continue to collectively respond to the government ' s proposals , AICC communication wing head Randeep Singh Surjewala said . All of them , including Rahul , favour an effective , implementable and inclusive GST , he said .
