Budding young authors are being asked to submit a first chapter of a historical novel of no more than 1 , 500 words , and a brief outline of the rest of the proposed novel of up to 500 words , for a chance of winning the Penguin Chalke Valley History Prize . It must be on one of the following themes : The Ancients Secret and Spies The Home Adventurers and Inventors or King and Country . There are two age groups : 11 to 14 and 15 to 18 . Entrants will be judged on historical research , writing skill and originality .
