SENATE TO EXPAND RECONCILIATION BILL ? Senate Republicans are looking to use budget reconciliation to repeal even more of Obamacare than the House did in its own version of the bill last month . Republican leaders are figuring out how much more of the law could get through the Senate s stringent reconciliation rules after three of the chamber s conservatives said that the House passed plan didn t go far enough . We re consulting with the parliamentarian to make it as broad as we can consistent with Senate rules , Sen . John Cornyn , the Senate s No . 2 Republican , told POLITICO . The Pro story .
