We found him exactly where we had started looking , just miles from La Tuna , his birthplace and where his mother still lives . It took time , required establishing trust at different levels of the cartel . On occasion it was dangerous , but it was not difficult . When we got there , we found he had groups of armed men around him , but they were not armies . Nor were they neurotically careful about their security . At no point were our phones , essentially travelling GPS signals , taken off us . At one point we were even allowed to use their internet system .
