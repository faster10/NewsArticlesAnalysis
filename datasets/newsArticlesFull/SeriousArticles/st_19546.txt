Though Zika virus does not cause mortality , there are concerns following suspected possible associations found between the virus and neurological problems like microcephaly , a condition of abnormally small head in babies . In May 2015 , Brazil reported its first case of the infection . Since then , the virus has spread within Brazil and to 24 other countries in the region . WHO has also called for a meeting of its International Health Regulations Emergency Committee on Zika virus on February 1 .
