Then , in September Vladimir Putin upped t he ante : Russia , he announced , was determined to continue supporting Assad as the legitimate ruler of Syria . He was Russia ' s long time ally ( in fact , Russia ' s only real ally in the Arab world ) . And , unlike Obama , Putin actually backed up his rhetoric with action major shipments of arms , artillery and tanks , as well as the deployment of 50 Russian jets with pilots and ground crews and the troops to protect them . It was soon clear they were targeting not mainly ISIS positions , but the strongholds of any rebel groups most threatening Assad .
