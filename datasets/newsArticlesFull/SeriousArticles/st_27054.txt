According to a press release , a high level meeting chaired by Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif was held at Prime Minister ' s House yesterday to review the regional situation . The meeting was attended by Chief of Army Staff General Raheel Sharif , Minister for Finance Mr Muhammad Ishaq Dar , Minister for Interior Chaudhdry Nisar Ali Khan , Advisor to the Prime Minister on Foreign Affairs Sartaj Aziz , Special Assistant to the Prime Minister on Foreign Affairs Tariq Fatemi , National Security Advisor Lieutenant General Nasser Khan Janjua ( Retd ) , DG ISI Lieutenant General Rizwan Akhtar , Foreign Secretary Aizaz Ahmad Chaudhry and DG Military Operations Major General Sahir Shamshad Mirza .
