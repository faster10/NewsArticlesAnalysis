As part of its report on bad ads in 2015 , Google says it blocked more than 12 . 5 million pharmaceutical ads that violated its healthcare and medicine policy and suspended upwards of 30 , 000 websites making misleading claims about weight loss . Rejected ads include those that attempted to sell blatantly counterfeit merchandise like fake purses or watches as well as ads for pharmaceuticals that either weren ' t approved for people to actually use ( not good ) or made blatantly false claims about their efficiency compared to prescription backed alternatives .
