Many voters see Mrs Clinton as the well qualified candidate of the past and see 74 year old Mr Sanders , the oldest candidate running this year , as idealistic and focused on the future . Among the issues that mattered most to voters , Mrs Clinton carried 77 of those who said winning in November was most important to them . Mr Sanders won 74 of those who said they wanted a candidate who cared about people like them and 83 of those who rated honesty and trustworthiness as their most important issue .
