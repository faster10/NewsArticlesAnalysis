Quarterback Brock Osweiler completed 20 of 27 passes for 250 yards , two touchdowns and no interceptions yesterday during the Broncos 17 15 victory against the Bears while Manning remained in Denver to recover from a reported case of plantar fasciitis . Osweiler s performance could determine how long he remains the starter , as the promotion could extend beyond this week , according to NFL Network and FOX Sports . While coach Gary Kubiak was noncomittal after the game , the Broncos reportedly will meet with Manning today to determine the length of his absence .
