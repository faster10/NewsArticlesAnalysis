The U . S . states are not the same . There are microeconomies inside of the U . S . macroeconomy . After all , a wage hike in New York is not the same as a wage hike in South Dakota because the cost of living is totally different . When the federal government enacts a broad price hike , it imposes a cost on businesses that will impact many companies in much more harmful ways than others . Luckily , we have some pretty good research on the topic , and even leading Democratic researchers on the minimum wage are worried that 15 would be too much , too fast . ( See here and here . )
