An Iowa man has been arrested on charges that he threw tomatoes at Republican presidential candidate Donald Trump during a campaign stop at the University of Iowa . Hayley Bruce , a University of Iowa spokeswoman , says 28 year old Andrew Joseph Alemao was charged Tuesday with disorderly conduct after officers say they saw him throwing two tomatoes toward Trump during a speech . It wasn ' t clear whether the tomatoes hit anyone . Secret Service and University of Iowa police officers arrested Alemao , and he was booked into the Johnson County Jail . Johnson County Jail officials say Alemao was released Wednesday morning without bond : Chicago Tribune
