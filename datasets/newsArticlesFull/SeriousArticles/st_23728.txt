In Lake County , Juan Rivera , who was convicted three times of the 1992 rape and murder of an 11 year old girl in spite of DNA evidence excluding him , spent 20 years in prison before the Illinois Appellate Court threw out his conviction in 2011 . Rivera ' s lawyers accused police of coercing him into falsely confessing . They allegedly interrogated him repeatedly over four days during which Rivera had little sleep , and they allegedly took advantage of his low IQ , his difficulty with English and a psychological breakdown he suffered while in custody .
