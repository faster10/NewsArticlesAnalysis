After that strong opening , Kasich disappeared for a while , but no one else really dominated while he was off screen . CNBC ' s John Harwood threw a fastball on corporate welfare by noting that Kasich opposed reauthorization of the Export Import Bank , which gives loans and financial guarantees to companies , yet authorized tax breaks to Ohio companies through the JobsOhio program . But Kasich largely brushed by the question and quickly pivoted to his standard line about how he helped negotiate the last balanced budget when he was in Congress .
