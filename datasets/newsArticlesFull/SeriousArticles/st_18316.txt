A week after the deadly attacks that killed at least 129 people in Paris , majorities of Americans now say they support stepped up military force against the Islamic State , including boots on the ground in the fight against the Islamic State , according to the latest ABC News Washington Post poll out Friday afternoon . A majority also opposed admitting refugees from the Middle East into the United States in light of the security situation in the region , but if they are admitted , an even stronger majority said that Christians should not be given special consideration for admission .
