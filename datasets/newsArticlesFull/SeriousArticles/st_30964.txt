Before the plan was even released , the Clinton campaign was worried . Clinton ' s reform proposal , released online in October , had many positive elements including a limited tax on high speed traders , a risk fee on the biggest banks and greater transparency in the derivatives markets , but it did not go much further than the reforms contained in the 2010 Dodd Frank bill . It said nothing about restoring Glass Steagall and did not call for the break up of the nation ' s dangerous mega banks , whose very size and complexity could bring down the U . S . economy .
