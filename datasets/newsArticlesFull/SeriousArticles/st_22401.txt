For both Al Qaeda and the Islamic State , killing civilians has been a tactic and a strategy . But they have disagreed over just how bloody to be . During the long insurgency against the American occupation of Iraq , Al Qaeda ' s leader there , Abu Musab al Zarqawi , oversaw a bloody campaign of suicide bombings . The attacks targeted both the American military and Iraqi civilians , including Muslims ? and especially Shiites . The group saw Shiites as rivals for power in Iraq but also as apostates who , under an extreme theology known as takfir , had betrayed Islam and deserved to die .
