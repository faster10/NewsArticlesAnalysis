Alderson s July 31 acquisition of Cespedes from Detroit , in return for minor league pitchers Luis Cessa and Michael Fulmer , will go down as one of the most memorable swaps in Mets history and one of the most impactful trade deadline deals in baseball history . Cespedes exceeded any and all reasonable expectations with his August and September performance , even generating chatter about a National League Most Valuable Player candidacy , and let him get standing ovations in Citi Field for years to come whenever he stops in wearing a visiting uniform .
