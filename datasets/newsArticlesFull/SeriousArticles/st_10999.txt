All indicators point that ongoing tensions between Saudi Arabia and Iran have peaked . Both are poised to continue struggling with the debris of over reaction , however . In the first place Iran over reacted to execution of a Saudi national by the Saudi government in the second place Saudi Arabia overreacted to an attack over its embassy in Tehran that had been condemned by the Iranian government and in the third place , some other countries chose to rupture their diplomatic ties with Iran unnecessarily . All these will now go through the pangs of retracing their rash steps .
