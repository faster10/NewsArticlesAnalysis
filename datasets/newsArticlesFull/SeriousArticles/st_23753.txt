Nadal , having faced more than his fair share of injuries during his career , had a rough 2015 . After winning at least one major title in 10 straight years , he failed to even reach a Grand Slam semifinal . But during the sleepy fall swing , when Nadal usually downshifts into energy saver mode , he played a full schedule and battled his way back up to fifth in the world rankings . Tennis fans rejoiced : their long international nightmare was over . In 2016 , we all told ourselves , Rafa once again would be winning major championships .
