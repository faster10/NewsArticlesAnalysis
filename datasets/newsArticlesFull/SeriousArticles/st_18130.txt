Netflix describes itself as " the world ' s leading Internet television network with over 69 million members in over 60 countries " . The company , which started operating as an online firm that provided DVDs on rental , has in fact become a pioneering video streaming service . It has in a way redefined the drawing room experience by offering people TV shows and movies through internet . The big advantage of Netflix is that it allows people to access their favourite TV shows on demand and not at a predetermined time .
