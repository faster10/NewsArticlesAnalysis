In the general election , the Republican electorate skews older than its Democratic counterpart . But , the new poll found Trump with his widest lead and broadest support in any national telephone poll since June , when Trump announced his candidacy . But two of the candidates who have demonstrated the most momentum of late are more than two decades younger : Marco Rubio and Ted Cruz , both 44 , potentially offering a stark contrast with Clinton , 68 , who , if elected , would be the oldest president to take office since Ronald Reagan .
