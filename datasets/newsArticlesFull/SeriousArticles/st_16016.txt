The perception that Donald Trump was bound to win in Iowa could well have discouraged many of his supporters to turn out and vote , as they would have thought he already had it in the bag , while giving those who want to stop him winning a reason to turn up . Dan Pfeiffer , a former adviser to Barack Obama , touched upon this weakness in Mr Trump ' s campaign , criticising his decision to hold a rally on voting day when his campaigners should have been focussed on getting their supporters out to vote .
