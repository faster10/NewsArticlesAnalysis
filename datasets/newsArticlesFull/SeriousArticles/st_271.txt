After the debate , hosted by the Fox Business Network and The Wall Street Journal , I think conservatives have a few promising Republican candidates to choose from . The tough part will be deciding which one of those qualified will do best against Democratic opposition . Whether that ' s Clinton or Sanders , she or he will appeal to many . A candidate pushing freebies , downplaying individual responsibility and failing to stress that our safety nets are meant for those in true need ? and that government dependence is a last resort ? will be hard to beat in today ' s society .
