Michael Oher goes back to The Blind Side : It may be time for Carolina s left tackle to start working on the sequel to the 2009 Hollywood movie starring Sandra Bullock and Tim McGraw . With the media corps expanded well beyond football writers , Oher already is expecting a ton of questions about the movie . The good news is Oher is now comfortable talking about it because his teammates rib him unmercifully about it . He wasn t comfortable in 2012 , when he was at the Super Bowl with the Baltimore Ravens .
