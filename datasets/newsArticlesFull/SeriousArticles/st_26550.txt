Big man on campus ( offense ) : So that s what happens when Ohio State hands the ball 30 times to Ezekiel Elliott . It s too bad , really , that we needed 12 games to witness a transcendent performance by Buckeyes star running back . ( His 274 yards against Indiana do not apply . ) Elliott churned for 214 yards and two touchdowns in the Buckeyes ' bounce back win , passing Eddie George for second place on the all time Ohio State rushing list . A week after he ran for a season low 33 yards on 12 carries against Michigan State , Elliott led the way as the Buckeyes posted their largest victory margin in Ann Arbor since 1961 .
