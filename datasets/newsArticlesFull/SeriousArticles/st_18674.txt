Salman has held two meetings related to the sale of Aramco shares , The Economist reported citing unidentified officials . Options include selling shares in the parent company , which controls oil exploration and production , to offering smaller units that operate petrochemical , refining and marketing , according to the report . The Economist said about 5 per cent of the company could be offered initially in Riyadh and more shares could be sold later , although the kingdom would retain control .
