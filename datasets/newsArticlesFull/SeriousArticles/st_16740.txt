Sharif arrived two days after a historic international deal lifted sanctions on Iran in return for a scaling back of its nuclear capabilities . Riyadh fears the agreement will only further embolden Iran , which it accuses of regional interference . SPA said the prime minister was accompanied by Pakistan ' s powerful army chief , General Raheel Sharif . The country ' s parliament last year refused to send forces to help a Saudi led Arab coalition fighting Iran backed Shia Huthi rebels in Yemen .
