Mufti Mohammad Sayeed created history when he became India ' s first Muslim Home Minister in 1989 . However , Sayeed ' s tenure as Home Minister was marred by the fact that it was around the same time that militancy had reared its ugly head in Jammu and Kashmir and when his own daughter Rubaiya was kidnapped by the JKLF militants . Rubaiya was later released in exchange for the release of five JKLF militants . Rubaiya ' s release for the exchange of the militants for the first time projected India as a " soft state " for the first time .
