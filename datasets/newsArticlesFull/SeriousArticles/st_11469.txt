The Vikings were backed up to second and 17 from their own 9 yard line . As we all know , they were able to score 10 points in the closing minutes to claim the victory as the Bears blew another close game against an NFC North rival in the closing minutes . I pinned the blame on the secondary in my On the Bears column after the game as strong safety Antrel Rolle and nickel cornerback Sherrick McManis made some key errors after the secondary had done a nice job against Minnesota s wide receivers for the first 58 minutes of the game .
