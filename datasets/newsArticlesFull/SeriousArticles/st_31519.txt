Old brick textile buildings have been transformed into trendy loft style apartments that have drawn thousands of newcomers . Celebrity chefs and gourmet grocers also have set up shop , spicing the neighborhood with a hipster foodie feel . Harpoon Brewery and popular microbreweries such as Trillium are making suds there . Gleaming office towers are going up alongside hundreds of residential units , and much of the 1 , 000 acre swath is bristling with cranes . Estimates vary , but some say the district employs 33 , 000 people and pumps 10 billion into Boston ' s economy .
