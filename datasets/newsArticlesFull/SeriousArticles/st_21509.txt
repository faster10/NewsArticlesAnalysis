The crowded skies over Syria pose a special challenge . The United States and Russia have agreed on a memorandum of understanding that mandates maintaining professional airmanship at all times , the use of specific communication frequencies and the establishment of a communication line on the ground , according to Lt Col Kristi Beckman , a spokeswoman at the US Central Command . US and Russian pilots operate at safe distances , she said , and if Syrian planes are near , it is standard practice for us to stand off until the airspace is clear .
