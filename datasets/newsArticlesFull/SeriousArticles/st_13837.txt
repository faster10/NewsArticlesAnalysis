STUDY FINDS ISIL MOBILIZATION IN U . S . UNPRECEDENTED : A six month study of Americans recruited to join the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant calls the terrorist group s mobilization in the United States unprecedented . The study by George Washington University s Program on Extremism examined social media accounts and legal documents of nearly 400 Americans who sympathize with ISIL . It found Americans involved in the group differ widely in race , age , social class , education and family background .
