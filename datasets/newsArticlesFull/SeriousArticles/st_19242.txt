Republican Sen . Lindsey Graham of South Carolina pressed both Carter and Marine Gen . Joseph Dunford Jr . , chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff , on whether the U . S . has a military strategy to take out the Syrian president . Graham noted that Russian , Iranian and the Hezbollah militant group all are supporting Assad . Carter and Dunford both repeatedly said that the U . S . was supporting moderate forces in Syria in the fight against the Islamic State , but not those fighting against Assad .
