At the debate , Ted Cruz came out swinging against Trump in his initial comments , mocking the tycoon ' s penchant for brash criticism . " I ' m a maniac , and everyone on this stage is stupid , fat and ugly , " joked Cruz , in an effort to , as he put it , get " the Donald Trump portion " of the program out of the way . Cruz ' s joke became one of the standout moments in the first half of the debate , gaining momentum on Twitter among news outlets and average Twitter users alike . The debate moved on to discussion of substantive issues . Foreign affairs , national security , immigration , reproductive rights and healthcare were the most tweeted about topics during the debate , according to Twitter .
