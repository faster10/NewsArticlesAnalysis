Colombian Deputy Health Minister Fernando Ruiz said Friday that his country has recorded 41 cases of Guillain Barre syndrome that appear to be linked to Zika infections . The mosquito that carries Zika isn ' t typically in our area , but another type that could carry the virus is . The CDC recommends that women who are pregnant or trying to become pregnant postpone travel to any area in which the virus is being transmitted . You should not take aspirin , ibuprofen or other nonsteroidal anti inflammatory drugs until you are certain you don ' t have dengue , another disease spread through the same mosquitoes .
