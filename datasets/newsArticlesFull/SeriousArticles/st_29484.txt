Rather than wither on the edges of politics , these parties have increasingly taken up real estate in the political center ? and even when the parties haven t assimilated into the mainstream , their ideas have . Their members posit a simple and seductive narrative of a cultural war being waged against the forces of European civilization . The specter is nearly always the same : the imminent creation of a so called Euro Arabia and the destruction of European civilization through cultural degradation fostered by a fifth column of Muslims in Europe .
