Brussels has been on its guard since it emerged that at least three jihadists behind the Paris assaults lived in the city . Abdeslam , who ' s now the focus of an international manhunt , comes from the Molenbeek district of the capital . Counter terrorism officers carried out 22 simultaneous raids in Brussels and the Belgian city of Charleroi , federal prosecutor Eric Van der Sypt told reporters early today . They detained 16 people , none of whom was Abdeslam , and didn ' t find any explosives or other armanents , he said .
