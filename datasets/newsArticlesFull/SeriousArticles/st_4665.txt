Hassan Khomeini , a 43 year old cleric with close ties to reformists , was not verified as having sufficient religious competence by the Guardian Council , his son Ahmad said on his Instagram account , despite " testimony from dozens of religious authorities " . Khomeini had hoped to be a candidate for election to the Assembly of Experts , a powerful group of clerics which monitors the work of Iran ' s current supreme leader , Ayatollah Ali Khamenei , and will be responsible for selecting his successor . Khamenei , now 76 , took over from the elder Khomeini who died in 1989 .
