Myanmar s military rulers , concerned that Ms Suu Kyi s popularity could threaten their hold on power , introduced a new constitution in 2008 that effectively bars her from holding the highest offices in the country . Article 59 , believed to have been written with her in mind , says that anyone married to a foreign citizen or whose children are foreigners cannot become president or vice president . Ms Suu Kyi was married to British historian Michael Aris , who died in 1999 . Her two sons are British citizens .
