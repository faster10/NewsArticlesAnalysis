Finally , the Government has an opportunity to lay out its commitment to fighting terror through the Strategic Defence and Security Review , announced tomorrow . Britain s Armed Forces have been subject to cuts motivated purely by the desire to save money . Now they enjoy a ring fenced budget of two per cent of GDP . Sufficient money must go into both the nuclear deterrent and conventional forces . After all , Britain needs to be able to respond flexibly to the terrorist threat . It must be able to protect its citizens at home while , if necessary , decisively projecting its power overseas .
