Marking the 10th anniversary of YouTube and tapping into the extraordinary success of the superstar vlogger ? video bloggers ? Jim Chapman , one of the stars of the medium , offers a thoughtful insight into the rise of this new breed of celebrity . Chapman explains why video sharing has become a powerful way to connect and asks why these superstars want to share their everyday lives . He meets , among others , Alfie Deyes ( now immortalised in Mademe Tussauds and also the boyfriend of fellow internet sensation Joella ) and KSI , the highest earning British vlogger .
