John Elway wants Manning to go out as one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time . While Manning has the numbers to back it up , a second Super Bowl win would go a long way to making his case for the best of all time . However , this isn t the same Peyton Manning that played in the Super Bowl last time around , and Cam Newton is arguably one of the best quarterbacks in the NFL this year . If the Broncos defense can t stop Newton , there is little chance that Denver can keep up . The Broncos win thanks to their defense , and this time it might not be enough to stop Cam Newton .
