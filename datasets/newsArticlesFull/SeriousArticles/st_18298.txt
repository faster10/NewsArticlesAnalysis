For example , Japan ' s beef tariff , which could be as high as 50 percent , would be reduced to 9 percent . Vietnam would eliminate tariffs on beef , and Malaysia would lock tariffs in at zero percent . For pork , Japan would eliminate duties on nearly 80 percent of product categories . Japan , which excluded rice from its prior trade agreements , would establish a new duty free quota for U . S . rice , while Malaysia and Vietnam would eliminate tariffs on rice . Japan , Malaysia , and Vietnam would eliminate tariffs on all fresh and processed fruits , including citrus . And Malaysia and Vietnam would immediately eliminate all tariffs , and Japan nearly all tariffs , on fresh and processed vegetables .
