Central Africa can be a dangerous place at the best of times . For a long while Brugger is marooned there ( his documentation has not come through , and in fairness to Liberia ' s authorities , it never does ) , and if he needs any reminder of what could go wrong , it all comes crashing home when one of his interviewees is assassinated . The movie ' s last 20 minutes play like a real life John le Carr spy thriller : The wheels start to come off the deceit and we have to wonder if he ' ll be able to extricate himself without serious injury .
