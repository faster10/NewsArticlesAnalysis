Murray already has prepared himself for the possibility of a summons from home if his wife Kim should go into labour early , and has said that he is prepared to leave the tournament without a final if necessary . What he was not expecting , however , was for Kim s father to be the one rushed to hospital . Even so , there is some precedent for a family emergency at the Australian Open . Two years ago , Andy s brother Jamie suffered gruesome full body cramps after waking up late , failing to hydrate , and then being sent out to play in 42 degree heat .
