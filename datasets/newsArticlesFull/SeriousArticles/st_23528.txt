The lead plaintiff is Deborah Peterson , whose brother , Lance Cpl . James C . Knipple of Alexandria , Virginia , was killed in the Beirut barracks bombing in 1983 . In 2001 , the Peterson plaintiffs , along with family members and the estates of 241 Beirut bombing victims , filed suit in U . S . courts against Iran . Six years later , a federal district court held that the evidence presented by the Peterson plaintiffs established Iran ' s liability , and the following year , the plaintiffs were informed that the Bank of Markazi had 1 . 75 billion in assets held in an account in New York .
