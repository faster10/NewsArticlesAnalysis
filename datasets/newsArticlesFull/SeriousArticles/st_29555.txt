Opec supply fell in December to 31 . 62 million barrels per day ( bpd ) from a revised 31 . 79 million in November , according to the survey , based on shipping data and information from sources at oil companies , Opec and consultants . Oil prices have more than halved in 18 months and hit an 11 year low in the wake of Opec ' s December 4 decision to keep its year old policy of no output restraint . The current crisis between Saudi Arabia and Iran expected to pump more oil as sanctions are lifted makes cooperation over supply even less likely , analysts say .
