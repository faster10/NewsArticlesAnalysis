What does all the above tell us about the race to come ? Nothing specific . In fact , it strongly suggests that specifics will continue to change unexpectedly and unpredicatably . But in does suggest something in general , which may or may not shed some light on what may happen next . If we think of the race in terms of factions , not individual candidates , we have four contenders in the race : the outsiders ( Trump , Carson , Fiorina ) , the outsider electeds ( Cruz , Paul , Huckabee and Santorum ) , the Koch Tea Party block ( Walker and Rubio ) , and establishment electeds ( everyone else ) .
