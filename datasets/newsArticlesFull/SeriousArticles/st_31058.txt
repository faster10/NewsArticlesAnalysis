Rebel groups in the northern Hama province , some of them armed with newly supplied TOW anti tank missiles , have blunted the Syrian Army s offensive and even captured two villages that were under regime control . In Aleppo province , the Syrian Army and its allies seized ground in an advance two weeks ago . But the offensive has since slowed , and a counter attack by the Islamic State , which controls territory to the east of Aleppo , now threatens to sever the regime s sole transport link to the city .
