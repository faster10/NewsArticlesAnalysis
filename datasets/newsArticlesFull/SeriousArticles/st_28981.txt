In October , Iran ' s supreme leader , Ayatollah Ali Khamenei , said Iran would walk away from the nuclear deal if new sanctions of any kind were imposed . But the administration has been clear that even after the most onerous sanctions are lifted under the nuclear deal , it would enforce those pertaining to Iran ' s missile , terrorism and human rights activities . The nuclear deal lifts UN ballistic missile sanctions on Iran within eight years , but U . S . sanctions on firms that sell arms to Iran will remain .
