Brady was sacked 38 times during the 2015 regular season . He was not sacked on Saturday during the Patriots 27 20 divisional round victory over the Chiefs in Foxborough . He s only been sacked more in two other seasons during his 16 year career . Brady was sacked 40 times during the 2013 season and a career high 41 times during the 2001 season , which was his second season in the league and his first as the starter , after he took over when Drew Bledsoe was sidelined by a hit from the Jets Mo Lewis .
