Volunteers and police carrying bottled water , filters and lead test kits knocked on doors in Flint on Tuesday , seeking to help residents in the MI city that ' s confronting a water crisis . The support of the National Guard will enable American Red Cross volunteers , who have been staffing the water resource sites since January 9 , to join the efforts of the water resource teams going door to door in Flint neighborhoods to distribute bottled water , water filters , replacement cartridges and testing kits .
