" Pakistan is deeply concerned at the recent escalation of tensions between the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the Islamic Republic of Iran , " Islamabad ' s foreign ministry spokesman Qazi Khalilullah said ahead of Sharif ' s trip . He said the prime minister had called for a peaceful settlement of differences in the interests of Muslim unity . " The purpose of the visit is to mediate and to end the stand off between the two countries , " a Pakistani government official told AFP , requesting anonymity .
