The series of statements and measures are very encouraging and indicate that China is very genuine and very serious about its relations with Africa , said Bimha , the Zimbabwean minister , adding that they also show that China wants to be a true friend of Africa . In the eyes of Tlohang Sekhamane , foreign minister of Lesotho , it is clear that China wants to be a partner looking for cooperation with Africa and it seeks opportunities of real win win cooperation instead of looking for a situation of dominance .
