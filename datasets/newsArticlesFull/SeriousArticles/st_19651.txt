As international efforts to fight the Islamic State group stepped up , French Foreign Minister Laurent Fabius said Russia was sincere in wanting to cooperate against IS in Syria . " There is an opening , so to speak , with the Russians . We think they are sincere and we must bring together all our forces , " he told France Inter radio . But world powers remain deeply divided over the future of Syrian President Bashar al Assad , who has strong backing from Moscow . US President Barack Obama said Thursday that Syria ' s brutal civil war could not end while Assad remained in power .
