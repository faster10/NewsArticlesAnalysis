Security rises as concern but economy still dominates : As the 2016 campaign goes on , the economy is the dominant issue for voters , but not to the degree it once was , according to a survey by Morning Consult . Thirty six percent say economic issues are most important to them when considering which candidates to support . That s down 10 points from a year ago . Meanwhile , 22 of voters say security issues are at the top of their minds when they think about national elections , second only to economic issues .
