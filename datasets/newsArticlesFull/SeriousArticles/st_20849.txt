Putin s spokesman cast Nemtsov , once spoken of as a contender to succeed Boris Yeltsin as president , a job Putin got , as quite an average citizen who was no political threat . Putin said something similar about reporter Anna Politkovskaya after she was murdered on his birthday in 2006 . What looked like a campaign to erase Nemtsov s memory soon began . A month after his murder , activists arrived to find that the flowers and other tributes had vanished . It is a routine that has been repeated , by conservative estimates , at least 15 times . Some say the real number is double that .
