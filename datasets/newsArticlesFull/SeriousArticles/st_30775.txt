Gunmen set off a car bomb outside a Baghdad mall Monday before entering , killing at least 18 people and wounding 50 others , Iraqi officials said . The Islamic State has purportedly claimed responsibility for the attack in the predominantly Shiite neighborhood , saying four of their fighters were behind it . Iraqi officials said there were more , some of whom were killed . The attack , which was reported by officials as a hostage situation , lasted about 1 . 5 hours , with Iraqi forces surrounding the building and fighting attackers inside .
