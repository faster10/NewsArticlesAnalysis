The question is rhetorical , intended only to suggest that perhaps someone ought to start thinking seriously about the thousands of teenagers who are lost to Chicago ' s streets each year . These are children without families to guide them , or whose parents are on drugs or imprisoned , children who rely on the system more than most of us to protect them , educate them and turn them away from self destruction and onto a road that will lead to , well , someplace other than a chalk outline on a city street .
