And the general picture is quite healthy . Most housebuilders are conservatively geared . They have reasonably good visibility on demand with the Government s Help to Buy scheme likely to run for at least another five years and interest rates expected to stay low for the foreseeable future . There is also plenty of land coming on to the market at reasonable prices ( because alternative uses , like farming , aren t so appealing ) . All this suggests that housebuilders should be able to maintain a sufficient margin to fund dividends with enough left over to invest in their businesses .
