Rubio criticizes Cruz over surveillance : Sen . Marco Rubio of Florida , also a Republican presidential hopeful , connected the threat from ISIS to recent action by Congress he said weakened the U . S . intelligence community s surveillance powers . Speaking at The Wall Street Journal CEO Council on Monday night , Rubio named Sen . Ted Cruz of Texas , one of his rivals for the GOP nomination , as one of two senators who voted to weaken the U . S . intelligence programs just in the last month and a half . Rubio was talking about the USA Freedom Act , Roll Call writes , which eliminated the federal bulk collection of telephone records and moved data to telephone companies .
