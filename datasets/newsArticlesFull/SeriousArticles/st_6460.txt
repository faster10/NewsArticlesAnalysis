Michelle Rhee The Washington schools chancellor made headlines last week when students in her school system showed progress on national math tests , D . C . being the only one of 11 urban districts to demonstrate significant increases . In June , Rhee fired some 250 young and tenured teachers for poor performance or failure to get a license , The Washington Post reported . On Monday , a Wall Street Journal editorial questioned why the Obama administration wasn ' t doing more to back her reform ideas .
