Common enemy for Cruz , Paul and Rubio : One subject almost certain to come up at the debate is the two year budget deal struck by Republicans and the White House . Watch for at least three of the contenders to bash it : Sens . Ted Cruz , Rand Paul and Marco Rubio . As National Journal writes , the deal lifts caps on domestic and military spending by 80 billion over two years . Cruz called the deal a slap in the face to conservatives who aim to cut spending and debt . Congress must still vote on the agreement .
