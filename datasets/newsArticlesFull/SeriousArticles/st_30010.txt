The struggle for regional clout between Saudi Arabia and Iran began heating up following the overthrow of Saddam Hussein ' s regime in Iraq , which replaced a Sunni Arab minority dictatorship with rule by the country ' s Shia majority , and a concomitant increase in Iranian influence there . The move , its repercussions already being felt across the Gulf and indeed much further afield , follows 47 executions carried out in Saudi Arabia of men found guilty of involvement in terrorism .
