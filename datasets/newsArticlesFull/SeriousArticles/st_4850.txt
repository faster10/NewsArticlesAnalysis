Last Wednesday ? November 11 ? Interior Minister Bernard Cazeneuve boasted about how his agents had caught a radical who had " threatened to blow up Toulon Naval Base " . Evidence against him released to the media included social media activity ( for which read Twitter and Facebook posts ) and the fact that he had ordered a knife and balaclava via mail order . So it was that a clearly disturbed loner with all the hallmarks of a low rent fantasist too became the government s War Against Terror story for Armistice Day . The real life lethal terrorists were , meanwhile , evidently overlooked .
