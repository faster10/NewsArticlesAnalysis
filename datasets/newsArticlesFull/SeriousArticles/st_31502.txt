Amir Hekmati , a former U . S . Marine charged with spying , was freed as part of a prisoner swap that included Washington Post journalist Jason Rezaian on January 16 . An Iranian court threw out a 2011 death sentence for Hekmati , but he was secretly retried in Iran and convicted of " practical collaboration with the U . S . government , " his sister told CNN on April 11 . He was sentenced to 10 years in prison , she said . Hekmati was detained in August 2011 during a visit to see his grandmother . His family and the Obama administration deny accusations he was spying for the CIA .
