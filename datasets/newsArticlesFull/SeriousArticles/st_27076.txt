FILE In this Jan . 14 , 2016 file photo , Academy President Cheryl Boone Isaacs announces the Academy Awards nominations at the 88th Academy Awards nomination ceremony in Beverly Hills , Calif . The film academy is pledging to double the number of female and minority members by 2020 , and will immediately diversify its leadership by adding three new seats to its board of governors . Isaacs announced the changes Friday , Jan . 22 , following a weeklong storm of criticism and calls for an Oscar boycott after academy members nominated an all white slate of actors for the second year in a row .
