In parallel , a different Irishness was making itself felt through a series of IRA atrocities . There was the 1974 Birmingham bombing , in which 21 civilians were murdered and 182 injured the 1974 Balcombe Street siege , in which two people were held hostage in their flat at the end of a 14 month campaign of 40 bombings and 35 murders , one of them of an IRA critic the Hyde Park and Regent s Park bombs against non combatant soldiers the March 1993 Warrington shopping street litter bin bomb that killed two children and the 1996 docklands bomb that killed two news vendors .
