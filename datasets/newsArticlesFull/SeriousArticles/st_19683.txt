The government said earlier this week that more than 11 , 000 refugees applications had been processed , and that chartered civilian flights would be used for most of the airlift . The previous Conservative government s sluggish response to the Syrian refugee crisis became an issue in the election , especially after the widespread publication in September of a photograph showing Aylan Kurdi , a 3 year old Syrian boy who drowned with his brother and mother off the coast of Turkey . The boy s aunt had been trying to bring the family to Canada .
