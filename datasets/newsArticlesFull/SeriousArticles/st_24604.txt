On September 28 , a Muslim family was attacked in a village outside Delhi by a Hindu mob that suspected the family of eating beef , an accusation the family denied . The father , Mohammed Ikhlaq , was killed , and his son seriously wounded . Weeks later , another Hindu mob in the Kashmir Valley in north India threw a homemade bomb at a truck suspected of carrying beef a young Muslim trucker , most of his body burnt , died days later . Then , on October 14 , a Muslim man was killed in the north Indian state of Himachal Pradesh when he was attacked by a group of Hindu activists who suspected him of smuggling cattle for slaughter .
