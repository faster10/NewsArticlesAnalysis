Jeb Bush: U.S. 'got nothing' for opening ties with Cuba
U.S. Republican Jeb Bush on Wednesday criticized the Obama administration for opening diplomatic ties with Cuba, saying "we got nothing in return.""I wouldn't call what we did a reset," Bush, the former governor of Florida and a possible contender for the Republican presidential nomination in 2016, said in a question-and-answer session following a speech. "I would call it bad negotiations."
