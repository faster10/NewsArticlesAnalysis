DiCaprio fans will be waiting with bated breath to see if unlucky Leo will finally come up trumps . Tipped for an Oscar nod for his performance in The Reverent , the omission of Johnny Depp ' s performance in Black Mass was hard to miss . Due to the Golden Globes format , which dutifully recognises performances in a comedy , musical and dramatic feature films , the best actor nods will no doubt read somewhat differently come Academy Awards time . Oscar winner Eddy Redmayne ( The Theory of Everything ) could well have this one in the bag , for his portrayal as a pioneering transgender woman .
