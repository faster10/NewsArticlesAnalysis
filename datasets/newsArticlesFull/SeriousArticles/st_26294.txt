The Raiders ' much maligned defense , while giving up yards , was actually bottom line effective through most of the game . In the first three quarters , it held the Lions to just nine points . The leading tacklers for the first three periods were linebackers Khalil Mack ( six solos ) and rookie Ben Heeney ( four solos , two assists ) . Heeney , the fifth round pick who played for a University of Kansas team that didn ' t win many games , may come to be seen as the Raiders ' steal of the 2015 draft .
