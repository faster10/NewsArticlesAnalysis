The authorities had completed all preparations on Monday night with water cannon and heavy deployment of police , rangers , ASF at the main artery , moving towards Jinnah International Airport ( JIAP ) from Old terminal where PIA head office is located . At noon , protesters , who raised placards and chanted slogans against the privatisation of PIA and the management , started marching towards JIAP to suspend flight operations , forcing the law enforcement agencies to treat them harshly and restrict their movement .
