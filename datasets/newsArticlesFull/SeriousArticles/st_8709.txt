Look for investors who don ' t just write you a cheque but can actually coach you through operational details , hiring great talent , help you make decisions about scaling the company or just keep your head down and focus on the product . There is a lot of strategic decision making that you face as an entrepreneur . If you scale too fast you ' ll be burning through your cash . If you don ' t scale fast enough , you won ' t be investing in your team . I think having an investor who has operational expertise is a critical element . And that they understand your vision and believe in you to build the company .
