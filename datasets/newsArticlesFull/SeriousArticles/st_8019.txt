The chancellor plans to take a gentler route to the same old destination . He is lucky to have the opportunity . He has also made sensible adjustments to his own plans . But political success will still depend on the performance of the economy . It will depend as much on how well the government delivers the services the public expects out of such a historically low share of GDP . Mr Osborne has defined his party s territory for a much needed debate on the size and role of the state . It is the opposition s job to offer a credible response .
