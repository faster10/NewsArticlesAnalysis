RIDER SPOTLIGHT : Sen . Harry Reid ' s ( D Nev . ) gambit to help Caesars Entertainment restructure debt out of court pits him against Sen . Elizabeth Warren ( D Mass . ) , Alexander Bolton reports for The Hill . The language Reid is trying to insert into the spending bill could also help Education Management Corp . , a private education company Warren is after . Eighteen law professors cautioned lawmakers this week not to rush the rider through . A loss for Caesars could cost Nevada thousands of jobs . The company is also Reid ' s fourth largest donor over the course of his career , according to OpenSecrets .
