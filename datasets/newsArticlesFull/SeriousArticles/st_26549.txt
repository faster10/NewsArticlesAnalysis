Team of the week : Say goodbye to Indiana s seven year bowl drought , which was tied with Colorado for the longest among FBS teams . The Hoosiers throttled Purdue 54 36 to win the Old Oaken Bucket behind a 350 yard , four touchdown performance by Nate Sudfeld . Devine Redding , in place of injured running back Jordan Howard , gained 144 yards on the ground . The Hoosiers ( 6 6 ) are bowl eligible for the first time since 2007 , with a chance for their second winning season out of the past 21 under fifth year coach Kevin Wilson .
