In the months leading up to the release of Spectre , the 24th Bond film and the fourth starring Daniel Craig , there was much online speculation about the character to be played by Christoph Waltz . Blofeld was last seen in For Your Eyes Only ( 1981 ) falling to his death a protracted legal battle over the rights to his name had kept him off the screen ever since . Waltz , with his vestigial Austrian accent and the imprinted memory of his cheerfully mad Nazi in Tarantino ' s Inglourious Basterds , seemed a perfect fit .
