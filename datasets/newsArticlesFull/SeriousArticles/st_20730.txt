Meanwhile , the UK needs to address a number of questions about its own policing . It is encouraging to note that the number of military personnel who could help the response to a Paris style attack is going to be increased . However , the police budget cuts expected to be unveiled in this Wednesday s spending review have become a source of considerable debate . Critics of the Government warn that the police are expected to be hit by a departmental reduction of up to 20 per cent . Mr Cameron will obviously have to reassure the public that Britain has a police force of a size necessary to guarantee public safety .
