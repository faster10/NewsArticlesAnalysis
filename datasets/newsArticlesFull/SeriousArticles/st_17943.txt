He appears in the list of 12 disciples called by Jesus , but does not become a main character in the drama until the Passion Narratives . In John ' s Gospel , he is the disciple who is concerned about Mary wasting money anointing Jesus ( John 12 . 4 6 ) . Soon afterward , he is arranging to hand Jesus over to the authorities . His story ends with his dramatic suicide ( Matthew 27 . 3 10 ) , and Luke tells us that his stomach burst open and his intestines gushed out when he died ( Acts 1 . 18 19 ) .
