Nepalese people form a human chain displaying placard which read " Transit is our right , Lets protest Indian blockade " , " Lets maintain national unity and social harmony " during a protest against blockade in Kathmandu , Nepal , Monday , Nov . 30 , 2015 . The Madhesi ethnic group has imposed a general strike in much of southern Nepal and blocked a key border point with India , causing a shortage of fuel and other goods in Nepal . People of Nepal also accuse India of imposing an " undeclared blockade , " which India denies . Niranjan Shrestha AP Photo
