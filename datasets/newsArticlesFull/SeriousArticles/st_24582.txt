Annette March Grier Annette March Grier grew up in her family ' s funeral home . After her mother ' s death , she created Roberta ' s House , a nonprofit in Baltimore that helps children and their families cope with grief . Since 2008 , more than 1 , 000 children have benefited from the group ' s free programs . " We ' re giving families in this city a sense of hope , " she said .
