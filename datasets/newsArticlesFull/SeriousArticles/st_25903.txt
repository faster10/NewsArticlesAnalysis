For kids and teens exposed to violence at home or in the community , boys are just as likely to be seriously hurt by a romantic partner , although the risk changes with age , according to a new study . " Most samples are not specifically high risk populations where youth are previously exposed to violence , " said lead author Dennis E . Reidy of the Division of Violence Prevention at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention in Atlanta . " Most of what we know suggests that girls are more likely to be victims of sexual violence . "
