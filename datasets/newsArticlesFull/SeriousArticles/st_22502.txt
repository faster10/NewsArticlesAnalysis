The report said that while the rate of warming is slower in the 2000s than it was in the 1990s it doesn ' t negate the 150 years of observations that show the world is warming . The report also says that more the 90 percent of the heat trapped by greenhouse gases lately has been absorbed into the oceans ' deep water , which for a while slows surface warming but not the long term trend . There is enough evidence on the science to warrant action , Sir Paul Nurse , president of the Royal Society , said in a news release .
