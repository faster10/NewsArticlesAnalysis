Richie Incognito named a captain in Miami : Ryan has made it a custom to name players captains against their former teams and when playing in regions where they either grew up or played college football . That tradition continued on Sept . 27 , when Incognito came to midfield at Sun Life Stadium for the coin toss prior to the Bills ' 41 14 win over the Dolphins . Incognito , who had been suspended by the Dolphins in 2013 and was later released before spending a year out of football , helped pave the way for the Bills ' most lopsided win of the season .
