Calvin Johnson ' s three touchdowns give him 11 in his career on Thanksgiving , an NFL record . He has at least one scoring grab in eight straight on the holiday , also a record . He finished the day with eight catches for 93 yards , and became the fifth player since the merger ( 1970 ) to record a catch in the first 130 games of his career . He can catch Jeremy Shockey for fourth on the list with a catch in his next six games . ( Only 60 more to catch Marvin Harrison ' s record of 190 games . )
