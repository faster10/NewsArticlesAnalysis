Pawel Krzysiek , spokesman for the International Committee for the Red Cross in Syria , accompanied an aid convoy on Monday to the town of Madaya , which has been besieged by forces loyal to the Syrian regime since July . Supplies were delivered as part of a deal under which convoys also entered two Shia villages , Fua and Kefraya , that are surrounded by rebels .
