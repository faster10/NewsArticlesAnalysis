Nearly a decade and a half ago , on Sept . 11 , 2001 , Al Qaeda seized the world ' s attention with a uniquely spectacular act of terrorism , and it proceeded to harass the United States and its allies with attacks and insurgencies on multiple fronts around the globe . But in recent years , it has been eclipsed by the Islamic State , which dazzled jihadists by swiftly conquering wide stretches of territory in Syria and Iraq , creating what it calls a caliphate and erasing a decades old colonial border .
