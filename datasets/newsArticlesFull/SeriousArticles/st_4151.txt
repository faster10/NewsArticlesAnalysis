One reason to get excited : Fangio dialed up the pressure on Sunday . Chicago harassed Broncos quarterback Brock Osweiler much of the afternoon , sacking the 6 foot 8 quarterback five times . And the pass rush came from a variety of angles . Defensive end Bruce Gaston , nose tackle Eddie Goldman and free safety Adrian Amos were among the defenders credited with sacks . Osweiler rarely had time in the pocket . He hurried many of his throws . The defense did enough for the Bears to win the game .
