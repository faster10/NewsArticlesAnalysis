Ronaldo was netting goals but Messi was doing better scoring and collecting trophies simultaneously . First , the four time Ballon d ' Or awardee went to Vincente Calderon , scored the winner against Atletico and won the 23rd La Liga for Barcelona with a game to spare . Next he hammered a brace against Athletic Bilbao to win the Del Rey . Thereafter , on the biggest occasions of all , the Argentine orchestrated Barca attack notched up their fifth Champions League at the expense of the Old Lady of Turin .
