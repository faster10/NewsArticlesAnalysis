Sports matter : As we have seen time and time again with the Olympic Games , politics do not cease to exist when players step onto the field , the court or the pitch . This tournament proved yet again what a critical window sport provides into the world we live in , particularly considering the fiery protests that greeted the Brazilian government when it signed on the dotted line to host . The billions spent on bringing the most watched sporting event in the world to Brazil put the country ' s government into a fragile state as protestors told FIFA to " GO HOME . " But the politics were not reserved for the streets of S o Paulo and Rio : Inside the stadiums we saw fans in blackface when Germany faced Ghana and a spike in the use of " Nazi " on Twitter when Germany faced both Brazil and the United States .
