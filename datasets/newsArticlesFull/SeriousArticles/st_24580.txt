Rabbi Elimelech GoldbergRabbi Elimelech Goldberg lost his 2 year old daughter to leukemia in 1981 . Today , his nonprofit , Kids Kicking Cancer , uses martial arts to help children battling serious illnesses manage pain during medical treatments . The group has provided free lessons and support for 5 , 000 children and their families . " They ' re often so afraid , " Goldberg said .
