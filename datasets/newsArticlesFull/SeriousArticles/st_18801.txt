Republicans rip Clinton s coal plan : Republicans rebuked Hillary Clinton s 30 billion plan to help coal communities rebound as the clean energy economy develops , Fox News reports , saying she backs policies that are crippling coal country in the first place . Republicans noted Clinton is backing the Environmental Protection Agency plan requiring states to cut emissions from coal fired power plants , a plan coal state representatives are fighting . A Republican National Committee spokesman said the Democratic presidential front runner is Public Enemy No . 1 for coal miners .
