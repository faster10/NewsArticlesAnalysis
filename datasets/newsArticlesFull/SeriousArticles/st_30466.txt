Counter terror units were active Monday in Li ge , Belgium , apparently chasing tips that Abdeslam was spotted in a BMW there . Liege is less than a half hour ' s drive from the German border and close to the Belgian town of Verviers , where in January Belgian police units were involved in a gunfight with jihadists . A local media outlet reported that the 26 year old Frenchman and former petty criminal managed to break through a police cordon near Li ge , but officials refused to confirm or deny the claim .
