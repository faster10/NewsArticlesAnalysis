When asked , only 41 percent of those surveyed said they were " somewhat " or " very " familiar with the three term former mayor . That compares with 66 percent for Sanders and 59 percent for Republican Senator Ted Cruz . Clinton and Trump , who have been in the public spotlight for decades , enjoy almost 100 percent name recognition . The Reuters Ipsos poll included responses from 1 , 673 people . It has a credibility interval , a measure of the poll ' s accuracy , of about 3 percentage points .
