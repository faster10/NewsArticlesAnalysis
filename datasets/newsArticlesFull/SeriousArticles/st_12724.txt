In Washington , former US secretary of defence , Bob Gates , declared that the administration s attempts to prevent Iran from stomping around the Middle East was very weak . In a comprehensive interview with Christopher Dickey in the Daily Beast , Gates called for something much broader than just more special ops forces to fight Daesh , the self proclaimed Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant , and so on . I am talking about a major US initiative in terms of an increased military presence , increased military support for our friends and allies , a major effort to get them to work together as a regional security effort , and a comprehensive strategy on how you push back against Iranian meddling .
