But this trip shows few signs of Obama embracing his globe trotter in chief status . The White House says it has planned time for what it calls people to people exchanges . The president is slated to meet with young people at a town hall in Kuala Lumpur , part of an initiative to influence young leaders , and is scheduled to visit a refugee center there in an effort to draw attention to displaced people around the globe . In the Philippines , he s due to speak at a coastal facility to tout maritime cooperation , noted deputy national security adviser Ben Rhodes .
