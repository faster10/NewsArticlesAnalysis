This sort of confusion and resultant security operations against the Kurds must be music to IS ears , as much of the Turkish government s energies are directed against a perceived Kurdish threat . This is bound to affect the effectiveness of Kurdish operations against IS , as they now have to face two fronts , leading one to wonder if the Turkish government is at all serious about fighting IS . The initial satisfaction in the US led coalition over Turkey s decision to join their ranks has almost disappeared because , on balance , it has probably weakened the coalition by weakening the Kurdish contribution on the ground , which was considered quite effective .
