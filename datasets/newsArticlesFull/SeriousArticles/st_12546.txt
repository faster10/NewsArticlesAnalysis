While Obama had several key surrogates in 2008 , including Virginia Gov . Tim Kaine and Missouri Sen . Claire McCaskill along with top Iowa Democrats , Clinton has swept up every major endorsement to date ( Massachusetts Sen . Elizabeth Warren is neutral for now ) . In New Hampshire , Clinton was introduced Friday by the state s popular Sen . Jeanne Shaheen , a former governor , and her firepower will be on display this weekend in Iowa , with Senate Democrats like Kaine , Cory Booker of New Jersey and Kirsten Gillibrand of New York rallying supporters on her behalf .
