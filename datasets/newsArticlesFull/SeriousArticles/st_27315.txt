" Room " is one of the most talked about movies this year , and it ' s easy to see why . Told from the perspective of 5 year old Jack ( Jacob Tremblay ) , who has only known the small room he was born in , this movie explores themes of survival , abuse and perseverance with deft skill . Led by masterful and nuanced performances from Tremblay and Brie Larson , who plays Ma , the film also benefits from impressive directing by Irish filmmaker Lenny Abrahamson . If you need a film to reference to make it seem like you ' re culturally knowledgeable , pick " Room . " It ' s one of those movies you never forget .
