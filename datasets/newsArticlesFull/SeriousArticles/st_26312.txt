It s all the same , but not the same . The Peanuts Movie , the big screen , 3D debut of the classic comic strip created by Charles M . Schulz , has everything you d expect : the Vince Guaraldi music ( including the joyously bopping Linus and Lucy ) , the kite eating tree , the Red Baron , the baseball diamond , the doghouse with the old school typewriter on top , the goofy dancing , the Psychiatric Help booth , and all those little kids with round heads , button eyes and a knack for philosophizing .
