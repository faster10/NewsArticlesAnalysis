In 2006 , 12 0 and undefeated Ohio State was a seven point favorite against Florida in the first BCS National Championship Game . Harvin , a five star freshman recruit of then Florida coach Urban Meyer , was one of many talented offensive players and his impact in the passing and running games made him a dangerous player . Against the Buckeyes , he showed that versatility with a game high nine catches for 60 yards and five rushes for 22 yards and a touchdown . In total , he led all skill players with 82 yards as the Gators stunned the Buckeyes , 41 14 .
