As the years went by , the legends grew , fuelled by the narcocorridas , songs composed about his exploits : stories of how he breakfasted with governors , had a direct line into the interior ministry , or simply that he took holidays and dined out largely at will . He was like a medieval baron with his court and bards , and did little to hide the fact that he was operating out of the hills in Sinaloa . We , as film makers , set out to find him , to show that El Chapo was at least hiding in plain sight and that if the authorities truly wanted him , they could pick him up pretty easily . We set out to prove that , as so often in the so called war on drugs , nothing was as it seemed .
