( Architectural Digest ) The great American graphic designer Milton Glaser once declared , " There are three responses to a piece of design yes , no , and wow ! Wow is the one to aim for . " Tommy and Dee Hilfiger apparently endorse that notion . The fashion mogul and his wife have planted their flag on a glorious stretch of beachfront just north of Miami with a home defined by spectacular moments , whimsical flourishes , and astonishing coups de th tre . In this case wow ! might be an understatement .
