Former OU quarterback to play for Texas A M ? Quarterback Trevor Knight has chosen to use his final year of NCAA eligibility to play at Texas A M after graduating from Oklahoma . Knight went to high school in San Antonio before enrolling at Oklahoma and redshirting in 2012 . He was the MVP of the Sugar Bowl win against Alabama following the 2013 regular season and was an Academic All Big 12 selection in 2014 . He played in six games this past season and graduated with a finance degree in December .
