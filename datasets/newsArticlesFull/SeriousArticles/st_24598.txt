Obama ' s kind words for his former secretary of state , in a Politico interview published on Monday , will help Clinton as she tries to link her campaign more closely with the president and so draw in more support from his backers . While never explicitly criticising Sanders , a senator from Vermont whose campaign is focused on pledges to redress social inequality and contain Wall Street excesses , Obama praised Clinton ' s experience and suggested several times that Clinton ' s messages are grounded in realism .
