Fran ois Hollande did not thank his opponent , Nicolas Sarkozy , during his acceptance speech , after defeating the incumbent with 51 . 6 of the vote in the French presidential runoff . But he should have , as he ran an anti Sarkozy campaign , promising to behave like a " normal president " in contrast to the impulsive , unpredictable and sometimes ostentatious Sarkozy . And it worked : Fifty five percent of the voters who cast a ballot for him did it to defeat Sarkozy rather than to elect Hollande .
