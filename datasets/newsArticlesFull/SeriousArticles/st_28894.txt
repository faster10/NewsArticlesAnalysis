Defensively , the Sooners lose a quartet of all conference performers in Sanchez , Alexander , defensive end Charles Tapper and linebacker Eric Striker . Tapper and Striker were the biggest reasons why Oklahoma featured one of the best pass rushes in the country , combining for 15 of the team ' s 39 sacks . Pressuring the quarterback was a calling card for Oklahoma this past season . Without Striker , Tapper and outgoing linebacker Devante Bond ( three sacks ) , featuring such a pass rush will be tough to replicate .
