It s an engaging way in which to relate a very familiar story , and the performers resist the urge to give it the full Cate Blanchett or Katharine Hepburn . What emerges is a tragic tale of two women who could , in another age , have been close friends and allies indeed , Elizabeth in an early letter to Mary refers to their knot of friendship . Instead , they fell foul of circumstances and their advisers . With religious war on the continent and Scottish nationalism on the rise , neither became puppets but both were undeniably buffeted in directions they might otherwise have avoided . Elizabeth never quite recovered from executing her rival Mary s son started a new dynasty . The debate over who won this grim struggle rages on .
