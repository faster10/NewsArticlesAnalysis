The Golden State Warriors made the San Antonio Spurs look ordinary on Monday . The Spurs , owners of the best defensive rating the NBA has seen in decades , were helpless against Stephen Curry and the Warriors ' brand of basketball mayhem . If not for the build up , you ' d have thought this was just another Golden State blowout : some highlights in the first quarter , a double digit lead in the second , a blowout by the third , stars rest in the fourth . For San Antonio , though , the 120 90 loss was its most lopsided in years .
