In Friday s 114 106 win over the Detroit Pistons , however , they gave a glimpse of just how good they can be when they play in harmony . For the first time all season , and just the ninth time since they came to be , each of them scored at least 20 points . Love led the way ( 29 points on 9 for 19 shooting including 5 for 7 from 3 with six rebounds and three assists ) , Irving was right behind him ( 28 points on 11 for 19 , four rebounds and two assists ) and James next ( 20 points on 7 for 16 , nine rebounds , eight assists ) .
