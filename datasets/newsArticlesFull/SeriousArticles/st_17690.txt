A local JMM leader and social activist , Jagdish Chowdhury , said , " In spite of being a historic place , it lacks most of the basic facilities like : adequate bathrooms , proper parking , sitting arrangements and accommodation facilities . Besides , rampant garbage near platform number 1 and 2 , where Netaji ' s idol has been installed clearly shows the apathy of the government authorities , " Chowdhury said adding that the local politicians including the former MLA , Mathura Prasad Mahto has demanded the authorities concerned to develop the junction as a historic place .
