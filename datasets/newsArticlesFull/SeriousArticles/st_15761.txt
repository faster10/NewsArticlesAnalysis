November 27 , 2015 EIRNS At the Queen Elizabeth led Commonwealth Heads of Government Meetings ( CHOGM ) at Malta , convened today , Canada s new Prime Minister , Justin Trudeau , has been set up as the spokesman to exert pressures to accept climate change diktats scheduled to be adopted at the November 30 to December 11 UN Climate Change conference in Paris . Many of our most vulnerable states and communities are already facing the adverse impacts of climate change , which can roll back decades of development gains for some it represents an existential threat . While renewables are becoming more reliable and cost competitive , they will probably never provide the baseload power we need to keep the lights on .
