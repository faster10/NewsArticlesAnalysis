The event should have been a breeze for Angela Merkel . The German chancellor was giving a speech on the importance of funding science at a new physics research center in the eastern city of Halle ? no problem for a physicist turned politician . But a few minutes into her address , Merkel was confronted by a heckler demanding a change in Germany s stance on refugees . Though the chancellor kept her cool , thanking the heckler and telling him , I will stand by my responsibility , the incident highlights the increasing hostility she faces over her open door policy on migrants .
