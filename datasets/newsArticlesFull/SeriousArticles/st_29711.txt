An airspeed restriction begins at a 60 mile radius from the center of Reagan National Airport . At the 30 mile radius , all aircraft must file a flight plan that identifies itself to air traffic control via a specific four digit transponder code ( a transponder is an electronic communication device that identifies a specific airplane on an air traffic controller ' s display screen to indicate authorization for the flight ) . Aircraft must enter the SFRA through specific flight " gates " that are displayed on a standard aviation map .
