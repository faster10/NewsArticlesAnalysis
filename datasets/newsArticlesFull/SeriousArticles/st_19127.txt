The evacuation plan aims to get the PM out of any possible crisis including an aerial attack . An aircraft with a hefty remote security cover will be on standby in Turkey for Modi . It will be equipped with advanced radar and warning system , a separate defence and communication systems . This will be different from the second plane which is already on standby for PM in case there is a problem in the Air India Jumbo Jet used currently by Modi . The summit is expected to draw more than 12 , 000 visitors . Modi ' s revised cover alone is going to be 1000 strong including the visible and hidden cordons . Indian agents from foreign units of RAW and IB have already sanitised the venues and hotel to be visited by Modi .
