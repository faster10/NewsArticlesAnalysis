Earlier , Nisar Khuhro told the house that some 144 , 595 jobless persons had them registered with employment exchange from January 1 , 2011 to December 31 , 2013 , of which 10 , 668 were provided with jobs . He said that the joblessness was a national problem and was not confined to the province alone . He said the power shortage had badly affected the industrial sector and scaled down their output . Trade sector is also confronted with the same problem , he said , adding that the government did not have the capacity to employee every jobless person .
