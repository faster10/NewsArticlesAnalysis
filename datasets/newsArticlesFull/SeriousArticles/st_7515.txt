ALL ABOARD CHRIS CHRISTIE S EMOTIONAL ROLLER COASTER . Chris Christie s ability to captivate his audiences on an emotional level is at the heart of his strategy to move voters into his camp , with the campaign now seeking to capitalize on some of their candidates most powerful moments with a new video series titled True Heart , which is named after Christie s answer to a debate question in which the candidates were asked what their Secret Service name would be . The first video , released this week , features an exchange Christie had with an Iowan man who suffered from a severe stroke and aphasia . I can see in your eyes the frustration and the sadness that this brings to you and it touches my heart , Christie tells the afflicted man in a video released by the campaign from a Coralville , Iowa , town hall .
