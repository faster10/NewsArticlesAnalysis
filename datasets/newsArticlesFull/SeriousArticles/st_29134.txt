Aiming at the renewal mechanism of its members , the Academy also announced changes to its voting procedures , with each new member s voting status only lasting 10 years , and renewed if the member has been active in motion pictures during that time . Members will receive lifetime voting rights if they have won or been nominated for an Academy Award , or have been renewed for three 10 year terms . Currently , about a third of the board members are women and Isaacs is its only African American .
