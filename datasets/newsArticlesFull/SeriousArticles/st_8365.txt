Lawrence Phillips , an imprisoned former NFL running back who was awaiting trial on charges of killing his cellmate last year , died Wednesday , January 13 , after being found unresponsive in his prison cell , the California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation said . The death of Phillips , 40 , is being investigated as a suicide , the department said . Phillips was sent to a California prison in 2008 after being convicted of domestic violence , false imprisonment and vehicle theft charges . While serving a 31 year sentence , authorities say , he killed his cellmate in April of last year . A trial was anticipated in Kern County , California .
