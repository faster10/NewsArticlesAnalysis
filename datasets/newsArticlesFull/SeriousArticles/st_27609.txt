At Blue Sky Studios in Connecticut , the talent charged with shepherding Peanuts to the screen had an in house nickname for the lines that supply each character s eye expressions : periwinkles ( as in , pair of wrinkles ) . And what these artists quickly realized is that if the eyes or noses are even slightly off , they don t read correctly to the viewer . Because these characters are so hyper familiar to several generations , getting the adapted proportions even a hair off could prove creative fatal .
