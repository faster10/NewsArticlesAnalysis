The spreading Isis menace is not confined to the Middle East . Reports suggest a growing contest for status , recruits and influence in Afghanistan , pitting Isis against al Qaida and the Taliban . A similar evolution is taking place in Bangladesh , one of the world s most populous Muslim countries . A recent spate of random machete attacks on westerners has exacerbated worries about the murders of secular bloggers . Although Dhaka officials blame local Islamists , there are well founded fears that Isis ( as well as al Qaida ) is organising there , and among India s large Muslim minority .
