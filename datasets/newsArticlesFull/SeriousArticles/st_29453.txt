Wednesday s announcement that General Electric Co . will relocate its world headquarters from Connecticut to Boston says a lot about the city s expanding reputation as a magnet for innovation and technology . It s becoming known as a place where businesses aimed at the future need to establish a presence . The planned move is also a major coup for Boston Mayor Martin J . Walsh and Massachusetts Governor Charlie Baker . Both vigorously courted the 300 billion company , which was also considering locations in other states .
