From one perspective the heat was off Zidane on his managerial debut , for which his old No 5 shirt was back on sale on the stadium shop . Selling his credentials to the dressing room will have taken him nano seconds . Persuading the fans of his love for the club was also a quick job . Which left only the question of how long his aristocratic disposition will survive the ravages of player disgruntlement , presidential whim and the outside pressure inflicted by Atletico and Barcelona . The Real board , of course , view the world as if these formidable rivals barely exit . To them , glory is a birthright .
