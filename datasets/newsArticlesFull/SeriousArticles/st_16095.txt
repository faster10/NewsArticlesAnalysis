Police officers inspect cars passing the ' Europa Bridge ' at the French German border crossing between Kehl and Strasbourg , France , 14 November 2015 . French President Hollande has declared a state of emergency after at least 120 people were killed in terrorist attacks in Paris , France , on 13 November 2015 . Security had been stepped up since Friday , November 13 just prior to the attacks due to an increased risk of terrorist attacks . In addition , the French government had decided to reinstate border controls prior to the forthcoming Paris Climate Summit to be held beginning on 30 November 2015 .
