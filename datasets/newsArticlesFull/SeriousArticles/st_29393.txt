Walgreens announced Tuesday it plans to buy rival Rite Aid for 9 . 41 billion in cash . The deal is worth 17 . 2 billion including acquired debt . Walgreens and Rite Aid are the second and third largest drugstore chains by sales in the United States , with a combined total of almost 13 , 000 stores . Walgreens said in a statement that " initially " Rite Aid stores will keep their names , but the company plans to create " a fully harmonized portfolio of stores . " The deal still has to be approved by the U . S . Federal Trade Commission , to ensure the merger complies with antitrust laws .
