Kim noted the significance of the timing of the test as being held in the year of the party congress , " which will be a historic turning point in accomplishing the revolutionary cause of Juche , " according to KCNA . Juche is the North ' s home grown state ideology that combines Marxism and extreme nationalism established by the state founder and the current leader ' s grandfather , Kim Il Sung . KCNA said Kim made the comments on a visit to the country ' s Ministry of the People ' s Armed Forces .
