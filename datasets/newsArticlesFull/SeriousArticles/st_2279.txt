In the wake of recent violence in Paris and San Bernardino , California , the two ascendant presidential candidates have been jockeying with each other to appear as the candidate most prepared to be commander in chief . Cruz has made that effort while attempting to seek a middle ground within his party . In an interview last week , he suggested that Rubio favored military adventurism , and he bashed the more aggressive Washington neo cons , remarks that drew ire from the conservative Wall Street Journal editorial board and from more hawkish Republicans .
