Derek Harvey , a former intelligence officer who specialized in terror networks in Iraq and Afghanistan , told us Snowden ' s disclosures were far more valuable to states like Russia and China than to terrorist groups like the Islamic State . " The general tradecraft vulnerabilities exposed in how terrorists were being monitored and exploited were well known to the sophisticated members of the terrorist and criminal communities , " he said . While he was still in Hong Kong , Snowden provided to the South China Morning Post the IP addresses of machines in China that were targeted by the NSA . This was an intelligence coup for the Chinese government , but of no value to terrorists like the Islamic State .
