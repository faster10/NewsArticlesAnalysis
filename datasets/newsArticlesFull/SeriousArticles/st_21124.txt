Chinese foreign minister Wang Yi has warned the US not to " make trouble out of nothing " in the disputed South China Sea amid reports that the American navy plans to send its ships close to islands claimed by China . The strong remarks followed recent remarks by US defence secretary Ash Carter during his Australia tour that " make no mistake , the United States will fly , sail and operate wherever international law allows , as we do around the world , and the South China Sea will not be an exception " .
