With respect to the first issue , it is worth keeping in mind at the start that there are different kinds of Christians , and different kinds of Christianity . Ted Cruz appeals most to the sorts one might describe as " the church militant . " His is a fighting kind of Christianity , that favors hitting back hard against Islamic extremism abroad and aggressive secularism here at home . That kind of Christianity is often jarring to those raised in the " God is love " kind of Christian faith that is commonly found among mainline Protestant denominations , and in many American Catholic congregations since the 1960s .
