Paolo Benetti , Ranieri ' s right hand man at Juventus , Roma , Internazionale and Monaco , appears to be the only major addition to the backroom team , adding invaluable European expertise to an increasingly international dressing room . " It is to the manager ' s credit that he did not just come in with all his own people and change absolutely everything , " Danish goalkeeper Kasper Schmeichel told the BBC this week . " He saw that he had a team and a style of play that was working quite well , and he also inherited a backroom team that were very good at what they did too and who knew the players already . "
