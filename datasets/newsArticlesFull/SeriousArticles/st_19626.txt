The Three Mile Island Nuclear Plant is seen in the early morning hours March 28 , 2011 in Middletown , Pennsylvania . The largest source of clean , sustainable and reliable energy available remains nuclear power . But to play a bigger role in reducing carbon emissions and air pollution , future advanced reactors must address four core challenges : They must be safer . They have to produce less radioactive waste . They need to pose lower risk of weapons proliferation . And they need to be less expensive .
