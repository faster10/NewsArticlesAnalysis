The relationship between sport and that song reached its apex at London 2012 . Watching Danny Boyle s magnificent opening ceremony you felt the whole occasion was gearing up to these five words : Ladies and Gentlemen David Bowie . It was all set up for the Dame . But despite Boyle s earnest entreaties , the man who best encapsulated Britain s creative heritage absented himself from the event . Instead of a personal appearance we were left with his recorded output . But that was more than enough .
