For much of the new year , Trump has hit Ted Cruz on everything from immigration and his ties to Goldman Sachs to speculation that he might not be eligible for the presidency based on his Canadian birth . In a new ad released on Friday , Trump launched an attack on the Texas senator ' s record on immigration , painting him as pro immigration reform and pro amnesty . Cruz , meanwhile , seared Trump , accusing him of being part of the system he purports to disown , referencing his opponent ' s support of eminent domain policies , which he called " a wonderful thing " in an interview with Fox News ' Bret Baier in October .
