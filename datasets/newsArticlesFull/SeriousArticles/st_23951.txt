The return to full fitness of Paul George is an exciting prospect for the Indiana Pacers and lovers of the NBA . The 25 year old missed the majority of last season after suffering a horrific broken right leg during a World Cup practice session with the USA team . The small forward is one of a few players in the league capable of containing the threat of James and his form in pre season suggests he ' s back to his best . Expect Indiana to at least back in the play offs with the two time All Star leading the charge .
