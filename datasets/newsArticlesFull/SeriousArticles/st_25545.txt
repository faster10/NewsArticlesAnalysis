Poor manufacturing practices and a lack of oversight lead to some contamination of alternative medicine . Other medicines , including traditional Chinese , Ayurvedic , and Mexican remedies , may be formulated with lead on the theory that heavy metals are therapeutic . The consequences are predictable : Kids have developed acute lead poisoning after taking Mexican remedies such as azarc n and greta , and the Indian remedy ghasard . A 2015 study found that 40 percent of a sample of 115 American adherents of Ayurvedic medicine tested positive for elevated BLLs .
