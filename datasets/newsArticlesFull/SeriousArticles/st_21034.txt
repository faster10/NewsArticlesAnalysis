Obama : Republicans can t handle CNBC : President Barack Obama mocked the Republican candidates who have called him weak on the world stage , suggesting they can t handle a bunch of CNBC moderators . In a reference to the debate held by the business network , Obama said in New York on Monday : Let me tell you , if you can t handle those guys then I don t think the Chinese and the Russians are going to be too worried about it . As the Washington Post writes , several of the Republican candidates have complained about the questions posed by the moderators and have demanded the Republican National Committee and networks abide by a set of changes to debate rules .
