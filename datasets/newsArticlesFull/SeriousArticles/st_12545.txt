Burress , 38 , who made the Super Bowl XLII winning catch that toppled the Patriots in 2008 and then accidentally shot himself in the leg nine months later which led to his first prison stint , appeared before Judge Pedro Jimenez Jr . in Mercer County ( New Jersey ) Superior Court Monday . Burress pleaded guilty to charges of failing to pay 46 , 000 in taxes on his 2013 return when he received 1 million in income according to reports . Burress last played in the NFL for the Steelers ( his original team ) during the 2012 13 season .
