( Boston , MA , 01 18 16 ) The annual Martin Luther King , Jr . Memorial Breakfast is Boston ' s premier and the nation ' s longest running event dedicated to the memory of Dr King . Speakers included Massachusetts Governor Charlie Baker , Boston Mayor Martin Walsh , U . S . Senator Elizabeth Warren , U . S . Senator Ed Markey , and Massachusetts Attorney General Maura Healey . Dr Ruth Simmons , president emerita of Brown University was the keynote speaker for the 2016 Breakfast . Governor Baker poses with some of the musicians from the Celestial Infernos a steel drum band from St . Cyprian ' s Episcopal Church in Boston .
