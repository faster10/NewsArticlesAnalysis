DELBENE TO PUSH ECPA ON COMMITTEE AGENDA ? The House Judiciary Committee is set to vote today on its Budget Views and Estimates , which is its broad agenda for fiscal year 2017 . Rep . Suzan DelBene is planning to offer an amendment to formally add the Email Privacy Act to that agenda . While the bill , which would update the 1986 Electronic Communications Privacy Act , isn t currently listed , a committee aide said the Budget Views and Estimates document is not an exhaustive list of what the committee plans to take up over the next year .
