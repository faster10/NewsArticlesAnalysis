Mohammed Emwazi ? Jihadi John ? is almost certainly dead , killed by a US drone strike in a joint British American operation in Raqqa , Syria . While nothing can bring back his victims , Emwazi s fate will surely bring a degree of closure for the families of David Haines , Alan Henning and the others whom he killed . Yet while Americans openly revelled in the killing by US special forces of Osama bin Laden , there are many in Britain who feel a nagging sense of unease about the manner of Emwazi s death .
