One did not have to look far in Rod Laver Arena on Thursday for signs of Johanna Konta s Antipodean lineage . In the players box was her elder sister Ava , flanked by fianc Shane Mumford , an Australian Rules star for the Sydney Swans . Courtside cognoscenti muttered excitedly that Konta was the first Australian born semi finalist in Melbourne since Wendy Turnbull in 1981 , which took distinction as quite possibly the most straw clutching piece of trivia ever contrived . In the land of her birth , a sense lingered that she was the one who got away .
