British sources say a proposal for an emergency brake that would allow EU member states to request that the number of EU migrants is slowed down if public services face too much pressure is in the mix . Whitehall sources suggested earlier this month that this was being seen in Downing Street as a rabbit out of the hat that could trump the prime minister s proposed four year ban on EU migrants claiming in work benefits . But Britain may struggle to shape the proposal in a way that would allow the prime minister to say he has a mechanism to bring down EU migration .
