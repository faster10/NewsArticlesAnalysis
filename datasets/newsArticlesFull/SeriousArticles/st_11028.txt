Too many things happened during Game 1 of the World Series between the New York Mets and the Kansas City Royals on Tuesday . ( Technically , too many things happened on Tuesday and Wednesday , as the game went to 14 innings and into a second day . ) The evening started with tragedy when news broke that Royals pitcher Edinson Volquez s father had died from a longtime heart condition right before Volquez was due to start in the opening game . After the game got underway with Volquez still ready to take the mound ? there were contradictory reports as to whether or not he was informed of his father s death before the game ? things got very strange from the very first pitch .
