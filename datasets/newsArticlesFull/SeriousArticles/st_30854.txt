A provincial source , speaking on condition of anonymity , told Arabic language al Sumaria satellite television network on Sunday that Daesh extremists kidnapped 78 educators across the province after they rejected Daesh self proclaimed education body and its radical curriculum . Ramadi is the capital of Anbar province with a predominantly Sunni population which has been traditional stronghold of IS ideology and probably was the most significant Iraqi city under IS control , after IS capital Raqqa which falls in Syria .
