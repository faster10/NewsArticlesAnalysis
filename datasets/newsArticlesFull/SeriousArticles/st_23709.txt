This undated image provided by the U . S . Army shows Sgt . Bowe Bergdahl , who had been held by insurgents in Afghanistan since 2009 . The White House announced Bergdahl ' s release on May 31 , 2014 . Bergdahl was released in exchange for five senior Taliban members held by the U . S . military . In March , the U . S . military charged Bergdahl with one count each of " Desertion with Intent to Shirk Important or Hazardous Duty , " and " Misbehavior Before The Enemy by Endangering the Safety of a Command , Unit or Place .
