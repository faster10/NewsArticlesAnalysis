Cynics might argue that U2 are trying to insert themselves into a tragedy that isn t theirs but the truth is , terrorism was embedded into each member s DNA before the group had even formed . As explored in the first gut wrenching part of the iNNOCENCE eXPERIENCE Tour , the sectarian violence that plagued both Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland during the 1970s was a part of their everyday experience . As such , they spoke to the tragic events of November 13 with what is now an increasingly rare compassion that embraced both sides .
