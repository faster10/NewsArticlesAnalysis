4 . He moved to Nashville in 2001 : Stapleton grew up in Kentucky , and before he devoted himself fully to music , he delivered pizzas during the day and played guitar gigs at night . I didn t know that songwriting could be an actual job , he explained to American Songwriter . I didn t realize people did that for a living . Then I met a songwriter named Steve Leslie , who heard some of my songs and thought they were pretty good , and invited me down to Nashville to try writing with him . The rest is history !
