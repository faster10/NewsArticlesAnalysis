An advance to a fifth Australian Open final ? even if it carries the danger of being the first man in the Open era to lose five finals at the same major without winning ? makes him , in his own words , very proud . So , too , does the fact that he and Jamie Murray have established themselves here as the only brothers to reach slam finals simultaneously .
