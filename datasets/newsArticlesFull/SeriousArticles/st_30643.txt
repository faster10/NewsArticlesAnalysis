Italian journalist Gianluigi Nuzzi speaks at the foreign press club to attend a press conference in Rome , Tuesday , Nov . 24 , 2015 . A Vatican tribunal on Tuesday rejected a journalist ' s request to dismiss charges against him for publishing confidential documents as a trial opened in the Holy See ' s latest leaks scandal . Journalists Emiliano Fittipaldi and Gianluigi Nuzzi are accused of having published books about Vatican waste , greed and mismanagement that were based in part on confidential Holy See documents .
