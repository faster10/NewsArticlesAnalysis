During the first few months of his candidacy , Carson made a few , ahem , highly questionable assertions The Pulitzer winning PolitiFact has rated 17 statements the neurosurgeon has made and deemed none ? zero ? to be even partially true . But his most frowned upon quotes delighted the base , including his belief that no Muslim was fit to serve as president ? an ironic statement by a man whose own ancestors were barred from voting in many places until the 1960s . Despite other deeply dubious claims ( German Jews could have avoided annihilation if laws had allowed them to carry handguns , he argued , again contradicting serious historians ) his trustworthiness ratings dwarfed those of any other Republican candidates .
