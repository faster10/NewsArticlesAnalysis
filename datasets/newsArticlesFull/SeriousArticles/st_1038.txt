A few examples : " Whiz Kid " Ken Rosewall stepping out of the amateur ranks to challenge long time pro champion Pancho Gonzales at Kooyong Stadium in 1957 . Eighteen years later , aging Grand Slam king Rod Laver taking on young gun Jimmy Connors for the " Heavyweight Championship of Tennis " at Caesar ' s Palace in Las Vegas . Thirteen years after that , the " Las Vegas traffic light , " Andre Agassi , showing no respect for old man Connors in a U . S . Open quarterfinal . In 2000 , 20 year old Marat Safin blasting groundstrokes at all time major champion Pete Sampras in the final at Flushing Meadows .
