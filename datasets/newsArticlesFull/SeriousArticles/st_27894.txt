Little Big Town took home song of the year and single of the year for Girl Crush , a terrific ballad about jealousy that became a flashpoint for controversy earlier this year after a report alleged that radio stations weren t playing the song because of its perceived lesbian overtones . Around the same time , the conversation about women in country music reached a fever pitch when radio programmer Keith Hill made his now infamous tomato comments ( they were referred to in the monologue last night ) , and Girl Crush , which features lead vocals from Karen Fairchild and was written by three women ? Liz Rose , Lori McKenna and Hillary Lindsey ? became a song that people wanted to see succeed , both to show country s support for women and to demonstrate LGBT tolerance .
