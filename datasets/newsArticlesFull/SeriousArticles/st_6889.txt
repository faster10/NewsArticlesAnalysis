Mannequins stand behind a broken window at a bridal boutique damaged by a car bomb explosion and attack that killed at least 18 people , in the commercial area of New Baghdad , Iraq , Tuesday , Jan 12 , 2016 . Gunmen stormed the shopping mall on Monday after setting off a car bomb and launching a suicide attack at the entrance . Touring the bombed mall on Tuesday , Iraq ' s Prime Minister Haider al Abadi described the attack as a " desperate attempt " by militants after they lost control of the key western city of Ramadi .
