The MSCI All Country World Index ended the first three days of 2016 lower by 3 . 3 percent , as U . S . stocks fell to a three month low and emerging market shares dropped to the cheapest since 2009 . Brent crude plunged to its lowest level since 2004 , while US oil fell below US 34 a barrel as supplies at a hub rose to a record . The dollar pared gains after Federal Reserve meeting minutes . Developing nation currencies sank to an all time low . Yields on 10 year Treasury notes dropped seven basis points to 2 . 17 percent .
