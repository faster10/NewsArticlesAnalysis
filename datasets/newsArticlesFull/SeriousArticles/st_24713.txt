The two Koreas remain in a technical state of war after their 1950 53 conflict ended in a truce , not a peace treaty , and the United States has about 28 , 500 troops based in South Korea . Earlier on Sunday , North Korea ' s state news agency KCNA quoted Kim as saying no one had the right to criticise its nuclear tests . " The DPRK ' s H bomb test . . . is a self defensive step for reliably defending the peace on the Korean Peninsula and the regional security from the danger of nuclear war caused by the US led imperialists , " it quoted Kim as saying .
