Nostalgia for anyone over 50 a horror show for anyone under 20 . The Back in Time strand , presented by Giles Coren , squeezes another series out of its formula of transporting families back to the last century in order to see how enormously life has changed in recent decades . Here the Ashby Hawkins family ? IT consultant and breadwinner Steph , house husband Rob , 16 year old Daisy and 12 year old Seth ? must forswear all modern technology and luxury to learn just how limiting life was in the Britain of the Fifties .
