At a congressional hearing Thursday in front of the House Oversight Committee , the State Department s Assistant Secretary for Legislative Affairs , Julia Frifield , argued essentially that her department is overwhelmed by requests from Congress . She pointed out that in 2015 alone , the State Department arranged 536 congressional and staff trips overseas , 2 , 500 briefings , handled more than 5 , 000 constituent cases for members of Congress , testified at 168 hearings , answered 1 , 700 congressional letters and provided hundreds of thousands of pages of documents .
