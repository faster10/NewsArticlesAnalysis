It was unwise to risk this country s relationship with one of its most important Gulf partners in order to avoid some bad headlines . Of course , it is always tempting to remind countries such as Saudi Arabia that in a democracy we can say what we want and they should just put up with it . But governments must also pay heed to wider strategic considerations , and avoid wilfully offending the sensibilities of key allies . This was , after all , the basis on which President Xi of China was welcomed to Britain on a state visit last week , something that did not go unnoticed in Riyadh .
