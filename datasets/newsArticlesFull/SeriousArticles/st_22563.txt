Amid the ' intolerance ' debate , Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Tuesday said any incident of " atrocity " is a " blot " on the society as well as the nation whose " pain " should be felt by all and asserted that unity and harmony is the only way to take the country forward . He said no questions can be raised over patriotism of any of the 125 crore Indians and nobody needs to produce a " certificate of patriotism " , a statement that assumes significance as some people have make controversial remarks like asking dissenting people to ' go to Pakistan ' .
