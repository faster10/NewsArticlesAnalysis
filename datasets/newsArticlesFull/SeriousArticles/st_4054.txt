Male politicians routinely pander to female voters with talk about women s issues , a catch all for anything having to do with abortion or economic empowerment . The script often includes a paternalistic anecdote about how much a candidate loves his sisters , mothers , wives and daughters . But using such tactics to check the woman box marginalizes both halves of the population . It treats women as a monolithic group , even as it excludes men by relegating them to bystander status in the fight for gender equality .
