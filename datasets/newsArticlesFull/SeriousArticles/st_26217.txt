Jim Schwartz carried off the field in Detroit : In one of the more bizarre moments of last season , linebacker Ty Powell and others carried Schwartz , the former Lions ' coach who was then Buffalo ' s defensive coordinator , off the field following the Bills ' 17 14 win on Oct . 5 , 2014 . Schwartz had downplayed the significance of the game when speaking to reporters beforehand , but afterward , players revealed that he told them during organized team activities in the spring that he wanted to be carried off the Ford Field turf if he won in his return . He got his wish .
