The jihadi menace also struck terror into the heart of one of the world s greatest cities , reducing Paris to tears and France to a country seized by an open ended emergency . The jihadi menace lives in the minds of alienated , radicalised young Muslim men and women it is rooted in the deprived hinterlands of Brussels and other European and Arab capitals it spreads furtively across the internet and social media and it feeds on ongoing violence from Libya to Yemen to Bangladesh . Most of all , it thrives amid Syria s agony .
