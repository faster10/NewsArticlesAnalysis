Amid the unrelenting campaign , rumours were floated about King Salman s health , although the early 2016 upsurge took on a particularly bizarre twist . Carefully orchestrated narratives appeared to help mere mortals gain insights into the man in charge of the most dangerous man in the world . The reference to Deputy Crown Prince Mohammad Bin Salman , who is Saudi Arabia s Minister of Defence , was ugly , though Mohsen Milani topped the rant catalogue in a Council on Foreign Relations essay that concluded that the Kingdom was desperate even if the adjective was both inaccurate and frantic .
