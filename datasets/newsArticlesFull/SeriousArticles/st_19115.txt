In the comedy skit , the president is asked about unrealized campaign promises , including closing Gitmo , combating global warming , allowing openly gay people to serve in the military , withdrawing from Iraq , improving the status of the fight in Afghanistan , reforming health care and several others . On screen the response on a check list was : that each item was " not done . " Humor with some truth in it is always dangerous . Make no mistake , a drumbeat of belittlement can damage a president .
