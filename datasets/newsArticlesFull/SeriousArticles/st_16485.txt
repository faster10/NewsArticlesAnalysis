When she had taken the elevator up the iconic tower shortly after 9 in the evening , the atmosphere was carnival like . Tourists were milling around and a couple of cops were chatting merrily . Half hour later , when she descended , terror was in the air . There were cops all around , barking instructions . People were running helter skelter . As she looked around bewildered , a cop approached her menacingly and shouted orders in French . " Can you tell me what has happened ? " she asked in English , only to receive an angry retort in more French .
