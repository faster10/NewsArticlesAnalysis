BEIJING , Nov . 14 ( Xinhuanet ) As the city of Antalya begins its final preparations for tomorrow ' s G 20 Leaders ' Summit , we take a look at what security measures are being put in place , what prospect there is for protests at the venue , and how the coastal city will benefit from the influx of visitors . Around 13 , 000 people are expected to attend the summit . This number includes a number of world leaders , 1500 press members , and the 1000 strong U . S . delegation . World leaders will include the Chinese President Xi Jinping , U . S . President Barack Obama , German Chancellor Angela Merkel , Russian President Vladimir Putin and Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe .
