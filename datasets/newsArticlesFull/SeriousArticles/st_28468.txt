China , Thailand , India and Singapore already have lucrative deals in place with Myanmar ' s military government . Neighboring Thailand depends heavily on Myanmar ' s offshore natural gas and hydroelectric dams to provide power to the Thai population . China has signed a deal to build a natural gas pipeline from the west coast of Myanmar into western China .
