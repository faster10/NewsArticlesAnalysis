Campaign insiders are abuzz about the possibility that the two candidates , who are running in opposite lanes of the primary , could team up to take down Rubio . The thinking ? Rubio is a competitor to both , making a play for the establishment vote Bush is seeking and the conservative support that Cruz is trying to lock up . Both have gone after Rubio in recent days . Bush has hammered the Florida lawmaker for missing Senate votes , while Cruz , during an interview on CNN last week , chose the ever loaded term moderate to label him .
