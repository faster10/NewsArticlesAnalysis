Notoriety : ? Khan was born in Saudi Arabia and raised in the United States by his Pakistani born parents . He founded Inspire magazine , an English language online publication that promoted al Qaeda s violent and intolerant ideology . The publication provided religious rationale and practical weapons instructions for followers around the world . Khan s article How to build a bomb in the kitchen of your mom provided a blue print for the type of pressure cooker bomb used in the 2013 Boston Marathon bombing by Tamerlan and Dzhokhar Tsarnaev . He was killed in the same drone strike that killed Awlaki .
