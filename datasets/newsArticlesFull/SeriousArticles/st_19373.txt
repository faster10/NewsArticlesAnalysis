Other teams tried to penetrate the Big Blue Monopoly : two different versions of New York Yankees , one New York Yanks , one Brooklyn Tigers , one Brooklyn Dodgers , even the Staten Island Stapletons . They all failed . The Giants played in the Polo Grounds when that was the signature address in New York sports , then switched to Yankee Stadium in 1956 , just in time for that palace to host some of the most important NFL games of the 50s and 60s . New York could sustain multiple baseball teams .
