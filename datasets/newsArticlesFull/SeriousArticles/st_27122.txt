The BND lists the areas in which Saudi Arabia is adopting a more aggressive and warlike policy . In Syria , in early 2015 , it supported the creation of The Army of Conquest , primarily made up of the al Qaida affiliate the al Nusra Front and the ideologically similar Ahrar al Sham , which won a series of victories against the Syrian Army in Idlib province . In Yemen , it began an air war directed against the Houthi movement and the Yemeni army , which shows no sign of ending . Among those who gain are al Qaida in the Arabian peninsula , which the US has been fruitlessly trying to weaken for years by drone strikes .
