Brent crude rose 0 . 1 to after Russia ' s energy minister said OPEC may meet to discuss output cuts . Crude oil was trading at over 34 a barrel in choppy trade on Friday . While the crude oil rally eased concern over the global economy temporarily , analysts are sceptical about whether it is sustainable . " I am worried about global market cues . Crude is likely to come down and if crude goes below 26 , that will have a very negative impact on the market , " said Alex Mathew , Head Research at Geojit BNP Paribas .
