Of those players who quickly moved past Hewitt , Federer is perhaps an unkind point of contrast with his balletic , low torque movements around the court . But its not just in aesthetic purity that we find sporting beauty . The bone jarring , ankle busting sheepdog routine that pushed Hewitt to the brink of physical meltdown was never pretty , but measured out across two decades there s still a kind of monumental grandeur to it . For Hewitt tennis has been an arduous solo voyage through every summit and valley that sport can present to one man .
