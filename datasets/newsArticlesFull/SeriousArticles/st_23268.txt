The 2012 BCS National Championship Game in New Orleans was the first and only title game of the BCS era to feature two teams from the same conference . After Alabama lost to LSU in overtime in their regular season matchup , the Crimson Tide shut down LSU and held the Tigers to only 92 yards of total offense . Norwood , who only had seven catches in the entire season , had four catches for a game high 78 yards . Alabama ' s passing success led to five field goals from Jeremy Shelley before Trent Richardson rushed for the only touchdown of the Crimson Tide ' s 21 0 win .
