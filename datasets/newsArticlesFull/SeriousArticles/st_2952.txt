China ' s leader diplomacy will start again at the beginning of the New Year . Chinese President Xi Jinping will visit Saudi Arabia , Egypt and Iran from January 19 to 23 . Xi ' s first visit of 2016 to the Middle East demonstrates China ' s high attention to the region . This visit will contribute to the strategic cooperation between China and the Middle East region and make " China ' s Voice " on the hot issues of Middle East heard by the world . It also will play a navigator role in enlarging China ' s " circle of friends " in the region and improving China ' s overall diplomatic arrangement in the New Year .
