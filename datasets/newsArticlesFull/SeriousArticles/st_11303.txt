In an interview with The Guardian , Abrahamson said that he used all of his knowledge to convince Emma Donoghue to let him direct the adaptation of her 2010 novel Room . Abrahamson wrote a 10 page letter touching on all the philosophical points Donoghue covers in the novel . The trick worked and how ! Abrahamson said the idea of keeping the narrative simple and drawing out the emotions in the film came to him from his children . He said they understood the shadows of the real life and chose not to engage with it .
