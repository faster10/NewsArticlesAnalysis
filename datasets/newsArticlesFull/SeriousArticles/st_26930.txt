The week ahead : Gov . Bruce Rauner and the four legislative leaders are scheduled to meet at the Capitol on Tuesday afternoon . The state ' s political leaders are expected to make statements in public and then retreat behind closed doors to talk . The symbolism is expected to be greater than the substance . The governor and lawmakers have failed to put in a spending plan in place for the budget year that started July 1 . The Illinois House also is in Tuesday , but the Senate is taking a wait and see approach before deciding whether to return to the Capitol before 2016 rolls around .
