Gregor Golland , an NRW state lawmaker from Merkel s CDU , said the state government , led by the Greens and SPD , more or less laughed at his party s attempts to get the problem of north African criminals on the agenda in 2014 and 2015 . They acted as if it did not exist , he said . Hans Willi Koerfges , an SPD lawmaker in NRW s parliament , denied his party had ignored the Antanzen phenomenon among north Africans , saying SPD members had expressed concern and it was already being looked into by the time the CDU raised it .
