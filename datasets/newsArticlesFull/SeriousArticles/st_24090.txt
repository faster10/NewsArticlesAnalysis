Vander Plaats endorsement for Cruz , which has long been expected , follows two other critical Iowa endorsements for the Texas senator . Last month , Rep . Steve King , an influential congressman from the conservative western part of the state , came out for Cruz , calling him the most substantive and electable of the conservative choices . And before that , Steve Deace , an Iowa radio host with a sizable following in the evangelical community , put his support behind the Texas senator . Vander Plaats , King and Deace are pillars of the evangelical establishment in Iowa , and offer Cruz ? who is already extensively organized in the state ? big networks of committed supporters likely to turn out on Feb . 1 .
