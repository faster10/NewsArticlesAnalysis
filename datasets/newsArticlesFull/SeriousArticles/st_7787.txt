Paris police shot and killed a man who tried to enter a police station with a knife and a fake suicide belt , CNN reports . The man was reportedly shouting " Allahu Akbar . " A printed image of the ISIS flag and an Arabic note claiming responsibility for the attack were found near the suspected attacker ' s body . The incident comes on the year anniversary of the Charlie Hebdo attacks , in which terrorists killed 12 people after storming the headquarters of the controversial French satirical newspaper Charlie Hebdo .
