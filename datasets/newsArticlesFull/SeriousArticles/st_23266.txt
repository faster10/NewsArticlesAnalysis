Jones filled in for the injured JT Barrett late in the Buckeyes ' win against Michigan . He started the next two games , the Big Ten Championship against Wisconsin and the Sugar Bowl against Alabama . Jones won both and led the Buckeyes to the National Championship game against Oregon and Heisman Trophy winner Marcus Mariota .
