1 . The loss was even worse than it looked for Les Miles . If the Tigers had bounced back from their back to back losses to Alabama and Arkansas with some early energy , re established their running game , and ultimately lost a nail biter on some strong Rebels play down the stretch , that would have been one thing . But the LSU on display on Saturday looked like every criticism of Miles ' teams come to life : a highly erratic passing game , a distinct lack of offensive creativity ( particularly when it came to using Harris ' s legs ) , defensive focus that came and went , and most notably some inexplicable late game sloppiness .
