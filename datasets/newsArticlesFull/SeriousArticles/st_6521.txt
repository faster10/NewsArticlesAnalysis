According to National AIDS Control Organisation ( NACO ) , there were 1 , 32 , 590 people living with HIV AIDS ( PLHA ) in Tamil Nadu in 2011 . UNAIDS estimates that new infections in India had come down by 57 per cent during the past decade from 2 . 74 lakh in 2000 to 1 . 16 lakh in 2011 . Wider access to anti retroviral therapy ( ART ) drugs in the third period of National AIDS Control Programme ( NACP ) from 2007 2012 has led to a 29 per cent reduction in annual AIDS related deaths in India , according to the U . N . agency .
