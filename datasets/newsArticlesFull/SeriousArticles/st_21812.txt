Muslims are bracing for retaliation : attacks on our mosques , curses at veiled women , assumptions made about the faces on the street that look Arab , black or Turkish ( and therefore most probably Muslim ) . To a certain extent the terrorists got what they wanted after the attacks on the Charlie Hebdo satirical magazine . The weeks that followed were a blur of anti Muslimism . Today , like then , as I write , newspapers report damage to mosques . In Pontivy , a fascist and anti Muslim rally took place on Saturday . A young French man of North African descent was assaulted and told to " go home . And in Marseille , a young woman wearing a headscarf was stabbed with a box cutter by a man who called her a " terrorist " .
