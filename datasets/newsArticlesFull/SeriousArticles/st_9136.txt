Including Ragland , Alabama might have four members of its front seven drafted in the first round of the 2016 NFL draft . Ragland has been arguably the nation ' s best linebacker all season , leading Alabama and ranking 14th in the SEC with 90 total tackles ( 51 solo ) . He has 6 . 5 tackles for loss with 2 . 5 sacks . But he gets help from a scary defensive line that houses possible first round picks A ' Shawn Robinson , Jonathan Allen ( team high 11 . 5 TFLs and nine sacks ) and Jarran Reed . That terrifying trio has registered 22 tackles for loss and 13 sacks this season .
