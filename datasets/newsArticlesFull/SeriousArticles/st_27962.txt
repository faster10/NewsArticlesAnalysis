GAME OF THE WEEKEND : No . 11 Virginia at No . 16 Louisville . The Cavaliers ( 16 4 , 5 3 ACC ) pulled off an improbable comeback to win at Wake Forest on Tuesday , keeping them amid a crowd in the top half of the league . Now they host the Cardinals ( 17 3 , 6 1 ) , who are alone in second place and have won four straight games . Another loss would sink the Cavs further back in the standings . In that Wake Forest win , Virginia coach Tony Bennett briefly scrapped his pack line defense in favor of a zone because " it shows you how desperate we were . . . . We just said , ' You never know . ' "
