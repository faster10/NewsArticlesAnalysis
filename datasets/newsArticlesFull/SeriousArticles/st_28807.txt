His big film break came in 1972 when Francis Ford Coppola cast him in the Oscar winning The Godfather . Vigoda played mafioso Sal Tessio , an old friend of Vito Corleone ' s ( Marlon Brando ) who hopes to take over the family after Vito ' s death by killing his son Michael Corleone ( Al Pacino ) . But Michael anticipates that Sal ' s suggestion for a " peace summit " among crime families is a setup and has him executed . Vigoda got to deliver one of the film ' s classic lines , when he mutters to consigliere Tom Hagen ( played by Robert Duvall ) as he is being led away to be killed : " Tell Mike it was only business . "
