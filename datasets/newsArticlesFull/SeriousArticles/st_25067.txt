NEW DELHI : The Opposition used its majority in the Rajya Sabha to prevail over the government into agreeing to Prime Minister Narendra Modi intervening in the debate on the making of the Constitution , as he did in the Lok Sabha . It managed to extend by debate by half a day to ensure that Modi speaks , hours after returning from the Climate Change convention in Paris on Tuesday . According to a source , Modi will reach Parliament House soon after landing on Tuesday morning and will express his views on the making of the Constitution as he did in the Lok Sabha last Friday .
