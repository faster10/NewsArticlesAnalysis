CLINTON CAMP HITS SANDERS ON WALL STREET REFORM ? The Clinton campaign moved Monday to get out ahead of a speech on Wall Street reform the Vermont senator is giving at Town Hall in NYC today , putting out a statement from former CFTC chair Gary Gensler who is now working on the campaign : Any plan to further reform our financial system must include strong provisions to tackle risks in the shadow banking sector , which remains a critical source of potential instability in our economy . This includes certain activities of hedge funds , investment banks like the now defunct Lehman Brothers , and insurance companies like AIG .
