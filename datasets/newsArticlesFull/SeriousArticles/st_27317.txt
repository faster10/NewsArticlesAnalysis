The Hollywood Foreign Press Association always likes to surprise with their winners in the TV show categories . Last year , it was Showtime ' s " The Affair " that took home the top prize for TV drama . This might finally be the year for the predictable winner . Featuring the biggest and most impressive ensemble cast on TV right now , " Game of Thrones " has just come off its best season yet . With incredible storytelling work by showrunners David Benioff and D . B . Weiss , the show is poised to win its second major award for its fifth season following the Emmy win for Outstanding Drama Series this year .
