The termination of the anti Iran sanctions with the recent implementation of the July 2015 nuclear deal between Tehran and world powers allows Iranian oil producers to re enter the market . But the deal s success should be judged by a single objective ? preventing Iran from getting a nuclear weapon . The nuclear accord was welcomed in Iran and in the western world , but Iran s Sunni neighbors and , of course , Israel were unhappy . This also means that across the world billions in frozen Iranian assets will become available .
