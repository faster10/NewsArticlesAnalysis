Mourning the dead : As the French observed three days of official mourning , ? ad hoc memorials grew and crowds gathered ? at the sites of the terrorist killings in Paris on Sunday . Prayers , services and marches were held in Denmark , Norway and Finland , according to the Associated Press . In Berlin , 500 people spontaneously began singing La Marseillaise ? after marking a minute of silence for the victims , AP reported .
