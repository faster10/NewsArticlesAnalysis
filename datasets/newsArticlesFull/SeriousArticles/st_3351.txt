A cluster of soldiers and security guards stood at the entrance to Sharm el Sheikh airport Saturday in a show of increased vigilance , screening drivers and passengers arriving in cars and buses . The Egyptian military also took control of registering stranded Russian passengers , according to Arkady Dvorkovich , the Russian deputy prime minister who is charge of the efforts to repatriate Russian from Egypt . He said that there were some 79 , 000 Russians who needed transportation home from there , from Cairo , and from the Red Sea resort of Hurghada .
