FILE In this file photo from Tuesday , Jan . 26 , 2016 , NFL Field Director Ed Mangan gestures while speaking to reporters about field preparation for Super Bowl 50 at Levi ' s Stadium in Santa Clara , Calif . Mangan , working his 27th Super Bowl , oversaw the installation of a brand new field earlier this month . The 75 , 000 square feet of grass from West Coast Turf in California ' s Central Valley was all in place by Jan . 12 and crew of about three dozen workers has been mowing , painting and protecting the field ever since .
