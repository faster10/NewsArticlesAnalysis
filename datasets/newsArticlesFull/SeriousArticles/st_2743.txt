Coming off both a shoulder injury and the second torn ACL of his career , Palmer miraculously finished the 2015 campaign fourth in the NFL with 4 , 671 passing yards , third in passer rating at 104 . 6 , second in passing touchdowns with 35 , and first with both 8 . 7 yards per attempt and an 82 . 1 QBR . He also led the Cardinals to the third most efficient passing offense in the league , per Football Outsiders ' DVOA ( Defense adjusted Value Over Average , which adjusts performance for down , distance , and opponent ) . Taking the combination of efficiency and explosiveness into account , the Arizona passing attack may very well have been the best in the league .
