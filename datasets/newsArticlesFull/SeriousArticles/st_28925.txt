Kerr said that Curry ' s range distorts the chess board , via CSN ' s Monte Pool , and there is not a more accurate way to describe it . Golden State ' s style might be best explained as an extreme form of attacking chess . Led by the most confident player in the NBA , the Warriors thrive in chaos , confusing and demoralizing opponents who seem a step or three behind . This effect is especially as pronounced at the raucous Oracle Arena , where the noise escalates even faster than the leads . The Spurs , despite their experience , leadership and usually crisp rotations , did not prove immune to it .
