Tomorrow I will attend the Vatican hearing : we shall try and see if we will have access to all the necessary means to defend ourselves , Fittipaldi said on Monday . This is not a trial against me , today the Vatican is putting on trial the freedom of the press , he said in a message on Facebook . But the Vatican has been known to come down harshly on journalists who are seen to be breaking rules , going so far as to revoke the accreditation of one journalist who published the pope s encyclical before it was officially released .
