Captain Sohail Baloch chairman JACPIAE held the Rangers personnel responsible for the deaths of his three colleagues . He said their protest would remain continued till the acceptance of their demands . However , the Rangers spokesman rejected the said allegation , saying that no Rangers personnel had fired at any protester to control the protest at JIAP . Advocate Javeed Mir on behalf of the JACPIAE had later submitted an application with a view to lodging an FIR of the said incident . Meanwhile , the PALPA Executive Committee announced that it , too , would join the protesters , saying that after the tragic killings , they had no choice but to instruct its members not to proceed to any airport of Pakistan till the next directive .
