Columbus , Georgia The Huey helicopter came in from the west , the " pop , pop , pop " of its blades announcing its arrival . It made a sharp right hand turn and flared to a landing in the grassy area . The ragtag troop of 10 passengers awaiting its arrival looked far from Army issue , dressed in Aloha shirts , T shirts , blue jeans , shorts and ball caps . They ducked their heads beneath the blades of the spinning rotor as they made their way to the copter from a protected area off to its front .
