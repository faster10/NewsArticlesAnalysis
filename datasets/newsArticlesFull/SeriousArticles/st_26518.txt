Trump bolsters N . H . lead : The New York Times writes that billionaire Donald Trump has bolstered his lead in New Hampshire , according to one poll , demonstrating his enduring popularity in the face of aggressive rivals . A survey from WBUR found Trump leading the field of Republican presidential hopefuls with the support of 22 of likely GOP primary voters . Ben Carson and Sen . Marco Rubio trail him , each with 11 . WBUR s poll from late October showed Trump leading Carson by just three percentage points . New Hampshire is the first state to hold presidential primaries .
