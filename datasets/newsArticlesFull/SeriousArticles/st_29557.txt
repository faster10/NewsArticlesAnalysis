The biggest monthly decline in output came from Iraq , the world ' s fastest growing source of supply growth last year . Exports from Iraq ' s main outlet , its southern terminals , have slipped from November ' s record level which had been boosted by delayed October cargoes , but are likely to reach new highs in the coming months , industry sources said . Shipments from Iraq ' s north by the Kurdistan Regional Government via Ceyhan in Turkey have edged lower , while those by Iraq ' s State Oil Marketing Organisation have remained at zero for a third month , the survey found .
