Interestingly , the state government move came within a few hours of Rajan leaving for India from Bali under the custody of CBI and a team of Mumbai police . Rajan had raised doubts over Mumbai police claim that a few in the force are hand in gloves with his rival and fugitive don Dawood Ibrahim . Bakshi defended the Mumbai police saying , " The Mumbai police have the capability to keep Rajan in their custody safe and all necessary arrangements in this regard had been made . . . There is also no question of having trust deficit in Mumbai police , the decision of handing over the case to CBI was taken under the provision of the UNTOC . "
