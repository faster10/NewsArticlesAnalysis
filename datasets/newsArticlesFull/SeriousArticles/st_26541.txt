We had watched how , during his first period in prison and , in the decade after his escape , his Sinaloa cartel had built up a drugs empire that was truly global , operating from the US to Europe to the Far East . His people established control over much of the supply of cocaine , heroin , marijuana and methamphetamine to North America . In Chicago , he was recorded establishing the wholesale price of kilos of heroin . The city awarded him the rare accolade of naming him Public Enemy No 1 , his only predecessor being Al Capone .
