There is a lot of work that needs to be done with the roster in Santa Clara , especially after a series of retirements following the 2014 NFL season . Building the team up through the draft and free agency is made easier by how much room under the salary cap that the franchise has for 2016 . This could make the team a real player in free agency , but could also help in acquiring a player or two through trades . While there are a lot of question marks regarding the team still , most of the San Francisco 49ers rumors from this offseason give the team a shot to rebound quickly .
