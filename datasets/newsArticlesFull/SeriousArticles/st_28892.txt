Defensively , Oklahoma has more to replace than originally expected with the surprising early departures of cornerback Zack Sanchez and linebacker Dominique Alexander to the NFL draft . But the secondary should still be the Sooners ' best in years with cornerback Jordan Thomas and safeties Ahmad Thomas and Steven Parker , who all received All Big 12 consideration this past year . Oklahoma also still boasts linebacker Jordan Evans as well as Charles Walker , who quickly emerged as one of the best defensive linemen in the league in 2015 .
