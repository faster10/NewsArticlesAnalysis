Chuck ( Paul Giamatti ) discovers that while he pursued Steven Birch ( Jerry O Connell ) , the rival Eastern District has made inroads in the Axe case , so he finagles to get control of a key witness . At the same time , Axe makes a play for Yum Time , a family owned bakery . Meanwhile , Wendy ( Maggie Siff ) faces a quandary when she discovers that one of the few female portfolio managers at Axelrod is about to have her career sabotaged . Mrs Axelrod ( Malin Akerman ) tries to suppress a tell all book about her husband ( Damian Lewis ) .
