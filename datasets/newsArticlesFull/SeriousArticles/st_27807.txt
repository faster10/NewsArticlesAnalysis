TOKYO : German automaker Volkswagen ( VW ) on Wednesday apologised for what many in the West have come to describe as ' Dieselgate ' ? or the scandal over emission fudging device in its diesel cars first detected by the US authorities ? and said the company is " doing all it can " to regain the trust of customers . " On behalf of our entire company I ' d like to apologize , " head of the company ' s passenger car division Herbert Diess said at the 44th Tokyo Auto Show that kicked off on Wednesday .
