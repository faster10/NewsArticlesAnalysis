Discussions focused on the preparations for the 2016 Warsaw and Brussels Conferences on Afghanistan and the latest regional security and economic developments . The U . S . and Afghanistan welcomed the decision taken by the European Union to host the Brussels Conference on Afghanistan on October 4 5 , 2016 . The three sides agreed to work together to support preparations for the Brussels Conference , and ensure that the efforts of the Afghan government and the donor community are closely aligned in the spirit of mutual accountability .
