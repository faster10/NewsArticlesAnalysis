Trade text coming soon : The Hill writes the Obama administration is preparing to release the text of the Trans Pacific Partnership trade deal , setting the stage for a host of policy fights . Obama is pressing lawmakers to back the deal , but several issues could slow its approval , the Hill says . Among them are a shorter period of exclusivity for pharmaceuticals than the industry had pressed for the lack of currency manipulation language that will satisfy some lawmakers and excluding the tobacco industry from certain legal protections for other agricultural sectors .
