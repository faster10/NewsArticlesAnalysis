The criticism is clearly a sensitive topic for Thompson , who noted that the Alliance for a Healthier Generation was among the first organizations that approached the company to use its " marketing might " to help kids . The group , which is working with McDonald ' s on its new health goals , was founded by the Clinton Global Initiative and the American Heart Association . " Others have said , ' McDonald ' s , don ' t advertise to kids , ' " Thompson said . " We ' ve always felt like , wow , if we had the impact that you think we have , why don ' t we leverage it to do something great for kids ? " Howell Wechsler , CEO of Alliance for a Healthier Generation , said the commitments made by McDonald ' s were worked out over the course of more than a year . He said the final agreement could help change the way people eat over time , given McDonald ' s enormous reach .
