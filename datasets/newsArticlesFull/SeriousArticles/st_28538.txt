Sunday night Djokovic will attempt to win a sixth Australian Open , a record held by Roy Emerson . He reaches his 19th Grand Slam final , putting him behind only Federer ( 28 ) and Rafael Nadal ( 20 ) as far as major final appearances . Djokovic s current streaks are otherworldly , as well : 13 straight matches ( dating back to London in November ) 20 consecutive Grand Slam match wins ( dating back to the Roland Garros final ) and 37 of 38 overall matches ( dating back to the start of the U . S . Open ) .
