1 . Motivated by Rambo movies : Have you seen the headband Landry wears under his helmet ? Landry got the idea this offseason from Sylvester Stallone ' s " Rambo " movies . Landry said the headband represents a warrior mindset every time he steps on the field . When watching the movies , Landry was amazed how Stallone was just " John " initially and transformed into the dominant force " Rambo . " Landry says he mentally envisions the same transformation when he puts on the headband before games and practices .
