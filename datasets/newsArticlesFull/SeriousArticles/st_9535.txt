There are eight college football conference championship games on tap this week , and the biggest favorites on the opening board are the Alabama Crimson Tide as they look to lock down a spot in the College Football Playoff . The Crimson Tide opened as 17 point favorites against the Florida Gators for the SEC Championship Game at the Georgia Dome on Saturday , with Alabama at 11 1 straight up and 7 5 against the spread on the season . Alabama is 4 0 both SU and ATS in its last four games against Florida , doubling them up 42 21 in their last meeting back in September 2014 .
