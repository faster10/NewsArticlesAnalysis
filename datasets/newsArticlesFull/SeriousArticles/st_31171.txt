But the Bolsa Familia program also provided a notable political advantage . The program was created by Rousseff ' s political party , the PT , the brainchild of her presidential predecessor and mentor , Luiz Ignacio " Lula " da Silva , and quickly generated credibility and support among the poor . Because of Rousseff ' s other anti poverty programs , such as Zero Fome ( Zero Hunger ) , which expanded food benefits to the poor , and the Brasil Sem Mis ria ( Brazil without Destitution ) program , the PT gained a reputation for meeting the poor ' s needs .
