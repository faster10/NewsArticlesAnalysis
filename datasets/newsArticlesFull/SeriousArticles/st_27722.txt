In some ways the Panthers had little choice but to not re sign Hardy when he became a free agent following the 2014 season . The moment a Charlotte judge convicted him of assaulting a female and making threats he was toxic to the only organization he had ever known . He missed most of 2014 as he sat on the commissioner s exempt list and faced a league suspension for part of 2015 despite his conviction being overturned when his accuser failed to show at an appeals trial amid rumors they were working out a financial settlement .
