COSTS PROMPT WHITE HOUSE TO REJECT PENTAGON PLAN TO CLOSE GITMO : The Wall Street Journal reports on the Pentagon s estimated 600 million plan to close the U . S . military prison : The Pentagon s latest cost estimate for closing the prison at Guantanamo Bay , Cuba , and building an alternative in the U . S . topped a half billion dollars , prompting the White House to reject it and send the plan back for revisions , according to administration officials . The cost dispute marked a setback for a top priority of President Barack Obama , and helps account for the administration s delay in submitting a plan to Congress for closing Guantanamo .
