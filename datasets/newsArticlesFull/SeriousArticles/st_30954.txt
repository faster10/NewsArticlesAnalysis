The most hyped film of 2015 is undoubtedly Star Wars The Force Awakens . As its release date gets nearer , Star Wars fans are going berserk to watch their favourite stars Harrison Ford and Carrie Fisher making a comeback on the big screen as Han Solo and General Leia Organa respectively . With this film , JJ Abrams will be introducing new stars to Hollywood Daisy Ridley , John Boyega , Adam Driver and Oscar Isaac . Though they are not exactly unfamiliar faces , they can ' t really be counted among the biggest stars of Hollywood .
