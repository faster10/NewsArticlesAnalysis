Nepal asks India to lift undeclared blockade : Nepal s prime minister has asked neighboring India to lift an undeclared blockade , saying the Himalayan nation is grappling with a severe fuel shortage and trouble obtaining medicine and food supplies blocked at the border . Prime Minister Khadga Prasad Oli said in a televised address that Nepal is facing a humanitarian crisis . Nepal has been struggling with severe fuel shortages for two months because India has restricted imports . India is unhappy with Nepal s new constitution , which was adopted in September .
