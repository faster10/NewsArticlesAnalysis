NOTED : SANDERS NARROWS CLINTON S NATIONAL LEAD TOO . Bernie Sanders is narrowing Hillary Clinton s national lead less than two weeks before the crucial Iowa caucuses , according to a new poll released Tuesday . ABC S RYAN STRUYK reports the Monmouth University poll shows Clinton with 52 percent support from Democratic primary voters nationwide and Sanders with 37 percent .
