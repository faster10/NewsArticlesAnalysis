Konta s poise in the face of such questions has been of a piece with her extraordinarily serene advance to the last four at the Australian Open , where she had never been beyond the second round of qualifying in three previous attempts . Even as she prepared to walk on court to face Angelique Kerber , she looked remarkably unruffled , chatting to officials about the International Women s Day ceremony staged beforehand . Only once the match began did she seem discomfited by the occasion and the opponent . Kerber did nothing spectacular in reeling off the first three games , but Konta was a tightly wound bundle of anxiety , shanking forehands and missing lines every which way .
