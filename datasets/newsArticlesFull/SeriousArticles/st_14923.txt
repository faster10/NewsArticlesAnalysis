As the cannons disgorged confetti and the strains of " Tiger Rag " echoed across the orange painted stadium , a very large player wearing warm up trousers and an ice pack on his left knee the size of a boulder stood away from his celebrating teammates and uttered perhaps the most important words in the aftermath of Clemson ' s 37 17 throttling of Oklahoma in the Orange Bowl . Touted as perhaps the nation ' s most balanced team heading into the contest , the Tigers held Oklahoma to a paltry 67 rushing yards .
