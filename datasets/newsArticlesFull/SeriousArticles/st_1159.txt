In an interview published Sunday in Britain ' s The Sunday Times newspaper , Assad slammed London ' s decision to begin strikes in Syria as " illegal " and said its actions would cause " terrorism " to spread . " It will be harmful and illegal and it will support terrorism as happened after the coalition started its operation a year or so ( ago ) , " he told the newspaper . Terror , he said , was like a cancer which needed to be tackled with a " comprehensive " strategy which would involve working with troops on the ground .
