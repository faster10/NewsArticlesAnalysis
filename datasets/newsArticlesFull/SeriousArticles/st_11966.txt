The placards carried by the activists read slogans like " India blockade is violation of human rights " , " Modi Ji End blockade " , " respect Nepal ' s sovereignty " , and " India blockade is crime against humanity " . Scores of trucks have been stranded at a key border checkpoint for over two months following protests by ethnic minorities over the Himalayan nation ' s new constitution introduced in September .
