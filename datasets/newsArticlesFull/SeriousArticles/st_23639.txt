Eicher Motors , the manufacturer of cult bike Royal Enfield lost production of 4000 motorcycles in November . " The floods will certainly have an impact on the delivery of vehicles on new year also but what the amount of impact we dont know right now . However , whatever the impact be will be communicated to the customers through dealers . As a consequence of floods , we have shut down our offices in the city and our plants in Thiruvottiyur and Oragadam since December 1st , " the company said in an email communication .
