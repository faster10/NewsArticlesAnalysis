Failed program cost Pentagon this much : USA Today writes a failed Pentagon program to plan and field a force of moderate Syrians to combat ISIS cost 2 million per fighter , or 384 million . The Pentagon pulled the plug on the program last month . The agency disputes the 2 million figure , however , saying the actual cost is 30 , 000 per trainee . A spokeswoman said the vast majority of the funds paid for weapons , equipment and ammunition , some of which the U . S . led coalition still has in storage .
