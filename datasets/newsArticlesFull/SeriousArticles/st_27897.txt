Stapleton s music chops were on dazzling display in his performance with Justin Timberlake , a moment of pure TV magic if there ever was one . Timberlake , who has lately demonstrated a clear intention to break into the country music world , is so famous that he can do whatever he wants , and the fact that he chose to come to the CMA awards and perform with a virtual unknown immediately added intrigue and gravitas to the performance . It didn t feel like a gimmick shoved together by desperate producers in the same way that Thomas Rhett and Fall Out Boy s sad collaboration did .
