VW is currently embroiled in its biggest ever scandal after it was exposed with fitting its diesel engines with sophisticated software to skew the results of pollution emissions tests . The revelations have rocked the auto sector around the world and cast a shadow over the German economy . But according to Ifo ' s survey , " the climate index for the automotive sector even continued to rise this month , " the think tank said . " Assessments of the current business situation and business expectations both improved . Firms plan to ramp up production . Exports , however , are not expected to provide further stimuli , " Ifo chief Sinn added .
