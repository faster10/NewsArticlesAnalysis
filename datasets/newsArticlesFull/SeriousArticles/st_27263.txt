GENEVA , Jan . 28 , 2016 ( Xinhua ) Bruce Aylward ( L ) , Assistant Director General of World Health Organisation ( WHO ) , and Sylvain Aldighieri , Unit chief for International Health Regulations epidemic alert and response of WHO , hold a joint press conference in Geneva , Switzerland , Jan . 28 , 2016 . Based on dengue epidemiology in the Americas and the over 2 million cases which are reported in the region every year , Sylvain Aldighieri estimated that there could be " between 3 and 4 million cases of Zika infections in the Americas .
