Some level of frustration tends to exist every year around this issue , and rightfully so , but this year is different . I think it ' s a mix of the complete absence of even a single black nominee , combined with the announcements by prominent African Americans in the industry , like Spike Lee and Jada Pinkett Smith , that they would be boycotting . Add in the voices of millions of black folk on platforms like Twitter and Facebook that , just a decade ago , existed in geographical silos , but are now available for the world to see and share . All of these factors , and more , have created a perfect storm of sorts for this issue to pick up a unique type of momentum .
