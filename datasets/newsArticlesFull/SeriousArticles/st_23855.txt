The 91 year old Schlafly first gained notoriety in the early 1960s with publication of her book , A Choice Not An Echo . It was a political manifesto that inspired the conservative insurgents who seized the Republican presidential nomination for Arizona Sen . Barry Goldwater in 1964 . Back then , Schlafly contended that kingmakers were controlling the Republican Party and making of it a mere echo of the Democrats . Schlafly believes that is still true , and her assessment resonates well with the current grass roots antipathy to Republican leaders who have done little for average working folks while serving the needs of the big money donors who finance their campaigns .
