On Tuesday , Iran s official IRNA news agency reported that Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi was on his way to the Saudi city of Jeddah to attend a meeting of the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation to discuss the attacks on Saudi s embassy in Tehran . In Riyadh , the Saudi Foreign Minister Adel Al Jubeir said that the lifting of sanctions on Iran as a result of its nuclear deal with world powers will be a harmful development if it uses the extra money to fund nefarious activities .
