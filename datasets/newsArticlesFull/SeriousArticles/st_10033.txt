The Foreign Office on Thursday said that the participation of Afghan President Ashraf Ghani and Sushma Swaraj , Indian External Affairs Minister , in ' Heart of Asia Conference ' , to be held in Islamabad on December 08 , is yet to be confirmed . The FO spokesman , Qazi Khalilullah , told a weekly press briefing that as per schedule , Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif and Afghan President Hamid Karzai , would inaugurate the conference , but ' we are yet to get confirmation from the Afghan president ' .
