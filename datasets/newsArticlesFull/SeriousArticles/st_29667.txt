In an open letter posted online Sunday , Lakers guard Kobe Bryant announced the current NBA season will be his last . " My heart can take the pounding , my mind can handle the grind , but my body knows it ' s time to say goodbye , " the 37 year old wrote on The Players Tribune . Bryant , now in his 20th season , has been with the Lakers since 1996 , when he was a 17 year old draft day trade . He has won five NBA championships and two Olympic gold medals , and was an NBA All Star selection 17 times and an NBA MVP . In a statement , NBA Commissioner Adam Silver called Bryant " one of the greatest players in the history of our game . "
