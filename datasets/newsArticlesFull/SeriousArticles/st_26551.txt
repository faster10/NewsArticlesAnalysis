Biggest face plant : Presented the opportunity to ruin Iowa s perfect regular season and playoff hopes and to pump life into this disappointing season with a second November victory over an unbeaten foe Nebraska fell flat . Blame quarterback Tommy Armstrong Jr . , who often threw the ball up for grabs , resulting in four interceptions . But why was he asked to throw 45 passes after the Cornhuskers intended to use a run heavy approach and never trailed by more than 11 points ? A low probability , fourth and 1 fade to the end zone , which fell incomplete with six minutes to play , summarized well the poor decision making and worse execution on display by Nebraska .
