Besides the wonderful repartee between Mulder and Scully , we were also treated to : a Peeping Tom moonlighting as a hotel manager a semi psychotic psychologist a pugilistic transsexual a wild game keeper who concealed his sadistic , serial killing ways with a cowardly veneer and a friendly , man sized lizard with two eyes ( not three ) and a preference for the old school " tighty white " briefs instead of more fashionable boxers . To top it all off , the episode made us confront the monster that lies within all of us and makes us do the freakish things every day ( like coming into to work to write this column ) .
