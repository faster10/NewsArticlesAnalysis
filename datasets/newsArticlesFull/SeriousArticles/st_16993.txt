The five time MVP has not started a game for the Broncos since mid November , with injury and poor form prompting Denver to rely on the talents of back up Brock Osweiler . Manning , who missed a good portion of the second half of the season with a foot injury , has recovered and has been deemed healthy enough to play . Kubiak sent Manning into the regular season finale with just more than eight minutes remaining in the third quarter , and Manning led the Broncos on four scoring drives that resulted in 20 points .
