In other developments , the cycling events for the Tokyo 2020 Olympics were moved to the Japan Cycle Sport Centre in Izu following months of negotiations . Staging a sports event is not that important . As the Olympic Summit agreed in October , the IOC is following up on its initiative to make the anti doping system independent of sports organisations . WADA will lead intelligence gathering funded by the IOC to make testing in the lead up to the Olympic Games in Rio de Janeiro as efficient and independent as possible .
