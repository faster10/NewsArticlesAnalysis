The underground explosion angered China , which was not given prior notice although it is North Korea ' s main ally , pointing to a strain in ties between the neighbours . The test also alarmed Japan and the White House said Prime Minister Shinzo Abe agreed with US President Barack Obama in a telephone call that a firm global response was needed . Obama also spoke to President Park Geun hye of South Korea to discuss options . A South Korean military official told Reuters the two countries had discussed the deployment of US strategic assets on the divided Korean peninsula , but declined to give further details . After North Korea last tested a nuclear device , in 2013 , Washington sent a pair of nuclear capable B 2 stealth bombers on a sortie over South Korea in a show of force .
