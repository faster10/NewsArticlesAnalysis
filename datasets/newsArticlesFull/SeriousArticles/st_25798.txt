Sunnyvale , California based Yahoo which paved the way for consumers to access and navigate the Internet in the 1990s has lost ground to younger rivals such as Google and Facebook . Yahoo ' s share of U . S . digital advertising spending is forecast to fall to 3 . 5 percent in 2017 , from 11 . 5 percent in 2009 , according to EMarketer Inc . The company ' s annual sales peaked at 5 . 4 billion in 2008 , and are projected to slip 8 percent this year to 4 . 04 billion , minus revenue passed on to partner sites .
