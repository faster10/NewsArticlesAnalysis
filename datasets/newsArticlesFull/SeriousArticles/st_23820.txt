Reports of roads caving in on main roads and massive traffic build up on arterial city roads only pushed more people to return early . The memories of being stuck on roads for hours together to cov er very short distances on flooded , choked roads hardly two weeks ago were fresh and alternative routes which were yet to get flooded were sought after as a fresh spell of showers began from Monday eve ning . The forecast from the regional meteorological centre in the city said that there was a trough formation in the southwest bay , which was rain making and isolated to very heavy rainfall was expected for a minimum of three days in coastal Tamil Nadu including parts of Chennai . SR Ramanan , director , regional meteorological centre told ET that rains could last for at least five days before the approaching trough moves west towards Sri Lanka .
