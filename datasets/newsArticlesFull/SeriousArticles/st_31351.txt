Like Rock Creek , some restaurants are building their business around a more healthful model . Seasons 52 , which has five locations in Florida and two in Atlanta , Georgia , takes a lighter approach to dining by featuring fresh foods that rotate with the seasons and dishes that contain a maximum of 475 calories . The chefs do this by taking out fat , lowering sugar and salt when possible , and relying instead on flavorful accents such as balsamic vinegar , lemon juice , garlic , and fruits . " We call it ' palate distraction , ' " explains Clifford Pleau , director of culinary development . " If you can give the mouth something else to focus on , it says , ' Wow !
