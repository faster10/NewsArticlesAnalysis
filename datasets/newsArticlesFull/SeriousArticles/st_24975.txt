McElwain isn t going to lose his job , of course , because everyone thinks he has performed it splendidly in taking a dormant Florida program to a 10 2 record and the SEC East Division crown . But McElwain has already seen Saban use the perception that Alabama should cruise to his advantage . Before Alabama faced lowly Charleston Southern at home Nov . 21 , Saban used the occasion to rail against any idea the Tide would roll , lacerating the media for suggesting such a thing and using his performance to let his players know complacency would not be accepted .
