The incident occurred when a peaceful protest by the employees of the PIA outside the Cargo Terminal of the Jinnah International Airport against the proposed privatisation of the national flag carrier turned into a bloody conflict . The clash also left seven people , including a newsman , injured as a law enforcement agency s personnel open fired to stop the protesters from entering the terminal . According to an eyewitnesses account , following a protest call by the PIA Joint Action Committee ( JAC ) , hundreds of PIA workers halted flight operations .
