Former N . Y . Assembly speaker convicted : Former New York Assembly speaker Sheldon Silver , once one of the state s most powerful politicians , was convicted Monday of charges that he traded favors to earn 5 million illegally and then lied about it . The jury in Manhattan federal court returned the verdict after a three week trial in which prosecutors claimed that the 71 year old Democrat repeatedly promised the favors so he could enrich himself . With the conviction , Silver automatically loses his Assembly seat .
