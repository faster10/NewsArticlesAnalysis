For five years , as a member of the House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence , I have been receiving near daily classified briefings on terrorist threats and operations . With a specific focus on Africa and the Arabian Peninsula , I have made nearly a dozen classified trips with intelligence and counterterrorism officers to the region to see firsthand where our efforts have made strides , as well as where rules of engagement for U . S . military and special forces continue to limit our ability to thwart terror networks .
