U . S . Rep . Dan Kildee , D Flint Township , wipes away tears as President Barack Obama speaks about U . S . Marine and Flint Central High School graduate Amir Hekmati in regard to the release of American prisoners from Iran , Sunday , Jan . 17 , 2016 , while watching the nationally televised address at his office in Flint , Mich .
