The city is an important ground for both Saudi Arabia and Iran . The import of Wahabi or Salafi ideology from Saudi Arabia to Hyderabad has led to a larger number of subscribers . The bringing home of a benign form of Wahabism , which only rejects intercession between the faithful and God , by NRIs notwithstanding the influence of rigid Salafism is on the rise . This rise is giving the Sufis , who call themselves the Ahl e Sunnatul Jamat collective nightmares . The Shiite community has the support of Sufis and the strained relations between the Sufis and the Salafis is well known . There are elements on both sides of the fence who are supporting these countries to protect their own interests .
