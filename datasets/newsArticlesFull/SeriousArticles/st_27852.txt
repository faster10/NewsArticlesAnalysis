The contrast with her opponent on Monday was marked . Makarova has a steady style but a volcanic temperament , and her high pitched self admonishments in her native Russian were so anguished that they drew giggles from the crowd . Makarova was burning plenty of emotional energy , and this may have proved crucial in the end , because Konta finished the stronger both mentally and physically .
