In the Oscars first several decades , the nomination of even one non white actor was an exception to the rule , few and far between . In some ways , this tracks with the homogeneity of the country that produced these films . Between 1940 and 1950 , according to the U . S . Census , America remained 89 percent white . ( During that period , there were 3 non white nominees out of 200 chosen , or 1 . 5 . ) In past 25 years , the number of non white actors earning nominations has steadily increased ? peaking at 5 , or one quarter of the 20 nominees , in 2006 . Since 2010 , 8 . 3 of acting nominees have been non white .
