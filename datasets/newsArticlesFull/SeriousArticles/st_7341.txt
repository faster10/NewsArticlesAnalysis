Zheng , the world No . 83 , was one of five Chinese women to reach the second round here . At 21 , she is combining her tennis career with studies for a degree in international relations , and clearly comes from an educated family , judging by her mother s literary output and her father s post as a professor of electrical engineering . But her own game could do with an extra spark if she is to lift her ranking much higher . For now , she is as pragmatic and consistent as Konta , but not quite as powerful .
