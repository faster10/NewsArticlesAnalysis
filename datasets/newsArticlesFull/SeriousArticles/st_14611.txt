Air strikes and indiscriminate shelling of civilian areas in Taiz on Wednesday killed 22 people and wounded 140 others , the International Committee of the Red Cross ( ICRC ) said in a statement , citing local hospitals as their sources . " The situation in Taiz is particularly dire , even by the standards of the appalling conditions all over Yemen , with the nearly half of the hospitals closed and streams of wounded people desperate for treatment , " the head of the ICRC delegation in Yemen , Antoine Grand , was quoted as saying .
