He put out a strong team here , the only real concession to the occasion a second start for Iwobi , a long term academy player but a genuine talent and a full Nigeria international . Iwobi made a bright start , too , playing to the left of central midfield in a 4 1 4 1 , with Callum Chambers hoovering up behind and Giroud the lone striker . It was from the left side that Kieran Gibbs had the first real sight of goal , cutting in and shooting at a good height for Pickford to palm the ball over .
