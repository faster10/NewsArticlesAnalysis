Outlook magazine , admitting its error , issued a statement , saying , " In this story , a remark ( " first Hindu ruler after 800 years " ) made by the late Ashok Singhal of the Vishwa Hindu Parishad was erroneously attributed to Union Home Minister Rajnath Singh . Outlook deeply regrets the lack of diligence in verifying the source of the statement . It was not our intention to denigrate the home minister or Parliament . Outlook sincerely regrets the embarrassment caused to Mr Rajnath Singh and Mr Mohammad Salim . "
