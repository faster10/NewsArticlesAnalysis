Standing and singing alongside his astoundingly talented wife Morgane , Stapleton and Timberlake delivered bluesy , crooning , utterly roaring vocals on Tennessee Whiskey and Timberlake s Drink You Away , and the entire crowd was held visibly rapt with awe . On the faces of every star , from Keith Urban to Zac Brown , read an expression that looked like a cross between the glee of Oh my gosh , they are really doing this , and the call to greatness of Oh my gosh , I could be doing this . Less than two hours after the performance , Tennessee Whiskey was the No 2 song on iTunes ( behind only Adele s Hello ) , and Traveller had reached the No 1 spot .
