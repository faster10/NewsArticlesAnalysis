Murray knew that his 2016 season would test every sinew , given the added pressure of defending his Olympic title in Rio de Janeiro this summer . But he could scarcely have expected such intense strain so soon . No sooner had he been anointed as BBC Sports Personality of the Year for a second time than he was pitched headlong into a five week Australian drama . The demands of the year s first major , with the conditioning required for marathon matches in furnace like conditions , are acute enough without the pressure of his family circumstances . His success in reaching a ninth slam final is extraordinary when set against the pain he knew that the pictures of his stricken father in law would cause the mother to be .
