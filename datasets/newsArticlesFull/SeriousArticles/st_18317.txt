Just a week before the Paris attacks , 73 percent of Americans said in a Quinnipiac University survey that they feared another major terrorist attack in the U . S . in the near future . In this poll , conducted in the days following the carnage in France , 81 percent said they saw another attack coming to the U . S . soon . ISIL has released at least two videos in the last week mentioning Washington and New York as specific targets , though U . S . intelligence has said there is no credible threat related to those videos and no credible intelligence link to the Paris attacks .
