Leonardo DiCaprio : " I want to share this award with all the first nations people represented in this film and all the indigenous communities around the world . It is time that we recognise your history and that we protect your indigenous lands from corporate interests and from people that are out there to exploit them . It is time that we heard your voice and protected this planet for future generations , " DiCaprio said as he held the Globe for best actor in a motion picture drama for The Revenant .
