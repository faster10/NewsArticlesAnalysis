Dallas Cowboys : The Cowboys elevated Kellen Moore from the practice squad for the second time this season to serve as the backup quarterback . This time Moore will back up Matt Cassel after holding the role for two games behind Brandon Weeden earlier in the season . He has not taken many practice snaps during the season , but Moore ' s experience with Scott Linehan in Detroit gives the Cowboys confidence he could handle the role if pressed into action . Moore has yet to play in an NFL game in his career . Todd Archer
