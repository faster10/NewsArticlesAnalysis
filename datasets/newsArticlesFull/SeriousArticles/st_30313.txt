Called the " Ending Federal Marijuana Prohibition Act of 2015 , " Sanders ' bill would allow states to call the shots on weed legalization , without current impediments from the centre . At present , laws on cannabis differ widely across US : Colorado , Washington state , Alaska and Oregon have legalised the sale and possession of marijuana for recreational use , while Washington , DC , has legalised personal use but not sale of the drug . But stores that sell marijuana even in states where it is legal cannot use banking channels because it infringes federal laws .
