The government has also sent two members of the Prime Minister s council on climate change who have for several years dealt with negotiations as well as held posts in the UN climate convention or its bodies . Ajay Mathur , who is also the director general of Bureau of Energy Efficiency is one of them . He has headed the UN s Green Climate Fund earlier as well as negotiated on issues of technology for the country at these talks . He will also be the key spokesperson for India at Paris . The second senior negotiator and PM council member in Paris would be J M Mauskar . A retired IAS officer now , he has negotiated for India at the climate talks for almost a decade . As critically he was elected as one of the first UN co chairs of the climate negotiations that led especially to the Paris agreement and brings the insider s knowledge to the Indian team . They will be assisted by India s ambassador to France Mohan Kumar and his team .
