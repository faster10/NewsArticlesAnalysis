It was a night when , at the club ' s instigation , inflatable bananas returned to City hundreds of them , brandished throughout the lower tier at the Etihad . City ' s connection with inflatable bananas goes back to 1987 when a fan called Frank Newton thought it would be funny to borrow a five foot inflatable banana from a friend of his , a toy collector called Allen Busby , and take it to a City game . He sparked a trend that slowly grew , thanks in part when fans began celebrating club striker Imre Varadi by renaming him Imre Banana and waving their yellow inflatables .
