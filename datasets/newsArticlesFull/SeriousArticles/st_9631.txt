Manning is a late 39 now , 40 is quickly approaching , and he looks like a football player at the end . The very act of moving a team down the field seems laborious . The Super Bowl , should he make it , appears to be his final performance . A 38 , Brady looks as brilliant as ever . That extra year and a half is worth two football seasons . Between that and the fact Manning played his rookie year and Brady did not , means Brady s body has had 36 fewer regular season and playoff games to be beaten , broken and worn down . Brady looks as if he could win another three Super Bowls while Manning wobbles toward his last .
