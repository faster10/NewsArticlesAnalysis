Trump has scheduled a Nov . 23 campaign stop in Columbus . Matt Borges , chairman of the Ohio Republican Party , told cleveland . com that a member of Trump ' s team reached out to inform him of the event . A web invitation says doors open two hours before the 7 p . m . speech at the Greater Columbus Convention Center . Admission is free . A Trump spokeswoman did not immediately respond to a request for further information . The visit Trump ' s first to Ohio as a candidate since an August debate in Cleveland will bring him to the home turf of one of his GOP rivals , Ohio Gov . John Kasich .
