Mets GM Sandy Alderson got mocked last season for setting a goal of 90 wins . But nobody was laughing when the Mets closed out a strong second half to reach that milestone this season . The golden armed foursome of Jacob deGrom , Matt Harvey , Noah Syndergaard and Steven Matz may be the backbone of a new dynasty . And in this postseason , Daniel Murphy has morphed into Babe Ruth . But with both Murphy and Yoenis Cespedes looking unlikely to return next season , the Mets hope to take advantage of the opportunity before them now .
