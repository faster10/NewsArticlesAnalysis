The Warriors had more than enough in reserve to breeze to a 118 105 win at Pepsi Center that improved them to 15 0 , tying the 1948 49 Washington Capitols and the 1993 94 Houston Rockets for the best start to a season in NBA history . The Warriors will have the chance to move past those teams into sole possession of the record on Tuesday , when they head home to take on the struggling Los Angeles Lakers at what promises to be an exceptionally raucous Oracle Arena , where Golden State has won 26 straight .
