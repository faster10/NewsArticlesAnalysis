One hundred and nineteen years ago , on January 23 , was born one of India ' s most iconic political figures Subhas Chandra Bose . Forty four years later , in 1941 , he left the country never to return , dying in a controversial aircrash on August 18 , 1945 . Many enquiries later , and with ample historical evidence available , the controversy refuses to die down . So obssessed have some people , including a few of Netaji ' s relatives , become with the mystery and INA saga that Netaji ' s contribution to the Mahatma Gandhi led freedom struggle has been completely forgotten .
