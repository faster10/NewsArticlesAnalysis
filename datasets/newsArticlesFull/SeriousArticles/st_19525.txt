In reply to an RTI application by Subhash Chandra Agrawal , a native of Chandini Chowk , Delhi , the Prime Minister ' s Office ( PMO ) on November 21 , 2014 , had revealed that it had a total of 41 files relating to Netaji , including secret , top secret and declassified files ( copy of PMO reply available with TOI ) . One of the secret files included File No 2 ( 658 ) 53 PMS . The PMO has cited the subject of the file as ' Jankinath Bhavan at Cuttack , birthplace of Shri Subhas Chandra Bose acquisition by the Orissa Government use of the building as a hospital by the Netaji Subhas Seva Sadan ' .
