In effect , Warren s speech also served to illustrate that the corporate tax ideas embraced by President Barack Obama and the next Senate Democratic leader , Chuck Schumer , have not won the day on the left . Americans for Tax Fairness , a liberal group , praised Warren for lashing out that Schumer had worked out genuinely terrible ideas on international tax reform with Sen . Rob Portman , an Ohio Republican . Obama has backed some of those ideas , including a lower rate on offshore corporate income , while Schumer and Portman have also called for an innovation box that would cut taxes on profits stemming from intellectual property .
