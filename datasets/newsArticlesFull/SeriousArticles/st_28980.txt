To his credit , President Hassan Rouhani is moving quickly to implement the nuclear deal . With a crucial election in February , he is desperate for the economic boost that is expected once sanctions are lifted . As required , Iran has shipped its stockpile of enriched uranium to Russia . Once Iran meets the other demands of the deal , like disassembling more than 13 , 000 centrifuges , the major powers agreed they would give it access to 100 billion in frozen assets from oil sales and allow it to do business again with the world .
