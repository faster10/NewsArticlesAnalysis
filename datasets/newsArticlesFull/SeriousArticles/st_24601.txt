In an indication of the scale of potential deals , the transport minister said at the weekend that Iran intended to buy 114 civil aircraft from Airbus a deal that could be worth more than 10 billion at catalogue prices . Airbus said on Saturday it had not yet held commercial talks with Iran . Entering the Iranian market is not without risks : indebted local banks , a primitive legal system , corruption and an inflexible labour market . Many foreign companies will remain wary that sanctions could snap back if Tehran is later found in breach of the nuclear agreement .
