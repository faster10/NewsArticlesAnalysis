President Eisenhower comes to the Mayflower Hotel in Washington for breakfast Feb . 2 , 1956 to find this gift waiting for him , the desk he used when he wrote his inaugural prayer , and the prayer itself inscribed on the silver plaque . The occasion is the fourth annual prayer breakfast for members of Congress , the Cabinet and other officials . From left to right are Conrad Hilton , hotelman , Sen . John C . Stennis , D Miss , the President , Howard E . Butt , Jr . , Corpus Christi , Texas businessman evangelist Sen . Carlson ( R Kan ) and Sen . Daniel ( D Tex ) .
