Arthur BloomArthur Bloom has used the healing power of music to help hundreds of injured soldiers recover their lives . His program , MusiCorps , pairs professional musicians with troops recovering at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center , helping them play instruments and write and record music . " By injecting music into this space , we can inject life , " Bloom said . " There ' s nothing injured about the way they do it . It ' s just good music .
