It is evident that Bowie , during his early music career , was not confined to the set standards of the industry . He took risks by making himself look different . Undeniably , he did bring about a revolution in rock music and added creativity into his music . However , now that he is gone , his music and legacy will remain forever . Another David Bowie can be born , but for that to happen , the musician should have a different way to view the world and his profession . Not everyone has the tendency to take the risk of being different . However , rookie musicians of today can learn from Bowie s music and his style . Although there will always only be one David Bowie , his legacy can give other musicians the motivation to adapt a different music style .
