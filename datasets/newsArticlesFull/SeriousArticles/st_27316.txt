The nominees for best TV comedy this year reflect changes to the industry itself : Four of the nominees are developed for streaming video services and two of them are cable series . The freedom offered by these formats is transferred into the exemplary quality of these shows , but " Veep , " with its sharp , satirical take on politics in D . C . , narrowly emerges as the winner in this category . With remarkable performances from an ensemble cast led by Julia Louis Dreyfus and supported by Tony Hale and Anna Chlumsky , " Veep " seems to get better and better with every political blunder .
