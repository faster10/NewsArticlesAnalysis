YANGON , MYANMAR NOVEMBER 09 : A person takes a picture with a camera phone as crowds gather for the election result announcement in front of the National League for Democracy ' s headquarters after Myanmar ' s first free and fair election on November 9 , 2015 in Yangon , Myanmar . The elections are Myanmar ' s first openly contested polls in 25 years , following decades of military rule . Noble laureate Aung San Suu Kyi appeared poised to win power in Myanmar on today despite her party ' s growing concerns about cheating in yesterday ' s historic election .
