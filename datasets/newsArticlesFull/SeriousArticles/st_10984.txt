The Redskins haven t won a playoff game since the 2005 season and this marked just the fifth playoff trip since owner Dan Snyder bought the team in 1999 . The talk Sunday naturally returned to 2016 and what the Redskins can do now . The problem is , before reaching training camp next summer there are plenty of questions : Will they re sign quarterback Kirk Cousins or have to use the franchise tag ? How many players will actually be back ? They have key free agents in addition to Cousins , such as four year starting running back Alfred Morris . End Jason Hatcher said he might retire .
