Legislative changes are also having an effect . The New York City initiated ban on trans fats has become far reaching at least eight other major metropolitan areas have legislated similar bans , as have restaurants , hotels , theme parks , cruise lines , and many other businesses . Meanwhile , New York City recently took another stand on upgrading restaurant menus . In a move affecting mostly fast food restaurants , the city ' s board of health recently asked food service establishments with standardized preparation methods that already have nutrition analyses to post calorie information on menus so customers can see it when deciding what to order .
