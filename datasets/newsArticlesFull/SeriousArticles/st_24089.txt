The Houston Texans have had an up and down season , losing five of their first seven games before a four game winning streak put them on track to win the AFC South title . After having a chance to win and clinch the top seed in the AFC , the Patriots ended up falling to the lowly Miami Dolphins and securing the No . 2 seed in the conference . Injuries have caused Brady to hold the ball longer while the play develops in front of him , and given defenders ample opportunity to break through the decimated O line and get to Brady .
