Alstom , the French electricity generation and rail transport company , is seeking contracts to work on the expansion of the metro in Tehran and building two more lines in the city of Mashhad , as well as participating in the electrification of the 600 mile ( 1 , 000km ) Tehran Mashhad railway . The French industrial group Bouygues and A roports de Paris are also in talks with Iran to construct the country s largest transport project , the second terminal at Tehran s Imam Khomeini international airport .
