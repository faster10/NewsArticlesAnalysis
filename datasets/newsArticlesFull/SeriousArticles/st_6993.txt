In 1994 , Republicans with a radically conservative view of the role of government , led by Newt Gingrich , won a stunning victory that gave them control of both houses of Congress . In 2010 , equally conservative Republicans captured the House and , in 2014 , added the Senate . In both the earlier periods and today , the ascendant Republicans have sought to use the budget process and the threat of government shutdown ? even government default ? to force a Democratic President to accept their agenda .
