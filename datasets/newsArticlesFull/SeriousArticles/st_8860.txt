THREE AMERICANS MISSING IN IRAQ WERE TAKEN FROM A BAGHDAD APARTMENT , via the NYT : Three Americans who have been missing in Iraq since Friday were probably kidnapped from a Baghdad apartment , according to security officials and local news reports . The apartment may have been the home of an Iraqi colleague who is also believed to have been abducted , the officials and reports said . News of the Americans disappearance emerged late Sunday night , but officials said they were probably taken two days before in Dora , a sprawling neighborhood in the southern part of the capital with a mixed population of Sunni and Shiite Muslims along with some Christians .
