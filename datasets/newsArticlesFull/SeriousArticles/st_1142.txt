Without offering no solution to the drug scourge during a town hall meeting in Bridgton , LePage blamed the problem on out of state drug dealers , who weren t only bringing heroin into Maine , but also leaving single white mothers behind . While LePage didn t clarify what he meant by these types of people , he claims it was not about race , his spokesman said . Race is irrelevant .
