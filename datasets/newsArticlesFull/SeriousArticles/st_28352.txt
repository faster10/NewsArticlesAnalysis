SCOOP : CLYBURN BACKS FCC LOOK AT SET TOP BOXES ? Democratic FCC Commissioner Mignon Clyburn sounds supportive of FCC Chairman Tom Wheeler s contentious proposal to open up the TV set top box market . I m always open to having a discussion on improving consumer choice , Clyburn said in an emailed statement to MT . The truth is that 99 percent of pay TV subscribers lease their boxes , she added . She said she s heard from interested parties expressing concerns about lack of competition in this industry and said she thinks it is right for the Commission to look at what we can do to create a more robust market for the devices .
