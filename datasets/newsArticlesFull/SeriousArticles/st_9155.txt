Heading into their Week 9 bye , the Seahawks were a . 500 team facing a make or break second half . They definitely made on the second half , going 6 2 and looking like the championship contender we ve come to expect . The Vikings are a team pointed in the right direction , led by ultra reliable running back Adrian Peterson and a young , emerging defense . Their quarterback play , however , will be their undoing .
