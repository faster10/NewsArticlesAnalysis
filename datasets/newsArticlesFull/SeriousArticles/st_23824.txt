In September this year , the state government transferred the sensational Sheena Bora murder case to the CBI stating that the Central agency would be able to probe the case holistically and also investigate the financial trail in the case ? something the government said the CBI could do more ably than the Mumbai Police . However , sources in the agency have now told The Indian Express that they are only concentrating on the murder case and financial links relevant to the murder . The agency says it is too understaffed for a detailed probe into alleged financial irregularities by Indrani or in her companies .
