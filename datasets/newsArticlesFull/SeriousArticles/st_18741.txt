But in some ways , Sanders ' focus on health care has forced an overdue conversation within the Democratic party . In 2009 2010 , President Obama framed health reform as an economic imperative ? a necessary strategy to get the bill through Congress during the recession , but a message that wasn ' t in keeping with liberal values that had motivated the decades long struggle for coverage expansion . Sanders has put the focus squarely back on the moral question : Namely , is health care a right for every American ? Despite their bitter exchanges , both Clinton and Sanders strongly reasserted their commitment to universal coverage .
