HILLARY CLINTON SHARPENS ATTACKS ON BERNIE SANDERS , ' WORRIED ' ABOUT HIS ' REVOLUTION ' . A day after Hillary Clinton called for a " spirited debate " with Bernie Sanders , Clinton sharpened her attacks in Iowa Tuesday just as a new poll was released showing the Vermont senator ahead in the Hawkeye State . Discussing their differences on health care , guns and Wall Street , Clinton said " now if that s the kind of revolution he is talking about , I am worried folks . " During the event in Ames in which Clinton was endorsed by the Brady Campaign , Clinton called Sanders out for voting against the Brady Bill five times .
