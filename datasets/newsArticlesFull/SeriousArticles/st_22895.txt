It may be argued that the balance has already undergone substantive transformation through the efforts of Suu Kyi herself and also the military s own reforms . In the coming days and months , the direction and the contours of the political contest will acquire greater clarity . There is little doubt that the equations between Suu Kyi and the military are going to remain vulnerable to the inherent pulls and tensions that are immanent in the two fundamentally opposite systems of power and authority . At the same time , the contest for political space is also likely to become more subtle .
