Oil prices were up 3 . 5 percent Monday after a 1 . 2 percent climb late in 2015 . That wasn ' t a huge move , and it disappeared as oil once again fell below 37 a barrel . Oil prices have recently been pulled down as China has slowed and cut back on its need for oil . As a powerhouse in the world , if China is slowing a lot , oil demand goes down in the many countries of the world that depend on selling oil to China too . So the price of oil goes down because there is a glut of oil available in the world and fewer eager users .
