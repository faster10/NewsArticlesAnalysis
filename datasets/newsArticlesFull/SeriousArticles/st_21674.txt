Agreement over a financial mechanism that would channelise new and additional money from the developed countries towards the developing and climate vulnerable countries is something that India has said it would push aggressively at Paris . It would also demand to see progress on the developed world s commitment to mobilise 100 billion per year for the developing nations by the year 2020 , and try to build pressure to ensure that the rich world commits itself to progressively increase this figure after 2020 .
