2 . Pup literature : Snoopy , of course , is not just a symbol of fantasy sequences , but also a vehicle for escape . For Schulz , creating the strip itself was a form of exerting control through escape , so Snoopy rose over the decades from sight gag quadruped to humanesque , wish fulfillment hero . So in The Peanuts Movie , Snoopy the doghouse novelist , after typing , It was a dark and stormy night , gets to pen his own adventures of derring do ? as the World War I flying ace rescuing a damsel beagle from the Red Baron ? as narrative counterpoint to Charlie Brown s soulful but quotidian existence .
