Even after Raonic s injury started to hamper his movement , Murray still had a worrying moment when serving at 4 3 in the fourth set . A bungled forehand putaway caused him such frustration that he suffered a temporary brain freeze , and had to deal with a break point that would have left Raonic eight points from victory . A cagey rally developed , which Murray precipitated by rushing to the net and pulling off a pair of textbook volleys . He celebrated with one of those chainsaw manoeuvres originally conceived by Lleyton Hewitt , punching his right fist downwards at the blue Plexicushion six times .
