Where Djokovic s vanquishing of Roger Federer had been clinical , Murray s semi final experience was far more arduous . There are guppy fish in Lake Ontario with a greater range of facial expressions than Raonic commonly displays , but the Canadian with the fluorescent arm sleeve and Exocet like serve was a study in aggression here . Not content with smashing two rackets , the 13th seed also berated umpire Jake Garner for not overruling a glaring Murray miss . Do your job ! he screamed . I already have to play him , not you . It was that type of match : tense , with an undercurrent of belligerence .
