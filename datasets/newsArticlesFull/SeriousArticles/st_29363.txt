The 60 second spot released by the Trump campaign that is set to air in Iowa , New Hampshire and South Carolina , opens with Cruz ' s uncomfortable interview with Fox News ' Bret Baier on Dec . 16 , in which he stammered as he explained his position and tactics during the 2013 Gang of Eight immigration reform talks . At the time , Cruz had proposed an amendment to the legislation that scrapped the bill s full pathway to citizenship for undocumented immigrants but left open a path to legal status . Cruz has since described his amendment as a " poison pill " designed to sink the legislation .
