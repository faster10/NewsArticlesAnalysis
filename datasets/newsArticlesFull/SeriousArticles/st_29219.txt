Puducherry and its suburbs continued to be lashed by heavy rains since last night and it recorded 15 . 2 cm rain in the last 24 hours ending 0830 hours today . Puducherry Chief Minister N Rangasamy has sought immediate interim financial assistance of Rs 100 crore from the Centre , to take up relief works . In a release , Rangasamy said he had written to Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Home Minister Rajnath Singh , seeking immediately an interim relief of Rs 100 crore to cope with the situation arising out of the rains .
