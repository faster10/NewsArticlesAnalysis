The Warriors are helping to usher in a new modern era of the NBA with teams showing preference for three pointers and faster pace offenses . The grind it out , physical style of basketball that was prevalent in the late ' 80s and ' 90s , no longer works nor allowed as the NBA has changed their rules to create a more free flowing game . However if the rules were never changed , Hall of Fame foward Charles Barkley believes that the Warriors would be constantly roughed up and wouldn ' t be as good as they are today .
