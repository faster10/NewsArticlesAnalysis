Clemson already has its spot at No . 1 in Mark Schlabach s Way too Early Preseason Top 25 , perfectly illustrating the notion that Dabo Swinney has built a program that is only getting started , not one that will fall back into its old ways . With Watson returning , the Tigers will have a legitimate shot to make it to the national championship game in Tampa , Florida , in 2017 . So will Florida State , which held the ACC flag for so many years on its own . Until Tuesday night , Florida State was the only school representing the ACC to play for a national championship going back to the Bowl Coalition , which set the table for the playoff system in place now .
