In Vizianagaram , appreciating officials of the AIDS Control Society for controlling positive cases from 1 . 8 per cent in 63 , 000 in 2014 15 to 1 . 27 during this current fiscal , Collector M . M . Nayak on Tuesday called upon NGOs and other positive network agencies to create awareness among people on the dreaded disease and achieve it to zero level . Addressing a gathering of officials , nursing school and college students at IMA Hall , he said adopting preventive measures was the best solution . Earlier , RDO Srinivasa Murty flagged off a rally at RTC complex in which NCC cadets from M . R . Autonomous College , Nursing College students and others participated .
