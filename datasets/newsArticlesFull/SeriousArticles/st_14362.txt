According to Mumbai police , out of the 75 cases registered against Rajan , they are sequencing the cases to ascertain those in which they have direct evidence of Rajan ' s involvement . " We are looking at around 15 cases in which we can put up strong case against Rajan and have direct evidence . There are a number of cases that are decade old and their papers are been looked for . But we believe the recent 15 cases registered against Rajan are the strongest and we have good evidence against him . "
