UN SG Ban Ki moon described the Saudi move breaking of diplomatic ties as deeply worrying . China said it was highly concerned . The US and Germany called for restraint , while Russia offered to mediate . Pakistan voiced concern , dark forces of terrorism and extremism could take advantage of any disunity in the Muslim world . It also deplored the storming of the Saudi Embassy in Tehran as unfortunate and deeply regrettable . It is the responsibility of states to provide full safety and security to all diplomatic missions and their personnel , the foreign office said in a statement .
