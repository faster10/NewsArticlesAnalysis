Key player : The Cardinals have a standout , versatile defensive player in Deone Bucannon . He was drafted as a strong safety , but plays more of a linebacker role in the Cardinals defense , which often uses extra defensive backs . His athletic ability will be very important in defending the Panthers ' diverse run game , and also he can help out against tight end Greg Olsen . Bucannon is also an effective blitzer . He ' ll be a key for the Cardinals in slowing down a Panthers offense that led the NFL with 500 points this regular season .
