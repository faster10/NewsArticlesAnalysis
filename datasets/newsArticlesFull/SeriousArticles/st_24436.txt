Russia and Turkey both publicize their efforts to fight ISIS in Syria , but each accuses the other of ulterior motives . Turkey ( along with other skeptics in the West ) complains that Russia targets anti regime rebel groups rather than ISIS , due to its longtime support for Syrian President Bashar al Assad . For its part , Russia resents Turkey ' s support for anti Assad rebel groups and argues that Turkey secretly helps ISIS in order to reap the economic rewards of allegedly free flowing oil from ISIS territory in Syria .
