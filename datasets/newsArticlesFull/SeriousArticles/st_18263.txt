The Revenant , Alejandro G . Inarritu s spectacular survivalist drama led the pack with 12 nominations , including best picture , director and Leonardo DiCaprio s fifth acting nomination and his fourth for best actor . Mad Max : Fury Road was in second place with 10 nominations , including best picture and director for 70 year old George Miller , who revived his post apocalyptic franchise 30 years after the release of his Mad Max Beyond Thunderdome . Cambridge born , three time acting nominee Matt Damon was also nominated for best actor for his frequently solo , frequently comic contribution to the multiple nominee The Martian . Johnny Depp , on the other hand , was overlooked for his performance as James Whitey Bulger in Black Mass .
