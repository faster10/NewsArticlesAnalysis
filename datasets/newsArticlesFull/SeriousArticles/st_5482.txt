Though the death knell was readied , it was never sounded . In the 35 games that followed the heavily scrutinized Sociedad loss , Barcelona lost two . When the season was over , Luis Enrique had won four trophies in his debut campaign . Barcelona were Spanish league and cup champions , European champions ( beating Guardiola ' s Bayern Munich in the semi final ) and UEFA Super Cup winners . It was a relentless sweep . Messi , who supposedly couldn ' t play for Enrique months before , scored 43 league goals and tallied 18 assists . Neymar , the cause of the devastating transfer ban , managed 22 goals . New signing Suarez , who had found the going tough early on , scored 16 times in the league and added a further 14 assists .
