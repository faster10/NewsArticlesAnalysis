The prospects for KXL approval by the US had been dim , but the ascension of the new Trudeau government , with its serious commitment to meeting greenhouse gas reduction targets matching those of the US , could change the dynamic , Goldwyn said . If Canada is able to meet its national targets by offsetting emissions elsewhere in the economy , the US perspective on the salience of the pipeline could change . The Harper government was never credible on greenhouse gas reductions this government could be very different .
