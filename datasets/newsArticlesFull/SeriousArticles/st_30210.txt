At the same time , Cruz helped cement the already solid status of evangelical conservatives as the dominant faction in the Iowa Republican caucuses . As in 2012 , more than 60 percent of GOP caucus goers identified themselves in entrance polls as evangelicals . Cruz locked up some key endorsements , including Family Leader CEO Bob Vander Plaats and Congressman Steve King , to help consolidate support from Christian conservatives . Trump had some evangelical support but also was relying on mainstream and moderate Republicans .
