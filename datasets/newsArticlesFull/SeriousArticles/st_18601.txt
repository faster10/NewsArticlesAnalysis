Weak growth signs in the US An unexpected drop in retail sales and the third consecutive monthly fall in industrial output in December , released on Friday , added to the latest indication that US economic growth braked sharply in the fourth quarter . Following those data , the Atlanta Federal Reserve ' s closely watched GDP forecast model showed the US economy is on track to grow 0 . 6 per cent in the fourth quarter , slowing sharply from 2 . 0 per cent growth in the third quarter . US companies ' fourth quarter profits are expected to decline more than four per cent , which would be the second straight quarterly decline . The S P 500 hit a 15 month low on Friday , ahead of a market holiday on Monday for Martin Luther King Jr Day .
