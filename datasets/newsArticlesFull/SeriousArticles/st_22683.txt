Last week , Gareth Ainsworth , the spirited Wycombe Wanderers manager and a man who spent much of his playing days providing the lead vocals for pub rock outfits , admitted that , given the opportunity by some generous deity , he would trade in everything he achieved in football for one appearance with the Rolling Stones . At the same time , Gary Whelan , drummer of the Happy Mondays , recently told me he would cheerfully tread the opposite path , handing over his entire musical career for five minutes in a Manchester United shirt . He is not alone , many a pop star wants to be a footballer . And vice versa .
