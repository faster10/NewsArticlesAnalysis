This second leg began at a steam train pace that did not waver until the sides broke for half time . Tour was positioned at the base of the home midfield and from there he made the game s opening run , to signal City meant business . This was answered by the jet heeled Gerard Deulofeu racing along the right and leaving Mart n Demichelis looking like a statue before he won a corner . The Spaniard took this and it was cleared by a Willy Caballero punch , but City were warned . The opener would come from some equally scintillating Everton attacking .
