Spokesman of the United States State Department John Kirby has said that the country will participate in the quadrilateral Afghan peace process set to take place in Pakistan next week . Pakistan and Afghanistan agreed to meet US and Chinese officials in a bid to prepare the ground for fresh peace talks with the Taliban . John Kirby said that Taliban representatives will not participate in the peace process . The state department spokesman also said that a strategy will be decided as to how the reconciliatory process can be moved forward .
