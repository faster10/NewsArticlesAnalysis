Tamil Nadu rainfall : 11 foreign and 9 domestic flights from the airport were put on hold as no landing or take off was allowed till late in the evening . Officials said they might review the decision at midnight to take a call on resuming operations . A senior airport official said while the runways were free of water logging , they suspended flight operations as a precautionary measure following waterlogging in some other areas , adding , they did not want to take a chance .
