This fiercely loyal supporter of Russian Prime Minister Vladimir Putin has adopted the bizarre habits one comes to expect from a Middle Eastern despot . Kadyrov races around his fiefdom driving his own black SUV , dressed in shiny track suits and baseball caps . His mansion , a half hour ' s drive out of Grozny , has its own horse race track , a show room full of antique guns and sabers , the Cyrillic initials " RK " carved into the building ' s fa ade and a private zoo full of lions , tigers and other big , endangered wildcats . The roars of one of these animals echoes throughout the compound as we wait until well after midnight for a press conference with the Chechen strongman .
