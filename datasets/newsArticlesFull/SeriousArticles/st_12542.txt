Republicans anti Muslim rhetoric soared in the week after the Paris terrorist attacks . One GOP presidential front runner said he would absolutely want a database of Muslims in the country . Another compared largely Muslim , Syrian refugees to rabid dogs . Yet another called for banning refugees from five Middle Eastern countries . The candidates say they re doing their due diligence to keep Americans safe from a new terror threat that has risen from the ashes in war torn Syria . Democrats say this is yet another example of Republicans pushing away minorities out of fear . You can bet Democrats are writing down the comments Republican candidates made last week about Muslims and refugees to bring up in the general election next year .
