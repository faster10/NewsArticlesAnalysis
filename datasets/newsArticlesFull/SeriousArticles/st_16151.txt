Every time I go to visit my dad in Bromley , I still marvel at the single minded determination of the young David Jones to get the hell out and the unexpected suburban escape he offered others even while he stayed . The Anglo Indian photographer Hy Money was struggling with her own identity in a rather racist London when she took her children to see him perform at the sitars mime and puppetry happening that was the Arts Lab in Beckenham and experienced an epiphany : It the Bowie home was so dark and red and mystic and it was exotic and there was this incense . Up until then I d come to this country and thought of myself as a child of the British Raj . And so I didn t want to think of myself as Asian . I didn t know what I was . She felt deeply moved by the experience .
