The player Luis Su rez is ordered to pay a fine in the amount of CHF 100 , 000 . The decision was notified to the player and the Uruguayan FA today . ' Such behaviour cannot be tolerated on any football pitch , and in particular not at a FIFA World Cup when the eyes of millions of people are on the stars on the field . The Disciplinary Committee took into account all the factors of the case and the degree of Mr Suarez ' s guilt in accordance with the relevant provisions of the Code . The decision comes into force as soon it is communicated , ' said Claudio Sulser , chairman of the FIFA Disciplinary Committee .
