The term climate neutrality is not formally defined in the UN climate convention but is often read to mean that countries can continue to emit some levels of carbon emissions in their domestic economies as long as they can offset these by taking action in other geographies . Large sections of civil society have criticised the idea for giving developed countries an easy way out to not take actions to reduce emissions from their own economic activity . India has repeatedly opposed the inclusion of new terms that are not defined clearly in to the Paris agreement at this late stage of the negotiations .
