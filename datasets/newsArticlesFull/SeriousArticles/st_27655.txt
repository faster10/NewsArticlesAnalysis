NEW YORK , NY JANUARY 11 : Flowers , candles and pictures sit at a memorial outside of the late musician and performer David Bowie ' s apartment that he shared with his wife on January 11 , 2016 in New York City . A growing memorial sits outside of the residence in lower Manhattan following news that Bowie died over the weekend following 18 month battle with cancer . Bowie was 69 and had just released his 25th studio album , " Blackstar , " to strong reviews on January 8 . ( Photo by Spencer Platt Getty Images )
