The Mets won 11 straight games in April , then went without David Wright , Daniel Murphy and Travis d ' Arnaud for long stretches of the first half . With backups playing in key spots , the offense nosedived , and the team treaded water only because of its elite young pitching . Yoenis Cespedes arrived in a trade and the Mets got healthy . And in a pair of critical series in the second half , the Mets swept the teetering Nationals , positioning themselves to claim the franchise ' s first division crown since 2006 .
