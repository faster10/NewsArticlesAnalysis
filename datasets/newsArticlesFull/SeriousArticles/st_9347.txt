The Chief Minister had complained of exhaustion during his hectic tour of Srinagar city on December 22 . He was flown to New Delhi on December 24 and was kept in the Intensive Care Unit ( ICU ) of AIIMS . The departed leader will be accorded State funeral . The state has also declared seven day mourning as a mark of respect to the departed leader . All State government offices and educational institutions in the State shall remain closed today . National State Flags will be flown half mast during the mourning on all buildings and places where these are flown .
