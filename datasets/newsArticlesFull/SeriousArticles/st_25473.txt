For his part , Sanders showed a more toned down , self aware side than his often grumpy and serious persona belies . When he was told there were going to be some non policy questions , he joked , how many pair of underwear do I have ? Am I really Larry David ? a reference to the comedian s recent biting caricature of him on Saturday Night Live , where he said the Vermont senator owned one pair of underwear . When Maddow paused to open an envelope with the questions , Sanders said , Watch , it s the underwear question .
