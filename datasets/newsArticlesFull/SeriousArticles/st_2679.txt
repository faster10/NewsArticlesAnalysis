Brady , in the 16th season of his astonishingly consistent National Football League career , recognises that personal landmarks are secondary to the duel with Manning . They might never take the field at the same time , but the headline individual match up of the New England Patriots confrontation with the Denver Broncos is such a draw that it stirs a time honoured debate across every barroom in America : which of these two , long since shoo ins to join Joe Montana and Dan Marino in the Pro Football Hall of Fame , is better ?
