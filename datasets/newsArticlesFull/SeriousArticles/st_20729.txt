Now a consensus about what to do looks nearer . France has successfully passed a resolution through the United Nations Security Council that approves all necessary measures against Isil in Syria . While the terrorists may be proficient , they are also isolated . This development gives military action the stamp of international approval ? which , in turn , strengthens David Cameron s case for joining the US led coalition carrying out strikes in Syria . He will lay out that case in the coming days and there should be a debate in the Commons soon . The preconditions for action laid out by the Foreign Affairs Select Committee appear largely to have been met . Britain could well be bombing by Christmas .
