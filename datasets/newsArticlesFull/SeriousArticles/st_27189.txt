He was thoughtful ? at times , ponderously so . He picked his enemies carefully , and with precious little subtlety . The balanced speech had enough elements of rhetoric around equality and uplift to be worthy of a liberal president , combined with enough braggadocio about American military supremacy to be worthy of the United States commander in chief . He described the kinds of regular people he had helped and named the mean foreign people he had killed . The United States of America is the most powerful nation on Earth , he said . Period .
