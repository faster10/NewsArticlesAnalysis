In a slew of pledges at his speech opening the Forum on China Africa Cooperation ( FOCAC ) , he also announced drought aid for the continent . " China is greatly concerned about the poor harvest caused by El Nino in many African countries and will provide one billion renminbi yuan ( US 156 million ) worth of emergency food aid to the affected countries , " he said . The two day FOCAC meeting is the second time China has brought together African leaders since the forum was launched in Beijing in 2000 .
