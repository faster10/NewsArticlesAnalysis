Honda is also going to make a splash with an all new model of its globally revered NSX sports car . The car carries on the same DNA from the first generation NSX , while incorporating a more lightweight body , new twin turbocharged inline V6 engine , which is installed in a midship configuration . The car also features , what is known as a three motor SPORT HYBRID SH AWD ? ( Super Handling All Wheel Drive ) powertrain that makes sure the driver gets the ultimate driving experience , while staying environmentally friendly . The powerplant is mated to a 9 speed Dual Clutch Transmission . Each front wheel is coupled to its own independent , highly efficient electric motor . The SPORT HYBRID SH AWD ? uses advanced torque vectoring , which distributes power to all four wheels .
