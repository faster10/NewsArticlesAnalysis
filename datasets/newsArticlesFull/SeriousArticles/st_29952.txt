U . S . soldiers who are elements of the 2nd Cavalry Regiment and Polish 12th Mechanised Brigade soldiers march during a partner training with Polish 12th Mechanised Brigade at the Drawsko Pomorskie military training area in Drawsko Pomorskie , Poland , near the Ukraine , on Monday . Over the next few weeks , the US soldiers will conduct training as part of so called ' Stryker Detachment ' , and then from March 12 will take part in the exercises with 1st motorized infantry battalion of the Polish 12th Mechanised Brigade
