On paper , defected Air Force officer As ad Al Zoubi was to head the Syrian opposition delegation , representing the Free Syrian Army ( FSA ) . This would be the first time that a military commander , rather than a civilian politician , heads the Syrian opposition delegation to any international convention since the outbreak of the Syrian war nearly five years ago . Supporting him as chief negotiator will be rebel commander Mohammad Alloush of Jaish Al Islam in rural Damascus , the brother of slain commander Zahran Alloush , who was killed in a Russian air strike in December .
