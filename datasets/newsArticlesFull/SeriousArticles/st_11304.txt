McCarthy is an alumni of Yale School of Drama . He has done it all from TV , Broadway , film to writing and direction . McCarthy ' s directorial debut The Station Agent with Peter Dinklage of Game of Thrones is one of his best pieces . He has been previously nominated for Best Original Screenplay for Pixar ' s Up , which he co wrote with Pete Doctor and Bob Peterson . He was associated with Game of Thrones , directing an unaired pilot episode of the show and , later on , producing the first ever episode of the HBO mega hit .
