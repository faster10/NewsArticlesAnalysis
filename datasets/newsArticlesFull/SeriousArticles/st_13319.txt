The first to be killed in this series was Salim Kurla who was bumped off on April 21 , 1998 in Andheri . He had been accused of sending men to Pakistan . After him , Jindran , a builder , was killed on June 29 , 1998 . The third target was a builder Majid Khan who was eliminated on March 1 , 1999 . Thereafter , Rajan eliminated Hanif Kadawala , another accused . This was followed by the murder of Akbar Sama Khan who was accused of loading RDX into suspect cars and scooters at Tiger Memon ' s garages in Mahim area .
