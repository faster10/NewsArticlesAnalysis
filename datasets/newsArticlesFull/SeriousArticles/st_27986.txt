In the aftermath of the Paris attacks , two pictures sent a powerful message about how international politics is changing . One was of United States President Barack Obama hunched in discussion in a hotel lobby with Russian President Vladimir Putin . The frosty body language of their previous meeting at the United Nations had given way to something more businesslike . The US and Russian presidents almost looked like colleagues . Picture number two was artfully released by the Defence Ministry in Moscow , showing a Russian bomb being loaded on to a plane in Syria with the words That s for Paris scrawled on the side .
