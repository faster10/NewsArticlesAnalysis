In our mind , the election is in November , but the candidates will be chosen long before then . I ' m not here to debate the merits of our primary system or whether or not it ' s fair for Iowa to play such a huge role in voting weeks ahead of the rest of us , but I do know this : If you choose to wait and see who all of the other states select , the candidate you prefer , or the candidate who may best represent your needs and concerns , that person may already be out of the running . It ' s fully possible that candidates could run away with the nomination before you even cast your vote in a primary .
