Lalu admitted RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat ' s statement for review of the reservation policy played a big role in pushing the poor towards the grand alliance . " They ( BJP leaders ) were thinking they would divide backward votes by playing communal cards . But Bhagwat let the cat out of the bag and everyone came to know about the BJP ' s game plan to snatch quota facility from backwards and dalits , " he said and added the polarization among backwards and dalits this time was more intensive than 1990 .
