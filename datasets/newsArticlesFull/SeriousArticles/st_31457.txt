Trump is looking to run out the clock until voters head to the caucuses on Monday night , the official kickoff of the presidential primary season . With just days to go , Trump has reopened a small lead in Iowa over Ted Cruz . While at first blush it may not seem logical to pick a fight with Fox News , the most powerful cable network on the right , and refuse to attend a debate in Iowa just days before they hold his fate in their hands , many think Trump ' s latest antics could help him deflate his opponents and lock up the caucus .
