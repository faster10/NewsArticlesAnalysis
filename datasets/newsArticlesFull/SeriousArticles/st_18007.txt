In a division filled with offensive firepower , the Suns believe defense wins games . Phoenix cemented that identity by signing Tyson Chandler , a veteran defender who should mesh well with emerging forward Markieff Morris ? so long as he can move past the fact that the Suns traded away his twin brother , Marcus . Feeding them the ball will be guards Eric Bledsoe , who may be in for an All Star campaign , and Brandon Knight . How they blend with a second unit of new , raw talent could determine their playoff chances .
