Speaking in Manila after meeting with Canada ' s prime minister , Obama said America had always been open to allowing people from war zones to find refuge in the United States , where they become " part of the fabric of American life " . Denouncing the " spasm of rhetoric " over refugees , Obama said refugees already faced the most vigorous vetting process for anyone admitted to the country . He added that " the idea that somehow they pose a more significant threat than all the tourists who pour into the United States every single day just doesn ' t jibe with reality . "
