That pesky carbon footprint . No matter how they spin it , the 190 global leaders now gathered in Paris to talk about climate change can t escape the carbon aftermath of their personal aircraft and ground transportation . The United Nation s own Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change itself says that such transport accounts for 23 percent of greenhouse gas emissions , and the percentage is expected to increase . The Swiss based Air Transport Action Group says the aviation industry alone produces 12 percent .
