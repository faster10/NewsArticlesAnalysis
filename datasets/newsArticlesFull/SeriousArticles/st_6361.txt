Kentucky happens to be the perfect test case for Obamacare . Other states with Republican governors have expanded Medicaid to cover the uninsured . But under Democratic Gov . Steve Beshear , Kentucky ' s state insurance exchange has been a national model , and through the combination of the exchange and Medicaid expansion funded by the law , Kentucky has cut its uninsured rate in half . The progress , however , was enabled by executive order , not legislation , and it can be unraveled similarly .
