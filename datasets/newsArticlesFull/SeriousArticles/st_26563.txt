ABOUT THE COWBOYS ( 3 8 ) : Matt Cassel will be back at quarterback for Dallas , which lost all seven games when Romo was sidelined the first time including four starts with Cassel under center . The Cowboys could do little against Carolina , getting 11 yards on 10 carries from Darren McFadden and two catches for 26 yards from Dez Bryant while being held to 14 points or fewer for the fourth time in the last seven contests . Dallas ' defense is eighth overall with an average of 332 yards allowed per game , but it has forced a league worst seven turnovers .
