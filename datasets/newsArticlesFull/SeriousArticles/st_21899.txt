Based in Las Vegas , the CES is a sprawling , eisteddfod of self congratulatory and dubiously ethical nonsense . Perfectly happy to simultaneously promote vinyl record decks that would fail the Ronco quality tests , Chinese assembly cities with working practices which would have Dickens and Lord Shaftesbury reaching for their pens and statutes , and Silicon Valley squillionaires who ' s every utterance is treated as the holy writ , CES has dragged the car makers into its lower orbits . In the last few years , the show has vyed with the Detroit motor show to pull in the early year automotive news , though what you see is often a lot less than what you re actually getting .
