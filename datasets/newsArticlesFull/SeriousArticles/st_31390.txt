Griffey and slugging catcher Mike Piazza make a class of two that will be inducted at Cooperstown , N . Y . , this July . Piazza received 365 votes ( 83 percent ) , finishing well above the required 75 percent for induction . Jeff Bagwell ( 71 . 6 percent ) , Tim Raines ( 69 . 8 percent ) , and Trevor Hoffman ( 67 . 3 percent ) , finished just shy , though if voting trends continue all three could be elected as early as 2017 . The elite speedster Raines , who has one more year remaining on the ballot , was 23 votes short of induction .
