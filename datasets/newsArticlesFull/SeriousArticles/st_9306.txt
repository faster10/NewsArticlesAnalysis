He maintained that the country can progress only when both samta ( equality ) and mamta ( compassion ) are guaranteed to all . Though he did not mention the lynching of a Muslim in Dadri allegedly for consuming and storing beef , Modi appeared to make an oblique reference to such incidents . " If there is any incident of atrocity against anybody , it is a blot on all of us , for the society as well as the nation . We should all feel the pain and take this to ensure such things do not happen , " he said .
