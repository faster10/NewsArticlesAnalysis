In 2010 , Mostefai had been the subject of a police S file for radicalisation but the state prosecutor said he had never been implicated in any case of a terrorist organisation . Several homegrown terrorists before him had previously been the subject of the same kind of police file before later carrying out horrific attacks , including Mohamed Merah who carried out a gun attack on a Jewish school in Toulouse in 2012 after deadly attacks on soldiers , and Cherif Kouachi , who carried out the Charlie Hebdo attacks with his brother .
