Stars praise Kathy Griffin's "Fashion Police" exit
Last Updated Mar 13, 2015 5:13 PM EDTStars are praising Kathy Griffin for parting ways with "Fashion Police" after just seven episodes. In a tweet, Griffin said her style didn't match up with the E! show's "creative direction." "There is plenty to make fun of in pop culture without bringing people's bodies into it. ... I do not want to use my comedy to contribute to a culture of unattainable perfectionism and intolerance towards difference," she wrote. Her move comes on the heels of controversy surrounding co-host Giuliana Rancic's red-carpet criticism of singer-actress Zendaya's dreadlocks.
