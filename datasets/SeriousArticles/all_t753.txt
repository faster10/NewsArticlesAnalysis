Shania Twain will launch her final tour this summer.Twain announced Wednesday that she would kick off her Rock This Country Tour on June 5 in Seattle. She will perform 48 shows and wrap the tour on Aug. 23 in Fresno, California.It is her first North American tour in over a decade.Twain will perform in New York, Los Angeles and Chicago. She will also visit Canada for shows in Toronto, Vancouver and Montreal.Twain completed a two-year residency at The Colosseum at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas. The singer, whose hits include "You're Still the One" and "That Don't Impress Me Much," is one of the top-selling acts in music.
