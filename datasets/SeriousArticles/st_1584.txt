Mufti Mohammad Sayeed ' s stint in the Home Ministry , at a time when militancy had begun to rear its ugly head in Jammu and Kashmir , would , however , be most remembered for the kidnapping of his third daughter Rubaiya by JKLF . The militants demanded freeing of five of their comrades in exchange for Rubaiya ' s freedom and let her off only after their demand had been met . The kidnapping and subsequent release of the militants , according to Mufti Mohammad Sayeed ' s rivals , projected India as a ' soft state ' for the first time .
