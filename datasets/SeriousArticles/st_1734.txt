How far can the Steelers go with that inconsistent defense ? With Ben Roethlisberger winging it and wondrous receiver Antonio Brown heading a deep cast of playmakers on the other end , Pittsburgh is equipped to engage in any given shoot out . The prolific offense is that good , although the ankle injury of running back DeAngelo Williams provides concern . On the flip side , the Steelers 30th ranked pass defense knows a thing or two about shootouts , too . These are not your father s Steelers , who won championship with a defense nicknamed the Steel Curtain . Now it may be a matter of the Steelers needing to have the ball last .
