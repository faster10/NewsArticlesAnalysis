In the immediate aftermath of the atrocities in Paris on Friday there were reports that the friendly international would be cancelled , had to be cancelled and it was a reaction which was also wholly understandable . But the decision by the French Football Federation to play England is instinctively the correct one . Sport , and football , sport s most popular sport , can be life affirming and connective in a way that few other things can be . Music , maybe , comes closest but it does not have that universally , socially , galvanising effect that belongs to sport . It does not have the same reach or dimension .
