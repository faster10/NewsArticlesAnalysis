Cam has a few more rushing yards than Peyton . Newton has almost five times as many rushing yards as Manning in just five seasons . His 599 attempts have gone for 3 , 207 yards and 43 scores . He has two seasons ( 2011 and 2012 ) with more rushing yards than Peyton has in his career and his 636 rushing yards this year fell just short of Manning ' s career total . In five playoff games , Cam has 176 rushing yards and two scores ( both scores came last week in the NFC title game ) . He has run for more yards than Peyton ' s playoff career rushing total in all but one of his playoff games .
