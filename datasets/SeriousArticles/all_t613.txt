Watch the first teaser for "Lip Sync Battle"
Watching celebrities attempt their best lip-syncing on "The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon" just didn't happen often enough. Soon we'll have an entire show dedicated to the art form.Spike TV unveiled the first trailer for the upcoming series, which finds "Pitch Perfect" star Anna Kendrick taking on Jennifer Lopez's "Booty" -- only to be joined by the "Booty" singer herself. That battle is on!There's also John Krasinski channeling Tina Turner -- short silver dress with sparkly fringe included. And Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson shakes it off for the cameras. That alone is worth a viewing.Hosted by LL Cool J, "Lip Sync Battle" premieres Thursday, April 2 on Spike at 10 p.m. ET.
