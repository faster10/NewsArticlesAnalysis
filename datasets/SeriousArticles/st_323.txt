But to make real progress the US , China and India must direct more publicand private resources to the climate challenge . Less than 2 per cent of global public research and development dollars are spent on renewable energy ? a paltry 5 billion in total . China , the US and India could show global leadership by directing , say , 5 per cent of their public research and development budgets towards climate challenges .
