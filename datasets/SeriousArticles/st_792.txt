The problem is not that the world is running out of oil . It is that it has far more than it can burn while having any hope of limiting the increase in global mean temperatures over the pre industrial levels to 2 . Burning existing reserves of oil and gas would exceed the global carbon budget threefold . Thus , the economics of fossil fuels and of managing climate change are in direct opposition . One must give . Technological change might undermine the economics of fossil fuels . If not , politicians will have to do so .
