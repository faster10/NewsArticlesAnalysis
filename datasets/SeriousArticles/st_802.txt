Chip Kelly gained his fame as coach of the Oregon Ducks . After a run of success at Oregon , he was hired by the Philadelphia Eagles to take the team to the next level . Kelly was the coach of the Eagles for three seasons , taking the team to the NFL Playoffs in 2013 . The team finished with a combined record of 26 21 in those three seasons , but slipped to 6 9 before he got fired during the 2015 NFL season . Kelly has been linked to a number of teams during this offseason , but the San Francisco 49ers rumors have been the most tangible .
