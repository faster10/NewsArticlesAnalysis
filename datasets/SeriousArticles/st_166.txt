Methane rule and new federal leasing policies : In his speech , Obama said he would " push to change the way we manage our oil and coal resources , " a phrase read to describe a long awaited Interior rule on coal and oil leasing on federal land . A source told Andrew and Elana that Interior could introduce a major renovation of the program as soon as next week . Meanwhile , other sources say that the administration might release a methane flaring and venting rule for public lands as soon as Friday , though that could slip .
