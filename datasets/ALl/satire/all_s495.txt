House to File Articles of Impeachment Against Obama
In a shocking move, several Tea Party affiliated members of the House of Representatives have announced that they will formally file Articles of Impeachment against President Barack Hussein Obama. The specific language of the articles has not been publicly released, but they relate primarily to the Benghazi terrorist attack and subsequent cover up, as well as the illegally altered implementation of the Affordable Care Act.
