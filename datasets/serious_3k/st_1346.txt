When McCarthy moved to New York at age 20 , her roommate , Brian Atwood , a friend from home who would go on to become a popular shoe designer , suggested she try stand up . " I really dressed to rival a drag queen , for sure , " she recalls . ( " It was the time of Lady Miss Kier , RuPaul and Lady Bunny , " says Atwood . ) " I had a gold lam swing coat on , a huge wig , big eyelashes , " McCarthy recalls . " I talked about being incredibly wealthy and beautiful and living extravagantly . "
