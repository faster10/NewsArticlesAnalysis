Rosewall also made it clear that he and his family needed the income , which is presumably no longer the case for Federer . Federer is approaching the 100 million mark in career prize money and earns well over 2 million per night for some exhibitions . That is quite a contrast with the 500 that Rosewall said he averaged per night during his professional barnstorming days , when he got a percentage of the gate from tour impresario Jack Kramer . " It ' s mind boggling , " Rosewall said of Federer ' s numbers , pausing for several seconds to consider them further . " Absolutely mind boggling . "
