Such strategic partnership is expected to represent a term of reference for the talks that will take place between the leaders of the two countries . The discussions will cover the bilateral , regional and international issues , including exchanging views on important issues such as threats and challenges facing world peace and security problems like terrorism and nuclear proliferation , and the situation in the Middle East , particularly in Syria , Yemen , and the occupied Palestinian territories . China and Egypt , which are currently members of the United Nations Security Council , maintain close contacts on all such regional and international issues .
