Speaking of ridiculous , it ' s been awhile since Thompson had one of those games where seemingly everything he put up went in . But on the eve of the announcement regarding NBA All Star reserves , Thompson showed why he probably belongs in Toronto on Feb . 14 . He hit 14 of 20 shots from the floor , including 7 of 12 from 3 point range , and also was 10 for 10 from the line . And unlike his career best of 52 , which he set last year as part of his unforgettable 37 point third quarter , his brilliance was more balanced . He scored 12 in the first , 14 in the second , 5 in the third and 14 in the fourth .
