The foundation for Kobe ' s superstardom was built in his time in the Philadelphia area . I was blessed to spend two years coaching him and his teammates , all of whom eventually took on Kobe ' s attitude that there is no room for second place . I have often said that Kobe is the most misunderstood professional athlete in the world . For all of the glitz and glamour of being a Laker and an NBA superstar , he is still the same kid I met at the JCC . He just wants his team to win .
