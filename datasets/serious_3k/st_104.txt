At CES , you ll be spending a lot of time on your feet , so many will argue that the most important item to bring is a pair of comfortable walking shoes . Other useful things to pack might be : mini power strips , spare AA batteries , an Ethernet adapter to make up for the convention hall s terrible Wi Fi , and twice as many business cards as you think you ll need . The goal is to pack as lightly as possible while still bringing enough to survive the war zone that is the largest show floor in history .
