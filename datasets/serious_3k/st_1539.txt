When the breakthrough finally came in the 12th , it was as Royals a run as you ll ever see : manufactured with clockwork efficiency by a seasoned collective . Perez singled to right field off Mets reliever Addison Reed and was spelled by pinch runner Jarrod Dyson , who promptly stole second . Alex Gordon grounded out to the first baseman , moving Dyson over to third . Christian Colon , tabbed to pinch hit for Luke Hochevar despite having not played in nearly a month , drove a frozen rope into the left center gap as Dyson darted home and an eerie hush fell on Citi Field .
