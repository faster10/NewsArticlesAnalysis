The Republican presidential frontrunner ? three words that still chill me to the core ? says he will not participate in this week s debate on Fox News . The decision comes in part , it seems , because of a press release Trump found offensive and , perhaps , because he s not fond of Fox News commentator Megyn Kelly . Kelly took Trump to the mat during the first Republican debate for misogynist language he s used to describe women , like pigs , dogs and fat slobs . Trump later suggested she asked the question because she was menstruating .
