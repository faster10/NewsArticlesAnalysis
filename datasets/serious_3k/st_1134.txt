STP ' s first album , Core , was released in 1992 , peaking at No . 3 and spawning multiple hits " Sex Type Thing , " " Plush , " " Creep , " and " Wicked Garden . " Their MTV Unplugged session in 1993 was in regular rotation , including a debut performance of " Big Empty . " Their second album , Purple , debuted at No . 1 in the U . S . in 1994 . Single " Interstate Love Song " was an instant hit , and " Vasoline " would soon follow .
