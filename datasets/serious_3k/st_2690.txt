From the time he escaped from prison through a tunnel six months ago , authorities began to examine any possible leads in the drug kingpin s network of accomplices . They arrested corrupt prison guards and officials , relatives who handed out bribes and oversaw tunnel construction , and his trusted pilots who flew him to safety in his home state of Sinaloa , reports the Washington Post .
