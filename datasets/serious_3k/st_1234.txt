WASHINGTON ? Boston Medical Center plans to move about 800 workers to the Schraftt Center in Charlestown by 2017 . The hospital said it will move IT staff and its subsidiary BMC Healthnet Plan into nearly 172 , 000 square feet of office space in Charlestown , freeing up space on its South End campus for clinical use and vacating pricier offices in the Back Bay . In terms of square footage , it is one of the largest lease deals signed in the city this year . Partners HealthCare plans to leave the Schrafft Center next year for new offices at Assembly Row in Somerville . ? TIM LOGAN
