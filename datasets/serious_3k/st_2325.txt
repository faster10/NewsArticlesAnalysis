When muscle injuries happened in Munich , Guardiola often accused the team doctors of not working fast enough . " When the docs say a player will be back in eight weeks , I want them back within seven . " Now Guardiola who was never considered to be a deeply emotional part of Bayern Munich feels the power of a club that is built around a group of former world class players like Rummenigge , Matthias Sammer and the soon returning Uli Hoeness . And that in his last season , one thing counts more than anything else : The treble ( or at least to win the Champions League ) stands above everything . For now Guardiola has to admit : " We are not prepared for the Champions League right now " . It is something everyone in Munich expects him to change and quickly .
