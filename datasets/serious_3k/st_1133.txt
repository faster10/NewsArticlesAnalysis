The United States , on the other hand , wants not only to eliminate ISIS , but also to get rid of Assad ' s regime for an eventual transfer of power in Syria . In the wake of the Paris attacks , the international community was hoping for a tighter alliance against ISIS in Syria with a wider coalition . The downed jet incident shows that , once again , this is an incredibly difficult vision to achieve given the complex relations in the region . The close proximity of Russian and NATO planes flying in Syrian skies was already a source of concern , but an incident of these proportions was certainly not expected .
