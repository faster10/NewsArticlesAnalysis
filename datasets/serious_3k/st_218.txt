Oakland Raiders : Justin Tuck became Oakland ' s NFL Man of the Year nominee Wednesday , which is the most prestigious award given to players for their off field work . Tuck has been on the injured reserve for several weeks because of a shoulder injury . However , as a leader in the locker room , Tuck has been around the team , providing mentorship and support , especially during a recent three game losing streak . Even though Tuck is 32 and in the last year of his contract , it ' s his leadership that could cause the Raiders to re sign him . Bill Williamson
