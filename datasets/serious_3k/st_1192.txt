The lead actress lineup includes two time Oscar winner Cate Blanchett , in the title role of the lesbian love story Carol , and Brie Larson , for her Golden Globe winning turn as the imprisoned mom in Room . Jennifer Lawrence earned her fourth nomination in six years , for the Miracle Mop inventor story Joy . Veteran British actress Charlotte Rampling scored her career first Oscar nod for 45 Years ( opening in Philadelphia on Friday ) , and Saoirse Ronan rounds out the five with Brooklyn , playing an Irish immigrant in early 1950s New York with incredible grace and nuance . ( Ronan received her first Oscar nomination for supporting actress in the 2007 release Atonement when she was only 13 . )
