Indonesia has seen attacks by militants before , but a coordinated assault by a team of suicide bombers and gunmen is unprecedented and has echoes of the sieges seen in Mumbai seven years ago and in Paris last November . Australian Attorney General George Brandis told the Australian newspaper he had no doubt Daesh was seeking to establish a footprint in Indonesia . The last major militant attacks in Jakarta were in July 2009 , with bombs at the JW Marriott and Ritz Carlton hotels . The country had been on edge for weeks over the threat posed by militants .
