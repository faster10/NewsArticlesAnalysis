Of course , publishers have been trying to address some of the same issues for some time . For example the game Joy Ride reportedly will allow a game player to almost win a race without actually moving at all . Slack was sceptical about the future of such approaches . " The problem with that is that it gets boring to have to do nothing in order to win a game , when the activity still takes time . There is also the chance that something unfortunate happens and the player ends up frustrated because they still fail to win even after spending a few minutes on it . With our solution we take the player right to the win the moment they want it . "
