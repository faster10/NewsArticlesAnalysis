A master at dealing with eliminating live and lame animals and , normally indirectly , people , THE CHENEY gladly takes on duties that include blasting things and even lawyers to kingdom come . Lame animals and lame people especially bring out the blood lust in the mysterious entity who has only occasionally been seen in the light of day over the last five years . It is rumored in hushed circles that he hibernates in an undisclosed location called THE LAIR that only a few know the location of , waiting for those times when he can be of influence again or in some way satisfy his abnormal lusts .
