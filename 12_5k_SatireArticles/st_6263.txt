Since her first semester on staff at McClintock High , Broderic has repeatedly gone out of her way to nurture disruptive or withdrawn students that nevertheless displayed a certain spark , investing considerable time and energy that reportedly has not resulted in even one enrollment in an institution of higher education . According to Broderic , despite years of working individually with such students during lunch and after school , urging them to submit to the school ' s literary magazine when they didn ' t think they were talented enough , and even helping pay for their school supplies when their families could not afford to do so , she had " absolutely zilch " to show for it .
