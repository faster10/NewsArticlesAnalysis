Harry is perturbed . He needs to rescue his beloved from the clutches of her evil publishing agent and high ranking Freemason Zak Spittle . In order to do this he has first to find his girl who is kept prisoner in a castle in the island of Corsica . Then he must free her mind from the hypnotic spells of Spittle and his cronies and to do all of this . . . he must rediscover his magical powers by undergoing trials and tribulations visited on him by the gods of Olympus . In the end he rescues the damsel in distress and they get married . . . . on Olympus of course with ex Prime MinisterGeorge Brown acting as best man .
