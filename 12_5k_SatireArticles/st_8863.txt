Finley , 58 , whose only UK chart hit to date is ' Because I love you so ' , was rushed off stage by 12 security men who covered the singer with his trademark Vivian Westwood tunic , as the gunmen started firing . Speaking from his hospital bed , which he had paid a roofer friend to have moved from Paris to his mum ' s house in Harlow , he told how that terrible night in Paris had devastated him . Fighting back the tears as he ate a banana recounted how one of 12 security men who had covered and rushed him to safety had unknowingly knocked his sunglasses onto the floor and in all the confusion , stood on them .
