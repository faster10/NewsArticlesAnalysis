Coming from as far away as Iowa , Florida , and 30 miles outside the city , the meandering throngs walked for blocks in every direction , stopping only to eat at caf s and admire the reflecting pool , all the while chanting inspiring slogans like " What a beautiful day " and " We should do this more often " amongst themselves . An energetic group , their intense commitment to stretching their legs could not be denied as map wielding marchers brought traffic to a standstill at several important crosswalks around the National Mall .
