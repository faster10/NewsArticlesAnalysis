Nowhere is the impending retro crisis more apparent , Williams said , than in the area of popular music . " To the true retrophile , disco parties and the like were common 10 years ago . Similarly , retro intelligentsia have long viewed ' New Wave ' and even late ' 80s hair metal retro as pass and no longer amusing as kitsch , " Williams said .
