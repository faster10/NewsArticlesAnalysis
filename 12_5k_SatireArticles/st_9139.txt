The police Chief in Granada said he is willing to negotiate with the Zeta Cartel to save the lives of countless people . He is going to order his men not to interfere with Zeta drug shipments coming into his city and he is going to instruct his men not to harass drug dealers working for the Zeta Cartel . The police Chief wants Zeta Cartel shipments to be marked with a specially designed Z to prevent counterfeiting of the Zeta identification , so the shipments could be allowed through .
