According to event organizers , each Index Finger Convention opens with Yankees Hall of Famer Yogi Berra asking the Key Question , that being : " Baseball mitts were designed with five finger slots , and at some point some players decided to no longer use the section created to encase the forefinger . Why ? " With that in mind , sources said , players use the meeting to explore those facets of forefinger placement that have vexed or intrigued them the most , not only during the previous baseball season , but throughout their entire careers .
