Joyous at the success of the reconciliation of their centuries old disputes , the summit leaders further elaborated on their newfound brotherhood . " We now realize that Christianity , Judaism , and Islam are all simply different denominations of the same spiritual lineage . . . . all of us directly tracing our ancestry back to Abraham . We were all , obviously , worshiping the same Creator back in the days of Abraham , so , of course , we are still worshipping the same God , today . We have just been using different words and symbols to describe our spiritual experiences . All of our bickering has really just arisen from a failure to communicate . "
