Michael Jackson has revealed that his obsession with children is not sexual in nature , but is instead linked to his desire to remain " forever young " . The forty five year old singer , once a child star himself , is said to be devastated by the allegations which assume that his interest in children is anything but benign . However , Jackson assures us that this is simply not the case : that it is not he that is grooming the children , but rather the other way round .
