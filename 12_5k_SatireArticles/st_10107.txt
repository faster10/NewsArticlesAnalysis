The money went missing as the planes landed in Scilly for refueling . With the help of her old client Mr Adnan Khashoggi , Ms . Mills removed the money from the planes . Each time they left the doors unlocked . When the planes reached Iraq , the pilots found the open doors and assumed the money had fallen off in the Mediterranean Sea . It was revealed that due to a fear of the pilots coming across as stupid , and hence potentially damaging their reputation , the White House decided to seal the documents containing information related to the whole transaction .
