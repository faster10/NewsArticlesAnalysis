Like Schoolhouse Rock , catchy songs will be used to enforce the ideals of the nation and the administration . A song per week will be aired on each daily episode and will be taught in music class . Dance steps to that song will be taught in physical education classes . When parents get home and view the mandatory rerun of the show after their evening news , they will also practice singing the song with their children .
