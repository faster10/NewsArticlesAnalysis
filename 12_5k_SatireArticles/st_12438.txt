Over the years , various ' mob tips ' have sent the FBI on hundreds of dead end leads in the search for one of America ' s most colorful union leaders . Some tips bordered on the bizarre , such as one from Anthony ' Fatboy ' Suliletti , a known Brooklyn hitman , who claimed Hoffa was dumped alive from a plane over Antartica . Then again this past week where an 84 year old mobster with dementia claimed Hoffa was encased in cement and molded into a merri go round horse in Asbury Park , New Jersey .
