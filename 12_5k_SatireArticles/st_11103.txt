The two leaders held talks which varied from comparing the size of their chests to bilateral trade in weapons . A Memorandum of Understanding was signed under which a contingent of Storm Troopers will train a newly formed elite regiment of Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh recruits to be known simply as the SS ( Saffron Stormtroopers ) . In return India will transfer highly complex Vedic technologies to the Galactic Empire . Modi also promised the seizing of agricultural land , for GE corporations to set up manufacturing plants and also a GE military base to protect trade routes from terrorism . The two leaders have also instructed their militaries to conduct joint anti terrorism exercises .
