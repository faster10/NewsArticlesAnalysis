" How To Pull Up Your Pants or , No One Wants To See Your Underwear " This show looks at the fashion of wearing pants that are twelve sizes to large ( boys ) or four sizes too small ( girls ) . It will explain that the literal meaning of the word " underwear " is to put this garment beneath your other clothes . Girls will be taught that it is easier to bend over when they are not afraid of splitting their pants or having them slide off . Boys will be shown that having two hands to do things with ( instead of using one to always hold up your pants ) allows you to do many things .
