Brothers ' n Sisters Multi Services is a tax preparation service that operates out of the back of a " dollar store in the Fordham section of the Bronx . The company is owned by Dr Obogu Tsinegti , a Nigerian national who entered the US in 2010 under a temporary student visa . The company is reportedly collecting copies of Social Security cards , state ID cards , federal tax returns , and birth certificates , and as part of the criteria , all government EBT cards must be surrendered , in exchange , people are given a debit card from the National Bank of Nigeria .
