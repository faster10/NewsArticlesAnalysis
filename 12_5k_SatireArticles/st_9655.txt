The documents indicated that in terms of bringing people out to FedEx Field in Landover , Maryland , more than just football games would take place . During halftime , an squadron of AV 8 Harrier " jump jets " which can take and land vertically based on how its jet engine nozzles are directed , could make pinpoint landings on the ten yard lines . Or the Washington team might enter the stadium on a couple of LCAC ( Landing Craft Air Cushion ) vehicles better known as hovercraft . It is also possible that a mock battle between two groups of M1A1 Abrams tanks might take place during a halftime complete with smoke and booms .
