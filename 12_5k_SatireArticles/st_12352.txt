On the television show , animated Barry and his friends ( Jesse , Al , Louis , Billy , Hanoi Janey , Jerry , Henry , George , Malik , Nancy , and Michelle ) live in Chicagoland . They will be overseen by a babysitter , crabby Uncle Joe ( playing somewhat the same role as Roy in the original Mickey Mouse Club ) . Chicagoland is ruled by kindly King Karl and Queen Oprah and is home of Barry ' s favorite sports team , the Chicagoland Marxists .
