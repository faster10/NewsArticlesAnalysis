A few years back , someone in the US Treasury Department decided that vending machine owners , and operators , and manufacturers needed a dollar coin to help run their vending machines at a greater profit . Of course , it also helped the life span of a paper bill because vending machine mechanisms add to the rather fast replacement costs of a typical dollar bill . So , this unnamed person ' contracted with the US Mint to produce 1 Billion dollars worth of the one dollar coins which are almost the same exact size as an existing quarter .
