Mayor Bloom and Colonel Klink watched from the mayor ' s mansion as one donut coffee shop , one of the two fast food restaurants , a few of the pizza parlors and some of the small fine dining cafes , the salted pretzel factory , the ice cream factory and the soda bottling plant went out of business . The increased loss of villager ' s jobs also caused the car dealership auto repair shop to close its doors . Additionally , the number of auto accident law suits against the town increased as a consequence of unsalted roads such that local taxes went up .
