NEW ORLEANS Big Etienne ' s , a popular stop for New Orleans style jambalaya , shrimp po ' boys , and gumbo , has become a near perfect habitat for Penaeus setiferus , the ubiquitous white shrimp used in jambalaya , shrimp po ' boys , and gumbo . " It ' s far too early to call this a bright side , but the restaurant ' s location on the Delta , combined with its rickety , shabby chic fisherman ' s d cor , have combined to create a serviceable ecosystem for this particular species of marine life , " said Juanita Colon of the Federal Department of Fisheries . Colon said if floodwaters recede significantly , many New Orleans parking lots would be suitable locations for the cultivation of dirty rice .
