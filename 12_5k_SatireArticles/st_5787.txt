Because Caf Karma a favorite meeting place for musicians , poets , and visual artists in the area offers 50 cent refills and features large , comfortable chairs , senators on both sides of the aisle considered it a natural venue for overcoming hurdles on a number of controversial issues . One free association exercise led by Sen . Ted Stevens ( R AK ) led to a bipartisan , comprehensive new proposal for stem cell research that features an accompanying theme song . A complete overhaul of the budget appropriations process was also drawn up , though the 128 page bill will have to be copied from napkin to paper form before going to a vote .
