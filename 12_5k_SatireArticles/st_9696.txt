Governmental police and Secret Service agents were called to the scene , but too late . THE CHENEY had now turned his attention and direction towards the White House . The joy of blood red murder gleamed in his eyes . With deep regret the same men who had once served him loyally now had to shoot him down . He was within 60 paces of the home of the President when he bit the pavement . THE CHENEY collapsed to the sidewalk with the look of savage lust still on his face .
