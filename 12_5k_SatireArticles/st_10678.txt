Jackson Hole , WY ( BNSE ) : Mired with increasing signals of an unexpected economic slowdown and the possibility of a double dip recession , the Federal Reserve announced a series of " unconventional " programs it is prepared to undertake in order to stabilize the economy . Standing prominently among various initiatives including further quantitative easing , maintaining record law interest rates , and increased buying of sovereign dept , the Federal Reserve plans to begin selling a full line of vitamin supplements are part of a multi level marketing campaign .
