" Shall we proceed ? Doctor , make your incision . " Doctor Barger held his scalpel and looked at it in an admiring way . Altoids wondered if he ever was going to start and , in his own time , made a perfect sternum incision . Next he called for Hatch Beam Clamps and sponges which Altoids had ready . She watched in spellbound fascination as the beating heart was exposed . She watched intently as Dr Barger used a wide spatula to expertly lift the heart from the cavity and gently irrigated it with saline solution . Then , all at once , he became very strange . From nowhere , he started to sing a popular Neil Diamond song directly at the beating organ .
