Shitzukaka Industrial , a Japanese company known for making millions by extracting heavy metals from human waste in their sewage treatment facilities , set up shop in a small rural town outside the city limits . Investors planned to make money from the sale of the heavy metals extracted , and provide employment for former drug addicts , and those recently released from prison for drug crimes . There was even talk of using some of the money for drug treatment programs . It was one of those win win deals , treatment for treatment , or so they thought
