Further legal action by the MFLA is pending , but they intend to spread the animal waste around to meet the EPA ' s mandated acceptable levels for the 1 , 000 tons per day of animal waste produced in Maryland . Any unused excess animal waste , in accordance with the MMS dictates , will be shipped daily by 18 wheel semi trucks to the animal waste banks located in parking lots at the EPA Washington DC headquarters , the MMMB headquarters located near the State House in Annapolis MD and all RSO facilities located around the state .
