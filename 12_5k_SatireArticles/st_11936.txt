With a team of four medical students , Dr Wright assembled two groups of undergraduate students attending the University of Toronto . One group was observed over a 72 hour period as its subjects engaged in regular sleeping habits the other group ' s subjects stayed awake for the same duration of time . To ensure that no one would fall asleep , test subjects were provided with food , videogames , and blaring Sting remixes . They were not permitted to consume caffeine or to stab themselves in the leg with their pens .
