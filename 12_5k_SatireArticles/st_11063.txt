Confirming the delivery of the summons was the court clerk at the International Court of Justice in The Hague . The case is a long standing patent lawsuit filed by the lawyers solicitors of America , Russia and the European Union ( also known as the plaintiffs ) are in unison against France ( also known as the defendant ) . The plaintiffs are claiming against the defendant for loss of income , false labeling and trademark infringement due to forced endorsement of the name French on products and services readily available in every country .
