The present capitol building , which was completed in 1977 , historically has been criticized as looking ' phallic ' and has been the source of many jokes about ' horney toad old legislators ' for over 30 years . It will be converted to a office complex and leased to various private enterprises in 2015 . One notable future tenant , that has expressed a strong interest in relocating to Tallahasse mainly because of the present state capitol ' s gender specific appearance , is the parent company of Penthouse Magazine which hopes to take possession of office space by 2015 .
