Despite being new to the music scene , Hiddleston has long chronicled his series of emotional and turbulent break ups in his choice of film roles . Friends of the actor have confirmed that his role as Loki in Thor and the Avengers was an expression of grief at the loss of a particularly mischievous lover . Similarly , Hiddleston ' s recent Emmy nominated role in The Night Manager came hot on the heels of the end of his relationship with a night manager . His brief but passionate fling with a Swedish Chef also inspired his cameo appearance in Muppets Most Wanted , projecting the pain he felt .
