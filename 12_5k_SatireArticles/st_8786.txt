ISIS have quickly established themselves as the new bad guy in town . They have been described by a leading expert on Middle Eastern terror groups as " mental , as mad as a box of frogs " . Glowing praise such as this has seen a huge rise in the number of British youngsters finishing University , eschewing the traditional " gap year " and leaving to join the group , who are so dangerous they are rumoured to eat anybody who looks at them " in a funny way " . The internet is now littered with videos of young men threatening to cut your ears off , but until now this has not been taken seriously because they have been doing it in a thick Brummie accent .
