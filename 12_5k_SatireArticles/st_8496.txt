Paraguayan women ' s javelin thrower Marta LaFarta says once she found out that the Olympics was going to be held in London in 2012 she has been trying to throw a javelin in a burqa just in case . " It is very , very hard to see through those tiny little eye holes " , LaFarta explains , " I almost killed my coach because I kept throwing in the wrong direction " . " I accidentally speared a cow the other day because my burqa twisted on me " .
