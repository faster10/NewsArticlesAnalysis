In a press conference yesterday in Los Angeles , representatives of LULAC ( the League of United Latin American Citizens ) , the NAACP ( National Association for the Advancement of Colored People ) , Jesse Jackson ' s Rainbow Coalition , BET ( Black Entertainment Television ) , and Univision ( a U . S . based Hispanic television network ) , all jointly protested the formation of this new network . They vowed to boycott the channel and demonstrate against all of the cable and satellite providers in their areas who aired the new network .
