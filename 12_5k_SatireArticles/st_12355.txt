The village had a single law enforcement traffic officer Lieutenant Harry Klink , as there was no violent crime to speak of . Mayor Bloom immediately promoted Klink to full Colonel in charge of the Bloomsburg Food Police . The colonel restricted the amount of salt and sugar used in cooking , in manufactured products and used to de ice the village ' s roads . Residents were prohibited from consuming more than a single 16 ounce soft drink and pizza vendors were prohibited from delivering two liter bottles of soft drinks with their pizza orders .
