Seven countries scored the maximum ten out of ten , and all were countries in the developed world . They were Germany , UK , Canada , USA , France , Australia and Denmark . Over 40 countries achieved a score of less than two and every country that scored less than five was in Latin America , Africa or Asia . Burkino Faso had the embarrassment of being the only country to score a zero , meaning its citizens lives " are worth less than the arbitrary swatting of a fly " according to Dr Bont .
