The president via an Executive Order will ban all private cars and taxis in downtown areas of all US major cities during a 12 hour work day . Also via Executive Order unemployed adults , welfare recipients , 16 year olds not in school , and people receiving unearned money from the federal government will be required to peddle the vehicles . Vehicle crews will be rotated every four hours during the 12 hour working day . Working people who pay taxes , the sick , disabled and truly needy , bonafide Social Security and Medicare recipients are excluded from peddling . The ACLU filed a civil rights lawsuit .
