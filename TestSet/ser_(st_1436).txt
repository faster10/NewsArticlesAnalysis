The cease fire had been in place since Dec . 15 as tentative peace talks began between Yemen s Saudi backed government and the rebels , but numerous violations had taken place . Now , heavy fighting has resumed , including intensified Saudi airstrikes , one that destroyed a care center for the blind in the capital , Sanaa . Several Saudi strikes have appeared to target hospitals and health centers , including a Doctors Without Borders facility in October . The Houthis have also launched new offensives in several areas this week .
