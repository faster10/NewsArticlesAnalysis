Researchers were surprised when a direct positive relationship was found between the number of tattoos the patient had and the patient ' s perceived fear and hatred of blood draws . The study was conducted on 11 , 000 patients from around the nation , revealing this finding to be statistically significant with p . 05 ( n 11 , 000 ) . Christine Hunt , the lead graduate student researcher stated : " We thought patients with tattoos would be more comfortable around needles and not mind the little pinch in the arm . But we saw quite the opposite . "
