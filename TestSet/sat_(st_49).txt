Several contemporary politicians had already alleged that Lenin worked for Germany , but without presenting any solid proof . Aleksandr Kerensky , Grigory Aleksinsky , and other Russian political opponents , had already accused the Bolsheviks , especially Lenin of being Imperial German agents provocateurs , but presenting forged and inconclusive evidence . British prime minister Winston Churchill had also blamed the Germans for the the rise to power of the Bolsheviks , writing " They transported Lenin in a sealed truck like a plague bacillus from Switzerland into Russia " .
