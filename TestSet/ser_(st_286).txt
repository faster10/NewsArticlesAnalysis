The Central government released Rs 900 crore recently pinning it to flood relief but a top official of the Tamil Nadu government clarified that at least Rs 500 crore of this money has been pending because of " unfair devolution " by the 14th Finance Commission , the rest has been pending for the last two years . Unlike other states , Tamil Nadu prides itself on not rushing to the Central government with a " begging bowl " for funds at the first sign of crisis , the official told Firstpost .
