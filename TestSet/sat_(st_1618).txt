WASHINGTON The Return of the Bushes , a forthcoming independent film , examines how generations of public exposure can shape modern day society and inform popular culture and politics . The documentary style expos highlights an era of dominance that on the surface seems to be fading the result of being " trumped in a manner of speaking . However , despite relatively low poll numbers , all signs point to a healthy comeback , prompting some of today ' s most vocal political and cultural leaders to chime in .
