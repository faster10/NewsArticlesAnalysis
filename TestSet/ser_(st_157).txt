For reasons of realpolitik ? from Moscow s power play in the Middle East to Europe s dependency on Russian gas ? the Government is reluctant to fracture relations irrevocably . Yet now that an official inquiry has reached a quasi judicial finding of guilt against the Russian state , David Cameron must act decisively . Not only should he widen sanctions against named Russians but he should emulate Heath s example by expelling all the diplomats in London suspected of working for the FSB . Anything less will be seen as weak in Moscow .
