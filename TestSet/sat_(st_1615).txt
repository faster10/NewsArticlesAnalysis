William Bruce Jenner has spent his life on the fringes of popularity . Olympic Gold Medal Decathlete in 1976 , his track and field records were over shadowed by Nadia Comaneci ' s Perfect 10 in gymnastics that year , and then his records were beaten the following Olympics . Trying to turn his career into something memorable , he tried acting , and briefly replaced Erik Estrada on the TV Show CHiPs , but nobody watched that season and Mattel never bothered to design an action figure for him .
