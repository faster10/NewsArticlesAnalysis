Meanwhile , Martha Palmer was in a troubling situation of her own . Her book club , which held regular meetings at a friend ' s house , broke out into an all female orgy after it was suggested by a regular attendee that Oprah Winfrey , who had recommended their most recent book , may have been having a lesbian affair with Gayle King , her best friend .
