The Mayo Clinic and Pevco hope to iron out this small issue of misplaced patients over the next few months though both state that losing patients is nothing new to healthcare . But if the system is perfected down the road , they envision a day where interhospital transport can be performed with pneumatic tubes , perhaps eliminating the need of ambulances and life flights . Several major healthcare institutions are excited about the potential and are preparing for future installation of patient pneumatic tubes by soundproofing rooms that may contain carrier receptacles .
