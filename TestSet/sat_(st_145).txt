" It is alleged that the first man to walk on the moon was Neil Armstrong in 1969 , " Erdo an said . " In fact , Muslim space explorers reached our satellite 334 years before that , in 1635 . Everyone knows the story of the famous aviator , Lag ri Hasan elebi , the " Ottoman Rocket Man " , who made the first successful manned rocket flight in 1633 . What you might not know , is that he attempted to reach the moon , two years later , and could very well have succeeded . "
