It is not the first grizzly bear attack in the state of Montana in 2014 , leading experts to fear that the increased proximity between human and brown bears could lead to more and more incidents . A hunter from Stevensville was mauled by a grizzly near Centennial Valley in June and had to be hospitalized in serious conditions . Two visitors hiking on a trail near Canyon Village in the north central part of the Yellowstone National Park in August , accidentally came between a grizzly and its cub and the mom attacked . The pair was treated for bites and claw wounds before being released from the hospital .
