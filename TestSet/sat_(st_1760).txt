According to two EPA officials , who are asking to remain anonymous for fear of losing their jobs , the Environmental Protection Agency is considering introducing a group of new laws in 2015 which would make it a federal crime to chop down a fir tree and bring it into your home . The law would directly apply to the holiday season , spanning from September 1st through March 15th , and violating all of the laws could bring a maximum fine of 15 , 000 , along with a maximum jail sentence of 28 months in a federal prison .
