From his perch as chairman of the U . S . House National Security Subcommittee , U . S . Rep . Ron DeSantis , R Fla . , slammed former U . S . Sec . of State Hillary Clinton after information was released showing she had staff send classified information over unsecure connections . Families of some of the four Americans killed in Benghazi have previously complained that the Clinton emails appear to show her passing along information about the attacks that they claim was denied to them .
