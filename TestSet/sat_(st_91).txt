Marseille A legal battle over the ownership of one of the most famous castles of France , has finally ended this morning with an astonishing result . Louis douard Reille Soult , a homeless man living on the streets of France ' s 2nd largest city and who is suspected by his relatives of having some mental health issues , was finally given the ownership of the property and a large part of the family fortune ( estimated to approximately 230M ) , despite the objections of his siblings , cousins and nephews .
