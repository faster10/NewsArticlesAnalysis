The part of the text that really caught M . Perrucci ' s attention is an episode taking place in the city of Sebaste ( near modern day Nablus , in the West Bank ) . The author first describes the arrival of a great leader in the town with a group of disciples and followers , causing many of the lower class people from neighbouring villages to gather around them . According to Velleius , that great man ' s name was I sous de Nazarenus , a Greco Latin translation of Jesus ' Hebrew name , Yeshua haNotzri .
