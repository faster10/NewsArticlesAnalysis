TECH SLUMP : Yahoo dropped after the struggling Internet company said it would scrap a spinoff of its big stake in the Chinese e commerce giant Alibaba . The stock lost 93 cents , or 2 . 7 percent , to 33 . 91 . Yahoo said it will instead explore breaking off the rest of its business into a new company . Other big tech names also fell . Apple , the world ' s most valuable publicly traded company , dropped 3 . 05 , or 2 . 6 percent , to 115 . 21 and Microsoft fell 1 . 01 , or 1 . 8 percent , to 54 . 79 .
