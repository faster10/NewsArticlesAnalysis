The spokesman said a massive gathering of people in Indian occupied Kashmir for Hurriyat leadership is a stamp of approval for the realisation of the right to self determination by the Kashmiri people as per the UN resolutions . The Indian authorities have detained key Kashmiri leaders to prevent them from holding a protest rally during an upcoming visit by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi to Held Kashmir . The Kashmiri leaders had called for a march by a million people near Modi s public meeting venue in Srinagar on Saturday , challenging India s sovereignty over the disputed region .
