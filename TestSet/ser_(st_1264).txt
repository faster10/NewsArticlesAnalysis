The Packers will hit the road in the first round of the playoffs , taking on Washington next weekend in the wild card round . However , while the matchup may sound like a favorable one Washington ' s offense has been producing at a high level over the last few weeks , as quarterback Kirk Cousins has been one of the league ' s best down the stretch . Meanwhile , the Packers have struggled in the playoffs away from Green Bay in recent years , and haven ' t won a road game in the postseason since 2010 .
