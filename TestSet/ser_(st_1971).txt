With Jackson as their offensive coordinator , the Bengals had the NFL ' s sixth best rushing attack in 2014 and the league ' s seventh best scoring offense in 2015 . Formational and schematic creativity has been the hallmark of his offense . At times , he had receiver Mohamed Sanu throwing passes , including a 14 yard touchdown pass to quarterback Andy Dalton . He regularly lined up extra offensive linemen into heavy sets to get the running game ignited , and sometimes threw to those linemen who were eligible receivers .
