" We were just standing around , getting ready to light a cross , when all of a sudden , Trump appears at the podium , and starts talking to us about how we can make America great again , " said Tommy Garrison , who is the Grand Imperial Wizard of the Hatfield Park , Virginia chapter of the Ku Klux Klan . " It was the damndest thing . There we were , about to set fire to a symbol of racially motivated terrorism , hatred , and violence , and who should appear but the candidate most willing to trade on those things for votes , " Garrison said in amazement .
