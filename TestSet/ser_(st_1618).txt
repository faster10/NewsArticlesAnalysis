Travel is so important in its capacity to expand the mind . It ' s exciting to start as young as possible you get to see how other cultures live , challenge your senses and try different cuisines . Travel also shows you the diverse wildlife we share this planet with and gives you perspective on just how huge the Earth is . My first time to Rome was when I was backpacking with my best friend around Europe for a month at 18 years old , so I remember that excitement of being away from home properly for the first time .
