The wife of George Halas , Virginia McCaskey , 91 , expressed the need for the deal after the " most shameful season in Bears History , " as she channeled her deceased husband . Virginia McCaskey participates in a weekly seance on Monday afternoons during the season ( Tuesdays when the Bears play on Monday Night ) and once a month during the off season in the depths of Halas Hall . She revealed that Papa Bear is still grumbling over the 55 14 drubbing the Bears suffered in a November game at Green Bay .
