It was well known throughout the 60 ' s , 70 ' s , 80 ' s and 90 ' s that the iconic jam band had no problem with concert goers recording live performances and trading them amongst themselves . But now , in lieu of Taylor Swift ' s ability to strong arm Apple Corp . , the band has seen the light in regards to their publishing legacy , and will begin cracking down on Dead Heads who don ' t register their bootlegs with ASCAP The American Society of Composers and Producers .
